## Methodology

> **Note**: This report uses AI-generated descriptions and analysis. While we strive for maximum accuracy, AI can make mistakes. Please verify all information against official sources.

This report was generated by an automated research tool designed to identify and document Zionist financial influence in California politics.

1. **Data Acquisition**: Donor information is scraped directly from the [California Secretary of State's PowerSearch website](https://powersearch.sos.ca.gov/) and retrieved via the [Federal Election Commission (FEC) API](https://www.fec.gov/data/). Behested payment data is retrieved from official [FPPC behested payment records](https://www.fppc.ca.gov/search-filings/form-800-series-search/form-803/).
2. **Consolidation**: Contributor names and employers are normalized to group variations (e.g., 'Sandberg, Sheryl' and 'Sheryl Sandberg') into single, accurate entities.
3. **Identification**: Gemini AI (2.5 Flash and Pro) is used to analyze contributors. It is strictly instructed to only identify individuals or organizations with documented, active support for Israel or Zionist advocacy, specifically excluding general Jewish communal or religious associations.
4. **Verification**: For every identified supporter, the tool performs a grounded search using Google Search to generate detailed reports. These reports require precise deep-links to verifiable evidence (financial reports, news articles, leadership rosters) and purge any claims that cannot be resolved to a specific source.
