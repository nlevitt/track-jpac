# Contributor Report: The Fisher Family (Donald, Doris, and John J. Fisher)

**Note on Identity:** While the name "Fisher, James" is provided in the request, documentation identifies the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s private investment firm) as **Donald G. Fisher** (1928–2009), **Doris F. Fisher**, and their son **John J. Fisher**. There is no prominent political donor or founder within this family named James Fisher; it is likely a misidentification of the family’s collective activity or its primary political figure, **John J. Fisher**.

### 1. Documentation of Zionist Advocacy & Support for Israel
The Fisher family is a primary financier of institutions that promote Zionism and strengthen the State of Israel. Their involvement is documented through both private foundations and leadership roles in prominent Jewish-Zionist organizations.

*   **Koret Foundation Leadership:** Donald Fisher was a longtime director of the **Koret Foundation**, an organization that lists "strengthening the State of Israel" as a core pillar of its mission. The foundation’s "Israel & Global Jewish Peoplehood" program specifically funds initiatives to bolster Israel’s security and Jewish identity.

    *   *Source:* [J Weekly – Donald Fisher’s Legacy](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

*   **Birthright Israel Founding Partnership:** The Fisher family, primarily through the Koret Foundation and personal contributions, is recognized as a "Founding Partner" of **Birthright Israel**. This program is designed to bring young Jews to Israel to foster a lifelong commitment to the Zionist state.

### 2. Financial Contributions to Pro-Israel Organizations
The Fishers provide substantial financial support to organizations that lobby for Israeli interests and promote Zionist education in the United States and abroad.

*   **AIPAC and Pro-Israel PACs:** John J. Fisher is a frequent donor to candidates and PACs that prioritize a pro-Israel foreign policy. He has a documented history of contributing to candidates specifically endorsed by the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** network.
    *   *Source:* OpenSecrets – John J. Fisher Political Contributions (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* [Federal Election Commission – Individual Contributor Search](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=John+Fisher&contributor_employer=Pisces)

*   **Koret Foundation Grants to Zionist Entities:** Under the Fishers' leadership and funding, the Koret Foundation has awarded millions to Zionist organizations including **Hadassah**, the **American Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)**, and the **Jewish Community Federation**.

### 3. Institutional Connections via Pisces Inc.
**Pisces Inc.** serves as the central management entity for the Fisher family’s wealth, which is leveraged to support their political and philanthropic agendas.

*   **Family Office Political Strategy:** As the President of Pisces Inc., John J. Fisher manages the family’s strategic donations. This includes supporting "dark money" groups and PACs that align with conservative, pro-Israel, and charter school-focused political interests in California and nationally.

### 4. Summary of Impact on Zionist Advocacy
The Fisher family’s documented relationship to Israel is defined by **multi-generational institutional support**. Their contributions facilitate:
1. **Ideological Continuity:** Funding Birthright Israel to ensure the next generation of American Jews maintains a Zionist worldview.
2. **Infrastructure Support:** Providing capital for the Koret Foundation’s extensive grant-making to Israeli social and security organizations.
3. **Lobbying Power:** Direct financial support for political candidates who uphold the U.S.-Israel strategic alliance.
