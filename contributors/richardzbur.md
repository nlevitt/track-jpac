# Contributor Report: Zbur, Richard

Richard Zbur is a member of the California State Assembly representing the 51st District. His political activity is characterized by explicit alignment with pro-Israel advocacy and the advancement of legislation designed to counter anti-Zionist sentiment in educational and public settings.

### 1. Documented Support for Israel and Zionism
Zbur self-identifies as "strongly pro-Israel" on his official campaign platform, where he pledges to combat activities he characterizes as "anti-Semitic" on college campuses. 
* **Official Campaign Policy:** [Priorities | Rick Chavez Zbur for State Assembly](https://www.rickchavezzbur.com/priorities)

Following the October 7, 2023, Hamas-led attacks, Zbur issued a statement affirming that he stands "firmly and unequivocally with Israel and its right to defend itself." In this communication, he defined Hamas as an organization "intent upon the destruction of the Jewish State" and called for critics of Israel's military response to "look inward and reevaluate."
* **Official Press Release:** [A Message from Assemblymember Rick Chavez Zbur on the Terrorist Attacks in Israel](https://a51.asmdc.org/press-releases/20231010-message-assemblymember-rick-chavez-zbur-terrorist-attacks-israel)

### 2. Legislative Advocacy and "Guardrails" on Ethnic Studies
Zbur is an associate member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, a body dedicated to promoting the interests of the State of Israel and countering anti-Zionist narratives. 
* **Membership Documentation:** [Assemblymember Rick Chavez Zbur Joins CA Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://a51.asmdc.org/press-releases/20230201-assemblymember-rick-chavez-zbur-joins-ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)

He has authored and co-sponsored key legislation focused on educational curricula:
* **AB 715:** Co-authored by Zbur, this bill creates an "Antisemitism Prevention Coordinator" for K-12 schools. According to critics and caucus descriptions, the bill establishes standards that classify certain "anti-Israel" narratives as discriminatory conduct and prohibits comparisons between the State of Israel and Nazi Germany.
* **AB 1468:** Zbur co-authored this legislation to impose "guardrails" on high school ethnic studies. Advocacy groups such as CodePink and the Council on American-Islamic Relations (CAIR) argue the bill is designed to "censor Palestinian voices" by restricting the curriculum to a "domestic-only" framework that excludes global perspectives on colonialism.
* **Sources:** [California lawmakers unite on bill to fight antisemitism in schools | JPost](https://www.jpost.com/diaspora/antisemitism/article-803978); [CA Israel Lobby Aims to Police Ethnic Studies | CodePink](https://www.codepink.org/ab1468)

### 3. Institutional Relationships and Recognition
Zbur maintains documented relationships with several prominent pro-Israel and Zionist organizations:
* **Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN):** ICAN has publicly supported Zbur's calls for federal investigations into university protests. 
* **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** Zbur is graded and endorsed by the California affiliate of DMFI, an organization dedicated to maintaining support for Israel within the Democratic Party.
* **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC):** Zbur has consistently championed JPAC’s priority legislative packages, which include funding for security grants for Jewish institutions and initiatives to combat BDS (Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions).
* **Sources:** [ICAN Supports Assemblymember Rick Chavez Zbur's Call for Investigation](https://icanaction.org/ican-supports-assemblymember-rick-chavez-zburs-call-for-a-federal-civil-rights-investigation-into-ucla/); [Elections - California Jewish Democrats (DFI Affiliate)](https://www.dfi-ca.org/elections)

### 4. Response to Student Protests
In April 2024, Zbur formally requested a federal civil rights investigation into the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) regarding its response to pro-Palestinian student encampments. He specifically cited instances where students who identified as "Zionist" were denied access to certain campus areas by demonstrators, characterizing these actions as violations of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act.
* **Formal Letter to Dept. of Education:** [Assemblymember Zbur Requests Investigation into Potential Violations of Title VI](https://a51.asmdc.org/press-releases/20240430-assemblymember-zbur-requests-investigation-potential-violations-title-vi)

CAIR-LA condemned Zbur’s response as "one-sided," alleging that he failed to acknowledge violence perpetrated against pro-Palestinian protesters and that his rhetoric "conflates antisemitism with legitimate criticism of the state of Israel."
* **Critical Documentation:** [CAIR-LA Condemns Assemblymember Zbur's Response to UCLA Violence](https://www.cair.com/press_releases/cair-la-condemns-assemblymember-zburs-response-to-recent-violence-against-ucla-anti-genocide-student-encampment/)
