# Contributor Report: Fisher, Harriet

**Note on Identification:** While the name provided is "Fisher, Harriet," the identifiers "(Gap Inc./Pisces Inc. founders)" and "(associated with Koret Foundation and Birthright Israel)" correspond to the **Doris and Donald Fisher** family, the co-founders of Gap Inc. No prominent donor or founder of Gap Inc. is named Harriet Fisher; however, the Fisher family's philanthropic activities through their investment vehicle, **Pisces, Inc.**, and the **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund**, are extensively documented in relation to the entities cited.

### Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Support for Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a significant donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the Jewish state. 
*   In the **Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor**, "Stacey and David Fisher" (family members) and the "Koret Foundation" (with which the Fishers are closely associated) are listed as major supporters at the $10,000+ level.
    *   Source: Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (via Issuu) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   The late Donald Fisher and his wife Doris Fisher were long-time financial supporters of the program, which is described as a "gift" aimed at fostering a "lasting connection to Israel."
    *   Source: Birthright Israel Foundation - Mission and Impact (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### 2. Association with the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family maintains a long-standing association with the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based grantmaking organization that has awarded over $1 billion to various causes, including significant funding for initiatives in Israel and Jewish life in the Bay Area.
*   The Koret Foundation identifies one of its core priorities as "strengthening the Jewish community at home and abroad," with a specific focus on projects in Israel.
    *   [Source: Koret Foundation - About the Foundation](https://koret.org/about/)
*   Donald Fisher was a close associate and fellow philanthropist within the same San Francisco circles that manage and fund the Koret Foundation's pro-Israel and Jewish community projects.
    *   [Source: JWeekly - Gap founder Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

#### 3. Political and Philanthropic Support via Pisces, Inc.
The family’s financial and investment office, **Pisces, Inc.**, serves as a primary vehicle for their political and philanthropic contributions. 
*   Doris Fisher and her sons (Robert, John, and William) have utilized Pisces, Inc. and associated funds to support organizations and political action committees (PACs) that advocate for conservative and pro-Israel causes.
    *   [Source: Forbes - Doris Fisher Profile](https://www.forbes.com/profile/doris-fisher/)
*   In 2019, it was revealed that Doris Fisher and her sons donated nearly **$9 million** to **Americans for Job Security**, a non-profit group that engaged in political advocacy.
    *   Source: Wikipedia - Doris F. Fisher (Political Views) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### 4. Community Leadership and Advocacy
The Fisher family are prominent members of **Congregation Emanu-El** in San Francisco, one of the oldest synagogues in California, which is actively involved in Israel advocacy and Zionist programming.
*   The Fishers have been major donors to the **Jewish Community Center of San Francisco (JCCSF)**, including a $1 million gift for its capital campaign and the donation of significant artworks. The JCCSF is a central hub for Jewish life and Israel-related cultural advocacy in the region.
    *   [Source: JWeekly - Donald Fisher Obituary and Community Contributions](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
*   Doris and Donald Fisher’s philanthropy often overlaps with other prominent Zionist donors in the Bay Area, such as the Taube and Koret families, to support the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which allocates millions to Israeli social and security programs.
    *   [Source: California Museum - Doris & Donald Fisher Biography](https://californiamuseum.org/inductee/doris-donald-fisher/)
