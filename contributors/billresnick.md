This report examines the political and philanthropic activities of Bill Resnick, focusing on his documented support for Israeli-related organizations and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Resnick, Bill

### Background and Identity
Bill Resnick (William Resnick), MD, MBA, is a Los Angeles-based psychiatrist, philanthropist, and mindfulness teacher. He is the son of Stewart and Lynda Resnick, the billionaire co-owners of The Wonderful Company. While his parents are known for high-profile donations to traditional Israeli institutions such as the Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) and the National Library of Israel, Bill Resnick’s advocacy is primarily channeled through organizations that identify as "pro-Israel, pro-peace" or focus on international human rights within a Jewish context.

*   **Professional Background:** Resnick is a psychiatrist who completed his residency at UCLA and currently serves on the advisory board of the UCLA Resnick Neuropsychiatric Hospital.
    *   [Source: UCLA Institute of the Environment and Sustainability](https://www.ioes.ucla.edu/person/bill-resnick/)
*   **Family Context:** He is a member of the Resnick family, whose philanthropic foundation has documented histories of providing millions in funding to the IDF and the American Friends of IDC (Herzliya), an influential Israeli think tank with military ties.
    *   [Source: Monthly Review Online - Resnick Family Funding](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)

### Relationship to J Street and Zionist Advocacy
Bill Resnick is documented as a significant supporter of J Street, a prominent advocacy group that describes itself as "pro-Israel, pro-peace." J Street’s primary mission is to promote a two-state solution and advocate for U.S. diplomatic leadership in the Middle East.

*   **Major Donor Identification:** Resnick has been identified as a major contributor to J Street and its educational arm, the J Street Education Fund (JSEF). These organizations advocate for political solutions to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict and frequently lobby the U.S. government on policies regarding Israel.
    *   [Source: J Street 2023 Financial Statement (Lists Resnick-affiliated family/donors)](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/J-Street-2023-Consolidated-Financial-Statements.pdf)
*   **Liberal Zionist Alignment:** Resnick’s support for J Street aligns with "Liberal Zionism," a perspective that supports the existence of Israel as a Jewish and democratic state while frequently criticizing the expansion of settlements and advocating for Palestinian sovereignty.
    *   [Source: J Street Mission and Principles](https://jstreet.org/about/our-mission-and-principles/)

### Involvement with American Jewish World Service (AJWS)
Beyond J Street, Resnick has held long-standing leadership roles in the American Jewish World Service (AJWS), a nonprofit organization that leverages Jewish philanthropy to support human rights and development in the Global South and Israel.

*   **Board Leadership:** Resnick served on the AJWS Board of Trustees for a decade (2012–2022) and held the position of Board Secretary. In this capacity, he influenced the organization's strategic direction, which includes advocacy for civil and political rights in international contexts.
    *   [Source: AJWS 2015 Annual Report - Board Listing](https://ajws.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2015_annual_report.pdf)
    *   [Source: AJWS Trustee Transition Documentation](https://ajws.org/story/bill-resnick-and-michael-stubbs/)
*   **Philanthropic Contributions:** Resnick and his husband, Michael Stubbs, are consistently listed as "Champions" ($100,000–$249,999) or higher in AJWS donor tiers. Their funding supports AJWS's mission, which is rooted in the Jewish tenet of *tikkun olam* (repairing the world) and involves advocacy for global justice.
    *   [Source: AJWS 2022 Annual Report - Supporters](https://ajws.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/AJWS_Annual_Report-2022-Supporters.pdf)

### Additional Political and Advocacy Giving
Resnick's political giving reflects a consistent focus on progressive Jewish and humanitarian causes.

*   **Human Rights and Policy:** He is a documented donor to Human Rights Watch (HRW), which has frequently published reports critical of Israeli government policies in the Palestinian territories.
    *   [Source: Human Rights Watch 2018 Annual Report - Donor List](https://www.hrw.org/sites/default/files/supporting_resources/2018_annual_report.pdf)
*   **Domestic Political Influence:** Resnick also contributes to the Democratic National Committee (DNC) and other Democratic fundraising committees, often alongside other pro-Israel donors.
    *   [Source: DNC Donor Overview - Scribd Archive](https://www.scribd.com/document/559419139/DNC-DONOR-LIST)
