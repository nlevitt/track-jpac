# Contributor Report: Re-Elect Scott Wiener for State Senate 2024

The committee **Re-Elect Scott Wiener for State Senate 2024** is the primary campaign vehicle for California State Senator Scott Wiener (D-San Francisco). Wiener has maintained a documented record of active pro-Israel advocacy, legislative support for Zionist organizations, and public defense of the State of Israel’s sovereignty and security.

## 1. Leadership in Zionist and Jewish Advocacy
Scott Wiener has held high-level leadership roles within organized Jewish political structures in California that prioritize the U.S.-Israel relationship.

*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** Wiener served as the **Co-Chair** of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus for the 2023–2024 session ([ca.gov](https://sd11.senate.ca.gov/news/20230106-jewish-caucus-announces-leadership-2023-2024-legislative-session)). In this capacity, he led efforts to secure state funding for Jewish institutions and advocated for legislation that aligns with the policy goals of major Zionist organizations.
*   **Legislative Priority Packages:** Under Wiener's leadership, the Jewish Caucus introduced "Priority Bill Packages" in 2024 that focused on combating "anti-Israel narratives" in education. This included **AB 2925**, which mandates that antisemitism be included in Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) training, and **SB 1277**, which enshrines the California Teachers Collaborative for Holocaust and Genocide Education ([ca.gov](https://sd11.senate.ca.gov/news/20240520-jewish-caucus-announces-2024-priority-legislation)).

## 2. Documented Support for Israel and Zionism
Wiener has consistently identified himself as a supporter of the State of Israel and has actively opposed movements seeking to delegitimize it.

*   **Personal Advocacy:** Wiener has explicitly stated, "I unequivocally believe in the importance of a Jewish state" and has described his advocacy as rooted in the need for a "safe haven for the Jewish people" ([ca.gov](https://sd11.senate.ca.gov/news/20210516-senator-wiener%E2%80%99s-statement-escalating-violence-israel-and-palestine)).
*   **Opposition to BDS:** Wiener has a history of supporting anti-BDS (Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions) measures, including co-authoring **AB 2844**, which prohibits state contracts with entities that boycott Israel ([ca.gov](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2016-10-04-governor-signs-anti-bds-bill)).
*   **Adoption of the IHRA Definition:** Wiener and the Jewish Caucus successfully advocated for the incorporation of the **International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA)** definition of antisemitism into California's Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Wiener#California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)). The IHRA definition includes certain criticisms of Israel as examples of antisemitism, a position supported by major Zionist organizations like the ADL and AIPAC.

## 3. Direct Engagement and Delegations
The 2024 campaign committee supported a candidate who engaged in direct diplomatic and witness-bearing activities in Israel.

*   **2024 Israel Delegation:** In February 2024, during his re-election cycle, Wiener participated in a delegation trip to Israel sponsored by the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC) Bay Area** and the **Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles** ([sacbee.com](https://www.sacbee.com/news/politics-government/capitol-alert/article285465227.html)).
*   **High-Level Meetings:** During the 2024 trip, Wiener met with **Israeli President Isaac Herzog** and visited sites of the October 7th attacks, such as Kibbutz Kfar Aza, to "bear witness" and strengthen ties between California and Israel ([ca.gov](https://sd11.senate.ca.gov/news/20240215-la-area-legislators-join-trip-israel-tensions-over-gaza-war-grow-home)).
*   **Funding Ties:** The JCRC and Jewish Federation paid nearly **$82,000** to send the delegation, which included Wiener, to Israel to foster "long partnership[s] on issues such as agriculture, technology and innovation" ([calmatters.org](https://calmatters.org/politics/2025/09/california-legislators-travel-2024/)).

## 4. Relationship to Pro-Israel Organizations
Wiener’s 2024 campaign and legislative record show a symbiotic relationship with established pro-Israel lobbying and community groups.

*   **JCRC Alignment:** The JCRC Bay Area has frequently highlighted Wiener as a key ally, stating they are "unwavering in support for Israel’s right to defend its citizens" and working closely with Wiener to amplify this messaging ([jcrc.org](https://www.jcrc.org/post/bay-area-united-with-israel/)).
*   **Budgetary Support:** Wiener has been instrumental in securing over **$240 million** in state budget funding between 2021 and 2025 for Jewish community priorities, which include the **Nonprofit Security Grant Program** used to protect synagogues and Jewish community centers—a top priority for Zionist advocacy groups ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Wiener#California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)).
