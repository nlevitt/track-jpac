# Contributor Report: FISHER, JODI

### 1. Corporate and Philanthropic Identity
The subject is a member of the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment firm and family office). The family’s philanthropic activity is primarily channeled through the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund** and the **Pisces Foundation**, which maintain deep institutional ties to pro-Israel and Zionist organizations.

*   **Gap Inc. / Pisces Inc. Origins:** The Fisher family holds approximately **33% of Gap Inc. stock**, giving them de facto control over the retail multinational. Source: Matrix BCG (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy
The Fisher family is identified as a "visionary" supporter of Zionist projects, specifically focused on strengthening the connection between the Jewish diaspora and the State of Israel.

#### Support for Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is a prominent, long-term donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that provides free 10-day trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit mission of "transforming the Jewish future" and "strengthening connections with Israel."
*   **Donor Level:** The **Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation** and the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund** (the family's primary charitable vehicle) are documented as contributors at the **$1,000,000 and above** level (Visionaries Society) in recent annual reports. Source: Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Mission Alignment:** Birthright Israel describes its work as "cementing [participants'] relationship to their country [Israel]." [Source: M&M Fisher Foundation](https://mmfisher.org/grant-partner/birthright-israel-foundation/)

#### Leadership in the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family exerts significant influence over the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a major focus on supporting the State of Israel. 
*   **Board Leadership:** **Robert J. Fisher** (son of Gap founders Donald and Doris Fisher) serves as a trustee on the Board of Directors of the Koret Foundation. Source: Koret Foundation Board of Directors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pro-Israel Grantmaking:** The Koret Foundation has granted tens of millions of dollars to Israeli institutions, including **Tel Aviv University**, the **Israel Museum**, and **Hillel International**, as well as projects aimed at bolstering Israel’s global image. [Source: Koret Foundation - Israel Grants](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)

### 3. Political and Advocacy Context
The Fisher family’s contributions often align with political figures and organizations that prioritize unconditional U.S. support for Israel.
*   **AIPAC and Lobbying:** Members of the Fisher family have been historically associated with **AIPAC** (the American Israel Public Affairs Committee) through both direct donations and participation in high-level advocacy events. [Source: Ynet News](https://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4984180,00.html)
*   **San Francisco Jewish Community Federation:** The family is a "pillar" of the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund** of San Francisco, which provides significant financial and structural support to Zionist initiatives in Northern California and Israel. [Source: J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
