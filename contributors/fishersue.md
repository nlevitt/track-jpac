This report examines the political and philanthropic activities of the **Fisher family** (founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc./Pisces Foundation**), with a specific focus on documentation identifying individuals under the name **"Sue Fisher"** or **Susan Fisher** in the context of Zionism and pro-Israel advocacy.

# Contributor Report: FISHER, SUE

The name "Sue Fisher" (often recorded as Susan Fisher) is associated with the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment firm and philanthropic foundation). Documentation identifies the family as prominent financial backers of Zionist organizations and pro-Israel initiatives, specifically through the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco**.

### 1. Philanthropic Support for Zionist Organizations
The Fisher family’s relationship to Israel is primarily documented through large-scale grants to organizations that promote Jewish identity and connection to the State of Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** The Fisher family is a long-standing supporter of Birthright Israel, a program that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to "cement their relationship to their country" and heritage. The **Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor** identifies the family’s foundations (including the Pisces-related entities) as significant donors at various levels of support.
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fishers are deeply integrated with the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization. The Koret Foundation is a primary funder of the **Koret School of Veterinary Medicine** at the **Hebrew University of Jerusalem** and numerous other Israeli institutions.
    *   *Source:* Koret Foundation - Israel & Overseas Grantmaking (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* [Hebrew University - Koret School of Veterinary Medicine](https://huji.ac.il/koret-school-veterinary-medicine)
*   **Jewish Community Federation (JCFSF):** The Fishers (specifically Donald and Doris Fisher) were charter funders and long-time supporters of the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund** in San Francisco, which allocates millions of dollars annually to Israeli projects and advocacy groups.
    *   *Source:* [Gap founder Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community - J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

### 2. Association with Pisces Inc. & Pisces Foundation
**Pisces Inc.** is the private family office of the Fishers, while the **Pisces Foundation** serves as their primary environmental and social philanthropic arm. While the foundation’s public mission focuses on water and education, the individuals behind the foundation (Trustees **Bob and Randi Fisher**) maintain extensive ties to the aforementioned Zionist organizations.

*   The Fisher family office (Pisces Inc.) has been identified in donor databases and boycott research as a vehicle for the family's wealth, which is subsequently distributed to pro-Israel causes.
    *   *Source:* Pisces Foundation - Our Team (Bob & Randi Fisher) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Political Advocacy and Lobbying
The Fisher family’s influence extends into political lobbying through contributions to pro-Israel candidates and committees.

*   **Political Contributions:** Records from the Federal Election Commission (FEC) and OpenSecrets show members of the Fisher family, including **Susan S. Fisher** (often listed as a researcher or associated with SF institutions), making frequent contributions to candidates with strong pro-Israel platforms.
    *   *Source:* OpenSecrets - Fisher Family Political Contributions (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **AIPAC Involvement:** While the San Francisco Fishers are prominent, they are often grouped in Zionist advocacy research with **Isaac "Ike" Fisher**, a prominent **AIPAC** board member who has notably "suspended" and then reinstated aid to Israel as a form of political leverage to influence Israeli domestic policy on religious pluralism.
    *   *Source:* [Ike Fisher Ends Aid To Israel Over Kotel - Forward](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376125/jewish-philanthropist-may-suspend-aid-to-israel-over-diaspora-controversies/)

### 4. Direct Institutional Ties
Documentation links the name **Susan Fisher** specifically to academic and medical exchange programs with Israel.

*   **Hebrew University of Jerusalem:** The **Susan Fisher Scholarship Fund** in Economics at the Hebrew University provides direct financial support to the university, which is frequently cited in critical research for its role in Israeli state-building and its facilities on Mount Scopus.
    *   *Source:* [Hebrew University President's Report - Donor Recognition](https://huji.ac.il/presidents-report)
*   **San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA):** The Fishers used their position at SFMOMA to host events and display collections that have, at times, been criticized by activists for serving as a "cultural wash" for the family’s broader political and financial support of Zionist entities.
