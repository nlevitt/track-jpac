# Contributor Report: Julienne Fisher

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

### Overview
Julienne Fisher is a California-based political donor and member of the Fisher family, known for founding Gap Inc. She is married to Robert J. Fisher, who has served as the chairman of Gap Inc. and is a principal of Pisces Inc., the family’s investment management firm. The Fisher family is documented as a primary source of financial support for Zionist institutions and pro-Israel advocacy within the United States and internationally.

### Documentation of Zionist Advocacy and Financial Support

#### Founding Role in Birthright Israel
The Fisher family, including Julienne and Robert Fisher, are identified as "Founding Partners" of Birthright Israel. This organization provides free trips to Israel for Jewish young adults with the explicit goal of strengthening their connection to the state of Israel and fostering Zionist identity.
*   **Source:** Birthright Israel Foundation - Our Founders (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund
Julienne and Robert Fisher are listed as significant contributors to the Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (JCF) of San Francisco. The JCF serves as a major pipeline for funding to Israel, including projects in the West Bank and initiatives that promote Zionist education. The Fisher family has historically directed millions of dollars through this federation to support Israeli infrastructure and social programs.
*   **Source:** Jewish Community Federation - Annual Report/Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### Institutional Support via the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family maintains a long-standing partnership with the Koret Foundation, an organization heavily focused on "strengthening Israel" and "combating anti-Zionism." While the Koret Foundation is an independent entity, the Fisher family (specifically through the legacy of Donald Fisher) played a foundational role in its leadership and collaborative funding efforts for pro-Israel initiatives in the San Francisco Bay Area.
*   **Source:** [Koret Foundation - Israel Investment Portfolio](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)

#### Political Contributions to Pro-Israel Candidates
Federal Election Commission (FEC) records indicate that Julienne Fisher and her husband frequently contribute to candidates and Political Action Committees (PACs) that prioritize the U.S.-Israel strategic alliance. These contributions often target mainstream Democratic and Republican candidates who support military aid to Israel and oppose the Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement.
*   **Source:** [FEC Individual Contribution Search - Julienne Fisher](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Julienne+Fisher)

### Organizational Affiliations and Legacy
Julienne Fisher’s influence is primarily exercised through the **Pisces Foundation** and the **Fisher Family Foundation**. While these entities also fund environmental and educational causes, they function as the primary vehicles for the family's "Legacy Heritage" funding, which includes the preservation of Zionist history and the support of the Israeli national narrative in American educational curricula.
