# Contributor Report: Larry Klein

Larry Klein is the current Mayor of Sunnyvale, California, and a documented participant in various Israel-related advocacy organizations and initiatives in the Silicon Valley region. His involvement is characterized by leadership roles in communal organizations that explicitly advocate for Israel and the signing of public statements in support of Zionist objectives.

### 1. Leadership in Israel Advocacy Organizations
Klein has served on the **Board of Directors of the Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC) of Silicon Valley**, the public affairs arm of the Jewish Federation of Silicon Valley (now Jewish Silicon Valley) ([Source](https://jcfny.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/JCF-2018-Annual-Report.pdf)). The JCRC of Silicon Valley explicitly defines its mission as advocating for the rights and protection of the Jewish community, both locally and internationally, with a central focus on "solid support for Israel" ([Source](https://issuu.com/jweekly.com/docs/j._the_jewish_news-12-20-24_covering_2024)). Following a 2024 merger with the JCRC Bay Area, the organization consolidated its efforts to strengthen its focus on combating "anti-Israel activities" in the region ([Source](https://www.jweekly.com/2024/07/10/silicon-valley-jcrc-merges-with-larger-bay-area-jcrc/)).

### 2. Public Signatures and Political Support
*   **Mayors United Against Antisemitism:** Klein is a signatory of the American Jewish Committee’s (AJC) "Mayors United Against Antisemitism" initiative ([Source](https://www.ajc.org/mayors)). The AJC is a global advocacy organization that characterizes support for Israel as a "central tenet" of its mission and works to advance Israel's standing in the international community ([Source](https://www.ajc.org/what-we-do/israel-and-the-region)).
*   **Jewish Democratic Coalition Participation:** Klein has been a featured speaker and attendee at town halls and events hosted by the **Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area** and the **Jewish Democratic Club of Silicon Valley** ([Source](https://lavozdeanza.com/news/2024/03/28/rep-ro-khanna-jewish-democratic-coalition-of-the-bay-area-hold-town-hall-on-antisemitism/)). These organizations engage in political advocacy that includes promoting "strong U.S.-Israel ties" and opposing the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement ([Source](https://issuu.com/jweekly.com/docs/j._the_jewish_news-12-20-24_covering_2024)).

### 3. Communal and Philanthropic Associations
Klein is documented as an individual supporter of **Jewish Silicon Valley** (formerly the Jewish Federation of Silicon Valley) ([Source](https://yesmeasureb.com/supporters/)). The organization's stated mission includes "strengthening the Jewish people... in Israel" and it maintains a "solid support for Israel" as a core institutional value ([Source](https://www.guidestar.org/profile/23-7407005)). Additionally, a "Judy and Larry Klein Fund" is listed as a donor-advised fund through the **Jewish Communal Fund**, a primary vehicle for charitable giving to Zionist and Jewish community causes ([Source](https://jcfny.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/JCF-2018-Annual-Report.pdf)).

### 4. Local Advocacy and Security
Following the events of October 7, 2023, Klein has actively coordinated with local Jewish institutions, such as the South Peninsula Hebrew Day School, to increase security infrastructure ([Source](https://lavozdeanza.com/news/2024/03/28/rep-ro-khanna-jewish-democratic-coalition-of-the-bay-area-hold-town-hall-on-antisemitism/)). This work is part of a broader regional effort by the JCRC and the Federation to defend the Jewish community's right to express "Zionist identity" safely in public and educational spaces ([Source](https://www.jweekly.com/2026/04/17/jewish-organizations-face-a-heartbreaking-choice-security-or-jewish-continuity/)).
