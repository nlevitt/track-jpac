# Contributor Report: Hertzberg for Senate 2018

"Hertzberg for Senate 2018" (FPPC ID# 1392758) is the candidate-controlled committee for Robert (Bob) Hertzberg's 2018 re-election campaign to the California State Senate, representing the 18th District. The campaign and the candidate maintained documented ties to Zionist advocacy organizations and supported legislative initiatives strengthening economic and political ties between California and Israel.

### Endorsements and Advocacy Affiliations
The 2018 campaign received the endorsement of **Democrats for Israel-Los Angeles (DFI-LA)**, a pro-Israel advocacy group within the Democratic Party. DFI-LA identifies its mission as serving as a "bridge between the Democratic Party and the Jewish and pro-Israel community" and backing candidates who demonstrate consistent support for Israel.
*   **Source:** [Sierra Club Endorsement List (referencing DFI-LA 2018 endorsements)](https://www.sierraclub.org/sites/default/files/sce/sierra-club-california/PDFs/2018-05-14%20Lara%20Sierra%20Club%20CA%20Endorsement%20Release.pdf)
*   **Source:** [Democrats for Israel-Los Angeles PAC Profile](https://www.cmu.edu/blacklisting/committees/committees1.txt)

Hertzberg was an active member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)** during the 2018 cycle. The caucus is dedicated to "countering anti-Israel narratives" and "strengthening California–Israel economic, technological and cultural ties."
*   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus Profile](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)

### Legislative Record and Anti-BDS Activity
Hertzberg supported **Assembly Bill 2844 (2016)**, a major legislative effort by the California Legislative Jewish Caucus to counter the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement. The bill requires companies entering into large state contracts to certify they are not violating civil rights laws by boycotting Israel.
*   **Source:** [Official Text and Status of AB 2844](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)
*   **Source:** [CLJC Anti-BDS Legislative History](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#2015-2017_Modest_allocations,_anti-BDS_legislation)

### Diplomatic and Economic Engagement
Hertzberg participated in high-level diplomatic and economic forums focused on the **California-Israel Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)**. This MOU, signed in 2014, facilitates joint research and business development between the two entities in sectors such as alternative energy and water technology.
*   **Source:** [Milken Innovation Center Update - California-Israel MOU Sessions](https://milkeninnovationcenter.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/Update-September-2017-V4.pdf)

In June 2017, leading into his re-election year, Hertzberg served as a "Technical Co-Chair" for an international legal seminar held in **Tel Aviv, Israel**, which included presentations on the "miracle" of the State of Israel's establishment and its geopolitical challenges.
*   **Source:** [INSOL International Tel Aviv Seminar Program](https://www.insol-europe.org/images/uploads/Technical_Programme_Israel_13.06.2017.pdf)

### Organizational Relationships
Hertzberg maintained documented relationships with several prominent Zionist and Israeli institutions:
*   **Friends of Sheba Medical Center:** Hertzberg attended the 70th-anniversary gala for the Friends of Sheba Medical Center, an organization that raises funds for Israel’s largest medical center.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal: Movers & Shakers - Sheba Medical Center Gala](https://jewishjournal.com/community/240409/jfs-building-grief-center-sheba-70th/)
*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** He is documented as attending major ADL events focused on combating "anti-Israel content" and antisemitism.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal: Shul Merger, ADL Entertainment Dinner](https://jewishjournal.com/community/233486/moving-shaking-shul-merger-adl-entertainment-dinner/)
