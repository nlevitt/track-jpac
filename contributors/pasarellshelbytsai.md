# California Contributor Report: Shelby Pasarell Tsai

## Professional Background and Affiliations
Shelby Pasarell Tsai is a California-based attorney and business executive who currently serves as the President of the Reed Union School District (RUSD) Board of Trustees in Tiburon, California [1, 4]. Professionally, she is the Vice President of Operations and Business Development for Costa Isabela Partners, a real estate development firm [4, 5]. Previously, she served as a senior litigation counsel at the law firm Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati [5, 8].

## Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
Tsai has been identified as a founding team member of **Israel Impact Ventures**, an organization that facilitates investment in Israeli technology companies [6]. This role involves the promotion and financial support of the Israeli tech sector, which is cited as a demonstration of active support for Israel's economy [6].

### Documented Affiliations:
*   **Israel Impact Ventures:** Founding team member focused on directing capital into the Israeli startup ecosystem [6].
*   **Impact Ventures (impactventures.biz):** This entity, which aligns with the description of "Israel Impact Ventures," focuses on turning Israeli early-stage startups into successful U.S. companies through business development and fundraising execution [6].
*   **Professional Legal Representation:** During her tenure at Wilson Sonsini, Tsai provided legal counsel to various corporate entities, including those involved in cross-border transactions and intellectual property litigation, within the San Francisco and Silicon Valley technology sectors [8].

## Political and Community Activities
In addition to her professional roles, Tsai is active in California local governance and philanthropy:
*   **Reed Union School District:** Elected to the Board of Trustees in 2022 and currently serves as President [1, 7].
*   **Foundation for Reed Schools:** Formerly served as Vice President of Family Giving for this foundation, which raises millions for local education [9, 10].
*   **YMCA of San Francisco:** Served as a member of the Board of Directors for the YMCA of Greater San Francisco, contributing to strategic oversight and governance [11, 12].
