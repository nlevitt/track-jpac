# Contributor Report: Fisher, Laura

Laura Fisher is a prominent San Francisco-based philanthropist and board member of organizations with documented ties to Zionist advocacy and the strengthening of U.S.-Israel relations. She is the wife of John Fisher, the billionaire owner of the Oakland Athletics and son of Gap Inc. founders Donald and Doris Fisher.

## Leadership and Institutional Affiliations

### Koret Foundation
Fisher has been identified as a board member of the **Koret Foundation**, an organization whose mission explicitly includes "strengthening the Jewish community at home and abroad" and "U.S.-Israel bridge-building." The foundation funds numerous initiatives aimed at reinforcing political, economic, and cultural ties between the United States and Israel.
*   **Mission Documentation:** [Koret Foundation - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Koret_Foundation)
*   **Grantmaking Focus:** [Koret Foundation - Priorities](https://koret.org/priorities/israel/)

### KIPP Northern California
Fisher serves on the **Board of Directors of KIPP Northern California**, a public charter school network that receives substantial financial support from the Koret Foundation. 
*   **Board Membership:** [KIPP NorCal 2024 Impact Report](https://kippnorcal.org/impact-report-2024/)
*   **Financial Linkage:** The Koret Foundation is listed as a primary supporter in the same report. [KIPP NorCal Supporters](https://kippnorcal.org/impact-report-2024/supporters/)

## Documented Support for Zionist Advocacy

### Hoover Institution
Fisher, alongside her husband John, is a documented donor to the **Hoover Institution** at Stanford University. The Hoover Institution hosts the **Koret Task Force**, an initiative funded by the Koret Foundation, and employs several fellows focused on Israeli studies and U.S.-Israel strategic policy.
*   **Donor Documentation:** [Hoover Institution 2008 Annual Report](https://www.hoover.org/sites/default/files/uploads/inline/docs/2008_annual_report.pdf) (See "John and Laura Fisher" under Koret-affiliated donor lists).

### Congregation Emanu-El
Fisher is a significant contributor to **Congregation Emanu-El** in San Francisco, specifically supporting the "Emanu-El Next" revitalization project. This project is facilitated by a "generous match from The Koret Foundation" and includes programming related to Israeli cultural and scientific achievements, such as the Weizmann Institute's ULTRASAT program.
*   **Revitalization Project and Koret Match:** [Congregation Emanu-El - Emanu-El Next](https://www.emanuelsf.org/next-revitalization/)
*   **Israel Programming:** [Emanu-El Israel Engagement](https://www.emanuelsf.org/israel/)

### Political and Ideological Alignment
The Fisher family has documented history of supporting political causes and candidates that align with pro-Israel lobbying interests. In 2024, Fisher and her husband contributed $100,000 to the transition team of San Francisco Mayor Daniel Lurie, a member of a prominent Jewish philanthropic family with extensive ties to the Koret Foundation.
*   **Political Contribution:** [Mission Local - Wealthy Donors to Mayor Lurie](https://missionlocal.org/2025/02/daniel-lurie-transition-donors/)
*   **Historical Context:** John Fisher’s academic background includes research into Holocaust survivors, a common theme in Zionist historical advocacy. [John Fisher (businessman) - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Fisher_(businessman))
