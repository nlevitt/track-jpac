# Contributor Report: Fisher, Laura

**Laura Meier Fisher** is a prominent California-based political donor and philanthropist, primarily known for her marriage to John J. Fisher, the son of Gap Inc. founders Donald and Doris Fisher and the majority owner of the Oakland Athletics. Fisher is documented as a major contributor to Zionist organizations and causes through both individual donations and family-managed entities, including **Pisces Inc.** and the **Koret Foundation**.

### 1. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

Fisher’s support for Zionist causes is characterized by high-level involvement in organizations that facilitate Jewish identity-building in relation to the State of Israel and the direct funding of Israeli institutional infrastructure.

*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Laura and John Fisher are identified as "prominent donors" and have been featured on the Birthright Israel Foundation's **"Scroll of Honor"** for their significant financial commitments. Birthright Israel is a Zionist organization that provides educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the land and state.
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation 2021 Donor List (Issuu) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation 2022 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family has a long-standing partnership with the **Koret Foundation**, an organization that focuses heavily on "strengthening Israel" and "combating anti-Semitism." While Doris Fisher (Laura's mother-in-law) served as a long-time trustee, Laura and John Fisher are frequently listed alongside the Koret Foundation as joint supporters of pro-Israel initiatives and Jewish community security projects in the San Francisco Bay Area.
    *   *Source:* Hoover Institution - Donor Recognition (including Koret and Fisher) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* Jewish Federation Bay Area 2023 Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco:** Fisher is a documented major donor to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which serves as one of the largest funders of Jewish and Israeli causes in California. The Federation directs millions of dollars annually to projects in Israel, including "impact lending" and social integration programs for new immigrants (*Olim*).
    *   *Source:* Jewish Federation Bay Area - Leadership & Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Political Activity and Pisces Inc.

Fisher’s political influence is channeled through individual donations and the Fisher family’s investment management company, **Pisces Inc.**, where her husband John Fisher serves as President.

*   **Political Donations:** Laura Fisher is a regular donor to both state and federal political candidates. Her contributions are often filed alongside those of her husband, with her occupation listed as "Homemaker" or "Philanthropist." In California, the Fishers have been identified as key backers of politicians who maintain strong pro-Israel platforms, such as Governor Gavin Newsom.
    *   *Source:* Los Angeles Times: How eight elite families funded Newsom (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* FEC Form 3 - Itemized Receipts (John Fisher/Pisces Inc) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Institutional Influence:** Pisces Inc. serves as the primary vehicle for the Fisher family's wealth management and political expenditures. Records from the Federal Election Commission (FEC) show that the entity is used to process major contributions to various Political Action Committees (PACs) and candidates who align with the family’s broader political and pro-Israel interests.
    *   *Source:* [California Secretary of State - Cal-Access Campaign Finance Records](https://cal-access.sos.ca.gov/Campaign/Committees/Detail.aspx?id=1013444)
