# Contributor Report: Jennifer Fisher

Jennifer Fisher is a San Francisco-based attorney and member of the Fisher family, which founded **Gap Inc.** and the family office **Pisces Inc.** She and her husband, Colin Fisher, are documented as significant donors to Jewish and Zionist organizations, particularly through the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy
Fisher’s support for Zionist causes is primarily channeled through major financial contributions to the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**. According to the Federation's 2023 Annual Report, the philanthropic reach of Jennifer and Colin Fisher "extends from the Bay Area to Israel and around the world" (Jewish Federation Bay Area 2023 Report, p. 38 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). 

The Federation serves as a central hub for Zionist advocacy in Northern California, funding the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC) Bay Area**, which explicitly works to mobilize support for the State of Israel and counter anti-Zionist sentiment ([JCRC Bay Area Advocacy](https://jcrc.org/)).

### 2. Association with Birthright Israel
Jennifer Fisher is associated with the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that sponsors heritage trips to Israel designed to strengthen Zionist identity among Jewish youth. The Fisher family’s involvement with Birthright is systemic; the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**, which Fisher supports as a high-level donor, is a long-standing partner and financial supporter of the Birthright Israel Foundation (Jewish Federation Bay Area Partner List (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 3. Financial Links to the Koret Foundation
Fisher is frequently documented alongside the **Koret Foundation** in philanthropic reports. The Koret Foundation is a prominent Zionist funding body that has donated hundreds of millions of dollars to Israeli institutions and US-based pro-Israel advocacy ([Koret Foundation: Support for Israel](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)). Jennifer Fisher has been listed as a contributor to organizations alongside Koret, such as the **Marine Mammal Center**, where she is documented as an individual donor in the same high-tier categories as the Koret Foundation (Marine Mammal Center Donor Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 4. Professional and Family Identity
*   **Family Wealth**: Jennifer Fisher is part of the San Francisco Fisher family, descendants and relatives of Gap Inc. founders **Donald and Doris Fisher**. The family manages its wealth through **Pisces Inc.**, which is listed as a corporate donor to major Jewish and Zionist causes ([Pisces Inc. Wealth Management Context](https://www.forbes.com/profile/fisher/)).
*   **Political Contributions**: Campaign finance records identify Jennifer Fisher as a lawyer at **Goodwin Procter LLP** (formerly at Duane Morris), where she has made contributions to political committees alongside other members of the Fisher family and Pisces Inc. executives (California Campaign Statement, 2022 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Board and Community Leadership**: Fisher’s leadership in the San Francisco Jewish community is noted through her participation in Federation events and giving circles that focus on "strengthening Jewish life" and maintaining the family’s "long-standing devotion" to Zionist-aligned philanthropy (Jewish Federation 2023 Report, p. 11 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
