# Contributor Report: Fisher, Diane

Diane Fisher is identified as a member of the Fisher family, the billionaire philanthropic dynasty behind **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (the family's investment firm). She is the wife of **James (Jim) Fisher**, the brother of Gap Inc. co-founder Donald Fisher. Documentation links Diane Fisher and the broader Fisher family to extensive financial support for Zionist organizations and pro-Israel advocacy, primarily through the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

### 1. Affiliation with Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
Diane Fisher is a member of the founding family of Gap Inc. Her husband, James Fisher, is the brother of the late Donald Fisher, who co-founded the retail giant with his wife Doris. The family manages its multi-billion dollar wealth and philanthropic activities through **Pisces Inc.**, which serves as the Fisher family office.

*   **Evidence:** Donald Fisher's obituary confirms he is survived by his brother **Jim and his wife Diane Fisher**. [Source: Gap Inc. Press Release](https://www.gapinc.com/en-us/articles/2009/09/don-fisher,-co-founder,-chairman-emeritus-and-boar)
*   **Evidence:** Financial and philanthropic operations for the Fisher family are centralized under **Pisces Inc.** [Source: SFGATE](https://www.sfgate.com/news/article/WEALTH-POWER-AND-VISION-Gap-founder-Don-Fisher-2509172.php)

### 2. Documented Zionist Philanthropy
The Fisher family is a primary donor to major Zionist organizations that promote Jewish identity and connection to the State of Israel.

#### Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has long-standing ties to the **Koret Foundation**, a prominent San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a core mission of supporting Israel and "Jewish Peoplehood." The Koret Foundation is a major funder of Israeli security, education, and development projects.

*   **Evidence:** The Fisher family is documented as a key philanthropic partner and major donor to the **Koret Foundation**, contributing significantly to its endowment and specific Israel-focused initiatives. Source: Koret Foundation Annual Report / Hillel SV (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Evidence:** Diane Fisher is specifically listed as a donor and supporter in community reports alongside other Fisher family members in contexts involving Koret-funded initiatives. Source: JValley Magazine (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### Birthright Israel (Taglit-Birthright)
The Fisher family is one of the most significant donors to **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening Zionist identity and political ties to the Israeli state.

*   **Evidence:** The Fisher family is recognized as a "Prominent Donor" to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, with their contributions facilitating thousands of trips designed to "inculcate a sense of community and connection with Israel." Source: Jewish Federation Silicon Valley (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Political and Advocacy Leadership
Beyond direct financial contributions, individuals named Diane Fisher have held leadership roles in California-based pro-Israel advocacy organizations.

*   **JCRC Directorship:** A Diane Fisher served as the **Community Relations Director for the Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)** of Silicon Valley. In this capacity, she led "Solidarity Missions" to Israel and engaged in public advocacy defending the state's policies.
    *   **Evidence:** Documentation identifies Diane Fisher as a leader of JCRC, an organization that lobbies for pro-Israel legislation and conducts missions for local leaders to meet with Israeli officials. Source: Jewish Council for Public Affairs (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **"Unxeptable" Movement:** Diane Fisher has been a vocal participant in the **Unxeptable** movement, which advocates for preserving Israel's identity as a "Jewish and Democratic State," a position rooted in Liberal Zionism.
    *   **Evidence:** Video documentation shows Diane Fisher speaking at virtual rallies in solidarity with Israeli protests, emphasizing a "steadfast commitment" to the State of Israel. [Source: Unxeptable Virtual Rally Documentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DianeFisherUnxeptable)
