# Contributor Report: Resnick, Lynda Rae

Lynda Rae Resnick is an American entrepreneur and philanthropist who serves as the vice-chairman of The Wonderful Company. Alongside her husband, Stewart Resnick, she has documented records of significant financial contributions and organizational leadership within pro-Israel and Zionist institutions, ranging from military support to academic and policy think-tanks.

### 1. Direct Military Support
Resnick’s philanthropic vehicle, The Resnick Foundation, has provided consistent financial support to the Israeli military apparatus. 
*   **Israeli Defense Forces (IDF):** Tax records indicate that between 2015 and 2022, the Resnick Foundation funneled approximately $2.4 million to the IDF. These donations were primarily processed through the **American Friends of the Israeli Defense Forces (FIDF)** and included specific allocations for "Israel's Navy Seals" and general military operations. 
    *   [Source: MR Online - California Billionaires Funding Israel's Occupation](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
    *   [Source: The Canary - Resnicks' Support for the IDF](https://www.thecanary.co/global/world-analysis/2025/01/13/the-resnicks-la-california/)

### 2. Policy and Advocacy Leadership
Resnick has held leadership roles in influential pro-Israel policy organizations that advocate for Israeli security and diplomatic interests.
*   **Washington Institute for Near East Policy (WINEP):** Lynda and Stewart Resnick have served as trustees and on the board of directors of WINEP. The organization was originally established as a spin-off from the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and focuses on promoting pro-Israel policy in U.S. foreign relations.
    *   [Source: Mondoweiss - Resnicks and the Washington Institute](https://mondoweiss.net/2013/07/oligarch-billionaire-sanctions/)
    *   [Source: BDSC - Pro-Israel Connections of the Resnicks](https://bdscoalition.ca/the-wonderful-company-is-not-so-wonderful-supporting-war-crimes-apartheid/)

### 3. Academic and Institutional Contributions
A significant portion of Resnick's Israel-focused philanthropy is directed toward institutions with documented ties to the Israeli defense and intelligence sectors.
*   **Technion-Israel Institute of Technology:** In 2022, the Resnicks pledged $50 million to establish the **Stewart and Lynda Resnick Sustainability Center for Catalysis**. The center’s stated goal is to "strengthen the State of Israel and its economy."
    *   [Source: Technion - Historic Gift to Establish Resnick Center](https://www.technion.ac.il/en/2022/12/historic-gift-to-the-technion/)
    *   [Source: Wonderful Company - Sustainability Center at Technion](https://csr.wonderful.com/articles/stewart-and-lynda-resnick-sustainability-center-for-catalysis)
*   **Reichman University (formerly IDC Herzliya):** The Resnicks have been primary supporters for over a decade. Stewart Resnick sits on the board of **American Friends of Reichman University**, an institution described as having close links to Israel’s military and intelligence establishment. 
    *   [Source: Reichman University - Donor Recognition](https://www.runi.ac.il/en/about/runifriends/donor-recognition/lynda-and-stewart-resnick/)
    *   [Source: MR Online - Resnick Foundation and IDC Herzliya](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
*   **National Library of Israel (NLI):** The Resnick Foundation donated $12 million to support the NLI campus in Jerusalem and established the **Lynda and Stewart Resnick Fellowship**, focusing on areas including Israel and Judaica.
    *   [Source: National Library of Israel - Resnick Fellowship](https://www.nli.org.il/en/research/fellowships/resnick)
    *   [Source: Wonderful Company - Legacy for Israel](https://csr.wonderful.com/articles/a-lasting-legacy-for-israel-and-the-world)

### 4. Community and Religious Organizations
Resnick’s support extends to prominent Jewish and Zionist communal organizations in the United States.
*   **Wilshire Boulevard Temple:** The Resnicks provided a $13 million cornerstone gift for the renovation of the temple’s campus in Los Angeles, establishing the **Lynda and Stewart Resnick Campus**.
    *   [Source: Jewish Journal - Resnick Family Donates $13 Million](https://jewishjournal.com/community/347253/resnick-family-donates-13-million-to-anchor-renovations-at-wilshire-boulevard-temples-university-campus/)
*   **Jewish National Fund (JNF) and Simon Wiesenthal Center:** Resnick is a documented supporter and donor to the JNF and the Simon Wiesenthal Center. Her association with these groups is part of a broader portfolio of Zionist philanthropy aimed at land development in Israel and advocacy against antisemitism.
    *   [Source: Jay's Valet - Client Testimonials referencing JNF](https://jaysvalet.com/testimonials/)
    *   [Source: Sony Pictures Classics - Biography of Lynda Resnick mentioning Simon Wiesenthal Center context](https://www.sonyclassics.com/thegreatestmovieeversold/presskit.pdf)
