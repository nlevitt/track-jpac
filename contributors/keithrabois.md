# Contributor Report: Rabois, Keith

Keith Rabois is a prominent venture capitalist and technology executive known for his active and outspoken advocacy for Israel and his substantial financial contributions to political causes framed by pro-Israel interests. His support for Israel intensified significantly following the events of October 7, 2023, which he has cited as a primary driver for his recent political realignment and philanthropic activities.

### 1. Pro-Israel Political Donations and Fundraising
Rabois’s financial support for Israel is largely channeled through political contributions to candidates and committees that support the Israeli government’s military and security policies.

*   **2024 Presidential Campaign:** Rabois pledged **$1 million** to Donald Trump’s 2024 presidential campaign. He explicitly stated that the "most important driver" for this contribution and his shift to the Republican party was the **October 7th attacks** and what he characterized as the Biden administration's insufficient support for Israel and failure to protect Jewish students from campus antisemitism [[1.7](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F_S6pZzM7C8), [1.12](https://alestiklal.net/en/article/how-tech-companies-are-getting-involved-in-supporting-us-presidential-candidates-massive-donations)].
*   **Congressional Fundraising:** Rabois has hosted high-profile fundraisers for pro-Israel candidates, including a **September 2024 event for JD Vance** [[1.2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Rabois)]. He also co-hosted a 2022 fundraiser for GOP Senate candidates such as **Blake Masters** and **JD Vance**, both of whom maintain strong pro-Israel platforms [[1.15](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Sacks)].
*   **United Democracy Project (UDP) Context:** While direct donor records for Rabois to AIPAC’s super PAC, the **United Democracy Project**, are frequently associated with his peer group of Silicon Valley donors (such as Joe Lonsdale and David Sacks), his public advocacy closely aligns with UDP’s mission to support pro-Israel candidates in primary elections [[1.6](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/05/jacob-helberg-donald-trump-silicon-valley-donations-palantir/)].

### 2. Public Advocacy and Zionist Rhetoric
Rabois is documented as a "pugilistic" advocate for the State of Israel, frequently using his platform on X (formerly Twitter) to defend the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) and criticize those he views as insufficiently supportive of the Zionist cause.

*   **Defense of the IDF:** Rabois has publicly asserted that the IDF is "the most ethical military in world history" [[1.2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Rabois)]. He has consistently rejected reports of Palestinian civilian casualties in Gaza, characterizing them as manufactured or exaggerated by legacy media outlets [[1.2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Rabois), [1.7](https://www.sfgate.com/tech/article/keith-rabois-elon-musk-x-holocaust-18538118.php)].
*   **Media and Social Platforms:** Rabois claimed that without Elon Musk's X, the world would likely be "confronting another Holocaust now," arguing that the platform is essential for bypassing "gatekeepers" like the *New York Times* and the *BBC*, which he accuses of being "stenographers for the terrorists" [[1.4](https://www.sfgate.com/tech/article/keith-rabois-elon-musk-x-holocaust-18538118.php), [1.9](https://www.vice.com/en/article/keith-rabois-elon-musk-x-second-holocaust/)].
*   **Opposition to the "Anti-Israel Left":** Along with his husband, **Jacob Helberg**, Rabois has voiced strong opposition to the progressive wing of the Democratic Party, citing their "anti-Israel" views as a reason to abandon the party entirely [[1.6](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/05/jacob-helberg-donald-trump-silicon-valley-donations-palantir/)].

### 3. Critical Documentation and Controversy
Rabois’s rhetoric has led to his inclusion in databases and reports that document extreme or polarizing pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Characterizations by Critics:** Investigative sites have categorized Rabois as a "pro-genocide" figure due to his social media posts that equate the entire Palestinian population with Hamas and advocate for collective punishment [[1.3](https://genocide.vc/the-curious-case-of-keith-rabois-a-pro-genocide-gp-at-founders-fund/), [1.10](https://spotlighthate.com/profile/keith-rabois/)]. 
*   **"Hamas Children" Phrase:** Documentation from advocacy groups highlights Rabois’s use of the term "Hamas children" to describe Palestinian minors, a phrase used by critics to demonstrate his dehumanization of civilian populations in conflict zones [[1.10](https://spotlighthate.com/profile/keith-rabois/)].
*   **Disavowal by Partners:** In some instances, his commentary on immigration and security—often linked to his broader geopolitical views on Israel and the "America First" movement—has been disavowed by colleagues, such as Khosla Ventures founder Vinod Khosla [[1.2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Rabois)].

### 4. Affiliations and Joint Advocacy
Rabois’s influence is often exerted in conjunction with a close-knit group of Silicon Valley figures who share his Zionist and pro-Israel perspectives.

*   **Marriage to Jacob Helberg:** His husband, **Jacob Helberg**, is a senior advisor at Palantir and a significant pro-Israel donor who has also contributed millions to Republican causes specifically due to the "anti-Israel" shift he perceives on the left [[1.15](https://www.forbes.com/sites/davidjeans/2024/07/11/jacob-helberg-donald-trump-silicon-valley-tiktok-ai/)].
*   **The "PayPal Mafia":** Rabois works closely with other members of the so-called "PayPal Mafia," including **Peter Thiel** and **David Sacks**, to host fundraisers and promote a political agenda that prioritizes Israeli national security and "sovereign technology" [[1.14](https://americanjournalnews.com/gop-senators-candidates-will-attend-fundraiser-hosted-by-donors-who-defended-rape/), [1.15](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Sacks)].
