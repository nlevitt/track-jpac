This report examines the political and philanthropic activities of **Joseph W. Kaempfer Jr.** (commonly known as Joey Kaempfer), a real estate developer and political donor. While identified in some contexts as a supporter of Zionist advocacy groups, primary documentation highlights a profile centered on real estate development and significant contributions to centrist and liberal political committees.

# Contributor Report: Kaempfer, Joseph

### 1. Overview of Identity and Professional Background
Joseph W. Kaempfer Jr. is the founder and chairman of **McArthurGlen Group**, a major developer and operator of designer outlet malls across Europe and Canada. He previously established the **Kaempfer Company**, a real estate development firm based in Washington, D.C., which was responsible for prominent commercial projects in the District and Northern Virginia.
*   **Source:** [Forbes - The Malling of Europe](https://www.forbes.com/forbes/1999/0222/6304154a.html)
*   **Source:** [Washington Post - Joey Kaempfer Profile](https://www.washingtonpost.com/archive/lifestyle/magazine/1990/07/16/joey/8b29f44f-c0d1-447a-8f1b-c1219b67323b/)

### 2. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
While some donor tracking suggests Kaempfer supports specific pro-Israel organizations, public records primarily link his name to these causes through corporate memberships or presence within philanthropic circles often shared by major Zionist donors.

*   **Philanthropic Associations:** Kaempfer is documented as a corporate member and donor to the **Tate** in London, appearing on donor lists alongside prominent pro-Israel figures such as Poju Zabludowicz (a major benefactor of BICOM) and Noam Gottesman. 
    *   **Source:** [Tate Report 2013/14 - Donations and Sponsorships](https://www.tate.org.uk/about-us/working-at-tate/tate-report-2013-14)
*   **International Trade and Policy:** Documentation from 1993 places Kaempfer in the context of European policy discussions regarding trade agreements with Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Financial Times Archive (1993) via Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/FinancialTimes1993UKEnglish/page/n7/mode/2up)

### 3. Political Contributions and Strategic Advocacy
Kaempfer's documented political giving is largely directed toward "Never Trump" Republican initiatives and Democratic campaigns, groups that generally maintain a mainstream pro-Israel stance but are distinct from right-wing Zionist advocacy.

*   **The Lincoln Project:** Kaempfer was identified as the second-largest donor to **The Lincoln Project** during the 2024 election cycle, contributing $720,000 to the committee.
    *   **Source:** [Capital Research Center - The Great Realignment](https://capitalresearch.org/article/the-great-realignment-part-2/)
*   **Democratic Committees:** During the 2024 cycle, Kaempfer donated at least $1.7 million to the **Harris Victory Fund**, $900,000 to the **Democratic Grassroots Victory Fund**, and over $400,000 to the **Democratic National Committee**.
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch - Progress Action Fund](https://www.influencewatch.org/political-committee/progress-action-fund/)
*   **Public Citizen:** Kaempfer is listed as a supporter of **Public Citizen**, an advocacy group focused on corporate and government accountability.
    *   **Source:** [Public Citizen News - November/December 2023](https://www.citizen.org/wp-content/uploads/PC_News_NovDec23_WEB.pdf)

### 4. Evaluation of Specific Advocacy Claims
The claim that Kaempfer "founded/chaired Christians United for Israel (CUFI) Action" is not corroborated by the organization's official records. 
*   **CUFI Leadership:** CUFI was founded by **Pastor John Hagee**, and its legislative arm, the **CUFI Action Fund**, is currently chaired by **Sandra Hagee Parker**, with **Gary Bauer** serving as its Washington Director. No evidence in IRS 990 filings or leadership rosters identifies Kaempfer in a founding or chairing capacity for these entities.
    *   **Source:** [CUFI Official Site - Leadership](https://www.cufi.org/about/leadership/)
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica - Christians United for Israel Action Fund 990](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/461868892)
*   **American Friends of Likud (AFL):** While AFL lists numerous supporters, Kaempfer is not listed among its current or historical board members (such as Ken Abramowitz or J. Philip Rosen) in primary legal or organizational documents. 
    *   **Source:** [GuideStar - American Friends of Likud Inc](https://www.guidestar.org/profile/13-3935945)
