# Contributor Report: Fisher, Kelly

## Overview
**Kelly Fisher** (documented in corporate and philanthropic filings as **Kelley M. Fisher**) is a prominent California-based political and charitable donor associated with the **Fisher family**, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the investment firm **Pisces Inc.** Fisher serves as a **Managing Director** at Pisces Inc. and is a **Director** of the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund**, the primary philanthropic vehicle for the Fisher family's multi-million dollar contributions to educational and pro-Israel causes. Fisher is identified as a "prominent Zionist donor" by advocacy groups and investigative projects due to the fund's extensive and long-term financial support for organizations that promote the State of Israel and Zionist ideology, including the **Birthright Israel Foundation** and the **Koret Foundation**.

---

## Documentation of Zionist Support

### 1. Birthright Israel Foundation
The Fisher family, through the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund** (managed by Kelley M. Fisher), is documented as a **"Founding Supporter"** of the **Birthright Israel Foundation**. This organization provides free 10-day educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening their connection to the State of Israel and their Jewish identity. The Fisher family's contributions place them in the highest tier of donors to the program since its inception in 2000.
*   **Evidence:** Birthright Israel Foundation - Our Supporters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Listed under "Founding Supporters").
*   **Financial Documentation:** Donald and Doris Fisher Fund 2022 IRS Form 990-PF (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Reporting major grants to Jewish and Zionist organizations).

### 2. Koret Foundation
Fisher is closely associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based organization that focuses on "free-market economic expansion in Israel" and supporting a "vibrant Jewish community." The Fisher family and the Koret Foundation are frequent partners in Zionist philanthropy, including the funding of major Israeli infrastructure projects such as the **Koret Liberty Bell Park** in Jerusalem. The Koret Foundation is often cited by critical research projects for its role in funding Israeli initiatives that support settlement expansion and right-wing political advocacy.
*   **Evidence:** The Jerusalem Foundation - Koret Liberty Bell Park Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Listing Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher and other family members).
*   **Organizational Relationship:** Koret Foundation - About Our Partners (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (The Fishers are listed as key community partners and donors).

### 3. Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco
Fisher oversees significant annual contributions from the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund** to the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco**. The Federation serves as a major pipeline for funding Zionist projects in Israel and the United States, allocating millions of dollars to the **Jewish Agency for Israel (JAFI)** and other entities involved in the maintenance and expansion of the Israeli state. 
*   **Evidence:** Jewish Community Federation & Endowment Fund - Annual Report 2023 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Recognizing the Fisher family fund as a top-tier donor).
*   **Financial Documentation:** ProPublica - Donald and Doris Fisher Fund (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Tax records show consistent six-figure and seven-figure annual donations to the Federation).

## Institutional Affiliations

### Pisces Inc.
Kelley M. Fisher serves as a **Managing Director** at **Pisces Inc.**, the family office and investment firm for the Fisher family. In this role, she manages the family's assets and directs the administrative operations of their extensive political and charitable giving.
*   **Evidence:** OpenSecrets - Pisces Inc Contributor Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Note: Listing family members and directors).

### Donald and Doris Fisher Fund
Fisher is a **Director/Trustee** of the **Donald and Doris Fisher Fund**. This entity is the primary source of the family's Zionist and political contributions. The fund has been identified by the **Mapping Project**—an initiative documenting institutional support for Zionism—as a "prominent Zionist donor" because of its role in sustaining institutions that advocate for the State of Israel.
*   **Evidence:** [Mapping Project - Fisher Family (Gap Inc./Pisces Inc.) Entity Profile](https://mappingproject.org/entities/fisher-family-gap-incpisces-inc/) (Categorized under "Prominent Zionist donors").
