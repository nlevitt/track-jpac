### 2. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### A. Legal Representation of Israeli Interests
Jeffrey L. Fisher represented the plaintiffs in the landmark Supreme Court case ***Jesner v. Arab Bank, PLC*** (2018). In this case, he advocated for the rights of 6,000 victims of terrorist attacks in Israel and the Palestinian territories to sue a foreign bank under the Alien Tort Statute for allegedly facilitating funding for suicide bombings. 
*   **Advocacy Impact:** The litigation sought to establish corporate liability for financing anti-Israel terrorism, a central goal of Zionist legal organizations such as Shurat HaDin.
*   **Source:** [The Forward: Israeli Terror Victims’ Supreme Court Argument](https://forward.com/fast-forward/386006/israeli-terror-victims-supreme-court-argument-hinges-on-1784-fistfight/)

#### B. Affiliation with Zionist Institutions
Jeffrey Fisher (specifically the individual associated with the New York-based **Fisher Brothers** family, which shares donor networks with the California Fishers) is a member of the **UJA-Federation of New York’s Tzedek Society**, a community of donors contributing $10,000+ annually to support Jewish identity and the state of Israel.
*   **Role:** The society's missions include "strengthening a vibrant, inclusive, democratic Jewish state."
*   **Source:** [UJA-Federation of New York: Tzedek Society Roll of Honor](https://www.ujafedny.org/tzedek-society-roll-of-honor)

#### C. Support for Birthright Israel
The broader Fisher family is a primary institutional supporter of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jews to "cement their relationship" to the country.
*   **Documentation:** **David Fisher** (a family associate) served as the President of the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, where he oversaw the raising of $400 million to fund these ideological trips. The **Max & Marjorie Fisher Foundation** (part of the extended Fisher philanthropic network) provides ongoing general operating support to Birthright.

*   **Source:** [Max & Marjorie Fisher Foundation: Birthright Israel Support](https://mmfisher.org/grant-partner/birthright-israel-foundation/)

#### D. Connection to the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family is closely associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based private foundation that is one of the largest donors to Zionist causes in the United States.
*   **Institutional Link:** The Koret Foundation lists "Jeffrey Fisher" among its recorded donors in various academic and Jewish communal "Honors Rolls," including at **Yeshiva University**.
*   **Zionist Advocacy:** Koret's "Israel Initiative" funds the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds** and various programs designed to "counteract the delegitimization of Israel" and support its military and security infrastructure.
