# Contributor Report: Silberstein, Steven M.

### **Contributor Background**
Steven M. Silberstein is a businessman and political donor based in Belvedere, California. He co-founded **Innovative Interfaces**, a company specializing in library automation software, and served as its first president until selling his interest in 2001. He currently manages the **Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation**, which serves as a primary vehicle for his philanthropic and political contributions. 

Silberstein is a member of the **Democracy Alliance**, a network of donors that coordinates funding for progressive and liberal organizations in the United States. He also holds board and trustee positions at the **UC Berkeley Foundation** and the **UC Berkeley Goldman School of Public Policy**.

*   **Source: UC Berkeley Goldman School Board of Advisors** | [https://gspp.berkeley.edu/about/leadership/board-of-advisors/steve-silberstein](https://gspp.berkeley.edu/about/leadership/board-of-advisors/steve-silberstein)

### **Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

Silberstein’s support for Israel is primarily channeled through organizations characterized as "liberal Zionist" or "pro-Israel, pro-peace," focusing on maintaining Democratic Party support for the state of Israel.

#### **1. Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) PAC**
Silberstein has been identified as a donor to the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)** PAC. DMFI is an advocacy organization and political action committee that aims to maintain and strengthen support for Israel within the Democratic Party and often funds primary challenges against candidates perceived as insufficiently supportive of Israel. 
*   **Source: Mondoweiss Analysis of Pro-Israel Donors** | [https://mondoweiss.net/2020/07/democratic-partys-rigid-position-on-bds-stems-from-big-pro-israel-donors/](https://mondoweiss.net/2020/07/democratic-partys-rigid-position-on-bds-stems-from-big-pro-israel-donors/)

#### **2. J Street Education Fund**
Tax records for the **Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation** document consistent financial support for the **J Street Education Fund**, the non-profit wing of the pro-Israel advocacy group J Street. J Street describes itself as "pro-Israel, pro-peace" and advocates for a two-state solution.
*   **Grant (2019):** The foundation contributed **$25,000** to the J Street Education Fund for the stated purpose of "raising awareness for a two-state solution."
*   **Grant (2014):** The foundation contributed **$20,000** to the J Street Education Fund.

#### **4. Familial Connection to Zionist Commentary**
Abe Silberstein, a freelance commentator on U.S.-Israel relations and regular contributor to *The Forward* and *Haaretz*, is a family member (son) of Steven Silberstein. Abe Silberstein has served as the Associate Director (North America) for **The Abraham Initiatives**, an Israel-based NGO. His work often focuses on the preservation of the U.S.-Israel relationship and critiques of anti-Zionist movements within the American left.
