# Contributor Report: FISHER, BRANDON N LAURA

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

This report documents the political and philanthropic activities of Brandon and Laura Fisher, members of the Fisher family—the billionaires behind **Gap Inc.** and the investment firm **Pisces Inc.** The family is among the most prominent political donors in California, with documented history of supporting Zionist causes and organizations that advocate for the State of Israel.

### Family and Institutional Affiliations
Brandon and Laura Fisher are associated with the multi-generational wealth of **Donald and Doris Fisher**, who founded Gap Inc. in 1969. The family manages its capital through **Pisces Inc.** (also known as the Fisher Family Office) and **Sansome Partners**.
*   **John J. Fisher** (President of Pisces Inc.) and his wife **Laura Meier Fisher** are primary directors of the family's philanthropic strategy. [Source: SFGATE](https://www.sfgate.com/news/article/A-s-NEW-ERA-JOHN-FISHER-Son-of-Gap-founder-2716382.php).
*   **Brandon Fisher** is identified as a member of the Fisher family and a grandchild of Donald and Doris Fisher. [Source: Washington Post/Scribd](https://www.scribd.com/document/392501064/Canto-a-Su-Amor-Desaparecido-0).

### Documented Support for Israel and Zionist Advocacy
The Fisher family’s relationship with Zionism and pro-Israel advocacy is documented through direct financial contributions to major Zionist institutions and leadership roles in organizations that support the State of Israel.

#### 1. Birthright Israel and Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has a documented history of funding **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel to strengthen Jewish identity and connection to the state.
*   The founders, Donald and Doris Fisher, were "charter funders" and longtime supporters of the San Francisco-based **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which facilitates major grants to Israel-based projects and Birthright. [Source: J. Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   The family is closely associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a major pro-Israel philanthropic organization in San Francisco. The Koret Foundation is a lead funder of the **Koret Israel Program**, which promotes economic and security cooperation between the U.S. and Israel, and has provided millions to **Birthright Israel**. Source: Koret Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### 2. Jewish Community Federation (JCF)
The Fisher family, including John and Laura Fisher, are major contributors to the **Jewish Community Federation (JCF)** of San Francisco.
*   The JCF serves as a primary vehicle for Zionist philanthropy in Northern California, funding programs that support the **Israel Defense Forces (IDF)** and settlement-adjacent projects via its Endowment Fund. [Source: J. Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   John J. Fisher’s foundational commitment to Jewish causes was documented as early as 1983 in his Princeton thesis, "Echoes of the Holocaust: Survivors in America Speak Out." Source: Wikipedia/Princeton (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### 3. Political Contributions and Pro-Israel Lobbying
The Fisher family uses their wealth to influence U.S. policy in ways that often align with pro-Israel interests.
*   In 2012, the Fisher family (Doris, John, Robert, and William) donated nearly **$9 million** to a political action committee (PAC) that opposed the Obama administration. Observers noted the family's alignment with conservative Republican policies, which included strong opposition to the Iran nuclear deal and a commitment to unconditional support for Israel. Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   John and Laura Fisher have collectively donated hundreds of thousands of dollars to local and national candidates who maintain pro-Israel platforms, including **$100,000** to the transition of San Francisco Mayor Daniel Lurie, another prominent Jewish philanthropist. Source: Mission Local (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### Relationship to Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
The family's support for Israel has led to public scrutiny and boycott campaigns against **Gap Inc.** (which includes Old Navy and Banana Republic).
*   **Gap Inc.** operates a franchise in Israel, secured through a deal with **Elbit Imaging Ltd.** Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Pisces Inc.**, the family investment arm led by John Fisher, manages the assets used to fund these Zionist initiatives, centralizing the family's political and religious influence. [Source: SFGATE](https://www.sfgate.com/news/article/A-s-NEW-ERA-JOHN-FISHER-Son-of-Gap-founder-2716382.php).
