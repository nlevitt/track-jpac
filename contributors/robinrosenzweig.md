# Contributor Report: Rosenzweig, Robin

Robin Rosenzweig (also known as Robin Rosenzweig Broidy) is a Los Angeles-based attorney and political donor with documented leadership roles and financial contributions directed toward Zionist advocacy and the support of the State of Israel. 

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Zionist Advocacy

Rosenzweig holds senior leadership positions in organizations that explicitly advocate for Israel and the promotion of pro-Israel narratives in academic and public spheres.

*   **The Fund to End Antisemitism, Extremism, and Hate:** Rosenzweig serves as the President of this organization. The fund’s stated mission includes financing research and initiatives that support "pro-Israel instructors on university campuses" and addressing why "curriculums fail to teach the pro-Israel point of view in the study of Middle East history." It also targets "anti-Israel protests" on campuses.
    *   *Source:* [The Fund to End Antisemitism, Extremism & Hate - Leadership](https://endantisemitismfund.com/leadership)
*   **Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles:** Rosenzweig has been identified as a Board Member and Vice Chair of this organization. The Federation's mission includes "forcefully combating the delegitimization of Israel" and managing the "Israel Action Network" to counter anti-Israel efforts locally and nationally.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Federation Los Angeles - Our Work](https://www.jewishla.org/our-work/israel/)
*   **Pro-Israel Lay Leadership:** In personal records, Rosenzweig has documented her involvement as a lay leader for several prominent Zionist organizations, including:
    *   **AIPAC (American Israel Public Affairs Committee):** A major pro-Israel lobbying group.
    *   **Israel Bonds:** A provider of debt securities issued by the State of Israel.
    *   **Friends of the Israeli Defense Forces (FIDF):** An organization that provides welfare and educational programs for Israeli soldiers.
    *   **American Friends of Magen David Adom:** A support organization for Israel’s national emergency medical service.
    *   *Source:* [Brandeis University Class of 1980 35th Reunion Yearbook, p. 12](https://www.brandeis.edu/alumni/reunion/past/35th-reunion-yearbook.pdf)

### 2. Legal and Political Documentation of Support for Israel

Rosenzweig’s support for Israel is a central component of her documented public and legal profile.

*   **Litigation Against Qatar:** In 2018, Rosenzweig and her husband, Elliott Broidy, filed a federal lawsuit against the State of Qatar. The complaint explicitly states that the couple was targeted by Qatari hacking operations due to their "vocal criticism of the State of Qatar’s support of terrorism" and their "more than 25 years" of supporting the State of Israel through donations to numerous organizations.
    *   *Source:* [Broidy Capital Management, LLC et al v. State of Qatar et al - Justia Dockets](https://dockets.justia.com/docket/california/cacdce/2:2018cv02421/706915)
    *   *Source:* [Broidy Amended Complaint - Scribd](https://www.scribd.com/document/380182613/Broidy-Amended-Complaint)
*   **Political Contributions:** Rosenzweig is a documented political contributor. Federal Election Commission (FEC) records show she has made significant donations alongside her husband, a prominent Republican fundraiser. In 2025, she was documented as receiving a $250,000 refund from the Trump inaugural committee, indicating a high level of preceding financial support for political operations that align with her advocacy goals.
    *   *Source:* [Brennan Center for Justice - Million-Dollar Donors](https://www.brennancenter.org/our-work/analysis-opinion/million-dollar-donors-flooded-trumps-second-inauguration)

### 3. Institutional Support and Religious Affiliation

*   **Wilshire Boulevard Temple:** Rosenzweig and her family are major donors to the Wilshire Boulevard Temple in Los Angeles. Documentation from 2019 indicates contributions between $25,000 and $49,999 to the temple’s endowment, supporting its educational and community programs which include "Israel awareness" initiatives.
    *   *Source:* [The Forward - Why did these rabbis seek a pardon for Elliot Broidy?](https://forward.com/news/462947/why-did-these-rabbis-seek-a-pardon-for-elliot-broidy/)
