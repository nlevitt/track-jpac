# Contributor Report: Bronfman, Kelly

Kelly Bronfman is a member of the Bronfman family, a prominent North American philanthropic dynasty. She is the wife of Samuel Bronfman II, the son of billionaire philanthropist Charles Bronfman. Documentation identifies her as a board member of The Andrea and Charles Bronfman Philanthropies (ACBP), a family of foundations that operated in the United States, Canada, and Israel before sunsetting in 2016.

### Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

Kelly Bronfman’s relationship to Israel is primarily documented through her leadership roles and financial contributions within the Bronfman family’s institutional framework, which explicitly prioritizes the strengthening of the State of Israel and Jewish identity linked to Zionism.

#### Board Membership: The Andrea and Charles Bronfman Philanthropies (ACBP)
Kelly Bronfman served on the board of The Andrea and Charles Bronfman Philanthropies. The organization’s mission statement explicitly included "Nurturing the unity of the Jewish people, whose soul is in Jerusalem" and "Improving the quality of life in Montreal, New York and Israel."
*   **Source:** [Berman Jewish Policy Archive - Open Letter from Charles Bronfman and Jeffrey Solomon](https://bjpa.org/content/upload/bjpa/open/Open%20Letter%20from%20Charles%20Bronfman%20and%20Jeffrey%20Solomon.pdf)
*   **Source:** [Jerusalem Center for Security and Foreign Affairs - American Jewry’s Comfort Level (referencing ACBP mission)](https://jcfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/american-jewrys-comfort-level.pdf)

#### Support for Birthright Israel
Through her role at ACBP, Kelly Bronfman was part of the leadership of the foundation that co-founded and served as a primary benefactor for **Birthright Israel (Taglit)**. Birthright is a program designed to provide free educational tours of Israel to young Jewish adults with the goal of strengthening their connection to the State of Israel and their Jewish heritage. The foundation described Birthright as a "strategic asset" and a "crucial mission" for the "precious State of Israel."
*   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post - Adelson, Bronfman call on Jews to invest in Birthright Israel](https://www.jpost.com/business-and-innovation/all-news/article-723696)
*   **Source:** [Wikipedia - Taglit-Birthright Israel History and Funding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Birthright_Israel)

#### The Kelly and Sam Bronfman Family Foundation
Kelly Bronfman and her husband manage their own private foundation, the **Kelly and Sam Bronfman Family Foundation** (also known as the Sam Bronfman II Family Foundation). While much of this foundation's documented activity focuses on environmental and health causes, it is also identified as a contributor to major Zionist and pro-Israel organizations, including the Jewish Federation and Endowment Fund of San Francisco.
*   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch - Kelly and Sam Bronfman Family Foundation](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/kelly-and-sam-bronfman-family-foundation/)
*   **Source:** [Jewish Federation of San Francisco Donor List (archived report)](https://www.jewishfed.org/sites/default/files/2012-2013-annual-report.pdf)

#### Institutional Zionist Influence
The foundations overseen by Kelly Bronfman have been characterized as "catalytic philanthropy" for the State of Israel. ACBP was involved in creating "People-to-People diplomacy" programming following the Oslo Accords and funded initiatives intended to "reinforce the importance of Israel" and "informal education as identity-builders."

*   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post - Charles Bronfman's Catalytic Philanthropy](https://www.jpost.com/opinion/center-field-charles-bronfmans-catalytic-philanthropy-440476)
