# Contributor Report: Resnick, Stewart A.

Stewart A. Resnick is an American billionaire businessman, chairman of The Wonderful Company, and a major donor to Israeli military, academic, and cultural institutions. His philanthropic and political activities include significant financial contributions to the Israeli Defense Forces (IDF), leadership roles in pro-Israel think tanks, and the endowment of scientific research centers in Israel.

### Documentation of Support for Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Military and Intelligence-Linked Contributions
*   **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF):** Between 2015 and 2022, the Resnick Foundation donated approximately $2.4 million to the FIDF, an organization that provides financial support and services to the Israeli military. Annual contributions ranged from $100,000 to $500,000 during this period.
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch - FIDF Donor Profile](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/friends-of-the-israel-defense-forces-fidf/)
    *   **Source:** [MR Online - California Pistachio Billionaires Funding Israel's Occupation](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
*   **American Friends of IDC (Reichman University):** Resnick has served as a board member of the American Friends of IDC (now Reichman University). This institution is noted for its annual Herzliya Conference, which frequently hosts high-ranking Israeli military and intelligence officials to discuss national security strategy.
    *   **Source:** [Reichman University - Donor Recognition: Stewart and Lynda Resnick](https://www.runi.ac.il/en/friends/donors/resnick-stewart-and-lynda/)
    *   **Source:** [Mondoweiss - Oligarch Valley: Billionaire Farmers and Pro-Israel Lobbying](https://mondoweiss.net/2013/07/oligarch-valley-billionaire-farmers-profit-from-the-iran-sanctions-they-lobbied-for/)

#### 2. Scientific and Academic Philanthropy
*   **Technion-Israel Institute of Technology:** In December 2022, Resnick pledged $50 million to establish the Stewart and Lynda Resnick Sustainability Center for Catalysis. The center is intended to bolster Israel’s economy and its standing as a global center for scientific innovation.
    *   **Source:** [Technion - Historic Gift from Stewart and Lynda Resnick](https://www.technion.ac.il/en/2022/12/historic-gift-to-establish-stewart-and-lynda-resnick-sustainability-center-for-catalysis/)
    *   **Source:** [American Technion Society - Resnicks Bet Big on Israel's Clean-Tech Future](https://ats.org/ats-news/resnicks-bet-big-on-israels-clean-tech-future/)
*   **Weizmann Institute of Science:** Resnick is a documented supporter of the Weizmann Institute and has been identified as a member of its Board of Governors.
    *   **Source:** [Weizmann Institute - International Board Institutional Reports (Listings)](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/InternationalBoard/members) (Referenced via institutional philanthropic records).

#### 3. Cultural and Political Advocacy
*   **National Library of Israel:** The Resnick Foundation donated $12 million to support the transformation of the National Library in Jerusalem, including the establishment of the Lynda and Stewart Resnick Fellowship focused on Judaica and Israel-related research.
    *   **Source:** [The Wonderful Company CSR - A Lasting Legacy for Israel](https://www.wonderful.com/csr/a-lasting-legacy-for-israel-and-the-world/)
*   **Washington Institute for Near East Policy (WINEP):** Resnick has served on the Board of Trustees for WINEP, a prominent pro-Israel think tank in Washington, D.C., that was originally established as a research offshoot of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC). WINEP advocates for policies that align with Israeli security interests, including aggressive sanctions against Iran.
    *   **Source:** [MR Online - Political Lobbying and the Resnicks](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
    *   **Source:** [Substack - Billionaire Zionist Philanthropists and Iran Policy](https://andrewbrel.substack.com/p/the-wonderful-resnicks)

#### 4. Business and Geopolitical Synergy
*   **Iran Sanctions Lobbying:** Critiques of Resnick's advocacy emphasize the intersection between his pro-Israel political stances and his business interests. By funding organizations like WINEP that lobby for sanctions on Iran, Resnick supports policies that reduce competition from Iranian pistachio producers, thereby benefiting his domestic pistachio monopoly.
    *   **Source:** [Mondoweiss - How Resnick Profits from Iran Sanctions](https://mondoweiss.net/2013/07/oligarch-valley-billionaire-farmers-profit-from-the-iran-sanctions-they-lobbied-for/)
