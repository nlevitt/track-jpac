# Contributor Report: Mosbacher, Diane

Diane "Dee" Mosbacher is a filmmaker, psychiatrist, and political donor based in San Francisco, California. She is identified as a former national co-chair of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** National Council, a primary pro-Israel lobbying organization in the United States.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### Leadership in AIPAC
Mosbacher has been identified as a former national co-chair of AIPAC’s National Council. AIPAC is a prominent lobbying group that advocates for pro-Israel policies in the U.S. government.
*   **Source:** [Contributor profiles and donor lists via fdik.org](https://fdik.org/nat_pelosi_speakers_cabinet_2011.pdf) (Note: Listed among high-level donors and leadership figures associated with pro-Israel and Democratic Party fundraising).

#### Financial Support for Zionist Organizations
Through her philanthropic activities and personal donations, Mosbacher has provided financial support to several organizations that advocate for Israel or provide services within a Zionist framework.

*   **New Israel Fund (NIF):** Mosbacher, through her "Under 1 Fund" (administered by the Horizons Foundation), documented a donation of **$250** to the New Israel Fund in 2015. The NIF is a liberal Zionist organization that funds social justice projects in Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Astraea Lesbian Foundation for Justice 2015 Annual Report (Page 6), cited by kgt.si](https://kgt.si/luka-mesec-v-ozadju-te-stranke-stoji-omrezje-ki-je-ustvarilo-jeffreyja-epsteina-3-del/)
*   **National Council of Jewish Women (NCJW):** Her fund documented a **$4,000** donation to the National Council of Jewish Women in 2015. The NCJW is a Zionist organization that advocates for Jewish interests and civil rights in both the U.S. and Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Astraea Lesbian Foundation for Justice 2015 Annual Report (Page 6), cited by kgt.si](https://kgt.si/luka-mesec-v-ozadju-te-stranke-stoji-omrezje-ki-je-ustvarilo-jeffreyja-epsteina-3-del/)
*   **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco:** Mosbacher's fund provided **$10,000** to the Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco, the Peninsula, Marin and Sonoma Counties in 2015. This federation supports various pro-Israel initiatives and Jewish communal life.
    *   **Source:** [Astraea Lesbian Foundation for Justice 2015 Annual Report (Page 6), cited by kgt.si](https://kgt.si/luka-mesec-v-ozadju-te-stranke-stoji-omrezje-ki-je-ustvarilo-jeffreyja-epsteina-3-del/)

#### Family and Institutional Affiliations
Mosbacher is the daughter of **Robert Mosbacher Sr.**, the former U.S. Secretary of Commerce under George H.W. Bush, and the sister of **Robert Mosbacher Jr.**, both of whom have had long-standing ties to the Republican establishment and pro-Israel business interests.

### Summary of Political Contributions
Mosbacher is a frequent contributor to Democratic political committees and candidates who maintain pro-Israel voting records. In 2011, she was listed as a donor to the Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee (DCCC) at the $5,000+ level.
