# Contributor Report: Josh Lowenthal for Assembly 2024

Josh Lowenthal is a member of the California State Assembly representing the 69th District (Long Beach) and is the primary figure associated with the campaign committee **Josh Lowenthal for Assembly 2024**. Documentation indicates a consistent relationship with Israel and Zionist advocacy through legislative co-sponsorship, participation in funded delegations to Israel, and active membership in the California Legislative Jewish Caucus.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Israel
Lowenthal has engaged in direct advocacy and symbolic support for the State of Israel, particularly following the events of October 7, 2023.

*   **Lawmaker Trip to Israel (February 2024):** Lowenthal participated in a three-day legislative delegation to Israel in February 2024. The trip was sponsored and led by major Zionist advocacy and communal organizations, including the **Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles**, the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)**, and the **San Francisco Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)**. The stated purpose was to "bear witness" to the October 7th attacks and meet with Israeli leadership, including President Isaac Herzog.
    *   [Sacramento Bee: California lawmakers take trip to Israel](https://www.sacbee.com/news/politics-government/capitol-alert/article285458022.html)
    *   [J Weekly: State legislators visit a devastated kibbutz](https://jweekly.com/2024/03/03/state-legislators-visit-a-devastated-kibbutz-meet-with-families-of-hostages/)
*   **Public Statements of Solidarity:** On the one-year anniversary of the October 7 attacks, Lowenthal issued a public statement describing himself as "heartbroken" and emphasized his ongoing concern for Israeli hostages. In December 2023, he utilized social media to amplify statements from the California Legislative Jewish Caucus decrying "antisemitic" public comments made during a Long Beach City Council meeting regarding a ceasefire resolution.
    *   [Signal Tribune: What did Long Beach’s representatives do in October?](https://signaltribune.com/2024/10/31/what-did-long-beachs-state-and-federal-representatives-do-in-october/)
    *   [Signal Tribune: Claims of antisemitic public comments](https://signaltribune.com/2024/01/02/what-did-long-beachs-state-and-federal-representatives-do-in-december/)

### 2. Legislative and Caucus Advocacy
Lowenthal is an active member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, a body described by critics as a "pro-Israel faction" that promotes political Zionism within state governance.

*   **Jewish Identity as Ethnicity (SB 1387):** Lowenthal co-sponsored Senate Bill 1387, which requires California state agencies to recognize Jewish identity as an ethnicity in demographic data. This measure was championed by the Jewish Caucus to improve "representation" and access to resources for the Jewish community.
    *   [JNS: California Senate committee advances bill classifying Jewish identity as ‘ethnicity’](https://www.jns.org/california-senate-committee-advances-bill-classifying-jewish-identity-as-ethnicity-in-state-data/)
*   **Campus Anti-Discrimination Training (AB 2925):** Lowenthal co-authored Assembly Bill 2925, which mandates that higher education DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion) trainings include the Jewish community as one of the most targeted groups for discrimination. The bill is part of a broader package of "priority bills" from the Jewish Caucus aimed at addressing campus antisemitism.
    *   [Jewish Federation: Federation Advocacy in Action: Key Wins](https://www.jewishfed.org/news/federation-advocacy-in-action-key-wins-for-the-jewish-community/)
*   **Opposition to Ceasefire Protests:** Lowenthal was present during the January 2024 disruption of the California Assembly by pro-Palestinian protesters calling for a ceasefire. In the aftermath, he supported caucus-led efforts to portray the protests as obstructionist and framed the resulting atmosphere as one of "fear and anxiety" for the Jewish community.
    *   [KSAT: Jewish protesters calling for cease-fire in Gaza disrupt California legislative session](https://www.ksat.com/news/politics/2024/01/03/jewish-protesters-calling-for-cease-fire-in-gaza-disrupt-first-day-of-california-legislative-session/)

### 3. Critical Perspectives on Zionist Alignment
Independent and progressive media outlets have documented Lowenthal's alignment with Zionist political goals as a defining feature of his legislative tenure.

*   **Criticism of Caucus Agenda:** Reports from **LA Progressive** and **CounterPunch** characterize Lowenthal and the Jewish Caucus as "progressive except for Palestine" (PEP), alleging that they work to "erase Palestinians from the political and educational discourse" while pushing the "goals of political Zionism and the Israeli government."
    *   [LA Progressive: Who Are They? The CA Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://www.laprogressive.com/racism/ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)
    *   [CounterPunch: What do they stand for?](https://www.counterpunch.org/2024/08/20/who-are-they-the-ca-legislative-jewish-caucus/)
*   **Voter Guide Rescissions:** In February 2024, the progressive outlet **Knock LA** issued a correction to its primary election voter guide, noting a change in its assessment of Lowenthal after reviewing his legislative introductions.
    *   [Knock LA: Progressive Voter Guide March 2024](https://knock-la.com/knock-la-voter-guide-march-2024-primary/)
