# Contributor Report: William P. Lauder

William P. Lauder is the Executive Chairman of The Estée Lauder Companies and a prominent political donor and philanthropist. His involvement in Zionist advocacy is primarily documented through his leadership roles in American-Jewish organizations and his funding of initiatives focused on Israel's security, Holocaust memory, and combating antisemitism.

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Zionist Advocacy

Lauder holds several leadership positions in organizations that prioritize Israel's security and the preservation of Jewish identity.

*   **American Jewish Committee (AJC):** Lauder serves on the Board of Governors of the American Jewish Committee, an organization that describes its mission as "advocating for the Jewish people and Israel" and "strengthening Israel’s place in the world."
    *   [Source: American Jewish Committee Leadership and Board of Governors](https://www.ajc.org/leadership)
*   **University of Pennsylvania (Penn):** As an emeritus trustee and former chairman of the Board of Trustees, Lauder has been a key figure in addressing campus-based Zionist and anti-Zionist activities. Following the October 7, 2023, attacks on Israel, he was involved in discussions regarding the university's response to antisemitism and pro-Palestinian demonstrations.
    *   [Source: University of Pennsylvania Board of Trustees Emeritus](https://secretary.upenn.edu/trustees-penn/trustees-university-pennsylvania)
    *   [Source: Truthout - Pro-Israel Billionaires and University Trustees](https://truthout.org/articles/pro-israel-billionaires-line-up-behind-nc-republican-leading-campus-witch-hunt/)
*   **USC Shoah Foundation:** Lauder is a member of the Board of Councilors for the USC Shoah Foundation, where he has endowed the William P. Lauder Junior Internship Program. This program is part of the foundation's broader efforts to use Holocaust testimony to combat "contemporary antisemitism" and support the "Jewish flame inside" young people.
    *   [Source: USC Shoah Foundation Profile - William P. Lauder](https://sfi.usc.edu/profiles/william-p-lauder)
*   **92nd Street Y (92NY):** He serves on the Board of Directors of the 92nd Street Y, which hosts the Bronfman Center for Jewish Life and promotes Jewish cultural and Zionist programming.
    *   [Source: 92NY Board of Directors](https://www.92ny.org/about/leadership)

### 2. Documented Relationship to Israel and Security

Lauder's relationship to Israel is characterized by financial support for its security and its status as a Jewish state.

*   **Support for Israel's Security:** Lauder has been identified as a contributor to organizations that advocate for Israel’s security and well-being. This includes alignment with the AJC’s advocacy for US-Israel security cooperation and the defense of Israel against international criticism.
    *   [Source: eJewishPhilanthropy - William P. Lauder Birthday and Board Roles](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/daily-kickoff-michigan-move-in-the-courts-nipped-in-the-budd/)
*   **Combatting Anti-Zionism:** Through his involvement with the USC Shoah Foundation, Lauder has specifically funded efforts to map and document antisemitic and anti-Zionist activities globally. The foundation describes these efforts as essential to ensuring the security of the Jewish people and the State of Israel.
    *   [Source: USC Shoah Foundation - Countering Antisemitism Initiative](https://sfi.usc.edu/about/countering-antisemitism)

### 3. Political Donations and Advocacy

Lauder leverages his financial resources to support political candidates and groups that align with pro-Israel and Zionist interests.

*   **Political Contributions:** Lauder has donated hundreds of thousands of dollars to political action committees (PACs) and candidates who take strong pro-Israel stances. In 2024, he was noted for funding efforts to oppose political candidates perceived as hostile to Israel’s interests, such as those associated with the Democratic Socialists of America (DSA).
    *   [Source: Truthout - Billionaire Donors Opposing Candidates Critical of Israel](https://truthout.org/articles/these-billionaires-have-already-spent-19-million-in-a-bid-to-defeat-mamdani/)
*   **Association with Family Advocacy:** While William P. Lauder maintains his own philanthropic portfolio, he is frequently linked to the broader Zionist advocacy of the Lauder family, including his uncle Ronald Lauder (President of the World Jewish Congress). Documentation notes that William P. Lauder’s corporate and personal influence often complements Ronald Lauder's more public diplomatic efforts for Israel.
