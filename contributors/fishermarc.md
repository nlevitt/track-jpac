# Contributor Report: FISHER, MARC

### 1. Summary Identification
**Marc Fisher** is identified in political donor contexts as a member or representative of the **Fisher family**, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (their family investment firm). The Fisher family is among the most prominent political and philanthropic donors in California, with a documented history of supporting Zionist causes through the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and various family-led charitable funds.

### 2. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### Support for the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has had a long-standing leadership role and financial relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based organization that identifies "Supporting Israel" as one of its primary pillars.
*   **Board Membership:** Donald Fisher (founder of Gap Inc.) served as a trustee of the Koret Foundation. Doris Fisher is currently listed as an **Honorary Director** [ [Koret Foundation Leadership](https://koret.org/about/) ].

#### Birthright Israel Foundation
The Fisher family, through the **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund**, is documented as a significant contributor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which finances heritage trips to Israel designed to strengthen Jewish identity and connection to the state.
*   **Donor Recognition:** The Fisher family fund is frequently acknowledged in donor honor rolls and annual reports as a key supporter of the program’s expansion  [Birthright Israel Foundation Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) ].
