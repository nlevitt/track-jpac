# Contributor Report: Re-Elect Scott Wiener for State Senate 2020

The political committee **Re-Elect Scott Wiener for State Senate 2020** (FPPC #1392654) serves as a primary campaign vehicle for California State Senator Scott Wiener. Documentation regarding this committee and Senator Wiener indicates a sustained relationship with Israel and Zionist advocacy organizations, characterized by legislative support for pro-Israel frameworks, participation in sponsored travel to Israel, and leadership within the California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC).

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and IHRA Standards
Senator Wiener has been a primary proponent of legislation that incorporates Zionist perspectives and definitions into California state policy. 
*   **AB 715 Support:** Wiener championed Assembly Bill 715, which aimed to curb antisemitism in schools by integrating federal guidelines that utilize the International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA) working definition of antisemitism. This definition has been documented by critics as a tool to stifle anti-Zionist activism by conflating criticism of Israel with antisemitism [Source: [CAIR California](https://ca.cair.com/sacval/news/the-dangers-of-ca-assembly-bill-715-how-this-amended-bill-threatens-to-censor-palestine-in-schools/)].
*   **Ethnic Studies Curriculum:** As co-chair of the CLJC, Wiener successfully advocated for changes to the California Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum to ensure that "anti-Israel content" was excluded and that Jewish American experiences were presented in a manner consistent with mainstream Zionist narratives [Source: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Wiener#California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)].

### 2. Opposition to BDS and Anti-Zionist Activism
Wiener has maintained a public stance against movements that challenge the legitimacy of the Israeli state.
*   **BDS Movement:** Wiener has explicitly condemned the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement, characterizing it as an effort seeking the "destruction of Israel" [Source: [Mission Local](https://missionlocal.org/2026/04/scott-wiener-house-parties-congressional-bid/)].
*   **Ceasefire Resolutions:** During the 2023-2024 conflict in Gaza, Wiener opposed a local ceasefire resolution in San Francisco, aligning with pro-Israel groups that viewed such resolutions as one-sided or harmful to Israeli security interests [Source: [Mondoweiss](https://mondoweiss.net/2026/01/the-shift-the-democrats-emerging-litmus-test-on-the-gaza-genocide/)].

### 3. Ties to Pro-Israel Organizations and Sponsored Travel
Senator Wiener’s relationship with Zionist advocacy is further documented through financial and organizational ties to major Jewish advocacy groups.
*   **Sponsored Delegations:** In February 2024, Wiener participated in a legislative delegation to Israel funded by the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)** and the **Jewish Federation of Los Angeles**. The trip, which cost approximately $82,000 for the group, was intended to "develop connections between California and Israel" and showcase the impact of the October 7 attacks [Source: [CalMatters](https://calmatters.org/politics/2025/09/california-legislators-travel-spending-2024/)].
*   **Caucus Leadership:** Wiener served as the co-chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus from 2023 until early 2026. Under his leadership, the caucus prioritized funding for Jewish community security and the promotion of pro-Israel educational standards [Source: [The Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/after-accusing-israel-of-genocide-scott-wiener-steps-down-as-calif-jewish-caucus-co-chair/)].

### 4. Campaign Financing and Donor Base
While Wiener has recently pledged to reject funding from the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) due to policy differences regarding the Netanyahu government, he continues to receive significant support from pro-Israel donors.
*   **Local Jewish Leadership:** Wiener has documented that a portion of his campaign contributions—approximately $45,000 in his 2026 congressional bid alone—comes from donors who are active in pro-Israel PACs. He identifies these contributors as "local Jewish community leaders" [Source: [Mission Local](https://missionlocal.org/2026/04/scott-wiener-house-parties-congressional-bid/)].
*   **Committee Records:** The "Re-Elect Scott Wiener for State Senate 2020" committee was audited by the California Franchise Tax Board for the period of 2016–2020, during which it processed over $1.3 million in contributions and expenditures [Source: [California Political Reform Audit Report](https://www.ftb.ca.gov/about-ftb/newsroom/political-reform-audit-reports/2022-01-31-scott-d-wiener.pdf)].

### 5. Shift in Political Standing (2026)
In January 2026, Senator Wiener faced a significant rupture in his relationship with the Zionist advocacy community after he publicly accused the Israeli government of committing "genocide" in Gaza. 
*   **Backlash and Resignation:** This statement led to his resignation as co-chair of the CLJC and formal condemnation from groups like the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)** and the **Jewish Journal**, who described his turn as lacking "moral clarity" [Source: [Jewish Insider](https://jewishinsider.com/2026/01/scott-wiener-israel-genocide-backlash-jewish-groups/)]. 
*   **Political Context:** Despite this shift, Wiener continues to maintain his support for the "U.S.-Israel relationship" and the existence of Israel as a Jewish state [Source: [Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/373976/state-senator-scott-wiener-to-step-down-as-jewish-caucus-co-chair/)].
