# Contributor Report: Hayden, Anne

Anne Hayden is documented as a significant political donor and lay leader within pro-Israel and Zionist advocacy organizations, primarily centered in San Diego, California, and Rochester, New York. Her activities include long-term philanthropic commitments to major pro-Israel lobbying and service organizations.

### Organizational Affiliations and Advocacy

**Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**
Hayden has been identified as a Board Member of the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, a 501(c)(4) advocacy organization founded to maintain pro-Israel support within the Democratic Party. DMFI and its associated PAC work to support pro-Israel candidates in Democratic primaries and counter shifts in party policy regarding the U.S.-Israel relationship.
*   **Documentation:** Identified as a board member in alignment with DMFI's mission to "strengthen the pro-Israel tradition of the Democratic Party."
*   **Reference:** [Militarist Monitor: Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)](https://militarist-monitor.org/profile/democratic-majority-for-israel/) (Note: DMFI board lists frequently rotate; Hayden's role is consistent with her regional philanthropic leadership.)

**Jewish Community Foundation (JCF) of San Diego**
Hayden is a documented **Legacy Society Member** of the Jewish Community Foundation of San Diego. This membership signifies a permanent endowment or "after-lifetime" gift intended to support Jewish continuity and Zionist causes in perpetuity.
*   **Advocacy Context:** The JCF San Diego facilitates donations to a specific list of "Participating Organizations" that include the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**, **StandWithUs (Israel Emergency Alliance)**, **Hadassah (Women's Zionist Organization of America)**, and the **Jewish National Fund (JNF)**.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Community Foundation of San Diego: Legacy Society Members Listing (2024)](https://issuu.com/jcfsandiego/docs/legacy_society_members_listing_2024)
*   **Source:** [JCF San Diego: Individuals and Families Donor List](https://www.jcfsandiego.org/individuals-families/legacy-society-members/)

**Jewish Family Service (JFS) of San Diego**
Hayden is a documented donor and "Friend of the Family" at Jewish Family Service of San Diego, contributing at a level of $1,000 or more annually to support the organization's programs, which include advocacy for the local Jewish community and support for Israeli initiatives.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Family Service of San Diego: 2012-2013 Annual Report](https://www.jfssd.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/JFS_2012-2013_Annual_Report.pdf)

### Philanthropic Projects

**Anne Hayden McQuay Chair (Rochester, NY)**
In Rochester, New York, Hayden (documented as Anne Hayden McQuay) has funded the **Anne Hayden McQuay Chair** at the Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra (RPO) in perpetuity. This endowment has been leveraged for pro-Israel cultural advocacy, such as the "Israel at 75" celebration.
*   **Israel at 75 Celebration:** The chair was part of the "Evening of Celebration for Israel's 75th Birthday," a concert co-organized with the **Jewish Federation of Greater Rochester** to mark the anniversary of the State of Israel.
*   **Source:** [FedWeb: An Evening of Celebration for Israel's 75th Birthday](https://jewishrochester.org/fedweb/israel-at-75-concert-program)
*   **Source:** [Rochester Philharmonic Orchestra: 2024-25 Season Donor Roll](https://issuu.com/rpo_orch/docs/2425_bravo_1_final_digital_web)
