# Contributor Report: Lee, Otto

Otto Lee is a Democratic National Committee (DNC) member and Santa Clara County Supervisor who has been documented as an advocate for maintaining U.S. military support for Israel and as a speaker for pro-Israel advocacy organizations.

### Pro-Israel Advocacy and Legislative Activity
Lee has used his position within the DNC Resolutions Committee to support Israel's military edge and oppose efforts to restrict weapons transfers.

*   **Opposition to Arms Embargo:** During a DNC Resolutions Committee meeting in August 2025, Lee vocalized opposition to "Resolution 18," which called for an arms embargo and the suspension of U.S. military aid to Israel. Lee argued that "every day, Israel is still being threatened" and asserted that it was "truly important" for the party to ensure Israel has weapons to defend itself.
    *   [Washington Post, August 26, 2025](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/2024/08/23/dnc-israel-gaza-arms-embargo-resolution/)
*   **Abstention from Restrictions:** Lee was identified by critical observers as one of five committee members who provided a "non-vote" or abstention on measures seeking to recognize Palestinian statehood and suspend military aid. Critics characterized these abstentions as calculated moves to satisfy "pro-Israel donors" and "lobbyist pressure" while avoiding a "no" vote that would alienate the party's grassroots base.
    *   [Mondoweiss, September 19, 2025](https://mondoweiss.net/2024/09/how-the-israel-lobby-is-fighting-to-turn-back-grassroots-support-for-palestine-in-the-democratic-party-and-why-it-wont-win/)
    *   [ZNetwork, September 3, 2025](https://znetwork.org/znetarticle/the-dncs-dark-money-hypocrisy-was-on-full-display-in-minneapolis/)

### Relationship with Advocacy Organizations
Lee has a documented relationship with the American Jewish Committee (AJC) and other Zionist advocacy groups through his public speaking and policy support.

*   **AJC Speaker and Contributor:** Lee was identified as a speaker at an AJC event specifically focused on discussing US-Israel relations and strategies for combating antisemitism. His legislative work within the DNC has directly incorporated AJC findings; for instance, he co-sponsored resolutions that cited the "AJC 2024 State of Antisemitism in America" report to justify policy positions.
    *   [TC Jewfolk, August 26, 2025](https://tcjewfolk.com/2024/08/26/full-august-2024-resolutions-packet-as-of-aug-20/)
    *   [DemRulz, August 27, 2025](https://demrulz.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/2024-Summer-Meeting-Resolutions-Packet-FINAL.pdf)
*   **Solidarity Statements:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Lee issued official statements condemning Hamas and affirming his support for the Jewish community. He has collaborated with groups such as Jewish Family Services of Silicon Valley and Jewish Silicon Valley to provide community resources.
    *   [Santa Clara County District 3 Official Site, October 17, 2023](https://supervisorlee.sccgov.org/news/statements-violence-israel-palestine-resources-available)

### Donor and Political Affiliations
Lee is identified as a political donor and operative who aligns with the "pro-Israel lobby" within the Democratic Party.

*   **Donor-Influenced Positioning:** Reports from pro-Palestinian advocacy outlets have categorized Lee as a "party insider" whose voting record on Israel is influenced by "donor concerns" and groups like Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI). His non-votes on aid restrictions are cited as evidence of prioritizing the interests of high-level donors and lobbying organizations such as AIPAC.
    *   [ZNetwork, September 3, 2025](https://znetwork.org/znetarticle/the-dncs-dark-money-hypocrisy-was-on-full-display-in-minneapolis/)
*   **Local Coordination:** Lee is frequently included in high-level correspondence involving pro-Israel advocacy and community security concerns, such as coordination between the Palo Alto Police Department and local rabbis regarding anti-Israel protests.
    *   [City of Palo Alto Public Records, March 8, 2024](https://www.cityofpaloalto.org/files/assets/public/v/1/agendas-and-minutes/human-relations-commission/2024/hrc-04.11.2024-packet.pdf)
