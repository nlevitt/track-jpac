# California Contributor Report: Shawn Steel Law Firm Inc.

This report examines the political activities and documented relationships of **Shawn Steel Law Firm Inc.** (and its principal, Shawn Steel) regarding the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy. Shawn Steel serves as a National Committeeman for the Republican National Committee (RNC) from California and is a former chairman of the California Republican Party.

### 1. Documented Zionist Advocacy and Institutional Affiliations
Shawn Steel has maintained a long-standing relationship with organizations dedicated to pro-Israel advocacy, most notably the **Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC)**. His involvement dates back to at least 2001, when he participated in meetings to strengthen ties between the California GOP and the RJC's Los Angeles chapter.

*   **RJC Leadership and Coordination:** In June 2001, Steel, as California GOP chairman, met with RJC leaders to facilitate Jewish involvement in Republican politics, emphasizing the party’s support for Israel's security interests.

### 2. Legislative and Policy Resolutions
Steel has utilized his position within the RNC to introduce and support resolutions that explicitly defend the State of Israel and condemn its critics.

*   **RNC Resolution on Antisemitism and Israel (2023):** In January 2023, Steel proposed a resolution to the RNC that condemned "anti-Israel hatred" and antisemitism. The resolution specifically targeted conservative figures like Nick Fuentes and Ye (Kanye West) for their remarks, while also criticizing Democratic Representatives Ilhan Omar and Rashida Tlaib for rhetoric the resolution characterized as "anti-Israel."
