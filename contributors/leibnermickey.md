# Contributor Report: Richard Leib (Identified as LEIBNER, MICKEY)

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

Research indicates that the individual identified as "LEIBNER, MICKEY" and described as the "Former Chair of UC Regents" is **Richard Leib** (commonly known as **Rich Leib**). Richard Leib served as the Chair of the University of California Board of Regents from 2022 to 2024 and is a documented advocate for Zionist causes and Israel-related initiatives. The name "Mickey Leibner" appears to be a conflation with **Michael "Mickey" Leibner**, a Washington D.C.-based attorney and pro-Israel advocate, but the institutional role and specific advocacy described are attributed to Richard Leib.

### 1. Identity and Institutional Role
Richard Leib is a San Diego-based businessman and political consultant who has held several high-level leadership positions in California’s public education system.

*   **UC Board of Regents Chair:** Leib was appointed to the UC Board of Regents in 2018 and served as its Chair from July 2022 to July 2024. During his tenure, he was the primary spokesperson for the board on issues regarding campus safety, antisemitism, and university investments.
    *   **Source:** [UC Office of the President: Board of Regents Leadership](https://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/press-room/uc-regents-chair-richard-leib-statement-uc-berkeley-school-law)
    *   **Source:** LA Times: Rich Leib heads the UC Board of Regents (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Documented Zionist Advocacy
Leib has explicitly identified as a "proud Zionist" and has utilized his platform as Regent Chair to advocate for the inclusion of Zionism in university life and to condemn anti-Zionist activities on campus.

*   **"Self-Proclaimed Proud Zionist":** Activist groups and news reports have documented Leib's self-identification as a Zionist. In 2024, he was targeted by campus protesters at UC Berkeley who circulated "wanted" posters labeling him a "self-proclaimed proud Zionist."
    *   **Source:** [Jewish News Syndicate (JNS): Fake wanted posters of Jewish California regents](https://www.jns.org/u.s.-news/fake-wanted-posters-for-jewish-california-regents-found-at-berkeley-campus)
*   **Opposition to Anti-Zionist Bylaws:** In October 2022, Leib issued a formal statement condemning a bylaw passed by several UC Berkeley Law student groups that banned Zionist speakers. He characterized Zionism as an "inextricable part of Jewish identity" and described the groups' actions as "reprehensible."
    *   **Source:** [University of California: Statement on UC Berkeley School of Law](https://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/press-room/uc-regents-chair-richard-leib-statement-uc-berkeley-school-law)
*   **Condemnation of Anti-Zionism as Antisemitism:** During university meetings, Leib has argued that aggressive forms of anti-Zionism on campus effectively attack the identity of Jewish students and has pressed for policies to address this "form of anti-Zionism."
    *   **Source:** CalMatters: UC regents tackle free speech amid Israel-Palestine conflict (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Support for Israel Causes
Leib’s advocacy extends to his official statements and private philanthropic associations with organizations supporting the State of Israel.

*   **October 7 Response:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Leib co-authored a statement describing the event as an "act of terrorism" and "horrific savagery," while rebuking faculty groups that defended the actions of Hamas or framed the conflict through a "colonial narrative."
    *   **Source:** [California Globe: UC Regent Denounces Pro-Hamas Statement](https://californiaglobe.com/fr/uc-regent-denounces-obnoxious-pompous-pro-hamas-statement-by-uc-faculty/)
*   **Philanthropic Ties:** Leib and his family are listed as donors to the Jewish Community Foundation of San Diego and have been identified as supporters of several pro-Israel organizations.
    *   **Source:** Jewish Community Foundation of San Diego: Our Supporters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   **Source:** [Daily Bruin: Richard Leib donor to several pro-Israel organizations](https://dailybruin.com/2012/10/01/a-seat-at-the-table)

### 4. Conflicting Identity: Michael "Mickey" Leibner
Documentation exists for a separate individual named **Michael "Mickey" Leibner**, who is frequently mentioned in pro-Israel political circles but does not hold the UC Regent Chair title.

*   **Professional Background:** Michael Leibner is a partner at the law firm Mayer Brown in Washington D.C., specializing in political law.
    *   **Source:** Mayer Brown: Mickey Leibner Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Advocacy:** He has been associated with the **American Friends of Nishmat**, an organization supporting the Nishmat Institute in Jerusalem, and is a recurring figure in the *Jewish Insider* "Daily Kickoff," which tracks major pro-Israel donors and advocates.
    *   **Source:** eJewishPhilanthropy: Michael “Mickey” Leibner, American Friends of Nishmat (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
