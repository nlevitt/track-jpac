# Contributor Report: Jacob Helberg

Jacob Helberg is a political donor, technology advisor, and United States government official who has documented ties to pro-Israel advocacy and Zionist organizations. Formerly a senior advisor to Palantir CEO Alex Karp and a commissioner on the U.S.–China Economic and Security Review Commission, Helberg currently serves as the Under Secretary of State for Economic Growth, Energy, and the Environment in the Trump administration. His political activities and public commentary are characterized by a focus on the intersection of national security, technology, and support for the State of Israel.

### 1. Political Contributions and Party Realignment
Helberg’s shift in political affiliation was explicitly motivated by his views on Israel. After donating hundreds of thousands of dollars to the Joe Biden 2020 campaign, Helberg became a major donor to Donald Trump’s 2024 campaign, contributing at least $1 million to $2 million.

*   **Rationale for Switch:** Helberg cited the Democratic Party’s perceived record on "Jew-hatred" and its shift toward "anti-Israel" sentiment as primary reasons for his realignment. [Source: Jewish Insider (May 17, 2024)](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/05/jacob-helberg-trump-donor-biden-israel-antisemitism/)
*   **Public Statements:** In May 2024, Helberg publicly stated, "If you support Israel, there’s only one choice in this election," referring to Donald Trump. [Source: JNS.org (July 23, 2024)](https://www.jns.org/jacob-helberg-wokeness-driving-generational-realignment/)
*   **Criticism of Opponents:** Helberg has criticized the Biden-Harris administration for calling for ceasefires in Gaza and has characterized Kamala Harris’s staff as potentially ending the U.S.-Israel alliance. [Source: JNS.org (July 23, 2024)](https://www.jns.org/jacob-helberg-wokeness-driving-generational-realignment/)

### 2. Role in TikTok Legislation
Helberg was a principal architect behind the *Protecting Americans from Foreign Adversary Controlled Applications Act*, which forced the divestiture of TikTok. He explicitly linked the urgency of the ban to the platform's portrayal of Israel and the proliferation of pro-Palestinian content.

*   **The "Galvanizing Force":** Helberg identified the "tidal wave of anti-Israel" content following the October 7 attacks as a "galvanizing force" that motivated lawmakers who were previously undecided on a TikTok ban. [Source: Wall Street Journal via Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=39682855)
*   **Allegations of CCP Influence:** He has argued that the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) uses TikTok to push "anti-Israel disinformation" and "state propaganda" to divide the U.S. from its allies. [Source: National Interest (Nov 20, 2023)](https://nationalinterest.org/feature/why-tiktok-national-security-threat-207399)
*   **Coordination with Jewish Groups:** Helberg collaborated with tech leaders and organizations like the Anti-Defamation League (ADL) to lobby for the ban based on the alleged spread of antisemitism on the platform. [Source: Straturka (May 09, 2024)](https://www.straturka.com/who-was-behind-the-tiktok-ban/)

### 3. Diplomatic and Economic Initiatives (Pax Silica)
As Under Secretary of State, Helberg launched "Pax Silica," a strategic initiative intended to build AI and supply chain security among "sovereign allies," with Israel as a central partner.

*   **Strategic Partnership:** In January 2026, Helberg signed a memorandum in Jerusalem with the Head of Israel’s National AI Directorate, Erez Eskel, to formalize a strategic AI partnership. [Source: Israel National News (Jan 16, 2026)](https://www.israelnationalnews.com/news/383742)
*   **Technological Normalization:** Helberg has promoted Pax Silica as a "new road toward normalization" between Israel and regional neighbors like Qatar and the UAE, based on shared technology supply chains rather than ideology. [Source: Jewish Insider (Feb 25, 2026)](https://jewishinsider.com/2026/02/jacob-helberg-pax-silica-qatar-israel-normalization/)
*   **Engagement with Netanyahu:** He participated in high-level meetings with Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu to discuss using AI to maintain Israel’s "technological advantage in defense." [Source: Jerusalem Post (Oct 1, 2025)](https://www.jpost.com/business-and-innovation/article-822557)

### 4. Affiliations and Public Commentary
Helberg’s Zionist advocacy frequently appears in his public-facing roles and media appearances.

*   **Palantir Technologies:** During his tenure as a senior advisor at Palantir, a company with significant defense contracts with Israel, Helberg supported the use of technology to assist allies including Israel. [Source: JNS.org (July 23, 2024)](https://www.jns.org/jacob-helberg-wokeness-driving-generational-realignment/)
*   **Hill and Valley Forum:** Helberg founded this forum to connect Silicon Valley venture capitalists with lawmakers, often highlighting Israel's "innovative technology ecosystem" and its role as a "critical partner." [Source: Apple Podcasts (May 03, 2025)](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/jacob-helberg-delian-asparouhov-christian-garrett-hill/id1744573887?i=1000654407887)
*   **Social Media Advocacy:** Helberg uses platforms like X (formerly Twitter) to publish pro-Israel messages, such as praising the Abraham Accords and defending Israel's military actions as essential for Western security. [Source: Israel National News (Dec 23, 2025)](https://www.israelnationalnews.com/news/382417)
