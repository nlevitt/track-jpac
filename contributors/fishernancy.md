# Contributor Report: FISHER, NANCY

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

Nancy Fisher is a San Francisco-based political contributor and philanthropist identified in donor databases as a significant supporter of Zionist institutions and Israeli advocacy organizations. She is associated with the Fisher family, founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family investment firm **Pisces Inc.**, whose collective philanthropic activities have focused heavily on Jewish identity, Holocaust remembrance, and educational initiatives in Israel.

### 1. Documented Support for Zionist Organizations

Fisher’s philanthropic record is characterized by substantial financial commitments to organizations dedicated to strengthening Jewish ties to Israel and documenting Jewish history for advocacy purposes.

*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Fisher is documented as a donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to "strengthen Jewish identity" and "connections with Israel." She has been listed in the foundation’s "Scroll of Honor" for contributions exceeding $10,000.
    *   Source: Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Koret Foundation:** Documentation from the **USC Shoah Foundation** lists Nancy Fisher alongside the **Koret Foundation** as a major donor. The Koret Foundation is a San Francisco-based organization known for its significant funding of pro-Israel causes and "Jewish community building."
    *   Source: USC Shoah Foundation Donor List (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies:** Fisher is a documented supporter of the **Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies**, a Jerusalem-based institution that integrates classical Jewish study with contemporary Zionist perspectives.
    *   Source: Pardes Institute Full List of Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **USC Shoah Foundation:** Fisher serves on the **Board of Councilors** for the USC Shoah Foundation and has conducted over 150 interviews for their archive. While the foundation’s primary mission is Holocaust documentation, critics and donor reports classify it within the Zionist advocacy ecosystem due to its role in institutionalizing Jewish historical memory in alignment with Zionist state narratives.
    *   [Source: USC Shoah Foundation Board and Donor Profile](https://sfi.usc.edu/profile/nancy-fisher)
    *   Source: USC Shoah Foundation Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Affiliation with Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.

Fisher is identified as part of the **Fisher family of San Francisco**, the founding family of **Gap Inc.** and the principals of **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment office). The family’s wealth, generated through these entities, has been utilized for decades to influence California politics and support a network of Zionist and Jewish-focused charities.

*   **Family Connection:** Nancy Fisher has been identified as a resident and executive director associated with the Fisher family estate at **2121 Vallejo St, San Francisco**, the long-time residence of Gap co-founders **Don and Doris Fisher**.
    *   Source: Property Records and Resident Documentation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   Source: The Origin Project Executive Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Note: Nancy Fisher is listed as Executive Director of this family-funded initiative).
*   **Political Donor Activity:** In California, the Fisher family is documented as a major donor block, contributing over $300,000 to figures like Governor Gavin Newsom and supporting initiatives that align with their philanthropic and political interests, including pro-Israel legislative support.
    *   [Source: InfluenceWatch: Pisces Foundation / Fisher Family](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/pisces-foundation/)

### 3. Zionist Advocacy and Educational Funding

Beyond direct institutional support, Fisher’s advocacy extends to educational and cultural programs that emphasize Jewish continuity and the legitimacy of the Israeli state.

*   **Holocaust Documentation as Advocacy:** Fisher’s work with the Shoah Foundation and the "Last Chance Testimony Fund" is frequently cited in donor reports as a "gift to the Jewish future," providing the historical framework used by Zionist organizations to justify and defend the existence of the State of Israel.

*   **Israel-Based Philanthropy:** Through her documented donations to institutions like the **Pardes Institute**, Fisher supports the cultivation of "Zionist leadership" among the Jewish diaspora.
