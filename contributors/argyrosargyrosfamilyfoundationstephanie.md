# California Contributor Report: Stephanie Argyros / Argyros Family Foundation

This report documents the political and philanthropic activities of Stephanie Argyros and the Argyros Family Foundation (AFF), with a specific focus on their documented financial and organizational support for the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy.

### 1. Overview of Contributors
The **Argyros Family Foundation** is a private 501(c)(3) foundation based in Costa Mesa, California, established in 1979 by George L. Argyros and his wife Julia Argyros. **Stephanie Argyros** serves as a Director of the foundation and is a principal executive within the family’s real estate and investment firm, Arnel & Affiliates.

*   **Financial Scope:** As of 2024, the Argyros Family Foundation reported total assets of approximately **$326.9 million** and annual giving exceeding **$34.4 million**.
*   **Documentation:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer: Argyros Family Foundation](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/953421867)

### 2. Documented Support for Zionist and Pro-Israel Organizations
The foundation’s giving history includes consistent support for organizations that advocate for Israeli state interests, land development in Israel, and pro-Israel educational initiatives in the United States.

The Argyros Family Foundation has a documented history of financial support for the Jewish National Fund (JNF), a Zionist organization central to land acquisition and development in Israel.
*   **Documentation:** In public tax filings, the foundation has been listed as a donor to the JNF, including a recorded grant of $30,000 for general operations.

### 3. Political Advocacy and Leadership
The Argyros family exerts influence through political action committees (PACs) that prioritize pro-Israel foreign policy and conservative domestic agendas.

*   **The New Majority PAC:** Founded in 1999 by George Argyros and other influential donors, this organization is California's largest Republican PAC. It consistently funds candidates who align with pro-Israel policy stances and conservative political frameworks.
    *   **Source:** Orange County Business Journal: Annual Inventory of Influence (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Diplomatic and Policy Ties:** George Argyros served as the U.S. Ambassador to Spain (2001–2004) under the George W. Bush administration and is a Trustee Emeritus of the **Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS)**, a think tank that provides policy analysis on Middle East security and U.S.-Israel relations.

### 4. Regional Philanthropic Integration
Stephanie Argyros and the foundation are primary underwriters for cultural and educational institutions in Orange County that frequently host pro-Israel programming or maintain formal ties with Israeli institutions.
*   **Chapman University:** The family has donated over $20 million to Chapman University (home to the Argyros School of Business and Economics). The university maintains a robust Holocaust and Jewish Studies program and has historically hosted significant pro-Israel speakers and events funded by the family's contributions.
    *   **Source:** [Chapman University: Forward Magazine Research Report](https://www.chapman.edu/page-not-found.aspx)
*   **Segerstrom Center for the Arts:** Stephanie Argyros is a lead donor and fundraiser for the Segerstrom Center, which often features Israeli cultural performers and has a leadership structure that overlaps with several pro-Israel advocacy organizations in Southern California.
