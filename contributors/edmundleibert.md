# Contributor Report: Richard Leib

### Profile Overview
Richard Leib is a California-based businessman, political consultant, and the former Chair of the University of California Board of Regents. Appointed to the Board in 2018 by Governor Jerry Brown, Leib was elected Chair in 2022 and served through mid-2024. Professionally, he is the president and CEO of Dunleer Strategies and has a long history in Democratic politics, having served on the staff of prominent pro-Israel U.S. Representatives such as Henry Waxman and Mel Levine.

### Zionist Advocacy and Public Statements
Leib has utilized his position as Chair of the UC Regents to issue high-profile defenses of Zionism and condemnations of anti-Zionist movements within the university system.

*   **Defense of Zionism as Identity:** In October 2022, following the adoption of bylaws by several UC Berkeley School of Law student groups that prohibited Zionist speakers, Leib issued a formal statement on behalf of the Regents. He characterized the groups' actions as a "disturbing development" and asserted that "for many of these students, Zionism is an inextricable part of their Jewish identity" ([University of California](https://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/press-room/uc-regents-chair-richard-leib-statement-uc-berkeley-school-law)).
*   **Condemnation of Anti-Zionist Faculty:** Leib has been a vocal critic of the UC Ethnic Studies Faculty Council. In November 2023, he described the council's letter—which criticized the university’s use of the term "terrorism" to describe the October 7 Hamas attacks—as "repugnant and offensive" (CalMatters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). 
*   **Opposition to Campus Protests:** During the 2023–2024 campus encampments, Leib pressured chancellors to enforce strict "time, place, and manner" policies to restrict pro-Palestinian protests. He advocated for a $7 million fund to "combat antisemitism" on campuses, a move seen by critics as a mechanism to suppress anti-Zionist political speech (CalMatters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### Documented Pro-Israel Financial Support
Leib’s financial contributions to pro-Israel causes are primarily facilitated through his family’s philanthropic vehicle.

*   **Leib Family Fund:** Richard Leib and his spouse, Sharon Rosen, operate the **Leib Family Fund** under the auspices of the **Jewish Community Foundation of San Diego**. The foundation is a major conduit for pro-Israel philanthropy in Southern California. Leib’s fund has been documented as a donor to University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) and other academic institutions specifically in support of Jewish and Israel-aligned programming (UCSB Annual Report of Private Giving (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### Critical Reception and Controversies
Leib’s advocacy has drawn significant criticism from student and faculty organizations who argue his Zionism biases his administrative neutrality.

*   **Open Letter for Resignation:** In December 2023, a coalition of over 300 faculty members and students called for Leib’s resignation. They cited his social media activity, specifically "liking" posts that described Palestinian activists as "terrorist sympathizers" and mockingly compared "Queers for Palestine" to "cows for McDonald’s" (Medium / Open Letter (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
