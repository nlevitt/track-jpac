# Contributor Report: Cottie Petrie-Norris for Assembly 2022

Cottie Petrie-Norris is a Democratic member of the California State Assembly representing District 73 (formerly District 74). Her 2022 re-election campaign, "Cottie Petrie-Norris for Assembly 2022," was formally endorsed by organizations focused on maintaining a pro-Israel orientation within the Democratic Party.

### Endorsement by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)
Cottie Petrie-Norris was endorsed by the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) PAC** during her 2022 campaign. DMFI is an advocacy group and political action committee that describes its mission as "strengthening the pro-Israel tradition in the Democratic Party" and ensuring the U.S.-Israel alliance remains a core Democratic value. 

*   **Documentation:** DMFI PAC included Petrie-Norris in its slate of endorsed candidates for the 2022 election cycle.
    *   [Source: Ballotpedia - Endorsements by Democratic Majority for Israel PAC](https://ballotpedia.org/Democratic_Majority_for_Israel_PAC)
    *   [Source: DMFI PAC - Endorsed Candidates List](https://dmfipac.org/candidates/)

### Advocacy and Public Statements on Israel
Petrie-Norris has maintained a documented public record of support for the State of Israel, particularly through her association with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)** and the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**.

*   **October 7 Response:** Following the October 2023 Hamas-led attacks, Petrie-Norris was identified as a key state ally by JPAC in their "In Solidarity with Israel" documentation. She was among a group of California legislators who issued formal statements condemning the attacks and affirming support for Israel’s security.
    *   [Source: JPAC - In Solidarity with Israel Statement](https://jpac-cal.org/in-solidarity-with-israel/)
*   **Legislative Record:** In 2024, Petrie-Norris supported **AB 715**, a bill focused on "educational equity" and "antisemitism prevention" in California schools. The bill was a priority for the California Legislative Jewish Caucus and pro-Israel advocacy groups.
    *   [Source: LegiScan - CA AB715 Roll Call](https://legiscan.com/CA/rollcall/AB715/id/1325875)
*   **Holocaust Remembrance and Public Commemoration:** Petrie-Norris has frequently participated in Israel-aligned commemorations, such as the official Holocaust Remembrance Day observations on the Assembly Floor. In 2024, she documented her participation in these events as part of her legislative outreach.
    *   [Source: Official Website of Assemblywoman Cottie Petrie-Norris - Catching Up With Cottie (May 2024)](https://ad73.asm.ca.gov/article/catching-cottie-57)

### Relationship with Pro-Israel Advocacy Groups
Petrie-Norris’s relationship with Zionist advocacy is further evidenced by her alignment with **Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA)** and her associate involvement with the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**. While she is not Jewish, she is frequently listed as an "associate member" or a reliable legislative ally of the caucus on matters regarding the U.S.-Israel relationship and state-level pro-Israel initiatives.

*   **Organizational Ties:** She is listed as an endorsed candidate by local pro-Israel Democratic chapters that vet candidates based on their support for a "strong U.S.-Israeli relationship" and opposition to the Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   [Source: Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA) Endorsements](https://dfi-la.org/endorsed/)
