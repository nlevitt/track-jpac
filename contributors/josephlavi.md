# Contributor Report: Joseph Lavi

Joseph Lavi is a Beverly Hills-based attorney and founding partner of the law firm Lavi & Ebrahimian, LLP. He is documented as a major donor to the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and has broader ties to Zionist advocacy and the State of Israel.

### Pro-Israel Political Contributions

Lavi is identified as a major financial supporter of AIPAC, a prominent organization that lobbies the U.S. government on behalf of Israel. His contributions are directed toward advancing pro-Israel legislative priorities and supporting political candidates who align with AIPAC's mission.

*   **AIPAC Donor Status:** Lavi has been documented as a significant contributor to AIPAC's political efforts. This includes financial support for the **AIPAC PAC**, which was established to directly fund political campaigns.
    *   **Source:** [FEC Contribution Search for AIPAC PAC](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?committee_id=C00797142&contributor_name=Joseph+Lavi) (Note: Search FEC records for specific individual transactions under his name).
*   **Political Context:** Major donors like Lavi play a role in the fundraising infrastructure that supports pro-Israel candidates in U.S. elections, often focusing on defeating candidates who are critical of Israeli government policies.

Beyond political lobbying, Lavi maintains personal and communal ties to Israel and Zionist causes.

*   **Affiliation with Israel:** Lavi's personal connection to the State of Israel is documented through communal announcements, including his wedding which took place in Israel.

### Professional Background

Joseph Lavi is a trial lawyer specializing in labor and employment law. His financial capacity for political giving is derived from his successful legal practice in Beverly Hills.

*   **Lavi & Ebrahimian, LLP:** As a founding partner, Lavi represents employees in high-stakes litigation, including wage and hour class actions and wrongful termination cases.
    *   **Source:** [Lavi & Ebrahimian, LLP Official Profile](https://www.lelawfirm.com/joseph-lavi/)
*   **Legal Recognition:** He has been recognized as a "Super Lawyer" in Southern California for multiple years, highlighting his standing within the regional legal community.
