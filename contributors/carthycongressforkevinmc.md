# California Contributor Report: Kevin McCarthy for Congress

This report examines the documented relationship between the California political committee **Kevin McCarthy for Congress** (FEC ID: C00420935 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)) and Zionist advocacy interests, specifically focusing on legislative support, diplomatic engagement, and financial contributions from pro-Israel interest groups.

### 1. Legislative Support for Zionist Objectives
As a primary leader in the U.S. House of Representatives, Kevin McCarthy has consistently authored and advanced legislation that aligns with the strategic and ideological goals of Zionist advocacy groups.

*   **Opposition to Anti-Zionist Movements:** McCarthy has been a vocal opponent of the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement. In 2019, he threatened formal action against members of Congress who supported BDS, framing the movement as a threat to the U.S.-Israel alliance (Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Defining Israel's Status:** In July 2023, McCarthy brought a resolution to the House floor (H.Res. 571) that declared Israel is "not a racist or apartheid state" and reaffirmed the U.S.'s "unwavering" support for the nation. This resolution was passed with significant bipartisan support as a direct rebuttal to progressive critiques of Israeli policy (Source: Worldwide Speakers Group (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Aid and Security Assistance:** McCarthy has advocated for full, unconditional funding of Israel’s security needs, including the $3.8 billion annual military aid package. During his 2023 address to the Knesset, he pledged that under his leadership, the U.S. would continue to prioritize Israel’s military superiority in the region ([Source: Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/mccarthy-extols-ties-at-knesset-vows-full-support-for-israeli-security-against-iran/)).

### 2. Financial Ties to Pro-Israel Interest Groups
The 'Kevin McCarthy for Congress' committee and its affiliated leadership PACs have received substantial financial backing from donors and organizations dedicated to Zionist advocacy.

*   **AIPAC and the Pro-Israel Lobby:** McCarthy is a major recipient of contributions from pro-Israel interests. In the 2018 cycle, he was identified as a top recipient of donations from pro-Israel groups (Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). His primary fundraiser and top advisor, Jeff Miller, is documented as having deep ties to both **AIPAC** and the **Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC)** (Source: Mondoweiss (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **AIPAC PAC Endorsement:** McCarthy was among the first group of candidates endorsed by the AIPAC PAC upon its formation in 2022, a list that specifically targeted candidates who "consistently support" the U.S.-Israel relationship (Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Mega-Donor Support:** Major Zionist philanthropists, such as the late Sheldon Adelson and his wife Miriam Adelson, have historically been the largest contributors to the Republican committees McCarthy has led, including the **Congressional Leadership Fund (CLF)**, which spent millions to maintain the GOP majority (Source: LA Times (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 3. Diplomatic Advocacy and Zionist Symbolic Actions
McCarthy has utilized his official platform to reinforce the ideological foundations of Zionism through symbolic gestures and diplomatic visits.

*   **Knesset Address:** In May 2023, McCarthy became only the second U.S. House Speaker in history to address the Knesset plenum. In his speech, he described Israel's rebirth as a "modern miracle" and affirmed the historical connection between the Jewish people and the land (Source: PBS News (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Suppression of Palestinian Commemoration:** In May 2023, McCarthy used his authority as Speaker to cancel a "Nakba" commemoration event at the U.S. Capitol that had been organized by Rep. Rashida Tlaib. He replaced the event with a "pro-Israel discussion" to celebrate the 75th anniversary of the founding of the state of Israel (Source: The Pittsburgh Jewish Chronicle/JTA (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Delegation Leadership:** Since entering Congress, McCarthy has led numerous freshman delegations to Israel, a practice he considers a "priority" for building a long-term commitment to the U.S.-Israel bond among new lawmakers (Source: Ami Magazine (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### Summary of Contributor Relationship
Documentation indicates that **Kevin McCarthy for Congress** maintains a symbiotic relationship with Zionist advocacy groups. McCarthy provides high-level legislative and diplomatic defense of Israeli state interests, while these groups and their associated donors provide the financial and organizational support necessary for his political leadership and conference expansion.
