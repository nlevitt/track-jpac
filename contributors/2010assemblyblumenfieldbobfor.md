# Contributor Report: Bob Blumenfield for Assembly 2010

Bob Blumenfield for Assembly 2010 was the political campaign committee for Robert J. Blumenfield’s re-election to the California State Assembly, 40th District. The committee supported Blumenfield’s legislative tenure, during which he established a documented record of support for the State of Israel through legislative initiatives, organizational leadership, and advocacy against the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and Strategic Partnerships
During his tenure in the California State Assembly (2008–2013), supported by the 2010 campaign committee, Blumenfield initiated several high-profile legislative efforts to formalize and protect the relationship between California and Israel.

*   **California-Israel Memorandum of Understanding (MOU):** Blumenfield authored Assembly Bill 1032 during the 2009–2010 Regular Session, which called for the creation of a strategic partnership between California and Israel. This legislation served as the precursor to the formal [Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)](https://www.gov.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/Israel_MOU.pdf) signed in 2014 by Governor Jerry Brown and Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, focusing on joint innovation in water conservation, alternative energy, and cybersecurity.
*   **Founding of the Jewish Caucus:** In 2012, Blumenfield was a [founding member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/about). The caucus was established to advocate for Jewish community interests and strengthen the state’s ties with Israel.
*   **Anti-BDS Resolutions:** Blumenfield co-authored [House Resolution 35 (2012)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201120120HR35), which condemned anti-Semitism on university campuses and explicitly rejected the "demonization and delegitimization of Israel." The resolution defined certain criticisms of Israel as anti-Semitic under the European Union Agency for Fundamental Rights' working definition.

### 2. Opposition to the BDS Movement
Blumenfield has consistently utilized his political platform to oppose the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement at both the state and local levels.

*   **Support for AB 2844:** As a Los Angeles City Councilmember, Blumenfield introduced [Resolution 15-0002-S150](https://clkrep.lacity.org/onlinedocs/2015/15-0002-S150_rpt_CLA_04-18-2016.pdf) in 2016 to formally support Assembly Bill 2844 (the California Combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions of Israel Act). The state bill sought to prohibit public entities from contracting with companies that participate in boycotts of Israel.
*   **Public Advocacy:** In a [2016 editorial for the Los Angeles Times](https://www.latimes.com/opinion/readersreact/la-ol-le-israel-bds-boycott-20160710-snap-story.html), Blumenfield argued that the BDS movement was "pernicious" and "counter to California’s policies" of collaboration with Israel, specifically citing the Los Angeles-Eilat Innovation and Cooperation Task Force he helped establish.

### 3. Affiliations and Leadership
Blumenfield’s political and professional career is marked by long-standing ties to pro-Israel and Zionist organizations.

*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** Blumenfield served as the [Chair of the Valley Advisory Board for the Anti-Defamation League](https://ballotpedia.org/Bob_Blumenfield) and was a member of the ADL’s Regional Board. His work with the ADL involved advocacy against anti-Semitism and support for Israel's security interests.
*   **Staff for Howard Berman:** Prior to his own election, Blumenfield served for over 15 years as a senior aide and District Director for [Congressman Howard Berman](https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-2008-may-17-me-blumenfield17-story.html), a prominent pro-Israel legislator and former chair of the House Foreign Affairs Committee.
*   **Solidarity Press Events:** In October 2023, following the escalation of conflict in the region, Blumenfield hosted a press event at LA City Hall to [stand in solidarity with Israel](https://blumenfield.lacity.gov/press-release/hamas-attacks-israel-la-city-councilmembers-stand-solidarity-israeli-people), stating that the city "unequivocally condemn[s] Hamas and stand[s] with the Israeli people."

### 4. Campaign Funding and Support
The 'Bob Blumenfield for Assembly 2010' committee was funded by a range of donors, including family and prominent political figures. 

*   **Independent Expenditures:** In his initial 2008 race, more than $154,000 was raised through [independent expenditures](https://www.latimes.com/archives/la-xpm-2008-may-17-me-blumenfield17-story.html) by his father and Congressman Howard Berman's campaign committee to support his election. These funds established the political foundation for his subsequent re-election in 2010 and his ongoing pro-Israel legislative agenda.
