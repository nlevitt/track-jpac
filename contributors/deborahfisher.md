# Contributor Report: Fisher, Deborah (Doris)

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

### Overview
The name "Fisher, Deborah" in the context of the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** is frequently associated in activist documentation with **Doris Fisher** (the co-founder of Gap Inc.) and the broader Fisher family of San Francisco. The Fisher family, particularly Doris Fisher and her late husband Donald Fisher, are prominent billionaires whose philanthropic and political activities include significant support for Zionist institutions and Israeli causes. They are frequently linked to the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**, both of which are central to Zionist advocacy and the strengthening of ties between the Jewish diaspora and the State of Israel.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Funding of Zionist Institutions and Israeli Causes
The Fisher family has a documented history of supporting organizations that promote Zionism and the State of Israel:
*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family is identified as a contributor to Birthright Israel, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening Jewish identity and connection to the Israeli state. While the **Max M. and Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation** (a separate entity from the Gap Fishers) is a founding partner of Birthright, the Gap Fisher family is also recorded as supporting the program through various Jewish federations and endowment funds.
    *   Source: Forbes Israel - World's Jewish Billionaires 2024 (Doris Fisher listing) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   [Source: J. Weekly - Gap founders leave stamp on Jewish community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
*   **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (San Francisco):** Doris and Donald Fisher were long-time major supporters of the S.F.-based Jewish Community Federation, which allocates significant funding to projects in Israel and pro-Israel advocacy in the United States.
    *   [Source: J. Weekly - Fisher Family Philanthropy](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
*   **Koret Foundation Association:** The Fishers are closely associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a major pro-Israel philanthropic body. They have co-funded numerous high-profile projects in San Francisco alongside Koret, such as the de Young Museum's expansion. Furthermore, the Fisher family's signature cause—charter schools—aligns with the Koret Foundation’s "Koret Task Force" on K-12 education, creating a strategic partnership in conservative and neoliberal policy advocacy.
    *   Source: Koret Foundation - Major Donor Recognition (The Fisher Family) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   [Source: J. Weekly - Donors to the de Young Fund](https://jweekly.com/2005/10/28/donors-help-make-de-young-a-success/)

#### 2. Political Contributions and Pro-Israel Advocacy
*   **AIPAC and Lobbying:** Members of the Fisher family, including Doris's son **John Fisher** (Executive Vice Chairman of Pisces Inc.), have been documented as donors to the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and other groups that lobby for U.S. military and diplomatic support for Israel. 
    *   Source: OpenSecrets - Political Contributions (John J. Fisher) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Opposition to Pro-Palestine Policy:** The family office, **Pisces Inc.**, and the Doris & Donald Fisher Fund have been identified by activist groups like the **Mapping Project** as part of a network of "Zionist power" that uses wealth to influence local and national policy in favor of Israeli interests and against Palestinian advocacy.
    *   [Source: The Mapping Project - Fisher, Doris Profile](https://mappingproject.org/entities/fisher-doris/) *(Note: This activist source identifies the subject specifically as a target due to Zionist funding).*

#### 3. Institutional Leadership and Representation
*   **Congregation Emanu-El:** The Fisher family holds a prominent role at Congregation Emanu-El in San Francisco, one of the oldest and most influential synagogues in California, which maintains strong institutional ties to the Israeli government and Zionist organizations.
    *   Source: Wikipedia - Doris F. Fisher (Personal Life) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pisces Inc. and Family Office:** **Pisces Inc.** serves as the family office for the Fishers. Through this entity, the family manages the wealth that fuels their philanthropic contributions to Zionist causes, including the aforementioned support for Birthright Israel and the Koret Foundation.
    *   Source: Pisces Inc. - Family Office Overview (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
