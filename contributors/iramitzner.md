# California Contributor Report: Ira Mitzner

### **Identification and Professional Profile**
Ira Mitzner is the President and CEO of **RIDA Development Corporation**, a real estate investment and development firm based in Houston, Texas, with significant operations in California. Mitzner oversees a global portfolio valued at approximately $14 billion, including major hospitality and convention center projects.

*   **California Nexus**: Mitzner is the principal developer of the **Gaylord Pacific Resort & Convention Center** in Chula Vista, California, a $1.35 billion project described as the centerpiece of the Chula Vista Bayfront redevelopment.

### **Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

#### **1. Leadership in Zionist Organizations**
Mitzner holds multiple leadership roles in organizations dedicated to the support of the State of Israel and the preservation of Jewish history.
*   **Yeshiva University (YU)**: Mitzner serves as the **Chairman of the Board of Trustees** for Yeshiva University, the flagship Modern Orthodox Zionist institution in the United States. In this role, he oversees the university's mission of integrating Jewish values with academic excellence and its deep institutional connection to Israel.
    *   *Source*: Yeshiva University News (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **State of Israel Bonds**: Mitzner is a member of the **National Board of Directors** and the Executive Committee for the Development Corporation for Israel (State of Israel Bonds). He previously served as the co-chair of the Houston Division of Israel Bonds.
    *   *Source*: [My Jewish Learning (Biographical Profile)](https://www.myjewishlearning.com/author/ira-mitzner/)
*   **American Society for Yad Vashem**: He is a member of the **Executive Board** of the American Society for Yad Vashem, which supports the World Holocaust Remembrance Center in Jerusalem.
    *   *Source*: [Texas State Directory](https://www.txdirectory.com/online/person/?id=67451)
*   **JINSA (Jewish Institute for National Security of America)**: Mitzner has been identified as a member of the **Board of Directors** for JINSA, an organization that advocates for a robust U.S.-Israel security relationship and military cooperation.
    *   *Source*: JINSA Board Archives (Reference to Security Advocacy) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **2. Institutional Zionist Advocacy**
Mitzner has leveraged his philanthropic influence to establish programs specifically designed to train future advocates for Israel.
*   **Lieberman-Mitzner Center for Public Service and Advocacy**: In 2023, Mitzner and his family gifted $10 million to found this center at Yeshiva University. The center’s stated mission is to develop "the next generation of government leaders and advocates who are deeply rooted in Jewish values and tradition, and **deeply connected to Israel**," including "special programming for Israel advocacy."
    *   *Source*: Yeshiva University (Establishment of Lieberman-Mitzner Center) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source*: Queens Jewish Link (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **3. Government Advisory Roles**
*   **Texas Holocaust, Genocide, and Antisemitism Advisory Commission (THGAAC)**: In 2023, Mitzner was appointed by Governor Greg Abbott to this state commission, which advises on Holocaust education and efforts to combat antisemitism and anti-Zionism.

Mitzner is a prolific donor to political candidates and committees that prioritize conservative and pro-Israel foreign policy.

*   **California State Contributions**: Documentation shows Mitzner contributed **$30,000** to the **Republican Party of San Diego County** during the 2024 election cycle, coinciding with his major business interests in Chula Vista.
