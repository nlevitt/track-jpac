# Contributor Report: Fisher, Alyne B.

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

**Fisher, Alyne B.** (and the associated **Fisher family**) are prominent California-based political and philanthropic donors identified as the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family investment firm **Pisces Inc.** Documentation of their relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy centers on their multi-generational leadership and financial support for the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and various San Francisco-based Zionist organizations.

### 1. Leadership and Governance: Koret Foundation
The Fisher family maintains a direct role in the governance of the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a documented mission to support Israel and combat "anti-Zionism."
*   **Board Membership:** **William S. Fisher** (son of Gap founders Donald and Doris Fisher) serves on the **Board of Directors** of the Koret Foundation. Under this leadership, the foundation has shifted its focus heavily toward pro-Israel advocacy and Zionist education. *   **Strategic Initiatives:** The foundation, under Fisher family governance, funds the **Koret Israel Economic Development Fund (KIEDF)** and initiatives designed to "counter the delegitimization of Israel." Source: Koret Foundation Israel Support (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Financial Support for Zionist Organizations
The Fisher family, primarily through the **Fisher Family Fund** (managed by **Pisces Inc.**) and the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, are documented donors to organizations dedicated to the promotion of Jewish identity and connection to the State of Israel.
*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family is a long-standing supporter of **Birthright Israel**, which provides free trips to Israel for young adults to strengthen Zionist identity. The family’s contributions are often channeled through the **Jewish Community Federation**, where they are listed as major "Pillar" donors. Source: Jewish Community Federation Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Impact on Settlement and Security:** The Koret Foundation, directed by Fisher, has provided significant grants to organizations such as the **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)** and the **AIPAC-affiliated American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF)**. Source: Koret Foundation 990 Tax Filings via ProPublica (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Corporate and Institutional Ties
As the founding family of **Gap Inc.** and owners of **Pisces Inc.**, the Fishers have utilized their corporate-derived wealth to establish a massive philanthropic infrastructure in San Francisco that integrates Zionist advocacy into mainstream cultural institutions.
*   **Pisces Inc.:** The family office, Pisces Inc., manages the investment portfolio and charitable giving of the Fisher brothers (**Robert, William, and John Fisher**), ensuring a consistent flow of capital to pro-Israel causes. [Source: Pisces Foundation/Pisces Inc. Overview](https://piscesfoundation.org/who-we-are/)
*   **SFMOMA and Koret Education Center:** The Fisher family's art collection is a centerpiece of the **San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA)**, where the **Koret Education Center** serves as a symbol of the intersection between the Fisher/Koret families and public cultural education. Source: SFMOMA Fisher Collection (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 4. Political Contributions
The Fishers are among California’s most prolific political donors, frequently supporting candidates and committees that favor strong US-Israel military and diplomatic ties.
*   **Federal Contributions:** Individual members of the Fisher family, including Doris and William Fisher, have made hundreds of thousands of dollars in contributions to candidates supported by **AIPAC** and the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**. [Source: Federal Election Commission (FEC) Donor Search](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Fisher%2C+William+S&contributor_name=Fisher%2C+Doris)
