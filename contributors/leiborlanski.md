# Contributor Report: Orlanski, Leib

### Overview
Leib Orlanski is a prominent Los Angeles-based corporate attorney and political donor with a documented history of engagement in Zionist advocacy and pro-Israel policy initiatives. He is currently a partner at the international law firm **K&L Gates LLP**, where he specializes in mergers and acquisitions, venture capital, and securities law 1.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation), 1.2 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). While some secondary sources have associated him with the University of California (UC) Board of Regents, official records do not list him as a former Chair or member of the Board; however, he has held numerous other leadership roles, including Chair of InterCounsel and the Southern California Marine and Intermodal Transportation System Advisory Council 1.3 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### Documented Zionist Advocacy and Support for Israel
Orlanski’s involvement in Israel-related advocacy spans several decades, encompassing high-level policy engagement, organizational leadership, and financial contributions.

#### 1. Federal Policy Advocacy (AWACS Debate)
During the Reagan administration in 1981, Orlanski was a documented contact for the White House regarding the **AWACS (Airborne Warning and Control System)** sale to Saudi Arabia. A letter from Joseph W. Twinam, then-Deputy Assistant Secretary for Near Eastern Affairs, addressed to Orlanski, detailed the administration's commitment to "assure fully that Israel retains qualitative military superiority in the region" 2.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). This correspondence indicates Orlanski's role as a liaison or advocate for pro-Israel defense interests during a pivotal period in U.S.-Israel security relations.

#### 2. Leadership and Philanthropy: '30 Years After'
Orlanski is a documented supporter and donor to **30 Years After**, an Iranian-American Jewish organization based in Los Angeles 3.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). The organization’s mission is to promote the civic and political involvement of the Iranian-Jewish community, with a specific focus on advocacy related to the **State of Israel** and monitoring Iranian nuclear developments 3.2 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). Orlanski has been listed as a major attendee and supporter of the organization's Civic Action Conferences, which feature plenary sessions on Israel and Iran policy 3.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### 3. Professional Representation of Israeli Interests
In his legal practice, Orlanski has represented firms with significant ties to Israel. He served as counsel for **MRV Communications, Inc.**, a company that operated through subsidiaries such as **North Hills Israel** 4.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). His legal work involved facilitating international investments and corporate restructuring for entities deeply embedded in the Israeli technology sector.

#### 4. California Political and Community Contributions
Orlanski is a frequent contributor to local and state-level political causes in California, often alongside other prominent pro-Israel figures in the Beverly Hills area 5.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). He has been documented as a supporter of the **Beverly Hills Education Foundation** and various municipal ballot measures, appearing on donor lists that represent the intersection of Southern California's Jewish community leadership and political lobbying 5.2 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
