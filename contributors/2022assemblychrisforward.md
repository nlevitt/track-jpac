# Contributor Report: Chris Ward for Assembly 2022

Chris Ward is a Democratic member of the California State Assembly, representing the 78th District since 2020. During his 2022 re-election campaign, his political committee, **Chris Ward for Assembly 2022**, maintained documented relationships with pro-Israel advocacy organizations and legislative caucuses dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship.

### 1. Endorsement by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)
Chris Ward was endorsed by the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, an advocacy group and political action committee established to ensure pro-Israel sentiment remains a core pillar of the Democratic Party platform. 

*   **Relationship:** DMFI PAC, which supports candidates "committed to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship," included Ward in its slate of endorsed candidates during the 2022 election cycle.
*   **Documentation:** Ward is listed among California candidates supported by DMFI PAC, an organization that specifically targets candidates who reject the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement and support continued U.S. military aid to Israel.
*   **Source:** [Ballotpedia: Endorsements by Democratic Majority for Israel PAC](https://ballotpedia.org/Endorsements_by_Democratic_Majority_for_Israel_PAC)
*   **Source:** [Jewish Insider: DMFI PAC announces slate of endorsements](https://jewishinsider.com/2022/01/dmfi-pac-announces-first-slate-of-house-endorsements/)

### 2. Membership in the California Legislative Jewish Caucus
Ward is an active member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, a legislative body that serves as a primary vehicle for Zionist advocacy within the California State Legislature.

*   **Institutional Alignment:** The Caucus explicitly identifies its mission as providing a "Jewish voice" in the Capitol and has officially expressed "enduring solidarity with the State of Israel."
*   **Legislative Statements:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Ward joined other Caucus members in thanking Governor Gavin Newsom for illuminating the State Capitol in blue and white, which the Caucus described as a "beautiful symbol of California’s enduring solidarity with the State of Israel."
*   **Source:** [CounterPunch: Who Are They? The CA Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://www.counterpunch.org/2024/08/20/who-are-they-the-ca-legislative-jewish-caucus/)
*   **Source:** [Official Jewish Caucus Letter (January 3, 2024)](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/sites/jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/files/2024-01/Jewish%20Caucus%20Letter%20to%20Colleagues%20-%20Jan%203%202024.pdf)

### 3. Documentation of Zionist Advocacy and Criticism
Ward's legislative record and public affiliations have been characterized by critics as prioritizing Zionist narratives and institutionalizing support for the Israeli state within California's educational and legal frameworks.

*   **"Progressive Except for Palestine" (PEP):** Critics have identified Ward as one of several "progressive except for Palestine" lawmakers who support social justice legislation domestically while concurrently working to "erase Palestinians from the political and educational discourse."
*   **School Curriculum Advocacy:** Ward has supported and co-authored legislation (such as AB 715) and caucus initiatives aimed at curbing what the Jewish Caucus describes as "anti-Israel indoctrination" in California schools, often by conflating criticism of Israel with antisemitism.
*   **Source:** [LA Progressive: The CA Legislative Jewish Caucus and Pro-Israel Advocacy](https://www.laprogressive.com/middle-east/the-ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)
*   **Source:** [California State Website: Governor Newsom signs bills cracking down on hate and antisemitism](https://www.gov.ca.gov/2024/10/07/governor-newsom-signs-bills-further-cracking-down-on-hate-and-antisemitism-in-california-schools/)
*   **Source:** [Voice of San Diego: UCSD Protests and Lawmaker Responses](https://voiceofsandiego.org/2024/05/10/sacramento-report-ucsd-protests-test-limits-of-campus-free-speech/)

### 4. Financial and Corporate Support
Ward has received financial contributions from corporate entities that engage in significant political lobbying and advocacy alongside pro-Israel organizations.

*   **Political Engagement:** In 2022, Ward received contributions from Verizon's "Political Engagement" fund, a corporate PAC that supports a wide array of pro-establishment candidates.
*   **Source:** [Verizon 2022 Political Engagement Statement (Annex A, page 7)](https://www.verizon.com/about/sites/default/files/Political-Engagement-Policy-and-Statement-2022.pdf)
