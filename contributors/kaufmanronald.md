# Contributor Report: Kaufman, Ronald

### Overview
Ronald "Ron" Kaufman is a prominent Republican strategist, lobbyist, and political operative who serves as a national committeeman for the Republican Party of Massachusetts and a senior advisor at the law firm Dentons. He has a documented history of leadership within pro-Israel advocacy circles, most notably as a long-standing board member of the Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC). His activities focus on aligning the Republican Party’s platform with Zionist priorities and maintaining U.S. institutional support for the State of Israel.

### Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC) Leadership
Kaufman is identified as a long-standing board member of the Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC), an organization dedicated to fostering ties between the American Jewish community and the Republican Party through pro-Israel advocacy.
*   **Organizational Ties:** Kaufman is described as a "close friend" and long-term associate of the RJC's executive director, Matt Brooks, frequently hosting and attending exclusive RJC events at Republican National Conventions. [Observer](https://observer.com/2016/07/the-best-party-in-cleveland-is-a-smoky-hideout-in-the-parking-garage/)
*   **Political Strategy:** Kaufman has been instrumental in the RJC's efforts to frame the Republican Party as the primary defender of Israel in U.S. politics. He has participated in campaigns to highlight what the organization describes as the "hemorrhaging support" for Israel within the Democratic Party. [Jewish News/Issuu](https://issuu.com/ujft/docs/jn_march_3_2025_web/s/54958102)

### Pro-Israel Advocacy and Institutional Support
Beyond his formal role with the RJC, Kaufman actively engages in legislative and institutional advocacy to secure funding and political backing for Israel.
*   **Legislative Lobbying:** In 2025, Kaufman led an advocacy team during Virginia Jewish Advocacy Day, meeting with state legislators to lobby for specific pro-Israel interests, including the maintenance of funding for the Virginia Israel Advisory Board. [Jewish News Virginia](https://www.jewishnewsva.org/advocates-receive-warm-welcome-on-virginia-jewish-advocacy-day-2025/)
*   **Political Framing:** Kaufman has publicly argued that Jewish security is inextricably linked to the survival of democratic institutions and the strength of the U.S.-Israel relationship. He has stated that "anything that threatens democracy makes Jews particularly nervous," using historical Jewish persecution as a rationale for robust Republican political engagement. [Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/349479/january-6-2021-awakening-to-a-nightmare/)
*   **Institutional Alignment:** Throughout his career as a White House political director and advisor to presidents George H.W. Bush and Mitt Romney, Kaufman served as a bridge between the GOP establishment and Zionist leadership, including figures like RJC founder Gordon Zacks and Max Fisher. [Miller Center](https://millercenter.org/the-presidency/presidential-oral-histories/ronald-kaufman-oral-history) | [Wayne State University Archive](https://reuther.wayne.edu/files/LP001150_Fisher.pdf)

### Professional and Political Influence
Kaufman utilizes his professional platform as a senior advisor at Dentons to influence policy that affects Zionist interests and U.S. foreign policy toward the Middle East.
*   **Lobbying Background:** Kaufman has a history of representing domestic and foreign interests through various government relations firms, including Dutko Government Relations and McKenna Long & Aldridge, often alongside other prominent pro-Israel lobbyists. [LegiStorm](https://www.legistorm.com/person/bio/155700/Ron_Kaufman.html)
*   **RNC Leadership:** As a high-ranking member of the Republican National Committee (RNC), Kaufman has influenced the party’s debate policies and platform development, ensuring that pro-Israel stances remain a central component of the GOP's national identity. [Ballotpedia](https://ballotpedia.org/Ron_Kaufman)
