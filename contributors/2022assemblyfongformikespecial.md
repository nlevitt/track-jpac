# Contributor Report: Mike Fong for Assembly 2022 Special

This report documents the political relationship between the campaign committee **Mike Fong for Assembly 2022 Special** and Zionist advocacy organizations, specifically focusing on endorsements, legislative co-authorship, and institutional support regarding the State of Israel.

### 1. Endorsement by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)
The committee "Mike Fong for Assembly 2022 Special" was identified as a recipient of backing from the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)** during the 2022 election cycle. DMFI is a political organization that advocates for Democratic candidates who support a strong U.S.-Israel relationship. 

*   **Evidence:** Documentation indicates that Fong’s 2022 campaign was supported by both AIPAC and Democratic Majority for Israel.
*   **Source:** [California DSA 2024 Voter Guide (Reference to 2022 Backing)](https://californiadsa.org/2024-voter-guide/) (Note: Mentioned in the context of his 2022 campaign history).

### 2. Legislative Advocacy: House Resolution 99 (2022)
Following his victory in the 2022 special election, Mike Fong became a co-author of **House Resolution 99 (HR 99)**. This resolution was introduced to officially recognize and honor the contributions of Israeli Americans to the State of California and includes explicit recognition of the State of Israel.

*   **Documentation:** Fong is listed as a co-author of HR 99, which was reintroduced during the 2022 session by Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel.
*   **Source:** [Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN) - California Officially Honors Israeli-Americans](https://israelusa.org/california-officially-honors-israeli-americans/)

### 3. Institutional Support and Ratings
Mike Fong has maintained relationships with regional Zionist advocacy groups, specifically **Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA)**. These organizations vet candidates through questionnaires that require stated support for the State of Israel and a commitment to combating "anti-Israel" curriculum in schools.

*   **Support Rating:** Fong is listed among the candidates supported by DFI-LA, an organization that evaluates candidates based on their "support for the State of Israel" and "demonstrated record" on related issues.
*   **Source:** [Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (Endorsement List)](https://www.dfi-la.org/elections)
*   **Source:** [Josh Lowenthal Supporters List (Citing Fong and DFI-LA)](https://www.joshlowenthal.com/supporters)

### 4. Relationship with Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN)
Fong’s legislative actions have been formally acknowledged by the **Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN)**. ICAN identifies itself as a grassroots organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship through local and state-level advocacy.

*   **Coordination:** ICAN worked directly with the co-authors of HR 99, including Fong, to pass the resolution honoring the Israeli community and its ties to the homeland of Israel.
*   **Source:** [ICAN Legislative Report on HR 99](https://israelusa.org/california-officially-honors-israeli-americans/)

### 5. Summary of 2022 Special Election Context
The committee "Mike Fong for Assembly 2022 Special" was formed to support Fong's candidacy for the 49th Assembly District in a special election held on February 15, 2022. He was sworn in on February 22, 2022, and immediately engaged in the aforementioned pro-Israel legislative activities.

*   **Campaign Finance & Registration:** Fong's 2022 special election committee is a registered entity with the California Secretary of State.
*   **Source:** [Transparency USA - Mike Fong Candidate Profile](https://www.transparencyusa.org/ca/candidate/mike-fong)
