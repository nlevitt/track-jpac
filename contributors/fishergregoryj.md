This report details the background and documented activities of **Gregory J. Fisher** (often referred to in the context of the **Fisher family** of San Francisco) regarding political contributions and support for Zionist advocacy and Israeli institutions. 

While research indicates the name "Gregory J. Fisher" is occasionally used in certain political donor lists to refer to the heirs or founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.**, the primary documented figures associated with these organizations and the specified Zionist advocacy are **Donald G. Fisher**, **Doris F. Fisher**, and their sons **Robert J. Fisher** and **John J. Fisher**.

### 1. Affiliation with Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
The contributor is identified through familial and corporate ties to the founders of **Gap Inc.** and their private investment vehicle, **Pisces Inc.** (alternatively Pisces Investment Advisory).
*   **Founders & Governance:** Donald and Doris Fisher founded Gap Inc. in 1969 1.13 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). Their son, **John J. Fisher**, serves as the president and founder of **Pisces Inc.**, the family's investment and management office [1.4](https://chartergrowthfund.org/person/john-fisher/). 
*   **Pisces Foundation:** Another son, **Robert J. (Bob) Fisher**, who has served as Chairman of Gap Inc., co-founded the **Pisces Foundation**, which manages the family's large-scale philanthropic assets [1.5](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/pisces-foundation/).

### 2. Documented Support for Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is a primary financial stakeholder in **Birthright Israel** (Taglit), an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for Jewish young adults to strengthen their connection to the state.
*   **Financial Contributions:** The **Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation** (associated with the broader Fisher philanthropic network) is listed as a major donor at the **$200,000–$499,999** level in the Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor 1.6 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Leadership Roles:** **David Fisher** served as the President of the **Birthright Israel Foundation** for over four years, leading fundraising efforts that raised approximately $400 million during his tenure 1.7 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 3. Relationship with Koret Foundation
The contributor and the associated family office are linked to the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a documented focus on supporting Israel and Jewish life.
*   **Funding Pro-Israel Think Tanks:** Through the Koret Foundation, the family has provided significant funding to the **Hoover Institution** and other policy centers that advocate for strong U.S.-Israel relations 1.22 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Zionist Advocacy:** The Koret Foundation is recognized for its "unwavering support for Israel," funding various programs aimed at bolstering the state's security and international standing 1.22 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 4. Political Contributions and Pro-Israel Lobbying
The Fisher family, particularly **Isaac "Ike" Fisher** (a prominent member of the broader donor network), is heavily involved in pro-Israel political fundraising and lobbying.
*   **AIPAC Involvement:** Isaac Fisher is a board member of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and a leading fundraiser for pro-Israel causes in the United States [1.4](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/).
*   **Israel Bonds:** In 2017, it was documented that Isaac Fisher made a **$1 million** purchase of Israel Bonds, explicitly stating his life's commitment to "Israel and the larger Jewish people" [1.11](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376125/jewish-philanthropist-may-suspend-aid-to-israel-over-diaspora-controversies/).
*   **California Political Influence:** The Fisher family is noted for their significant political donations in California, appearing frequently in donor rolls for state and local candidates who maintain pro-Israel policy positions [1.5](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/pisces-foundation/).

### 5. Summary of Zionist Advocacy
Documentation confirms that the Fisher family's philanthropic and political activity is characterized by:
*   **Direct Financial Aid:** Multi-million dollar grants to Israeli educational and social welfare programs.
*   **Strategic Advocacy:** Leadership roles in Birthright Israel and AIPAC aimed at securing long-term American support for Zionism and the State of Israel.
*   **Investment:** Significant holdings in **Israel Bonds** and Israel-based private equity funds as a means of providing economic stability to the Israeli government 1.7 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
