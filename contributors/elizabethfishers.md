# Contributor Report: Elizabeth S. Fisher

### Overview and Professional Identity
Elizabeth S. Fisher is a prominent San Francisco-based political donor and philanthropist. She is the spouse of **Robert J. Fisher**, the former chairman of **Gap Inc.** and son of company founders Donald and Doris Fisher. Elizabeth Fisher currently serves as a Managing Director of **Pisces Inc.**, the Fisher family's investment and management firm, and is a co-founder of the **Pisces Foundation**.

### Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy
Fisher’s support for Israel and Zionist causes is primarily established through her leadership and financial contributions to several key organizations known for their pro-Israel advocacy.

#### 1. Association with the Koret Foundation
Elizabeth Fisher is frequently documented as a "lead donor" and institutional partner alongside the **Koret Foundation** in major San Francisco-based projects. The Koret Foundation is a significant funder of Zionist organizations, including **AIPAC** (the American Israel Public Affairs Committee) and the **Jewish Community Federation**.

#### 2. Support for Birthright Israel
Fisher is identified as a donor associated with **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults, a program often characterized by critics as a tool for Zionist nationalist indoctrination.
