This contributor report examines the documentation of Susan Rose's philanthropic and political support for the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy. Susan Rose is a prominent New York-based philanthropist, real estate executive, and a former president of **Hadassah, The Women’s Zionist Organization of America**. Her relationship with Israel is characterized by substantial financial contributions to pro-Israel political action committees and leadership roles in major Zionist and Jewish advocacy organizations.

# Contributor Report: Rose, Susan

### 1. Leadership in Zionist Advocacy Organizations
Susan Rose has held high-level leadership positions in the most influential Zionist and Jewish advocacy organizations in the United States.

*   **President of Hadassah**: Susan Rose served as the National President of **Hadassah, The Women’s Zionist Organization of America**, which is the largest Jewish women’s organization in the U.S. and maintains a explicitly Zionist mission of supporting healthcare and education in Israel while enhancing Jewish life in America.
    *   *Source:* [Hadassah IRS Form 990 (2013) via ProPublica](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/display_990/136227614/2014_11_EO%2F13-6227614_990_201312)
*   **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**: Rose is a long-standing member of the **Board of Directors** and the **Executive Committee** of the AJC, a global advocacy organization that counters antisemitism and lobbies for Israel’s security and diplomatic standing.
    *   *Source:* [AJC 2022 Slate of Officers and Board of Directors](https://www.ajc.org/news/2022-slate-of-officers-executive-committee-and-board-of-directors)
    *   *Source:* [AJC 2020 Slate of Officers and Board of Directors](https://www.ajc.org/news/2020-slate-of-officers-executive-committee-and-board-of-directors)

### 2. Major Financial Contributions to Pro-Israel PACs
Rose is documented as a significant donor to political entities that promote unconditional U.S. support for Israel and oppose candidates critical of Israeli policies.

*   **AIPAC and United Democracy Project**: Documentation identifies Rose as a major contributor to **United Democracy Project (UDP)**, the Super PAC launched by the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC). UDP engages in multi-million dollar spending campaigns to defeat political candidates who take critical stances on Israel.
    *   *Source:* [AIPAC Corporate Power Brokers Report (In These Times)](https://inthesetimes.com/article/aipac-united-democracy-project-squad-primary-donors)
*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**: Rose is a recurring donor to **Democratic Majority for Israel**, an advocacy group and PAC that works to maintain pro-Israel sentiment within the Democratic Party and supports candidates aligned with Zionist interests.
    *   *Source:* [DMFI Donor Analysis (Jewish Insider)](https://jewishinsider.com/2023/03/daily-kickoff-chicagos-jewish-vote-boy-meets-congress/)

### 3. Philanthropic Support for Israeli Institutions
Beyond political advocacy, Rose’s philanthropy directly funds cultural and academic institutions that strengthen the relationship between the U.S. and Israel.

*   **America-Israel Cultural Foundation (AICF)**: Rose is a documented sponsor of the AICF, which provides grants and scholarships to support artistic life in Israel and promotes Israeli culture internationally.
    *   *Source:* [AICF Artist Sponsorship Records](https://aicf.org/artist/gil-shohat/)
*   **Ben-Gurion University (BGU)**: Rose is recognized as a donor to **Ben-Gurion University of the Negev** through its Canadian and American "Friends of" branches, supporting large-scale infrastructure and psychological rehabilitation projects in Israel.
    *   *Source:* [BGU Canada "The Way Forward" Gala Donor List](https://bengurion.ca/the-way-forward-gala-toronto/)
*   **Hebrew University of Jerusalem**: She is a contributor to the **Canadian Friends of Hebrew University**, funding academic research and "innovation" programs within the Israeli university system.
    *   *Source:* [Canadian Friends of Hebrew University 2016 Annual Report](https://cfhu.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/2016-annual-report.pdf)

### 4. Direct Zionist Advocacy Statements
As a leader of Hadassah, Rose has been a vocal proponent of Zionism as a central component of Jewish identity and a necessity for Jewish survival.

*   **Zionist Identity Advocacy**: In her capacity as a Zionist leader, Rose has advocated for the integration of Zionist education in American Jewish life, framing support for the State of Israel as a fundamental civic and religious duty.
    *   *Source:* [Zionist Identity and Hadassah Programming (Hadassah.org)](https://www.hadassah.org/inspire-zionism)
