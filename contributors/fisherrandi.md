# Contributor Report: Fisher, Randi

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

Randi Fisher is a prominent San Francisco-based philanthropist and a member of the Fisher family, the billionaire heirs to the **Gap Inc.** fortune. Alongside her husband, Robert (Bob) J. Fisher, she co-founded the **Pisces Foundation**, an environmental grantmaking organization, and is a principal of **Pisces Inc.**, the family's investment office. Fisher is documented as a significant contributor to a network of institutions that support the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy, often in partnership with the **Koret Foundation**.

### Documentation of Zionist Advocacy and Israel Support

Fisher’s relationship to Zionist causes is characterized by high-level financial support for Jewish communal organizations that prioritize the strengthening of ties between the United States and Israel.

*   **Association with the Koret Foundation:** Fisher and the Pisces Foundation are frequent co-funders of major civic and Jewish projects alongside the [Koret Foundation](https://koret.org/), one of the largest pro-Israel philanthropic organizations in the U.S. These collaborations include funding for the Golden Gate National Parks Conservancy (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) and the [San Francisco Jewish Community Federation](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/). The Koret Foundation is explicitly dedicated to "strengthening the State of Israel" and has provided extensive support to Israeli security, education, and settlement-related initiatives.
*   **Jewish Community Federation Funding:** The Fisher family, including Randi, are long-standing supporters of the [Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund of San Francisco](https://jewishfed.org/). This federation serves as a primary conduit for funding to Israel, including support for **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young adults to foster Zionist identity. While the president of the Birthright Israel Foundation, David Fisher, is from a different branch of the Fisher lineage (descendants of Max Fisher), the Gap Fisher family's contributions to the SF Federation [directly support Birthright's local and national operations](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   **Support for the US Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM):** Randi and Mark Fisher are documented as donors to the US Holocaust Memorial Museum (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). Within the framework of Zionist advocacy, the USHMM is frequently cited by critics as an institution that aligns Holocaust memory with the historical and political legitimacy of the State of Israel.

### Inclusion in the Mapping Project

Fisher and her family office, **Pisces Inc.**, have been specifically targeted by activist documentation for their role in the "Zionist donor" network.

*   **The Mapping Project:** The Mapping Project (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation), an activist database designed to track entities it claims support the "colonization of Palestine," explicitly lists **Pisces Inc.** and the Fisher family. The project identifies the firm as a vehicle for "Zionist influence" due to its financial ties to the Koret Foundation and other pro-Israel lobbying and cultural groups. It categorizes the Fishers as part of a "network of wealth and power" that sustains Israeli state policy through domestic philanthropic and political channels.
*   **Political Lobbying Ties:** Through Pisces Inc. and personal contributions, the Fisher family has a documented history of supporting political figures and groups that maintain a staunch pro-Israel platform. Financial records show members of the Fisher family contributing to the [American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Fisher%2C+Robert&contributor_employer=Pisces+Inc), the primary pro-Israel lobbying organization in the United States.
