# Contributor Report: Lowy, Ruth

### Institutional Leadership and Zionist Advocacy
Ruth Lowy has served in high-level leadership roles within major Zionist organizations in the United States. She is identified as a **Past Chair of the Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles**, an organization that defines "Support for Israel" as one of its primary missions. 
*   **Mission and Policy:** The Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles explicitly states its core mission includes supporting the State of Israel and the Jewish people globally, actively funding programs within Israel to strengthen its national infrastructure and security. [Source: Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles - Mission](https://www.jewishla.org/about-us/)
*   **Programmatic Support:** During her tenure and involvement, the Federation has facilitated extensive funding for programs such as **Taglit-Birthright Israel**, which sponsors ideological trips to Israel, and the **Jewish Agency for Israel**, which focuses on immigration (Aliyah) and Zionist education. [Source: JFEDLA - Support for Israel](https://www.jewishla.org/our-work/israel-and-overseas/)

### Documented Financial Support for Zionist Organizations
Lowy is recorded as a consistent donor to organizations that advocate for Israeli interests and Zionist policy in the United States.
*   **Jewish Federation Contributions:** Ruth Lowy is a high-level donor to the Jewish Federation’s annual campaign, which directly allocates millions of dollars to projects in Israel. She has been recognized at the **Lion of Judah** level, a designation for women who contribute $5,000 or more annually to the Federation's campaign to support its Israel-focused and communal programs. [Source: Winkler Lowy Foundation - JFEDLA Support](https://www.24-7pressrelease.com/press-release/511116/winkler-lowy-foundation-led-by-janine-lowy-donates-to-jewish-federation-of-greater-los-angeles)
*   **Political Advocacy:** Lowy’s philanthropic activities align with the broader **Lowy family** legacy of Zionist commitment. The family has been characterized as "proudly Zionist," with extensive ties to the **Institute for National Security Studies (INSS)** in Tel Aviv and the **Lowy Institute**, both of which play roles in shaping Israeli and regional security discourse. [Source: The Klaxon - Lowy Family Zionist Advocacy](https://theklaxon.com.au/nation-suffering-from-slide-in-values-says-billionaire-who-made-550000-in-secretive-payments-to-liberal-party/)

### Support for Israel-Centric Communal Programs
Lowy’s support extends to educational and communal institutions that promote a Zionist worldview and identification with the Israeli state.
*   **Camp Ramah:** Lowy has provided financial support to **Camp Ramah**, a network of Jewish camps that integrates Zionist ideology and Hebrew language immersion into its core curriculum to foster long-term identification with Israel among youth. [Source: Jewish Journal - Camp Ramah Philanthropy](https://jewishjournal.com/community/365538/ramah-lights-up-sinai-temple-jnf-usa-global-conference/)
*   **Holocaust Education and Israel:** As a donor to the **United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM)**, Lowy supports programs that often link the memory of the Holocaust to the necessity of the State of Israel, a central tenet of modern Zionist advocacy. [Source: USHMM - Donor List 2008-09](https://www.ushmm.org/m/pdfs/20090812-annual-report-2008-2009.pdf)
