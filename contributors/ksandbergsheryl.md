# Contributor Report: Sandberg, Sheryl K

Sheryl Sandberg, former Chief Operating Officer of Meta Platforms and founder of Lean In, has a documented history of Zionist advocacy and financial support for Israeli organizations. Her relationship to Israel includes public declarations of identity, high-level diplomatic engagements, and multi-million dollar contributions to pro-Israel and Israeli-based entities.

### 1. Pro-Zionist Public Declarations
Sandberg has publicly identified as a Zionist, particularly following the events of October 7, 2023. In November 2024, she stated at the Jewish Federations of North America’s General Assembly, "I sit on this stage as a proud Zionist and a proud Jew, in a way I wouldn’t have a year and a half ago."
*   **Source:** [Jewish Federations of North America](https://www.jewishfederations.org/fed-hub/sandberg-at-general-assembly-i-am-a-proud-zionist)

She has also advocated for the inclusion of Zionism in educational curricula, emphasizing a need for "factual representation of Jewish history and Zionism" in schools.
*   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post](https://www.jpost.com/diaspora/antisemitism/article-828859)

### 2. Financial Contributions to Israeli and Zionist Organizations
Sandberg has directed significant philanthropic funds to organizations that provide medical, humanitarian, and advocacy services in or for Israel:
*   **United Hatzalah:** In 2021, Sandberg donated $5 million to the Jerusalem-based volunteer emergency medical service United Hatzalah. The gift established the "Adele and Dr. Joel Sandberg Women’s Unit" in Israel, named after her parents.
    *   **Source:** [The Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/sheryl-sandberg-donates-5-million-to-united-hatzalahs-womens-unit/)
*   **IsraAID:** In 2020, Sandberg and her fiancé pledged $2.5 million to IsraAID, an Israeli-based humanitarian aid organization that operates globally and within Israel.
    *   **Source:** [IsraAID](https://www.israaid.org/news/facebook-coo-sheryl-sandberg-and-fiance-donate-2-5-million-to-israaid/)
*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** In 2019, Sandberg donated $2.5 million to the ADL to support anti-hate and anti-bias programs. The ADL is documented as a pro-Israel advocacy group that identifies anti-Zionism as a form of antisemitism.
    *   **Source:** [JNS.org](https://www.jns.org/facebooks-sheryl-sandberg-pledges-personal-donation-of-2-5-million-to-adl/)

### 3. Diplomatic Engagements and Missions to Israel
Sandberg’s relationship with the Israeli state is evidenced by several high-level meetings and participation in organized missions:
*   **ADL Women’s Leadership Mission:** Sandberg participated in an ADL Women’s Leadership Mission to Israel, which included meetings with high-ranking Israeli officials. 
*   **Meeting with President Reuven Rivlin:** During a 2019 visit to Israel, she met with then-President Reuven Rivlin. During this trip, she also visited Yad Vashem and United Hatzalah facilities.
    *   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post](https://www.jpost.com/diaspora/facebook-chief-donates-25-mil-to-adl-to-fight-hate-after-halle-shooting-605063)
*   **United Nations Advocacy:** In December 2023, Sandberg headlined an event at the United Nations co-sponsored by the Israeli Mission to the UN, appearing alongside Israeli Ambassador Gilad Erdan to advocate for global recognition of sexual violence committed by Hamas on October 7.
    *   **Source:** [JNS.org](https://www.jns.org/sheryl-sandberg-un-silence-unacceptable-for-israeli-women-all-women/)

### 4. Media and Advocacy (Post-October 7)
Since late 2023, Sandberg’s advocacy has focused on documenting Israeli victims of the October 7 attacks. 
*   **"Screams Before Silence":** Sandberg produced and fronted the 2024 documentary *Screams Before Silence*, which focuses on sexual violence against Israeli women. She has described the film as "the most important work of her life" and has screened it globally to advocate for the Israeli perspective on the conflict.
    *   **Source:** [Calcalistech](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctechnology/article/sj00p9v9jr)

### 5. Family Legacy and Institutional Ties
Sandberg’s Zionist involvement is rooted in a family history of activism. Her parents were involved in the movement for Soviet Jewry, and her great-aunt, Frieda Sandberg Lewis, served as the national president of Hadassah, the Women’s Zionist Organization of America, in the 1980s.
*   **Source:** [Hadassah Magazine](https://www.hadassahmagazine.org/2023/12/17/sheryl-sandbergs-inherited-jewish-activism/)
