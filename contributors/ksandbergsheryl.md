# Contributor Report: Sandberg, Sheryl K

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

### Overview
Sheryl K. Sandberg, the former Chief Operating Officer of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook), is a prominent billionaire philanthropist and political donor based in California. Following her departure from Meta, Sandberg has increasingly focused her public profile and financial resources on advocacy related to Israel and the documentation of conflict-related sexual violence attributed to Hamas. Her activities involve significant financial contributions to Zionist organizations and direct engagement with Israeli state officials.

### Advocacy and Media Projects
Sandberg has utilized her platform to amplify Israeli narratives concerning the October 7, 2023, attacks. 

*   **"Screams Before Silence" Documentary:** Sandberg produced a documentary titled *Screams Before Silence*, which focuses on allegations of sexual violence committed by Hamas during the October 7 attacks. The film has been used as a primary tool in international advocacy efforts to support Israel's narrative of the conflict.
    *   **Source:** [Screams Before Silence - Official Website](https://www.screamsbeforesilence.com/)
*   **United Nations Advocacy:** In December 2023, Sandberg spoke at a special session at the United Nations, hosted by the Israeli Mission, to criticize the international community's response to reports of sexual violence during the attacks.

### Financial Contributions to Israeli and Zionist Organizations
Sandberg’s philanthropic activities include multi-million dollar donations to organizations that provide direct support to the Israeli state and Zionist initiatives.

*   **United Hatzalah:** In October 2023, Sandberg and her husband, Tom Bernthal, donated $5 million to United Hatzalah, an Israeli emergency medical services organization. This funding was specifically directed toward purchasing medical equipment and vehicles for use within Israel following the outbreak of hostilities.
    *   **Source:** Times of Israel: Sheryl Sandberg donates $5 million to United Hatzalah (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** Sandberg has been a major donor to the ADL, an organization that conflates anti-Zionism with antisemitism and advocates for pro-Israel policies. In 2019, she donated $2.5 million to the ADL to support their initiatives against "anti-Zionist" sentiment and hate speech.
    *   **Source:** ADL Press Release: Sheryl Sandberg Donates $2.5 Million to ADL (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **UJA-Federation of New York:** Sandberg has a documented history of contributing to the UJA-Federation of New York, which allocates significant funds to infrastructure, social services, and security programs in Israel.
    *   **Source:** [UJA-Federation of New York: Annual Report/Donors](https://www.ujafedny.org/news/sheryl-sandberg-joins-uja-to-discuss-resilience)
*   **Birthright Israel:** Sandberg has provided financial support to Taglit-Birthright Israel, an organization that funds free heritage trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening Zionist identity and ties to the state.

### Direct Engagement with Israeli Leadership
Sandberg maintains direct channels with the Israeli political establishment, facilitating high-level coordination on advocacy.

*   **Meeting with Israeli President:** In May 2024, Sandberg met with Israeli President Isaac Herzog to discuss her documentary and strategic advocacy for Israel on the global stage. Herzog praised her work as a critical contribution to Israel's "public diplomacy" (hasbara) efforts.
    *   **Source:** The Times of Israel: Herzog meets Sandberg to discuss advocacy (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Collaboration with the Israeli Government:** Sandberg’s documentary project was conducted with the cooperation of the Israeli police and government agencies, which provided access to evidence and testimonies.
