# Contributor Report: Bloomberg, Michael

Michael Bloomberg is a major financial donor and political advocate for Israeli institutions and Zionist initiatives. His involvement includes multi-million dollar contributions to medical, educational, and municipal infrastructure in Israel, as well as public opposition to the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Board Roles
Bloomberg has held formal and informal leadership positions within organizations focused on strengthening ties between the United States and Israel.
*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Bloomberg is a documented member of the **Honorary Board** of the Birthright Israel Foundation. He has been recognized as a "Guardian of the Jewish Future" by the organization and has attended high-level galas to facilitate its fundraising efforts.
    *   [Billionaires in World Politics (Oxford University Press excerpt via DOKUMEN.PUB)](https://dokumen.pub/billionaires-in-world-politics-9780198852711.html)
*   **Bloomberg-Sagol Center for City Leadership:** In 2022, Bloomberg established this center at **Tel Aviv University** in partnership with Israeli philanthropist Yossi Sagol. The program trains Israeli municipal leaders to improve city governance across the country.
    *   [Ynet News: Bloomberg donates $27.8 million to reconstruction in northern Israel](https://www.ynetnews.com/business/article/rk8f2aif0)
*   **Proxy Representation:** Martin Geller, who serves as a Senior Advisor to Bloomberg and CFO of the Bloomberg Family Foundation, holds a seat on the board of the **Birthright Israel Foundation** and the **American Israel Education Foundation** (the charitable arm of AIPAC).
    *   [Geller & Company: Martin J. Geller Profile](https://www.gellerco.com/team/martin-j-geller/)

### 2. Financial Contributions to Israel
Bloomberg’s philanthropy includes large-scale matching grants and the funding of infrastructure named after his family members.
*   **Magen David Adom (MDA):** Following the October 7, 2023 attacks, Bloomberg matched $44 million in donations to the American Friends of Magen David Adom (Israel’s national emergency service), resulting in an $88 million total gift. He previously funded the **William H. Bloomberg MDA Station** in Jerusalem.
    *   [Forbes: Billionaire Tracker: Mike Bloomberg and Other Ultra-Wealthy Donors to Israeli Groups](https://www.forbes.com/sites/kerryadolan/2023/10/11/billionaire-tracker-mike-bloomberg-yuri-milner-and-the-other-ultra-wealthy-donors-to-israeli-groups-following-the-attacks-by-hamas/)
    *   [American Friends of Magen David Adom: $88 Million Gift Led by Michael Bloomberg](https://afmda.org/israels-emergency-medical-service-to-receive-88-million-in-gift-led-by-michael-bloomberg/)
*   **Hadassah University Medical Center:** Bloomberg funded a mother-and-child health center at the Hadassah hospital in Jerusalem, named after his mother, Charlotte R. Bloomberg.
    *   [Philanthropy Roundtable: Important Contributions to the Health of the Jewish State](https://www.philanthropyroundtable.org/magazine/article/founding-funders/)
*   **Cornell-Technion (Roosevelt Island):** While Mayor of New York, Bloomberg championed a $2 billion technology campus on Roosevelt Island as a joint venture between Cornell University and the **Technion–Israel Institute of Technology**. He personally donated $100 million to the project.
    *   [Mondoweiss: Michael Bloomberg’s record on Israel](https://mondoweiss.net/2016/01/michael-bloomberg-has-been-a-fervent-supporter-of-israel-to-a-point/)
*   **Reconstruction Projects:** In 2024, Bloomberg pledged $28 million to the **Bloomberg Philanthropies Regional Initiative** to rehabilitate Israeli communities in the north and south that were impacted by conflict.
    *   [Lifestyles Magazine: $28 Million New Gift for Reconstruction in Northern Israel](https://lifestylesmagazine.com/28-million-new-gift-for-reconstruction-in-northern-israel-from-michael-bloomberg/)

### 3. Political Advocacy and Public Statements
Bloomberg has used his political platform as Mayor and a presidential candidate to advocate for Israeli security and diplomatic interests.
*   **Opposition to BDS:** Bloomberg has described the BDS movement as "an outrage" and "totally misplaced," arguing that it targets the "only democracy in the Middle East."
    *   [Jewish Virtual Library: Michael Bloomberg Biography and Views on Israel](https://www.jewishvirtuallibrary.org/michael-bloomberg)
*   **Unconditional Military Aid:** During his 2020 presidential campaign, Bloomberg pledged that he would never impose conditions on U.S. military aid to Israel, regardless of the policies of the sitting Israeli government.
    *   [Times of Israel: Bloomberg to AIPAC: I’ll never condition aid to Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/bloomberg-to-aipac-ill-never-condition-aid-to-israel-no-matter-whos-pm/)
*   **Solidarity Missions:** In July 2014, Bloomberg defied a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) ban on flights to Israel by flying into Ben-Gurion Airport on El Al during the Gaza conflict to demonstrate that the airport was safe and to protest the ban.
    *   [The Forward: 4 Jewish Things To Know About Michael Bloomberg](https://forward.com/news/434444/michael-bloomberg-jewish-identity-israel-philanthropy/)
*   **Genesis Prize:** In 2014, Bloomberg was the first recipient of the **Genesis Prize** (often called the "Jewish Nobel"), awarded in Jerusalem by the Israeli Prime Minister for commitment to Jewish values and the State of Israel.
    *   [eJewishPhilanthropy: Michael Bloomberg Receives Genesis Prize](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/michael-bloomberg-receives-genesis-prize-honoring-life-achievements-in-public-service-philanthropy-and-entrepreneurship/)
