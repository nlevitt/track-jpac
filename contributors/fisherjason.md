### 1. Overview of Identity and Corporate Affiliation
The subject is identified as a member of the Fisher family, the founders of the San Francisco-based retail giant **Gap Inc.** and the family investment firm **Pisces Inc.** (also known as Pisces Group). The family is among the wealthiest in California and is heavily involved in political and philanthropic sectors.

*   **Gap Inc. / Pisces Inc.:** Donald and Doris Fisher founded Gap Inc. in 1969. Their three sons—Robert, William, and John—have held various leadership roles. **John J. ### 2. Documented Support for Zionist Organizations
The Fisher family’s relationship to Israel is evidenced by multi-generational involvement and multi-million dollar contributions to organizations that advocate for and provide direct support to the State of Israel.

#### Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has long-standing ties to the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a mission that includes "strengthening Israel."
*   **Board Involvement:** Donald Fisher served as a director of the Koret Foundation. Source: Philanthropy Hall of Fame (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Zionist Advocacy:** The Koret Foundation is a significant funder of pro-Israel media and advocacy groups, including the **Committee for Accuracy in Middle East Reporting in America (CAMERA)** and the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**. Source: Koret Foundation Grantee List (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a major financial supporter of **Birthright Israel**, which provides free trips to Israel for young Jews to strengthen their connection to the state.
*   **Philanthropy:** The Doris & Donald Fisher Fund and Pisces Inc. principals are listed as donors to the Birthright Israel Foundation. Source: Birthright Israel Foundation Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Hillel International:** Principals of the family, including "Mary and Jason Fisher" (affiliated with the broader Fisher philanthropic network), are listed as supporters of **Penn Hillel**, which partners with Birthright Israel to facilitate Zionist engagement on campus. Source: Penn Hillel Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

The family’s political influence is utilized to support candidates and committees that maintain a strong pro-Israel stance in the U.S. government.

*   **Republican Political Contributions:** John J. Fisher is a major donor to Republican candidates and PACs that prioritize U.S. military aid to Israel. He has contributed hundreds of thousands of dollars to the **McCarthy Victory Fund** and other GOP leadership committees. Source: Fox Business (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
