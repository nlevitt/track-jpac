# Contributor Report: Jeffrey Katzenberg

Jeffrey Katzenberg is an American entertainment executive and political fundraiser who serves as a prominent advocate for Israel and Zionist causes within the United States. His relationship with Israel is documented through substantial philanthropic leadership, public advocacy for the U.S.-Israel relationship, and high-level engagement with pro-Israel organizations.

## 1. Zionist Ideology and Public Statements
Katzenberg has frequently articulated a personal and cultural connection to Israel, viewing support for the state as a core component of his Jewish identity.

*   **Cultural Affinity:** In a 2013 interview, Katzenberg described Israel as a "jewel" and an "amazing beautiful gem." He stated that he had personally led visits to Israel for high-profile figures such as Jerry Seinfeld, Ben Stiller, and Chris Rock to foster an appreciation for the country ([Jewish Journal, July 2013](https://jewishjournal.com/cover_story/118939/)).
*   **Sense of Obligation:** Katzenberg has emphasized that his Jewish identity involves a "connection to your roots and to Israel, a sense of belonging and a sense of obligation" ([The Forward, September 2016](https://forward.com/food/348981/jeffrey-katzenberg-hosts-700-person-shabbat-dinner-at-burning-man/)).
*   **Maintenance of Bipartisan Support:** During an interview with *Jewish Insider*, Katzenberg noted the importance of ensuring Israel remains a bipartisan issue within U.S. politics. He remarked, "we are fighting to maintain Democratic support [for] Israel and we have" ([Times of Israel, October 2018](https://www.timesofisrael.com/daily-kickoff-republicans-and-democrats-struggle-to-own-conversation-on-jewish-vote/)).

## 2. Leadership and Philanthropy
Katzenberg’s support for Israel and Zionist advocacy is largely channeled through institutional leadership and financial contributions to major Jewish organizations.

*   **Simon Wiesenthal Center:** Katzenberg is the **Founding Chairman** of the International Leadership Council of the Simon Wiesenthal Center. He is a longtime major donor and board member, describing the organization as essential in fighting "antisemitism, hate and terrorism" ([Wiesenthal Center Donor Page](https://www.wiesenthal.com/support/donate-to-international-leadership-council.html)). 
*   **Institutional Funding:** He has been identified as a major funder of the Anti-Defamation League (ADL) and is frequently listed as one of the most prominent Jewish donors to organizations that advocate for Israeli security and interests ([The Forward, September 2016](https://forward.com/food/348981/jeffrey-katzenberg-hosts-700-person-shabbat-dinner-at-burning-man/)).
*   **October 7 Solidarity:** Following the October 2023 Hamas attacks, Katzenberg was a signatory of a joint statement from entertainment industry leaders that "unequivocally stand in solidarity with Israel in the global fight against terrorism" ([TheWrap, October 2023](https://www.thewrap.com/entertainment-leaders-joint-statement-israel-hamas-war/)).

## 3. AIPAC and Advocacy Engagement
Katzenberg has a documented history of participation in the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and other high-level pro-Israel events.

*   **AIPAC Policy Conference:** Katzenberg has been a recurring attendee and participant at the annual AIPAC Policy Conference in Washington, D.C., often appearing alongside other major pro-Israel donors and leaders ([Heritage Florida Jewish News](https://heritagefl.com/story/2012/03/09/news/peres-visits-hollywood-stars/1.html)).
*   **Diplomatic Engagement:** He is characterized as being "always in the room" during visits by high-level Israeli dignitaries and officials to Los Angeles, utilizing his influence to bridge the gap between the entertainment industry and the Israeli government ([Jewish Journal, July 2014](https://jewishjournal.com/hollywood/131973/)).
*   **Advocacy within the Democratic Party:** As a national co-chair for various Democratic presidential campaigns, Katzenberg has been described as an "ardent supporter of Israel" whose presence in the party leadership serves as a bulwark against movements like Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) ([Mondoweiss, July 2020](https://mondoweiss.net/2020/07/democratic-partys-rigid-position-on-bds-stems-from-big-pro-israel-donors/)).

## 4. Documented Political Contributions
While much of Katzenberg's political giving is directed toward the Democratic Party, analysts consistently categorize him as a "pro-Israel megadonor" whose funding is contingent on a candidate's support for the U.S.-Israel alliance.

*   **Campaign Giving:** Katzenberg and his wife have donated more than $30 million to political candidates and causes since 1989. Sources explicitly link his financial status to his role as a "leading man" alongside other pro-Israel figures like Sheldon Adelson and Haim Saban in shaping the national conversation on Israel ([Jewish Currents, December 2015](https://jewishcurrents.org/jeffrey-katzenberg-and-dreamworks)).
*   **Pro-Israel PAC Alignment:** In 2020, Katzenberg was highlighted for contributing $112,000 to the Democratic Congressional Campaign Committee (DCCC) in a month where several pro-Israel donors made large contributions specifically to counter the influence of politicians supportive of Palestinian rights ([Mondoweiss, July 2020](https://mondoweiss.net/2020/07/democratic-partys-rigid-position-on-bds-stems-from-big-pro-israel-donors/)).
