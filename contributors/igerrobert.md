# Contributor Report: Robert A. Iger

### Overview of Relationship to Israel
Robert A. Iger, the Chief Executive Officer of The Walt Disney Company, has documented a consistent record of support for Israel through corporate initiatives, public statements, and institutional advocacy. His engagement is primarily characterized by financial contributions to Israeli humanitarian organizations, condemnation of anti-Israel violence, and a stated commitment to the safety and sovereignty of the Jewish state.

### Documentation of Zionist Advocacy and Support

#### 1. Response to the October 7, 2023, Attacks
In the immediate aftermath of the October 7 attacks, Iger issued a public statement condemning the "horrific terrorist attacks targeting Jews in Israel." Under his leadership, The Walt Disney Company pledged **$2 million** in humanitarian aid to the region. This included:
*   **$1 million** to Magen David Adom, an affiliate of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies that provides emergency medical and blood banking services in Israel.
*   **$1 million** to other nonprofit organizations operating in the region, with a primary focus on providing aid to children.
*   **Employee Gift Matching:** The company matched employee donations up to $25,000 for relevant charities.

**Source:** [The Forward](https://forward.com/fast-forward/564998/disney-michael-bloomberg-israel-hamas-donations/), [The Jerusalem Post](https://www.jpost.com/business-and-innovation/article-768143), [The Walt Disney Company News](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/the-walt-disney-company-donates-to-support-humanitarian-relief-following-terrorist-attacks-in-israel/)

#### 2. Institutional Advocacy and Recognition
Iger has a long-standing relationship with major pro-Israel and Jewish advocacy organizations:
*   **Simon Wiesenthal Center:** In April 2019, Iger received the Simon Wiesenthal Center's Humanitarian Award, the organization's highest honor. The award recognized his support for the Center, whose mission includes "standing with Israel" and "defending the safety of Jews worldwide." During his acceptance speech, Iger emphasized the need to "renounce and reject hate in all forms," specifically citing the dangers of extremist propaganda.
*   **Internal Advocacy:** Following the October 2023 attacks, Iger sent a targeted internal note to "Shalom," Disney’s internal Jewish employee organization. In the note, he expressed "shock and sadness" and "profound outrage," explicitly identifying the attacks as "horrific terrorist attacks carried out by Hamas in Israel."

**Source:** [The Walt Disney Company Official Release](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/disney-chairman-and-ceo-bob-iger-receives-simon-wiesenthal-centers-humanitarian-award/), [The Wrap](https://www.thewrap.com/disney-bob-iger-hamas-israel-attacks-memo-jewish-employees/), [The Los Angeles Times](https://www.latimes.com/entertainment/la-et-ct-bob-iger-simon-wiesenthal-center-20190411-story.html)

#### 3. Participation in Industry Initiatives
Iger is frequently cited alongside other Hollywood executives as part of an industry-wide effort to support Israel. While celebrity signers often dominate open letters, Iger’s advocacy is primarily expressed through corporate policy and high-level institutional participation.
*   **Creative Community for Peace (CCFP):** While Disney released its own independent statements of support for Israel, these were coordinated in timing and sentiment with the CCFP’s open letter signed by over 700 entertainment industry leaders, which called for the return of hostages and expressed solidarity with the Israeli people.
*   **Public Defense of Israel's Right to Exist:** His public condemnation of Hamas and the allocation of corporate funds to specifically Israeli emergency services (Magen David Adom) have been documented as direct expressions of support for the state's sovereignty and survival.

**Source:** [Jewish Insider](https://jewishinsider.com/2023/11/robert-iger-disney-israel-hamas-war-humanitarian-aid/), [Inside the Magic](https://insidethemagic.net/2023/10/disney-supports-israel-antisemitism-history-ld1/)

### Political Donor Context
Iger has served as a significant political donor and advisor, notably as a member of President Trump’s Strategic and Policy Forum in 2017 (before resigning over the Paris Agreement) and as a prominent donor to Democratic causes. His political influence has often intersected with his role in maintaining Disney's institutional support for Jewish and Israeli-related humanitarian causes.
