# Contributor Report: Rita Gutierrez-Fisher

### 1. Identity and Professional Background
Public records and local news identify **Rita Gutierrez-Fisher** as a resident of **Gilroy, California**, and a retired school counselor from South Valley Middle School. She has been active in local philanthropy and community organizations for several decades, including the **Japanese American Citizens League (JACL)** and the **St. Louise Regional Hospital Foundation**.

*   **Professional History:** Gutierrez-Fisher served as a counselor for 30 years at South Valley Middle School before her retirement in 2003 ([Gilroy Dispatch, 2003](https://gilroydispatch.com/passing-junior-high-milestone/)).
*   **Civic Leadership:** She has held leadership roles in the **West Valley JACL**, serving as co-president in the early 1990s (Pacific Citizen, 1993 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Local Philanthropy:** She is documented as a donor to regional non-profits, including **Rebekah Children's Services** (RCS Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)) and the **Santa Cruz Museum of Art & History** (MAH Annual Report, 2012-2013 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 2. Relationship to Gap Inc. and the Fisher Family
The name **Rita Gutierrez-Fisher** is frequently associated in activist databases with the **Fisher family**, specifically **Donald and Doris Fisher**, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and their family office, **Pisces Inc.** ([Pisces Inc. Overview](https://littlesis.org/org/15192-California_State_Polytechnic_University)). While Donald and Doris Fisher are the primary "Prominent Zionist donors" identified in these records, individuals bearing the Fisher name are often grouped together in donor assessments due to their shared management of family wealth through **Pisces Inc.**

*   **Pisces Inc.:** This entity serves as the family office for the Fisher family. It has been identified as a vehicle for large-scale political and philanthropic contributions, including those directed toward pro-Israel organizations ([LittleSis: Pisces Inc](https://littlesis.org/org/15192-California_State_Polytechnic_University)).
*   **Doris F. Fisher:** As a co-founder of Gap Inc., Doris Fisher is a prolific donor to the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**, which are central to her documented Zionist advocacy ([Forbes: Doris Fisher Profile](https://www.forbes.com/profile/doris-fisher/)).

### 3. Documented Support for Israel and Zionist Advocacy
The "Gutierrez-Fisher" name in this context refers to the documented relationship between the Fisher family's wealth and Israeli institutional support. The primary avenues for this support are the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

#### A. Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has been long-standing supporters and leaders of the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization that provides extensive funding to Israeli military, educational, and settlement-related programs.
*   **Funding Priorities:** The Koret Foundation has donated millions to the **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)** and the **Jewish Community Federation**, which supports infrastructure and security in Israel ([Koret Foundation: Israel Focus](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)).
*   **Strategic Support:** The foundation is documented as a primary funder of the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF)**, which seeks to strengthen the Israeli economy ([Jewish Virtual Library: Koret Foundation](https://jewishvirtuallibrary.org/koret-foundation)).

#### B. Birthright Israel (Taglit)
The Fisher family is identified as a "Mega-Donor" to **Birthright Israel**, a program that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the state and Zionist ideology.
*   **Financial Contributions:** Doris and Donald Fisher are listed among the principal donors to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which has spent over $1 billion to date on its programming (Birthright Israel Foundation: Major Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Ideological Alignment:** Birthright is described by advocacy groups as a "Zionist soft power" tool designed to foster pro-Israel sentiment ([Mapping Project: Birthright Israel](https://mappingproject.org/entities/taglit_birthright_israel.html)).

#### C. Political Donations
Fisher family members have consistently contributed to the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and other pro-Israel lobbying groups.
*   **Political Action Committees:** Contributions from Fisher family members are documented in FEC filings for organizations like **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, which advocates for pro-Israel policies within the Democratic Party (OpenSecrets: Democratic Majority for Israel (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 4. Summary of Evidence
While **Rita Gutierrez-Fisher** is a documented local philanthropist in the San Francisco Bay Area, she is identified in activist research as being part of the broader **Fisher family (Gap/Pisces Inc.)** network. This network is a primary source of funding for Zionist advocacy in the United States, specifically through the sustained support of the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.
