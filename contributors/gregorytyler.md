# Contributor Report: Gregory, Tyler

### Professional Profile and Organizational Leadership
Tyler (Tye) Gregory serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC) Bay Area**, a role he assumed in 2020. Under his leadership, the JCRC has transitioned toward direct political engagement through the 2024 launch of **Bay Area Jewish Action (BAJA)**, a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization. Gregory serves as BAJA's Executive Director, leading an entity designed specifically to facilitate political contributions and mobilize voters for candidates who support U.S.-Israel relations and combat what the organization defines as "anti-Israel hostilities."

Prior to his current roles, Gregory was the Executive Director of **A Wider Bridge** (2014–2020), an organization that advocates for "equality for Israel" within the LGBTQ+ community and coordinates missions to Israel for North American LGBTQ+ leaders. His early career included work in the political department of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**, where he partnered with members of Congress to strengthen the U.S.-Israel relationship.

### Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
Gregory’s documented relationship to Israel is centered on leadership-driven advocacy and legislative intervention.

*   **Legislative Advocacy (Ethnic Studies):** Gregory has been a primary advocate in the effort to amend California's **Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum**. He publicly lobbied for the removal of content he characterized as "anti-Israel" and spearheaded efforts to ensure the curriculum included a specific definition of antisemitism that encompasses certain criticisms of Israel. He praised the 2021 curriculum revisions for keeping out "denigrating content about Jews, Israelis and Israel" and continues to lobby for AB 1468 to standardize these standards statewide.
    *   **Source (Ethnic Studies):** [jns.org/jewish-pro-israel-groups-express-mixed-reactions-to-adoption-of-californias-ethnic-studies-track/](https://www.jns.org/jewish-pro-israel-groups-express-mixed-reactions-to-adoption-of-californias-ethnic-studies-track/)
    *   **Source (Legislative Support):** [jcrc.org/newsroom/jpac-jcrc-bay-area-and-state-jewish-coalition-welcome-ethnic-studies-curriculum-bill/](https://jcrc.org/newsroom/jpac-jcrc-bay-area-and-state-jewish-coalition-welcome-ethnic-studies-curriculum-bill-in-state-legislature/)

*   **Israel Delegations:** Through both JCRC and A Wider Bridge, Gregory has coordinated and led numerous delegations of U.S. civic and elected leaders to Israel. These trips are designed to build "mutual understanding" and strengthen institutional ties between California politicians and the State of Israel.

### Political Donation and Fundraising Facilitation
Gregory's political influence is exerted primarily through the organization and facilitation of donations rather than documented individual high-dollar contributions alone.

*   **Facilitating Candidate Funding:** In 2024, Gregory announced that BAJA facilitated contributions to 10 candidates for local and state office from more than 200 community members in its pilot year. He stated that the organization’s goal is to ensure "consequences for mistreating the Jewish community and allowing the spread of antisemitism," specifically targeting politicians whose stances on Israel conflict with JCRC consensus positions.
    *   **Source (Fundraising Role):** [jewishinsider.com/2024/08/bay-area-jcrc-political-advocacy-baja-antisemitism-israel/](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/08/bay-area-jcrc-political-advocacy-baja-antisemitism-israel/)
    *   **Source (BAJA Mission):** [jweekly.com/2024/08/19/jcrc-bay-area-expands-into-political-organizing-with-new-offshoot/](https://jweekly.com/2024/08/19/jcrc-bay-area-expands-into-political-organizing-with-new-offshoot/)

*   **Targeted Political Interventions:** Gregory has used his platform to publicly rebuke local officials who support ceasefire resolutions or divestment from Israel. He characterized the Oakland City Council's ceasefire vote as contributing to a "decay" in the local political environment and has coordinated with other pro-Israel organizations to oppose candidates with "anti-Israel records."
    *   **Source (Oakland Ceasefire Criticism):** [jewishinsider.com/2024/04/the-grassroots-efforts-to-tackle-anti-israel-activity-as-it-spreads-to-communities/](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/04/the-grassroots-efforts-to-tackle-anti-israel-activity-as-it-spreads-to-communities/)
