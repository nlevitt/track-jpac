# Contributor Report: Berman, Marc

### Overview
Marc Berman is a Democratic member of the California State Assembly, representing the 23rd District (formerly the 24th District) since 2016. He is a prominent member of the [California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/members), an influential body within the state legislature that advocates for policies aligned with the interests of the Jewish community and maintains documented ties to Zionist advocacy.

---

### Legislative Delegations and Trips to Israel
*   **AJC Project Interchange (2018):** In July 2018, Berman participated in a week-long legislative delegation to Israel sponsored by [Project Interchange](https://www.ajc.org/news/bipartisan-delegation-of-california-state-legislators-visit-israel-explore-bilateral), an educational institute of the American Jewish Committee (AJC). The trip was designed to "strengthen Latino-Jewish, Asian-Jewish, and interfaith relations" and included briefings on Israel's "geo-strategic realities" at the borders of Syria and Gaza, as well as meetings with senior Israeli leaders ([Source](https://www.ajc.org/news/bipartisan-delegation-of-california-state-legislators-visit-israel-explore-bilateral)).
*   **Diplomatic Focus:** The delegation aimed to foster [bilateral collaboration](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2018-08-15-trip-jittery-israel-eye-opener-california-assemblymembers) between California and Israel, particularly in fields such as water management and technological innovation ([Source](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2018-08-15-trip-jittery-israel-eye-opener-california-assemblymembers)).

---

### Policy Stances and Zionist Advocacy
*   **Opposition to BDS:** Berman has been a vocal critic of the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement. In 2019, he led opposition to a draft of the [California Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum](https://www.jweekly.com/2019/09/17/bay-area-lawmaker-hears-community-concerns-on-ethnic-studies-draft/), stating that it contained "anti-Jewish bias" and "delegitimizing" language regarding the State of Israel by including favorable mentions of BDS and terminology like "Nakba" ([Source](https://www.jweekly.com/2019/09/17/bay-area-lawmaker-hears-community-concerns-on-ethnic-studies-draft/)).
*   **Solidarity with Israel:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Berman joined other members of the Jewish Caucus in a statement expressing ["enduring solidarity with the State of Israel"](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2023-10-07-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks) and affirming its "right and responsibility to defend its civilians" ([Source](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2023-10-07-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks)).
*   **Opposition to Ceasefire Protests:** In November 2023, Berman endorsed a CLJC statement [denouncing protesters](https://www.wsws.org/en/articles/2023/11/20/atsh-n20.html) at the California Democratic Convention who used slogans such as "from the river to the sea," labeling such chants as "completely unacceptable" ([Source](https://www.wsws.org/en/articles/2023/11/20/atsh-n20.html)).
*   **Holocaust Education:** Berman authored [Assembly Concurrent Resolution 37](https://a23.asmdc.org/press-releases/20170424-berman-leads-holocaust-remembrance-ceremony-state-assembly) (2017) to proclaim California Holocaust Memorial Day, a cornerstone of the Jewish Caucus's legislative agenda often linked to broader advocacy for Jewish security and Israeli recognition ([Source](https://a23.asmdc.org/press-releases/20170424-berman-leads-holocaust-remembrance-ceremony-state-assembly)).

### Organizational Relationships and Financial Support
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC):** Berman maintains a close relationship with [JPAC California](https://www.jweekly.com/2022/05/19/hundreds-descend-on-sacramento-to-lobby-for-jewish-interests/), the largest single-state Jewish lobbying organization in the U.S., which advocates for pro-Israel policies and state funding for Jewish institutions ([Source](https://www.jweekly.com/2022/05/19/hundreds-descend-on-sacramento-to-lobby-for-jewish-interests/)). 
*   **Political Contributions:** During his initial 2016 campaign, Berman was one of several candidates to receive a [$3,000 contribution](https://jewishjournal.com/community/213506/jewish-state-legislators-ready-make-impact/) from a public affairs committee affiliated with the Jewish Caucus, established specifically to support "prospective members" who aligned with the caucus's mission ([Source](https://jewishjournal.com/community/213506/jewish-state-legislators-ready-make-impact/)).
*   **Endorsements:** He is endorsed by the [Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area (JDCBA)](https://www.jdcsv.com/endorsements), an organization that requires candidates to support "Israel as a Jewish and democratic state" as part of its endorsement criteria ([Source](https://www.jdcsv.com/endorsements)).
