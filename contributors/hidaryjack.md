# Contributor Report: Jack Hidary

### **Profile Summary**
Jack Hidary is a technology executive and entrepreneur currently serving as the CEO of **SandboxAQ**, an artificial intelligence and quantum technology company spun out from Alphabet Inc. He is a prominent figure within the Syrian-Jewish community in Brooklyn and is documented as a national leader in pro-Israel advocacy organizations.

---

### **Leadership in Zionist Advocacy Organizations**

#### **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**
Hidary is identified as a **National Leader** and member of the **National Board of Governors** for the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**, a prominent organization that advocates for Israel and Jewish interests globally. 
*   **Documentation:** [AJC National Board of Governors Identification](https://www.ajc.org/news/ajc-national-board-of-governors-member-jack-hidary-attends-2023-global-forum)

#### **Creative Community for Peace (CCFP)**
Hidary is a signatory of major open letters organized by the **Creative Community for Peace**, an organization that combats the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement and supports Israel’s presence in international cultural events.
*   **Action:** Signed the 2024 open letter supporting Israel’s inclusion in the Eurovision Song Contest and rejecting calls for a boycott against the state.
*   **Documentation:** [Creative Community for Peace Signatories List](https://www.creativecommunityforpeace.com/blog/2024/02/14/entertainment-leaders-support-israels-inclusion-in-eurovision/)

---

### **Political and Public Support for Israel**

#### **Advocacy for US-Israel Relations**
During his public ventures, Hidary has been characterized as an "unabashed supporter" of the State of Israel. His public platform has been used to promote strengthening the technological and security alliance between the United States and Israel.
*   **Documentation:** [IMAGE Magazine: Profile on Jack Hidary’s Pro-Israel Support](https://www.imageusa.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/09/September-2013-low-res.pdf) (See page 68/70)

#### **Community and Institutional Ties**
Hidary maintains deep ties to institutions that prioritize Zionist education and activism, such as the **Yeshivah of Flatbush**. He has facilitated programs involving high-ranking Israeli officials and advocates.
*   **Engagement:** Mentored students and facilitated participation in the **AIPAC Policy Conference**.
*   **Knesset Interaction:** Hosted Israeli Knesset members, including Amir Ohana and Ayelet Shaked, during organizational events.
*   **Documentation:** [Yeshivah of Flatbush: Israel Activism and AIPAC Participation](https://www.myflatbushlife.com/2017/04/the-israel-activism-commission-at-aipac.html)

#### **Pro-Israel Media Initiatives**
Hidary has been identified as part of a coalition of media and tech billionaires courted for high-level pro-Israel media campaigns designed to counter anti-Israel sentiment and Hamas-related narratives in the aftermath of October 7.
*   **Documentation:** [Semafor: Billionaires Courted for Pro-Israel Media Drive](https://www.semafor.com/article/11/09/2023/billionaires-are-teaming-up-for-pro-israel-anti-hamas-media-drive-report)
