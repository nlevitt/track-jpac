# Contributor Report: The Fisher Family (Gap Inc. / Pisces Inc.)

**Note on Entity Identification:** While the request refers to "THERMO FISHER SCIENTIFIC INC. PAC," the provided context (Gap Inc./Pisces Inc. founders, Koret Foundation, and Birthright Israel) pertains to the **Fisher family of San Francisco**, specifically **Donald Fisher** (1928–2009), **Doris Fisher**, and their sons (Robert, William, and John). **Thermo Fisher Scientific Inc.** is a separate multinational biotechnology corporation (founded by Chester G. Fisher) with distinct operations and its own PAC. This report focuses on the documented Zionist advocacy of the Fisher family and their associated entities, as specified in the context.

### 1. Institutional Affiliations and Zionist Advocacy
The Fisher family’s support for Israel is primarily channeled through the **Koret Foundation** and the **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund**. These entities maintain institutional relationships with organizations dedicated to the promotion of Jewish identity and the strengthening of the State of Israel.

*   **Koret Foundation:** Doris and Donald Fisher served as longtime leaders and donors to the Koret Foundation. The foundation is a major financier of Israeli development and Zionist educational programs. It has donated over $100 million to Israel-related causes, including the Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF).
    *   **Source:** [Koret Foundation - Israel Giving](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)
*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family is documented as a significant donor to Birthright Israel (Taglit), an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jews to "strengthen Jewish identity" and "build a lasting bond with the land and people of Israel."
    *   **Source:** [JWeekly - Donald Fisher's Community Legacy](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
    *   **Source:** Birthright Israel Foundation - Major Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Political Contributions and Pro-Israel Advocacy
Through **Pisces Inc.** (the family office) and personal contributions, the Fisher family has been active in California and national politics, often supporting candidates and causes aligned with pro-Israel policy frameworks.

*   **Political Spending:** The Fisher family is among the most prolific political donors in California. While much of their spending focuses on charter school advocacy, they have consistently supported federal candidates who receive high ratings from the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and other pro-Israel lobbying groups.
    *   **Source:** OpenSecrets - Fisher Family Political Contributions (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   **Source:** Los Angeles Times - The wealthy families behind Gavin Newsom (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Support for the Jewish Community Federation:** Donald Fisher was a "longtime supporter" of the San Francisco-based Jewish Community Federation, which allocates funds to various Israeli security and social programs.
    *   **Source:** Jewish Community Federation & Endowment Fund - Legacy Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Corporate and Business Links to Israel
Separate from the family’s personal philanthropy, **Thermo Fisher Scientific Inc.** (often conflated with the family due to the name) maintains a direct physical and economic presence in Israel, which has made it a subject of interest for those tracking corporate ties to the region.

*   **Operational Presence:** Thermo Fisher Scientific operates **Thermo Fisher Israel Ltd.**, with facilities in Ness Ziona and Airport City. The company provides laboratory equipment, chemicals, and reagents to the Israeli medical and defense research sectors.
    *   **Source:** [Thermo Fisher Scientific - Israel Operations](https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home.html)
*   **Post-October 7 Support:** Following the 2023 Hamas attacks, Thermo Fisher Scientific's CEO, Marc Casper, was among the corporate leaders who signed public statements expressing solidarity with Israel and denouncing the attacks.
    *   **Source:** [Yale School of Management - Companies Condemning Hamas Attacks](https://som.yale.edu/story/2023/list-companies-have-condemned-hamas-terrorist-attack-israel)

### 4. Summary of Documented Influence
The Fisher family (Gap/Pisces) exerts influence on Zionist advocacy through three primary pillars:
1. **Philanthropic Capital:** Sustained multi-million dollar grants to the Koret Foundation and Birthright Israel.
2. **Educational Advocacy:** Funding programs that link Jewish identity with the State of Israel.
3. **Political Patronage:** Leveraging Pisces Inc. and individual wealth to support pro-Israel political infrastructure in the United States.
