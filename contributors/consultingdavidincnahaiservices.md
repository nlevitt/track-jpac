# Contributor Report: David Nahai Consulting Services, Inc.

David Nahai Consulting Services, Inc. is a Los Angeles-based firm specializing in renewable energy, water conservation, and real estate, led by President **David Nahai**. Beyond its commercial activities, the firm and its principal are documented participants in Zionist advocacy and maintain extensive leadership ties to organizations dedicated to the support of the State of Israel.

## Leadership in Zionist Organizations

### Bnai Zion Foundation
David Nahai has served as the **Chairman of the Bnai Zion Foundation**. Bnai Zion is a Zionist organization that funds capital projects in Israel, including medical centers and educational facilities, to bolster the country’s infrastructure.
*   **Source:** [LADWP News Archive (November 20, 2007)](https://www.ladwpnews.com/h-david-nahai-appointed-ladwp-general-manager/)
*   **Documentation:** Nahai received the foundation’s "Celebration of Life Award" in 2003 for his service to the organization.

### Jewish National Fund (JNF)
Nahai is a member of the **Major Donors Committee of the Jewish National Fund**. The JNF is a global Zionist organization that manages land and infrastructure in Israel, primarily through its Keren Kayemeth LeIsrael (KKL) branch, with a focus on settlement development and environmental projects.
*   **Source:** [LADWP News Archive (November 20, 2007)](https://www.ladwpnews.com/h-david-nahai-appointed-ladwp-general-manager/)
*   **Context:** His involvement in the Major Donors Committee places him among the organization’s high-level financial supporters.

### Magbit Foundation
Nahai has served as a **Board Member and Master of Ceremonies** for the Magbit Foundation. This organization provides interest-free loans specifically to students in Israel to ensure they can complete their higher education and contribute to the Israeli economy.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Journal (May 25, 2006)](https://jewishjournal.com/community/13149/)
*   **Zionist Context:** At a Magbit gala hosted by Nahai, the Israeli Consul General praised the "uniquely strong sense of Zionism" within Nahai’s community.

## Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles
David Nahai is a **past Chair** of the Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles. The Federation’s mission includes "ensuring the Jewish future" by funding programs in Israel and supporting the country’s security and social welfare.
*   **Role Documentation:** [David Nahai Professional Biography](https://www.davidnahai.com/about)
*   **Mission Context:** The Federation characterizes its work as taking care of the Jewish people "here, in Israel, and around the world."
*   **Nahai’s Statement:** In an interview, Nahai stated his involvement is intended to "ensure that we continue to keep our sense of community, our bonds and our Jewish identity."
*   **Source:** [Jewish Journal (February 10, 2022)](https://jewishjournal.com/community/344933/former-ladwp-ceo-david-nahai-reflects-on-his-work-in-the-environmental-sector/)

## Documented Advocacy and Public Representation

### Promotion of Israeli Technology
Nahai frequently uses his professional platform in the water and energy sectors to advocate for the adoption of Israeli technology and models in the United States, positioning Israel as an essential global leader in sustainability.
*   **Milken Institute:** In 2014, Nahai was a speaker at the Milken Institute’s Global Conference specifically regarding "The California-Israel Innovation Project."
    *   **Source:** [David Nahai Official Site - Events](https://www.davidnahai.com/events)
*   **Writings:** He authored a piece for the *Jewish Journal* titled "The Future of Water in Los Angeles: What the Israeli Experience Can Show Us," which promotes Israeli state policy as a blueprint for American infrastructure.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal (July 11, 2012)](https://jewishjournal.com/commentary/opinion/106346/)

### Political and Community Mobilization
Nahai has been a key figure in **30 Years After**, an organization dedicated to mobilizing the Iranian Jewish community for political activism. This involvement often focuses on ensuring that the community remains a potent pro-Israel voting and donor bloc in U.S. politics.
*   **Source:** [Beverly Hills Weekly (September 25, 2008)](https://issuu.com/bhweekly/docs/issue_469)
*   **Documentation:** Nahai was a featured speaker at a 30 Years After rally titled "The Iranian Jewish Community at Crossroads," which aimed to increase the community's political participation and influence.
