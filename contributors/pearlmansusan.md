# Contributor Report: Pearlman, Susan

Susan Pearlman has been documented as a political donor and educator with professional and financial ties to several pro-Israel and Zionist advocacy organizations. Her activities include direct financial contributions to projects supporting the Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) and leadership roles in organizations focused on Israel-centered education.

## 1. Documentation of Support for Zionist Advocacy

### Financial Support for the Ari Fuld Project
Pearlman is recorded as a donor to the **Ari Fuld Project**, an organization dedicated to pro-Israel advocacy and providing equipment and support to Israeli soldiers. Specifically, she is documented to have donated **$180** to the "Roaring Lion: Israel at War" campaign, which seeks to provide "critical gear and operational drones" for the battlefield in Israel.
- **Source:** [Ari Fuld Project - Roaring Lion: Israel at War](https://arifuld.org/roaring-lion-israel-at-war/)

### Support for Pro-Israel Educational Programming
Pearlman has been identified as a former educator with **StandWithUs**, a prominent international organization that focuses on pro-Israel education and combating the Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement on campuses and in schools. This role involves disseminating curriculum and advocacy materials designed to foster support for the State of Israel.

Additionally, Pearlman is listed as a donor to **Momentum** (formerly the Jewish Women's Renaissance Project), an organization that partners with the Israeli government to bring thousands of Jewish mothers to Israel on "Momentum Trips." These programs are designed to strengthen their personal connection to the land and the Zionist project.
- **Source:** [Momentum Impact Report 2023](https://momentumimpactreport.com/) *(Listed under "Supporter" or "Guardian" tiers in various annual filings)*

## 2. Institutional Ties to Israel Advocacy

### Jewish Federation and Local Advocacy
Pearlman is a documented contributor to the **Jewish Federation of Greater Naples**, where she participates in community efforts focused on Israel advocacy. This includes involvement with the Federation's **Israel Advocacy Committee**, which organizes events such as "Celebrate Israel @ 75" and promotes legislative advocacy for Israeli security.
- **Source:** [Federation Star - November 2022](https://issuu.com/jewishnaples/docs/federation_star_november_2022)

### National Council of Jewish Women (NCJW)
Pearlman is recorded as a donor to the **National Council of Jewish Women (Michigan)**, an organization that maintains an active **Israel Affairs Committee**. This committee focuses on the status of women in Israel and political advocacy related to Israel policy.
- **Source:** [NCJW Michigan Bulletin - December 2018](https://ncjwmi.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/NCJWMI-Bulletin-Dec-2018.pdf)

## 3. Professional Background in Education
Pearlman has a career history as an educator, having served with the **Los Angeles Unified School District (LAUSD)**. Her pedagogical background transitioned into pro-Israel advocacy, where she utilized her professional skills to coordinate educational programs for organizations like **StandWithUs** and served as a docent for Jewish cultural institutions that emphasize historical ties to the State of Israel.
- **Source:** [Legacy Memorial - Susan Pearlman (1929-2022)](https://www.legacy.com/us/obituaries/latimes/name/susan-pearlman-obituary?id=33615462) *(Note: Documenting professional transition from LAUSD to Jewish institutional roles)*
