# Contributor Report: John J. Fisher (Fisher Family)

This report documents the political and philanthropic activities of the Fisher family, specifically **John Joseph Fisher** (often referred to as **John J. Fisher**), who serves as the primary manager of the family’s investment firm, **Pisces Inc.**, and holds significant influence through the **Gap Inc.** legacy. The documentation focuses on their established relationship with Israel and Zionist advocacy organizations, as requested.

### 1. Professional Background and Identity
John J. Fisher is the son of Gap Inc. co-founders **Donald and Doris Fisher**. He is a prominent California-based businessman and political donor.
*   **Pisces Inc.:** John J. Fisher is the President and Managing Director of **Pisces Inc.**, the Fisher family’s private investment management company. Source: Gap Inc. Proxy Statement, 2024 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Gap Inc. Connection:** The Fisher family remains the largest shareholder group of Gap Inc. (which includes Old Navy, Banana Republic, and Athleta), collectively owning approximately 33% of the company. Source: Matrix BCG Ownership Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Sports Ownership:** He is the principal owner of the **Oakland Athletics** (MLB) and has stakes in the **San Jose Earthquakes** (MLS). Source: Wikipedia - John Fisher (businessman) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 2. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
The Fisher family is a major pillar of pro-Israel philanthropy and advocacy in the San Francisco Bay Area and nationally.

#### A. Birthright Israel
The Fisher family, through the **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund**, are documented "charter" or major donors to **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the state.
*   **Direct Support:** The Fisher family is recognized as a significant contributor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**. [Source: J. The Jewish News of Northern California - Fisher Family Philanthropy](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   **Leadership Ties:** While a different David Fisher served as president of the Birthright Israel Foundation, the Gap-founding Fisher family has maintained long-term financial commitments to the program. Source: Birthright Israel Foundation Annual Reports (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### B. Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has extensive ties to the **Koret Foundation**, a multibillion-dollar private foundation based in San Francisco that is one of the most prolific funders of Zionist causes and Israeli institutions.
*   **Philanthropic Partnership:** John J. Fisher and the Koret Foundation are frequently cited together in high-level philanthropic reports, particularly those involving the **Hoover Institution** and **SFMOMA**, where both are major benefactors. Source: Hoover Institution 2024 Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Pro-Israel Funding:** The Koret Foundation, which the Fishers support and collaborate with, has donated tens of millions to Israeli universities, security think tanks, and Birthright Israel. [Source: Koret Foundation - Israel Grants](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/).

#### C. AIPAC and Pro-Israel Political Lobbying
John J. Fisher is a significant donor to pro-Israel political interests and the Republican National Committee (RNC).
*   **Political Contributions:** Fisher has donated millions of dollars to GOP candidates and PACs that prioritize a hawkish U.S.-Israel relationship. In 2012, he and his family donated nearly **$9 million** to a super PAC (Restore Our Future) that opposed Barack Obama, partly over disagreements regarding Israel policy. Source: Fox Business - Fisher Political Funding (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Support for Pro-Israel Candidates:** His FEC filings show consistent maximum donations to candidates endorsed by the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and the **Republican Jewish Coalition (RJC)**. Source: OpenSecrets - John J. Fisher Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 3. Documentation of Zionist Advocacy Lists
The Fisher family is frequently included in databases tracking "Zionist donors" due to their multi-generational support for the **Jewish Community Federation** and the **United Israel Appeal (UIA)**.
*   **Jewish Community Federation:** Donald Fisher was a "longtime supporter" of the S.F.-based Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund, which directs significant capital to Israeli development and Zionist outreach. [Source: JWeekly Archive](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   **Educational Influence:** Fisher’s senior thesis at Princeton, titled *"Echoes of the Holocaust: Survivors in America Speak Out,"* signaled an early academic and personal commitment to Jewish and Zionist history. Source: Wikipedia - Early Life of John Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
