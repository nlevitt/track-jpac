# Contributor Report: California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership PAC

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

This report provides an evidence-based overview of the **California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership PAC** (Committee ID: [1438782](https://cal-access.sos.ca.gov/Campaign/Committees/Detail.aspx?id=1438782)), its relationship to the California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC), and its documented support for Israel and Zionist advocacy.

### 1. Organizational Structure and Affiliation
The California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership PAC is a state-level political action committee primarily funded by and dedicated to the interests of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)**. While frequently conflated in public discourse with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)**—a 501(c)(4) advocacy organization now rebranded as "Jewish California"—the Leadership PAC serves as the political financing arm for the Caucus's leadership and priorities.

*   **Legal Status:** The PAC is registered as a "Recipient Committee" in California ([Transparency USA](https://www.transparencyusa.org/ca/committee/california-jewish-legislative-caucus-leadership-pac-1438782-payee)).
*   **Leadership:** The PAC is controlled by the leadership of the CLJC, which currently includes Co-Chairs **Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel** and **Senator Scott Wiener** (California Legislative Jewish Caucus Official Site (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 2. Documented Relationship to Zionism and Israel
The PAC and its parent Caucus explicitly incorporate support for the State of Israel and the countering of anti-Zionism into their core mission and legislative strategy.

*   **Mission Statement:** The California Legislative Jewish Caucus defines its mandate to include "countering antisemitism, anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" and "strengthening California–Israel economic, technological and cultural ties" (Wikipedia: California Legislative Jewish Caucus (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Anti-Zionism Advocacy:** The Caucus identifies "anti-Zionism" as a primary target for its legislative efforts, often framing it as a form of antisemitism to be addressed through state policy (J Weekly (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Solidarity with Israel:** Following the events of October 7, 2023, the Caucus issued statements of "enduring solidarity with the State of Israel" and successfully advocated for the State Capitol to be illuminated in blue and white (LA Progressive (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 3. Legislative Record and Zionist Advocacy
The PAC supports candidates and leaders who advance a specific "Priority Bill Package" that centers on pro-Israel and Zionist-aligned objectives.

*   **Anti-BDS Legislation (AB 2844):** The Caucus spearheaded California’s "Anti-BDS" law, which prohibits the state from contracting with companies that participate in boycotts of Israel ([Official Bill Text: AB 2844](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)).
*   **Campus Speech and "Anti-Zionism" (SB 1287/AB 715):** The Caucus championed bills designed to combat "hostile environments" on campuses, which critics and proponents alike identify as targeting pro-Palestinian and anti-Zionist demonstrations (CounterPunch (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **California-Israel MOU:** The Caucus was instrumental in the passage of **SCR 121**, which formally endorsed a memorandum of understanding between California and Israel for economic and technological cooperation ([California Legislative Information](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201320140SCR121)).

### 4. Financial Activity and Candidate Support
The Leadership PAC facilitates the flow of capital from corporate and institutional donors to candidates who align with the Caucus's pro-Israel platform.

*   **Donor Base:** The PAC has received contributions from major corporate entities, including **Pfizer Inc.** and the **California-Nevada Conference of Operating Engineers**, as well as individual Caucus members (Pfizer 2017-2018 Political Contributions Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); SomaliScan: Pfizer Contributions (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Candidate Contributions:** The PAC has made significant expenditures to support California Democratic candidates, such as **Assemblymember Freddie Rodriguez**, and to bolster the campaigns of its own members who lead pro-Israel initiatives ([Ballotpedia: Freddie Rodriguez Campaign Finance](https://ballotpedia.org/Freddie_Rodriguez)).
*   **Strategic Influence:** Critics characterize the PAC's funding as a means to ensure a "pro-Israel faction" remains influential within the California State Legislature, enabling the block to exercise leverage over the Speaker and committee chairs (LA Progressive (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 5. Critical Analysis of "Zionist" Identification
External observers and advocacy groups have documented the PAC's specific dedication to Zionist ideology.

*   **Criticism from Jewish Voice for Peace (JVP):** Board members of JVP have stated that the Caucus and its PAC "legislatively push the goals of political Zionism and the Israeli government," rather than general Jewish community interests (CounterPunch (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Identification as "Pro-Israel Faction":** Political watchdog groups and independent media have identified the PAC and its affiliated legislators as a "pro-Israel faction" that seeks to conflate anti-Zionism with antisemitism to suppress dissent against Israeli policy (LA Progressive (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
