# Contributor Report: Spieker, Carol

Carol Spieker (also documented as Carol J. Spieker) is a prominent Silicon Valley philanthropist and political donor based in Atherton, California. Alongside her husband, real estate developer Warren E. "Ned" Spieker Jr., she is a documented financial supporter of major pro-Israel advocacy organizations and political action committees (PACs) that lobby for U.S. support of Israel and Zionist interests.

### 1. Documented Support for AIPAC and Pro-Israel PACs
Spieker is a recurring donor to the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and its affiliated political arms. Her contributions are documented through federal election filings, where she is identified as a significant donor to PACs dedicated to maintaining and strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship.

*   **AIPAC PAC:** Carol Spieker is listed as a donor to the AIPAC PAC, which was established to provide direct financial support to pro-Israel candidates in U.S. elections.
    *   **Source:** [FEC Individual Contribution Records - Carol Spieker (AIPAC PAC)](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Carol+Spieker&committee_id=C00797225)
*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** Spieker has contributed to the **Democratic Majority for Israel PAC**, an organization that works to maintain pro-Israel support within the Democratic Party and opposes candidates perceived as critical of the Israeli government.
    *   **Source:** [FEC Individual Contribution Records - Carol Spieker (Democratic Majority for Israel PAC)](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Carol+Spieker&committee_id=C00694174)
*   **United Democracy Project (UDP):** Through her family trust and individual filings, Spieker is linked to the donor network of the **United Democracy Project**, AIPAC’s Super PAC, which spends millions in primary elections to defeat candidates critical of Israel's policies.
    *   **Source:** [OpenSecrets - United Democracy Project Donors 2022 Cycle (Search for Spieker)](https://www.opensecrets.org/outside-spending/detail/2022?cmte=C00799031&tab=donors)

### 2. Institutional Philanthropy: Jewish Community Federation
The Spiekers maintain the **Carol & Ned Spieker Fund** at the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (JCF)** of San Francisco. The JCF is a primary vehicle for Zionist philanthropy in the Bay Area, allocating significant annual funding to Israeli infrastructure, security, and organizations that promote Zionist identity in the United States.

*   **Jewish Community Federation Honor Roll:** The Carol & Ned Spieker Fund is consistently documented as a donor to the JCF, which channels millions to organizations such as the **United Jerusalem Foundation** and the **Jewish Agency for Israel**.
    *   **Source:** [San Francisco Chronicle - Jewish Community Federation Major Donor List (December 2021)](https://www.sfchronicle.com/projects/2021/season-of-sharing-major-donors/)
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund - Annual Report 2022-23 (Recognition List)](https://jewishfed.org/community-impact/annual-reports-and-financials)

### 3. Broad Zionist Advocacy and Influence
Beyond direct political donations, Spieker’s philanthropic footprint includes support for conservative and hawkish policy centers that advocate for a robust U.S. military and diplomatic commitment to Israel.

*   **Hoover Institution:** Carol and Ned Spieker are listed as significant contributors to the **Hoover Institution** at Stanford University, which hosts numerous fellows who produce research and policy recommendations supporting Israeli security and U.S. interventionism in the Middle East.
    *   **Source:** [Hoover Institution - Book of Lists (Donor Recognition)](https://www.hoover.org/sites/default/files/uploads/documents/HooverBookofLists.pdf)
*   **Institute of World Politics (IWP):** The Spiekers have provided "significant funding" to the **Institute of World Politics**, a graduate school that emphasizes "peace through strength" and maintains strong ties to the U.S. intelligence and defense communities, often aligning with pro-Israel strategic interests.
    *   **Source:** [PR Newswire - Several Significant Donations Impact The Institute Of World Politics (April 2019)](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/several-significant-donations-impact-the-institute-of-world-politics-300825287.html)
