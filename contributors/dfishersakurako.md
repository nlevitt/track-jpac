As of 2024, **Sakurako D. Fisher** (also known as Sako Fisher) is a prominent California-based philanthropist and political donor. She is the wife of William S. Fisher, an heir to the Gap Inc. fortune and founder of Manzanita Capital. Fisher’s documented relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is characterized by substantial financial support to organizations dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship and fostering Jewish identity through Israel-centric programming.

# Contributor Report: Fisher, Sakurako D.

### Overview of Philanthropic and Political Profile
Sakurako D. Fisher operates as part of the prominent Fisher family of San Francisco, whose wealth originates from the founding of **Gap Inc.** and the management of **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment office). Her philanthropic activity is frequently conducted in tandem with her husband, William S. Fisher. Their documented contributions focus on institutional maintenance of Zionist ideology through education, cultural exchange, and direct support for the State of Israel.

*   **Institutional Affiliations:** Fisher serves on the Board of Trustees at **Stanford University** ([Stanford Profiles](https://profiles.stanford.edu/sakurako-fisher)) and is the former president of the **San Francisco Symphony** ([San Francisco Symphony](https://waitingroom.sfsymphony.org/?c=sfsymphony&e=sitesafetynet&ver=aspnet-4.4.1&cver=26&man=Site%20Safetynet&enqueuetoken=eyJ0eXAiOiJRVDEiLCJlbmMiOiJBRVMyNTYiLCJpc3MiOjE3NzcxMDkwNzU1MTEsImV4cCI6MTc3NzEwOTMxNTUxMSwidGkiOiI3YjdlMGM5YS05MDYwLTQ5ZGEtOTQwYy05NWViNDBjZTcxOWUiLCJjIjoic2ZzeW1waG9ueSIsImUiOiJzaXRlc2FmZXR5bmV0IiwiaXAiOiIyMTYuNjUuMTA1LjI0MiJ9.4O4htdDT8hFmBlCRzuN1TWg5hh3yDCYEnj0vqE9dip8.riEZdevBzzF0IudDGtuT1NT1vLKE-cHZb28F2ayM0YI&t=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfsymphony.org%2FAbout-SFS%2FBoard-of-Governors)).
*   **Philanthropic Vehicle:** Much of her giving is channeled through the **William and Sakurako Fisher Family Foundation** and the **Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation** (the latter established by her in-laws).

### Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Support for Birthright Israel
Sakurako and William Fisher are documented as significant donors to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for Jewish young adults with the explicit mission of "strengthening Jewish identity and connection to the Jewish homeland" (Birthright Israel 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). 
*   In the 2024 "Scroll of Honor," the **Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation** is listed as a donor at the **$200,000–$499,999** level (Birthright Israel Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   The foundation specifically funds the **Hillel’s Israel Fellows** program, which places Israeli emissaries on U.S. college campuses to "respond to anti-Israel rhetoric" and build pro-Israel alliances (Max M. & Marjorie S. Fisher Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

#### 2. Association with the Koret Foundation
Fisher is a documented partner and co-donor with the **Koret Foundation**, one of the most active Zionist philanthropic organizations in the Bay Area. The Koret Foundation’s mission includes "strengthening the US-Israel relationship through bridge-building activities" and funding programs like the **Shalem Jewish Peoplehood Project** in Jerusalem (Koret Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   Fisher and her husband are frequently listed alongside the Koret Foundation as primary funders for major San Francisco institutions, including the **San Francisco Opera** (SF Opera 2023 Program (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)) and **Cal Performances** (Cal Performances Donor List (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   The Koret Foundation has historically used these partnerships to promote "U.S.-Israel bridge-building" initiatives (Wikipedia: Koret Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

#### 3. Institutional Funding at Stanford University
The Fishers have utilized their donor status at Stanford University to establish programs that intersect with Zionist interests and international relations.
*   They established the **Sakurako and William Fisher Family Directorship of the Stanford Global Studies Division** (Stanford News (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   They provide substantial funding to the **Center on Democracy, Development, and the Rule of Law (CDDRL)** and the **Taube Center for Jewish Studies**, both of which maintain active partnerships with Israeli academic and state institutions ([Stanford CDDRL](https://cddrl.fsi.stanford.edu/news/gift-connection-bright-future-lies-ahead-summer-fellows-program-cddrl)).

#### 4. Jewish Community Federation (JCF)
Fisher is a long-standing donor to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund of San Francisco**. The JCF is a central clearinghouse for Zionist philanthropy, directing millions of dollars annually to Israeli NGOs, security initiatives, and pro-Israel advocacy in the United States (Jewish Community Federation 2024 Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### Documentation of Political Contributions
While Sakurako Fisher is primarily identified as a philanthropic donor, her political giving aligns with the broader Fisher family strategy of supporting candidates and PACs that prioritize U.S.-Israel military and diplomatic ties.
*   Federal Election Commission (FEC) records show regular contributions to the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco's PAC** and other pro-Israel donor circles ([FEC.gov Search](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Sakurako+Fisher)).
*   The Fisher family office, **Pisces Inc.**, is documented as a vehicle for political and philanthropic expenditures that support the institutional status quo of the pro-Israel lobby in California (Pisces Foundation Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
