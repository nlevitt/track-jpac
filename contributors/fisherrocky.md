# Contributor Report: Robert "Rocky" Fisher

**Robert Joseph "Rocky" Fisher** (commonly known as Bob Fisher) is a prominent American businessman, heir to the Gap Inc. fortune, and managing director of the Fisher family’s investment firm, **Pisces Inc.** He is the son of Gap Inc. co-founders Donald and Doris Fisher. Fisher has been identified in various research contexts as a significant contributor to Zionist causes and pro-Israel organizations through both personal wealth and family-controlled philanthropic vehicles.

### 1. Organizational Affiliations & Zionist Support
Fisher and his family are documented as major financial pillars for several key Zionist and pro-Israel institutions.

*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family, primarily through the **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund**, is a long-term supporter of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that sponsors educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the state. Robert J. Fisher is explicitly listed as a prominent "Zionist donor" in databases tracking the intersection of corporate wealth and pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Koret Foundation:** While not a board member, Fisher is a central figure in the San Francisco philanthropic network that includes the **Koret Foundation**, a major funder of Israeli security, education, and Zionist identity programs. The Fisher family frequently co-funds large-scale projects with Koret, reinforcing a shared strategic agenda in Jewish and pro-Israel philanthropy.

*   **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco:** The Fisher family has provided multi-generational leadership and funding to the Federation, which serves as a primary conduit for philanthropic capital flowing from the Bay Area to Israel. Robert Fisher's father, Donald, served on its board, and the family’s foundations continue to funnel millions through the Federation's endowment funds.

### 2. Financial Vehicles and Political Advocacy
Fisher manages the family's financial influence through **Pisces Inc.** and several private foundations.

*   **Pisces Inc.:** As the managing director of this family office, Robert Fisher oversees assets that have been used to support political candidates and committees aligned with pro-Israel policy. Pisces Inc. is frequently cited in campaign finance disclosures as a source of contributions to both Democratic and Republican entities that maintain a strong pro-Israel stance.
    *   *Source:* [InfluenceWatch: Pisces Foundation Profile](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/pisces-foundation/) (Overview of the Fisher family's philanthropic and political vehicle).
    *   *Source:* FEC Disclosure: Democrats for Israel Committee / Pisces Inc. (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Federal record of financial transactions involving Pisces Inc. and pro-Israel political interests).

*   **Doris & Donald Fisher Fund:** This private foundation is a primary vehicle for the family’s "Zionist giving," providing sustained support to the **KIPP Foundation** (charter schools) and various Jewish communal organizations that promote Zionist identity and Israeli state interests.
    *   *Source:* [Forbes: Robert Fisher Profile](https://www.forbes.com/profile/robert-fisher/) (Summary of wealth and philanthropic history).

### 3. Summary of Documented Relationship to Zionism
Robert "Rocky" Fisher’s relationship to Zionist advocacy is characterized by substantial, institutionalized financial support. He is part of a "power elite" within the San Francisco donor class that integrates corporate leadership (Gap Inc.) with the funding of organizations like **Birthright Israel** and the **Koret Foundation**, which are dedicated to the ideological and material support of the State of Israel.
