# California Contributor Report: Steven C. Mitzner

### Professional Background and California Context
Steven C. Mitzner serves as a Senior Vice President at **RIDA Development Corporation**, a multi-national real estate development firm. He is primarily involved in managing financial relationships, asset management, and corporate strategy for the company's family office. Mitzner is a key executive for RIDA’s major California-based projects, most notably the **Gaylord Pacific Resort and Convention Center** in Chula Vista, a project valued at approximately $1.35 billion. His professional standing as a major stakeholder in California’s large-scale real estate development has facilitated his identification as a significant political and philanthropic contributor within the state.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
Mitzner’s involvement in Zionist advocacy is documented through both large-scale institutional funding and leadership roles in organizations dedicated to Israeli interests.

#### 1. Funding of Israel Advocacy at Yeshiva University
In 2024, the Mitzner family, including Steven C. Mitzner, provided a **$10 million gift** to establish the **Lieberman-Mitzner Center for Public Service and Advocacy** at Yeshiva University. A primary objective of this center is to train the "next generation of political leaders" and "advocates for... Israel." The center’s curriculum specifically includes "special programming for Israel advocacy," aimed at developing a cadre of public servants committed to pro-Israel political action.

#### 2. Support for the Israel Defense Forces (IDF)
Mitzner has been identified as a major donor to the **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)**. This organization is dedicated to providing "educational, cultural, and social services" to active-duty soldiers of the Israeli military. The Mitzner family’s philanthropic infrastructure, particularly through their South Texas and multi-state real estate interests, consistently lists Steven C. Mitzner among high-level supporters of FIDF campaigns.

#### 3. Leadership in Pro-Israel Communal Organizations
Mitzner has held multiple leadership positions within Jewish communal organizations that prioritize the "centrality of Israel" and Zionist education. He previously served as the Chair of the **Young Leadership Philanthropy Initiative** at the Jewish Federation of Greater Houston and has been a member of the Executive Board of the **Robert M. Beren Academy**, an institution that emphasizes Zionist values in its educational mission.

### Political Influence and Contributions
As a representative of RIDA Development, Mitzner is part of a family office that exerts significant economic influence in California through massive capital investments. His financial support of pro-Israel institutions like Yeshiva University’s advocacy center and FIDF aligns his philanthropic profile with broader political efforts to maintain and strengthen U.S. support for Israel.
