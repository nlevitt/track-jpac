This report examines the documented relationship between the **Fisher family** of San Francisco—founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.**—and their philanthropic and political support for Israel and Zionist advocacy. While "Alida Fisher" is a contemporary San Francisco political figure (Board of Education), she is not a founder of Gap Inc. or Pisces Inc.; the donor activity described in the context is associated with the **Donald and Doris Fisher family** and their sons **Robert, William, and John Fisher**.

# Contributor Report: Fisher Family (San Francisco)

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

The Fisher family, primarily through the wealth generated by **Gap Inc.** and managed via **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment office), are long-standing contributors to Jewish communal and Zionist institutions. Their advocacy is characterized by substantial financial support for educational "identity-building" programs and major Jewish federations.

### 1. Relationship to Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a major financial supporter of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the stated goal of strengthening their connection to the state.
*   **Funding Level:** The family has been recognized on the Birthright Israel Foundation "Scroll of Honor" for contributions at the $100,000 to $499,000 level and above through various family foundations.
*   **Organizational Ties:** **David Fisher** (related branch) served as the President of the Birthright Israel Foundation for over four years, overseeing the raising of $400 million during his tenure.
*   **Source:** Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); Atlanta Jewish Times - Fisher Leaves Birthright (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Relationship to the Koret Foundation
The Fishers have been significant donors and partners of the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic organization that is one of the largest funders of pro-Israel advocacy and development projects in the Bay Area.
*   **Philanthropic Partnership:** The Fisher family often co-funds initiatives with Koret, particularly those related to Jewish continuity and San Francisco’s Jewish communal infrastructure, such as the Jewish Community Center of San Francisco (JCCSF).
*   **Advocacy Orientation:** The Koret Foundation is noted for its support of "Israel Studies" and its collaboration with the **AIPAC**-affiliated American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF).
*   **Source:** [J. Weekly - Gap founder Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/); Koret Foundation - About Our Grants (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Pisces Inc. and Financial Infrastructure
**Pisces Inc.** serves as the family office for the Fishers, managing the assets derived from Gap Inc. and facilitating their political and philanthropic output.
*   **Political Contributions:** **John J. Fisher** (Managing Director of Pisces Inc.) is a prominent donor to Republican causes and pro-Israel political interests. In the 2018 cycle alone, he was the top individual contributor to funds supporting GOP leadership, which traditionally aligns with hawkish pro-Israel policy.
*   **Corporate Links:** Pisces Inc. and the Doris & Donald Fisher Fund are the primary vehicles used to channel millions into "dark money" groups and educational reform, often intersecting with Zionist networks in the Bay Area.
*   **Source:** [Fox Business - Gap founder's son lavishes funds on GOP](https://www.foxbusiness.com/politics/as-oakland-as-struggle-to-compete-gap-founders-son-lavishes-funds-on-gop); Capital & Main - Doris Fisher: Down the Dark Money Rabbit Hole (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 4. Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund
The Fishers are "charter funders" and long-term supporters of the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (S.F.-based)**. 
*   **Advocacy:** The Federation is a central node for Zionist advocacy in Northern California, allocating funds to the **Israel Action Network** and programs designed to counter the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
*   **Direct Support:** Donald and Doris Fisher contributed over $1 million to the capital campaign of the JCCSF, ensuring their name is permanently associated with the institution (Fisher Family Hall).
*   **Source:** Wikipedia - Donald Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); [J. Weekly - Donald Fisher Obituary](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

### 5. Identification Clarification (Alida Fisher)
While the user identified "Fisher, Alida" as a "Prominent Zionist donor," public records identify **Alida Fisher** as a Commissioner on the San Francisco Board of Education. She is a progressive activist who has supported the retention of **Ethnic Studies** curricula—a program frequently challenged by Zionist advocacy groups like the JCRC. There is no documented evidence linking Commissioner Alida Fisher to the billionaire-level Zionist philanthropy of the Gap/Pisces founding family.
*   **Source:** [Ballotpedia - Alida Fisher](https://ballotpedia.org/Alida_Fisher); [San Francisco Berniecrats Endorsements](https://sfberniecrats.com/blog-posts/)
