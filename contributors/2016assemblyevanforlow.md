# Contributor Report: Evan Low for Assembly 2016

This report documents the relationship between the political entity 'Evan Low for Assembly 2016'—the campaign committee for California State Assemblymember Evan Low—and advocacy for the State of Israel and Zionist causes. Evan Low has served in the California State Legislature since 2014 and has been a member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, where he has supported pro-Israel policy initiatives and economic partnerships.

### 1. Legislative Support for Anti-BDS Initiatives
Evan Low supported the passage of **AB 2844** (the California Combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions of Israel Act) in 2016. This legislation, a priority for the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, requires state contractors to certify they are not engaged in boycotts of "a sovereign nation" (specifically targeting the BDS movement against Israel).
*   **Documentation:** The legislation was identified as a "Legislative Jewish Caucus priority bill" targeting the BDS movement during Low's 2016 term ([Sacramento Bee](https://www.sacbee.com/news/politics-government/capitol-alert/article100344557.html)).
*   **Legislative Context:** AB 2844 prohibits companies seeking state contracts from violating nondiscrimination laws through their policies toward Israel ([California Legislative Information](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)).

### 2. California-Israel Economic Partnerships
Through his leadership in the California Legislative Tech Caucus, Low has promoted economic and technological collaboration between California and Israel.
*   **Economic Strategy:** Low's Tech Caucus foundation was involved in efforts to foster economic ties following a 2015 agreement between California and Israel focused on academic research, cybersecurity, and water management ([CalMatters](https://calmatters.org/politics/2020/02/california-legislators-nonprofits-secret-donors/)).
*   **Strategic Collaboration:** He has advocated for leveraging Silicon Valley's technological infrastructure to strengthen the "transnational security corridor" shared with Israel's innovation sector ([California-Israel Economic Impact Report 2025](https://icanaction.org/statement-on-the-11th-anniversary-of-the-signing-of-the-california-israel-strategic-partnership/)).

### 3. Public Advocacy and Solidarity Statements
Low has issued formal statements of support for Israel and has participated in lobbying efforts coordinated by Zionist and Jewish advocacy organizations.
*   **Statements of Solidarity:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Low stated that the California Asian American Pacific Islander (AAPI) Legislative Caucus stands in "unwavering solidarity with Israel" and noted they "always have [and] always will" ([Lost Coast Outpost](https://lostcoastoutpost.com/2023/nov/22/for-california-leaders-calls-for-ceasefire-in-gaz/)).
*   **Community Advocacy:** Low has been a regular participant in the annual **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC)** summit, where he spoke on the "allyship" between the Jewish and AAPI communities regarding shared political values ([J. Weekly](https://www.jweekly.com/2023/05/12/more-than-300-jews-lobby-state-legislators-at-annual-jpac-summit/)).
*   **Symbolic Support:** During his 2024 political activities, Low publicly wore a yellow wristband in support of hostages held in Gaza and criticized pro-Palestinian protesters at the California State Capitol, characterizing their actions as "vile hate" ([J. Weekly](https://www.jweekly.com/2024/11/14/state-of-the-jews-election-fallout-and-community-resilience/)).

### 4. Organizational Affiliations
Low's relationship to Israel is formally integrated into his caucus memberships and organizational alliances.
*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** Low is a documented member of this caucus, which serves as the primary legislative vehicle for pro-Israel and Jewish community advocacy in Sacramento ([JPAC California](https://jpac-california.org/who-we-are/jewish-caucus/)).
*   **Diplomatic Stance:** Low has publicly supported a "two-state solution" as a means to ensure the "prosperity and national sovereignty" of the State of Israel alongside a Palestinian state ([San José Spotlight](https://sanjosespotlight.com/on-the-record-congressional-candidate-evan-low/)).
