# Contributor Report: Marc Berman for Assembly 2024

Marc Berman for Assembly 2024 is the campaign committee for Marc Berman, a Democratic member of the California State Assembly representing the 23rd District (formerly the 24th District). Berman has served in the Assembly since 2016 and is a prominent member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)**, where he participates in legislative and advocacy efforts focused on Zionism and the State of Israel.

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and Support for Zionism
Berman has been a consistent participant in the California Legislative Jewish Caucus’s efforts to pass legislation that supports Israeli interests and restricts anti-Zionist activity within state institutions.

*   **Anti-BDS Legislation:** Berman is an active member of the caucus that introduced and successfully advocated for **AB 2844** in 2016. This law requires companies entering into large state contracts to certify they are not violating civil rights laws through the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement against Israel.
    *   Source: [California Legislative Jewish Caucus Official Site - AB 2844 History](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2016-10-04-governor-signs-anti-bds-bill)
*   **IHRA Definition of Antisemitism:** Berman supports the caucus's successful effort to incorporate the **International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA)** definition of antisemitism into California’s K-12 Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum (ESMC). This definition has been criticized for conflating criticism of the State of Israel with antisemitism.
    *   Source: [Wikipedia - California Legislative Jewish Caucus Legislative Record](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#2017-2019:_JPAC_partnership,_increased_allocations,_ethnic_studies_guardrails)
*   **Ethnic Studies "Guardrails":** Berman has endorsed legislative "guardrails" designed to prevent "anti-Israel content" from being taught in California schools. In 2024, he was part of the caucus pushing **AB 2918**, which sought to strengthen disclosure requirements to ensure "anti-Zionist content" is excluded from ethnic studies.
    *   Source: [J. Weekly - State Bills Focusing on Antisemitism and Holocaust Education](https://jweekly.com/2024/09/20/state-bills-focusing-on-antisemitism-and-holocaust-education-training-await-newsoms-signature/)
    *   Source: [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - 2024 Priority Bill Package](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2024-09-20-state-bills-focusing-antisemitism-and-holocaust-education-training-await-newsoms)

### 2. Delegations to Israel and Institutional Ties
Berman has participated in organized trips to Israel facilitated by pro-Israel advocacy organizations. These trips are designed to foster bilateral collaboration and "intensive dialogue" with Israeli officials.

*   **AJC Project Interchange Delegation:** In July 2018, Berman participated in a delegation to Israel sponsored by **Project Interchange**, an educational institute of the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**. The trip focused on "bilateral collaboration" and exposing California legislators to Israel’s strategic environment.
    *   Source: [CA.gov - Bipartisan Delegation of California State Legislators Visit Israel](https://www.gov.ca.gov/2018/07/24/bipartisan-delegation-of-california-state-legislators-visit-israel-explore-bilateral-collaboration-opportunities/)
*   **Caucus Partnerships:** Through the CLJC, Berman maintains a close partnership with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)**, the largest statewide pro-Israel advocacy network.
    *   Source: [LA Progressive - Analysis of California Legislative Jewish Caucus Ties](https://www.laprogressive.com/racism/ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)

### 3. Public Stances and Statements
Following the events of October 7, 2023, Berman has used his platform to advocate for Israeli security and condemn anti-Zionist protests.

*   **Condemnation of Anti-Zionist Protests:** Berman publicly denounced protesters who shut down the 2023 California Democratic Party Convention, stating, "I will never accept groups that storm through security chanting for the elimination of Israel." He characterizes chants such as "from the river to the sea" as calls for the annihilation of Israel.
    *   Source: [World Socialist Web Site - California Democratic Convention Interruptions](https://www.wsws.org/en/articles/2023/11/20/itdy-n20.html)
*   **2024 Solidarity Letter:** On January 3, 2024, Berman co-signed an official letter from the Jewish Caucus to colleagues, which defended the state of Israel's "right to exist" and criticized "far-left ideologues" for creating a hostile environment for Zionist students and professors.
    *   Source: [Official Letter - California Legislative Jewish Caucus January 3, 2024](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/sites/jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/files/Letter%20to%20Colleagues%20-%20Jewish%20Caucus%20-%20Jan%203%202024.pdf)

### 4. Campaign Contributions and Endorsements
The campaign committee 'Marc Berman for Assembly 2024' has received financial support from various corporate and political entities, including those with documented pro-Israel interests.

*   **Financial Support:** Berman’s campaign has received contributions from corporations like **Visa** ($3,000 in 2024) and **Uber** ($5,500 in 2023), as well as being listed as a "legislative partner" by organizations that frequently lobby for pro-Israel causes in Sacramento.
    *   Source: [Visa 2024 Political Engagement Report](https://usa.visa.com/content/dam/VCOM/download/about-visa/visa-2024-political-engagement-report.pdf)
    *   Source: [Transparency USA - Marc Berman Campaign Finance Summary](https://www.transparencyusa.org/ca/candidate/marc-berman)
*   **Political Alliances:** Berman has endorsed and been endorsed by other pro-Israel candidates and groups, including those supported by the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**.
    *   Source: [Ballotpedia - Marc Berman 2024 Endorsements and Allies](https://ballotpedia.org/Marc_Berman)
