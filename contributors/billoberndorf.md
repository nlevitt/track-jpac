# Contributor Report: Oberndorf, Bill

**Bill Oberndorf** (William Ernst Oberndorf) is an American billionaire investor and Republican political donor. He co-founded the investment firm **SPO Partners** and currently serves as the chairman of the **American Federation for Children**, an organization focused on school choice and educational privatization. Oberndorf is identified as a major financial supporter of pro-Israel organizations and Zionist advocacy, though his historical donation record includes support for diplomacy-focused initiatives such as the Iran Nuclear Deal.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

Oberndorf is frequently identified by investigative and advocacy organizations as a key financier within the pro-Israel donor network.

*   **Identification as a Major Pro-Israel Donor:** Oberndorf is listed alongside prominent pro-Israel megadonors (such as Paul Singer and Haim Saban) in reports detailing the funding of candidates who support unconditional military aid to Israel. For instance, investigative reports have noted his inclusion in the donor class that funds groups like **AIPAC** and **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)** to boost political candidates like Senator Cory Booker.
    *   **Source:** [NJToday.news - Booker bats for billionaires, but betrays bottom 80 percent of Americans](https://njtoday.news/2023/12/17/booker-bats-for-billionaires-but-betrays-bottom-80-percent-of-americans/)
*   **Support for AIPAC-Linked Super PACs:** Oberndorf has been linked to the **United Democracy Project (UDP)**, a Super PAC established by AIPAC. His name appears in donor networks associated with UDP's efforts to influence Democratic primaries in favor of pro-Israel candidates.
    *   **Source:** [GRIID.org - DeVos Family Reader (Section on United Democracy Project)](https://griid.org/2024/05/19/devos-family-reader/)
*   **Jewish Community Philanthropy:** Bill and Susan Oberndorf (along with Peter and Yael Oberndorf) are listed as contributors to the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**. In 2024, the Federation reported mobilizing over $22 million in response to the October 7 attacks to support security and humanitarian needs in Israel, as well as combating antisemitism and supporting Hillels on American campuses.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Federation Bay Area - 2024 Annual Report](https://jewishfed.org/news/publications/2024-annual-report)

### 2. Local Political Influence and Zionist Labeling

In San Francisco, Oberndorf’s political contributions to "moderate" and conservative causes have been characterized by critics as part of a "Zionist project" due to the candidates and policies he supports.

*   **Prosecution of Gaza Protesters:** Critics and labor organizations have identified Oberndorf as a "Zionist billionaire" whose funding supported the appointment and campaign of San Francisco District Attorney **Brooke Jenkins**. Jenkins has faced criticism for filing felony charges against "Golden Gate 26" protesters who blocked the Golden Gate Bridge in an April 2024 action calling for a ceasefire in Gaza.
    *   **Source:** [Labor for Palestine - SF DA Brooke Jenkins, Drop The Charges!](https://laborforpalestine.net/2024/08/12/sf-da-brooke-jenkins-drop-the-charges-demand-palestine-supporters-of-the-golden-gate-26-laborvideo/)
*   **Funding Pro-Israel Candidates:** Through his leadership in the PAC **Neighbors for a Better San Francisco**, Oberndorf has supported local candidates with ties to pro-Israel lobbying, such as **Trevor Chandler**, a former lobbyist for AIPAC.
    *   **Source:** [48 Hills - Campaign notebook: The dizzying web of big-money influence](https://48hills.org/2024/02/campaign-notebook-the-dizzying-web-of-big-money-influence/)

### 3. Historical Nuance: The Iran Nuclear Deal

While identified as a pro-Israel donor, Oberndorf's past financial activity includes support for the **Ploughshares Fund**, an organization that was a primary advocate for the **Iran Nuclear Deal (JCPOA)**. This position was notably at odds with the stance of AIPAC and other mainstream Zionist organizations at the time.

*   **Largest Donor to Ploughshares:** In 2015, the **Susan and Bill Oberndorf Foundation** was identified as the largest donor to the Ploughshares Fund, contributing over $100,000 to support diplomacy-based efforts regarding Iran’s nuclear program.
    *   **Source:** [VDOC.PUB - The Iran Nuclear Deal: Bombs, Bureaucrats, And Billionaires](https://vdoc.pub/documents/the-iran-nuclear-deal-bombs-bureaucrats-and-billionaires-7m51f5068lq0)
    *   **Source:** [Iranian Review of Foreign Affairs - Pro-Iran-Deal Actors and US foreign policy](https://www.csr.ir/files/en/news/2022/1/31/5301_938.pdf)
