# Contributor Report: Michael Moradzadeh

### Profile Summary
Michael Moradzadeh is the Founding Partner and CEO of **Rimon, PC** (Rimon Law), a global law firm with an office in Tel Aviv and a significant practice focused on the Israeli technology sector. Moradzadeh is a prominent figure within the Iranian-American Jewish community in Northern California and has been identified as a founder of the **Iranian American Jewish Federation (IAJF)** (specifically associated with young leadership or regional chapters that promote U.S.-Israel relations). His documented activities include leadership roles in Israeli emergency services organizations and public advocacy for the Israeli state.

---

### 1. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### Support for Israeli National Defense and Self-Defense Advocacy
In October 2023, Michael Moradzadeh was a signatory to a public statement issued by leading global attorneys condemning the October 7 attacks and affirming "Israel's undeniable right and obligation to pursue justice" and "defend itself against further aggression." The statement explicitly rejected "moral equivalence" between Hamas and the Israeli military and called on the legal profession to denounce "terrorism against Israel."
*   **Source:** [iPetitions: Leading Lawyers Statement](https://www.ipetitions.com/petition/leading-lawyers-statement-condemning-terrorism)
*   **Source:** [BusinessWire: Leading Partners and Attorneys Condemn Terrorism in Israel](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20231012674431/en/Leading-Partners-and-Attorneys-from-Prominent-Law-Firms-Around-the-World-Today-Published-the-Following-Statement-Condemning-the-Terrorism-in-Israel-and-Supporting-Israel%E2%80%99s-Right-to-Self-Defense)

#### Leadership in Israeli Emergency and Medical Services
Moradzadeh and his wife, Nomi Deutch, serve on the **International Board** of **United Hatzalah of Israel** (specifically the Northern California Regional Board). United Hatzalah is a Jerusalem-based non-profit volunteer emergency medical services organization that operates throughout Israel.
*   **Source:** [United Hatzalah: International Board Listing](https://israelrescue.org/international-board/)

#### Institutional Presence in Israel via Rimon Law
As the founder and CEO of Rimon Law, Moradzadeh has oversaw the establishment of the firm’s office in **Tel Aviv**. The firm explicitly markets its "expertise in Israel," highlighting its work assisting Israeli companies with IPOs and cross-border transactions. Moradzadeh himself is listed as one of the firm's primary attorneys with "expertise in Israel."
*   **Source:** [Rimon Law: Israel Practice and Team](https://rimonlaw.com/expertise/israel/)
*   **Source:** [Rimon Law: Michael Moradzadeh Profile](https://rimonlaw.com/team/michael-moradzadeh/)

#### Community and Educational Leadership
Moradzadeh holds a board position at the **Wornick Jewish Day School** in Foster City, CA. The school’s curriculum and mission include a significant focus on "care for Israel" and "living a vibrant Jewish life," particularly emphasized in the wake of the 2023-2024 conflict. He is also a documented donor to **Chabad of the North Peninsula**, an organization that supports Jewish life and Zionist-aligned values in the Bay Area.
*   **Source:** [Wornick Jewish Day School: 2023/2024 Annual Impact Report (Page 21)](https://issuu.com/wornickjds/docs/wornick_impact_report_23_24_final)
*   **Source:** [Chabad of the North Peninsula: Partner Recognition](https://www.chabadnp.com/templates/articlecco_cdo/aid/5341215/jewish/Our-Partners.htm)

#### Personal and Educational Ties
Moradzadeh's professional background includes studies at **Ben-Gurion University of the Negev** in Israel. His name, "Rimon," is the Hebrew word for pomegranate, a traditional Jewish and Israeli symbol chosen specifically for the firm's branding.
*   **Source:** [JWeekly: Profile of Michael Moradzadeh and Nomi Deutch](https://jweekly.com/2010/12/10/three-rabbis-two-lovebirds-and-now-one-baby-girl/)
*   **Source:** [Rimon Law: Official Biography](https://rimonlaw.com/team/michael-moradzadeh/)
