# Contributor Report: Kraft, Joshua

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Philanthropic Focus
Joshua Kraft serves as the President of **Kraft Family Philanthropies**, the philanthropic arm of the Kraft Group. The organization’s stated mission includes "strengthening the State of Israel" and building connections between the United States and Israel through various programs and investments. Under Joshua Kraft's leadership, the foundation manages several entities, including the **Foundation to Combat Antisemitism (FCAS)**, which explicitly works to counter "attempts to delegitimize the State of Israel" and the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.

### 2. Financial Support for Zionist and Pro-Israel Organizations
Through Kraft Family Philanthropies and the Kraft Family Foundation, Joshua Kraft oversees substantial financial transfers to organizations that advocate for Israel or support its military and institutions. Documented recipients include:

*   **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF):** Contributions totaling $161,000 between FY11 and FY17.
*   **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC):** The Kraft Group LLC, owned by the family, donated $1 million to AIPAC’s super PAC, the **United Democracy Project**, in 2022.
*   **MassChallenge Israel:** The family has invested over $1.2 million to expand this program in Jerusalem, aimed at integrating Israel’s "Startup Nation" into global markets.
*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** Joshua Kraft personally donated $150,000 to this organization in 2021.

### 3. Advocacy and Missions
Joshua Kraft is active in the family's "Touchdown in Israel" missions, which facilitate trips for NFL players, coaches, and local political leaders to Israel. These missions are designed to foster "strong connections to Israel" and have been characterized by critics as public relations efforts to normalize the Israeli state’s policies through sports diplomacy. Kraft has also identified himself as a "supporter of Israel" in public interviews, asserting that a "strong Israel is good for the United States."

### 4. Political Contributions to Pro-Israel Candidates
Joshua Kraft has a history of donating to political candidates who align with pro-Israel advocacy. In 2020, he donated $2,800 to **Antone Melton-Meaux**, a primary challenger to U.S. Representative Ilhan Omar; Melton-Meaux's campaign was notable for receiving millions of dollars from pro-Israel groups due to Omar's criticism of Israel.
