# Contributor Report: Google Client Services LLC (Mark Isakowitz)

### **Contributor Overview**
**Google Client Services LLC** is a corporate entity utilized by Google (Alphabet Inc.) for government affairs, lobbying, and political contributions. **Mark Isakowitz** served as Google’s Vice President of Government Affairs and Public Policy for the U.S. and Canada from 2019 until December 2024, acting as the company's "top lobbyist" in Washington, D.C. In late 2024, Isakowitz transitioned to become the Chief of Staff for U.S. Senator-elect David McCormick (R-PA).

*   **Professional Profile:** [American Jewish Committee (AJC) - Mark Isakowitz Bio](https://www.ajc.org/news/mark-isakowitz)
*   **Departure from Google:** [Bloomberg Law: Google's Top Lobbyist Departs](https://news.bloomberglaw.com/tech-and-telecom-law/googles-top-lobbyist-departs-amid-continued-regulatory-scrutiny)

### **Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

#### **1. Professional Background with AIPAC and AJC**
Mark Isakowitz has a documented history of involvement with prominent pro-Israel advocacy organizations. He is frequently identified as a former lobbyist for the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**, a leading organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship. Furthermore, Isakowitz is a prominent member and speaker for the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**, an international advocacy organization that supports Israel’s security and Jewish peoplehood.

*   **AJC Advocacy:** [AJC Global Forum Speaker Listing](https://www.ajc.org/news/mark-isakowitz)
*   **Context of Support:** [Jewish Insider: Move to McCormick’s Office Linked to Israel Support](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/12/mark-isakowitz-dave-mccormick-chief-of-staff/)

#### **2. Participation in the Herzliya Conference**
Isakowitz has been a documented participant and benefactor of the **Herzliya Conference**, Israel's premier global policy gathering focused on national security and the "Balance of Israel’s National Security." He is listed as a "Benefactor" in official conference proceedings, alongside major Israeli and international pro-Israel donors and leaders.

*   **Conference Documentation:** [Herzliya Conference 2009 Proceedings (Page 66)](https://www.runi.ac.il/media/5wupz1i4/herzliyaconference2009.pdf)

#### **3. Public Statements on Israel and Zionism**
Isakowitz has explicitly cited a candidate's commitment to Israel as a deciding factor in his professional career choices. Upon joining Senator-elect Dave McCormick’s staff, Isakowitz stated that the Senator’s "commitment to Israel and defending the Jewish people at a perilous time" was a primary motivation for his acceptance of the role. He has also expressed deep commitment to fighting "anti-Zionism" and antisemitism.

*   **Direct Quote on Israel Commitment:** [Times of Israel: Implementing Vision - Isakowitz Interview](https://www.timesofisrael.com/pittsburgh-jewish-chronicle/implementing-vision/)
*   **Commitment to Pro-Israel Leadership:** [Jewish Insider: Daily Kickoff - Isakowitz on McCormick](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/12/mark-isakowitz-dave-mccormick-chief-of-staff/)

#### **4. Lobbying Firm Context**
Prior to joining Google, Isakowitz founded and managed the lobbying firm **Fierce, Isakowitz & Blalock**. The firm is noted in transparency records for representing corporate and advocacy interests in Washington. In the context of pro-Israel advocacy, Isakowitz’s firm has been frequently categorized alongside "hired gun" firms that navigate foreign policy and trade issues relevant to the U.S.-Israel strategic alliance.

*   **Lobbying Disclosure (Mark Isakowitz as President):** [U.S. Senate Lobbying Disclosure Form](https://lda.senate.gov/filings/public/filing/42152864-4286-4f40-848e-28c081e7d2e8/print/)
