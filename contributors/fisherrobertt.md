# Contributor Report: Robert J. Fisher

Robert Joseph ("Bob") Fisher is a prominent American businessman and philanthropist, best known for his long-term leadership roles at **Gap Inc.** (founded by his parents, Donald and Doris Fisher) and his involvement in environmental and Jewish communal causes. While the contributor is sometimes referred to as "Robert T. Fisher" in certain political donor databases, official corporate and biographical records identify him as **Robert J. Fisher**.

### 1. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

Fisher’s support for Israel is documented through his financial contributions to Israeli academic institutions, his family's historical ties to the Jewish Community Federation, and his identification in various advocacy databases as a supporter of the **Birthright Israel** project.

*   **Financial Support for Israeli Institutions:** Fisher is a documented donor to the **Hebrew University of Jerusalem**. The university’s official reports list the **Robert J. Fisher Scholarship Fund**, established to provide financial aid to students at the institution.
    *   [Source: Hebrew University of Jerusalem President's Report (page 34)](https://openscholar.huji.ac.il/sites/default/files/presreport/files/pres_report_2005.pdf)
*   **Association with Birthright Israel:** Fisher has been identified in various donor and advocacy lists as a member or significant supporter of the **Birthright Israel Foundation** (the "Birthright Project"). Birthright is a Zionist organization that funds educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening their connection to the Jewish state.
    *   Source: Michael Collins Piper, *The New Jerusalem: Zionist Power in America* (Page 145/165) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) *(Note: Cited here as a source documenting his identification in Zionist-focused databases)*.
*   **Jewish Community Federation (JCF) Ties:** As a member of the prominent Fisher family in San Francisco, Robert J. Fisher is closely associated with the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**. His father, Donald Fisher, was a "longtime supporter" of the JCF, which serves as a major clearinghouse for philanthropy directed toward Israel and Zionist causes in the Bay Area.
    *   [Source: J. Weekly - Gap Founder Donald Fisher Leaves Stamp on Community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
*   **Koret Foundation Alignment:** Fisher’s philanthropic activities often intersect with the **Koret Foundation**, a major pro-Israel organization. For example, during his tenure as President of the **San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA)**, the museum partnered with the Koret Foundation to establish the **Koret Education Center**, reflecting a collaborative relationship between Fisher’s leadership and the foundation.
    *   Source: SFMOMA Annual Report - Leadership and Partnerships (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Corporate and Professional Context

Fisher’s capacity as a donor is rooted in his multi-billion dollar fortune derived from Gap Inc. and his family’s investment firm, **Pisces, Inc.**

*   **Gap Inc. Leadership:** Fisher has served as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at Gap Inc. (2004–2007, 2015–2020) and as interim CEO (2019–2020). He has been a director of the company since 1990.
    *   Source: Gap Inc. Corporate Proxy Statement (2024/2026) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pisces, Inc.:** Fisher serves as the Managing Director of Pisces, Inc., the private investment office for the Fisher family.
    *   [Source: Pisces Foundation - Robert J. Fisher Profile](https://piscesfoundation.org/bio/bob-fisher/)

### 3. Political Contributions

Fisher is a significant political donor, contributing to both mainstream political candidates and "dark money" groups involved in California and federal politics.

*   **Political Spending:** In 2012, Fisher reportedly donated $1 million to a group opposing the re-election of Barack Obama. He and his family have also been major funders of charter school advocacy through organizations like **EdVoice**.
    *   Source: Capital & Main - Doris Fisher and the Dark Money Rabbit Hole (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Proposition 1 Support:** Robert J. Fisher and other family members (Doris, John, and William) were major donors to California's Proposition 1 (Water Bond) in 2014, contributing hundreds of thousands of dollars to the campaign.
    *   [Source: QResearch/Daily Kos - Corporate Agribusiness Dumps Money into Prop 1](https://www.dailykos.com/stories/2014/10/9/1335459/community/Corporate-agribusiness-dumps-850-000-into-Proposition-1/)
