This research report outlines the documented relationship between California Assemblymember (and U.S. Representative-elect) Laura Friedman and her advocacy for Israel and Zionist organizations.

# Contributor Report: Laura Friedman for Assembly 2022

## Executive Summary
Laura Friedman served as a member of the California State Assembly from 2016 to 2024 (representing the 43rd and 44th districts) and was elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2024. Throughout her tenure in the California Legislature, she maintained a public identity as a "progressive, pro-Israel legislator" and was a member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus. Her political activity is characterized by legislative support for the State of Israel, endorsements from major Zionist advocacy groups, and participation in state-sponsored delegations to Israel.

## Legislative Record and Zionist Advocacy
Friedman’s legislative record includes several actions specifically focused on maintaining and reaffirming California’s ties to the State of Israel.

*   **ACR 154 (2022):** Friedman co-authored Assembly Concurrent Resolution 154 in 2022. This resolution was specifically designed to reaffirm the state of California’s friendship with Israel and acknowledge the cultural and economic ties between the two entities.
    *   *Source:* [California Legislative Information - ACR 154 (2021-2022)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billHistoryClient.xhtml?bill_id=202120220ACR154)
*   **Anti-Antisemitism and DEI Legislation:** In her final term in the Statehouse, Friedman authored and successfully passed legislation requiring university Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) programs to include education on antisemitism and the "Jewish experience," specifically framing Jewish history in connection to Israel as a central narrative.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider - Friedman vows to continue pro-Israel legacy](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/laura-friedman-adam-schiff-california-israel-progressive/)
*   **Jewish Caucus Membership:** Friedman was an active member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, which advocates for policies supporting Israel’s security and combats the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement at the state level.
    *   *Source:* [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Member Profile](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/members)

## Campaign Endorsements and Financial Backing
Friedman’s 2022 and subsequent campaigns received significant endorsements and financial backing from organizations dedicated to pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Democrats for Israel Los Angeles (DFI-LA):** For her 2022 re-election campaign to the Assembly, Friedman received a "Strong Support" rating and endorsement from DFI-LA, an organization that evaluates candidates based on their support for the State of Israel and opposition to anti-Zionist platforms.
    *   *Source:* [DFI-LA 2022 Primary Candidate Endorsements](https://dfi-la.org/DFI-LA-2022-Primary-Candidates-Endorsements)
*   **Jewish Democratic Council of America (JDCA):** Friedman has been consistently endorsed by the JDCA, which supports candidates who align with pro-Israel foreign policy and maintain strong ties to Zionist institutions.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Dems - Endorsed Candidates](https://jewishdems.org/endorsed-candidates/)
*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** She was endorsed by the DMFI PAC, a group formed specifically to support pro-Israel Democrats and counter anti-Zionist influence within the party.
    *   *Source:* [DMFI PAC - 2024 Endorsement Slate](https://dmfipac.org/dmfi-pac-endorses-65-house-democrats/)
*   **AIPAC Support:** Following her transition to federal politics in 2024, Friedman was identified as an "AIPAC-backed" candidate, receiving significant support from the American Israel Public Affairs Committee to succeed Adam Schiff.
    *   *Source:* [The Jerusalem Post - AIPAC-backed candidates win big](https://www.jpost.com/us-elections/article-828005)

## Public Positions and Institutional Ties
Friedman has documented her personal and professional commitment to Zionist advocacy through travel and public statements.

*   **2019 Israel Delegation:** Friedman visited Israel for the first time in 2019 as part of a formal delegation of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus. This trip focused on security, water technology cooperation, and strengthening the "California-Israel partnership."
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Virtual Library - Laura Friedman Biography](https://www.jewishvirtuallibrary.org/laura-friedman)
*   **Defense of Israel’s Military Actions:** In public forums, Friedman has consistently affirmed Israel’s "right to defend itself" and has been critical of progressive activists who characterize Israel’s actions in Gaza as genocide, instead calling for "nuanced" support of the state.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider - Friedman highlights nuanced conversations on Israel](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/laura-friedman-adam-schiff-california-israel-progressive/)
