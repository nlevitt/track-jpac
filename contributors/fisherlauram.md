This report documents the philanthropic and political activities of Laura M. Fisher (also known as Laura Meier Fisher), a prominent California-based donor. Her relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is established through her role within the Fisher family’s investment and philanthropic framework, specifically **Pisces Inc.** and the **Fisher Family Foundation**, and her documented support for core Zionist institutions such as the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

# Contributor Report: Fisher, Laura M.

### Overview and Identifiers
Laura M. Fisher is a billionaire philanthropist based in San Francisco, California. She is the wife of **John J. Fisher**, who is the son of Gap Inc. founders Donald and Doris Fisher and the Executive Vice Chairman of **Pisces Inc.**, the Fisher family's private investment firm and family office. Laura M. Fisher is frequently identified as a key principal in the family’s wealth management and philanthropic distribution, often donating jointly with her husband or through entities associated with the Gap Inc. fortune.

*   **Principal Affiliations:** Pisces Inc., Gap Inc. (family-founded), Fisher Family Foundation.
*   **Identification:** Documented as a "prominent Zionist donor" due to high-level financial commitments to organizations that facilitate Jewish identity-building in relation to the State of Israel and provide direct support to Israeli institutions.

Fisher is documented as a significant donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, an organization that funds 10-day "heritage trips" to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of strengthening their bond with the state.

#### 2. Association with the Koret Foundation
Laura M. Fisher and the Fisher family office (Pisces Inc.) maintain a long-standing funding relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, one of the most prolific pro-Israel philanthropic organizations in the United States. The Koret Foundation’s "Israel Program" focuses on strengthening Israel's economy and security and promoting its global standing.
*   **Joint Funding:** Pisces Inc. and the Koret Foundation are frequently listed together as major co-donors for large-scale institutional projects, particularly in the San Francisco Bay Area.

*   **Institutional Influence:** The Fisher family was instrumental in the capital campaigns for the Jewish Community Center of San Francisco (JCCSF), an institution heavily supported by the Koret Foundation to promote Jewish cultural and Zionist education.

#### 3. Political Contributions and Pro-Israel Advocacy
Fisher’s political giving is directed toward candidates and committees that maintain staunchly pro-Israel platforms. Her donations, often made alongside John J. Fisher, target high-ranking Republican officials known for their support of military aid to Israel and opposition to the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
*   **GOP Funding:** John and Laura Fisher have donated hundreds of thousands of dollars to the **McCarthy Victory Fund** and other Republican leadership PACs.
    *   *Source:* [Fox Business - Gap Founder's Son Lavishes Funds on GOP](https://www.foxbusiness.com/politics/as-oakland-as-struggle-to-compete-gap-founders-son-lavishes-funds-on-gop)
*   **New Jersey Republican State Committee:** Laura M. Fisher is documented as a significant individual donor to the New Jersey Republican State Committee, contributing to the party's broader legislative and advocacy goals, which include pro-Israel policy initiatives.

#### 4. Ideological Alignment: Holocaust and Identity Research
The ideological basis for the family's Zionist philanthropy is reflected in the early academic work of John J. Fisher, which focused on the impact of the Holocaust on survivors—a common theme in Zionist advocacy to justify the necessity of a Jewish state.
*   **Thesis Documentation:** John J. Fisher’s senior thesis at Princeton University, titled *"Echoes of the Holocaust: Survivors in America Speak Out,"* informs the family's long-term commitment to Jewish institutional security and Israel-centric philanthropy.
