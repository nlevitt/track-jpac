# Contributor Report: Silberstein, Steve

Steve Silberstein is a California-based philanthropist and political donor who co-founded the software company Innovative Interfaces. His documented political and philanthropic activity includes significant support for liberal Zionist advocacy, specifically through organizations that support a two-state solution and characterize themselves as "pro-Israel, pro-peace."

### Relationship to J Street and Zionist Advocacy
Silberstein is a documented financial supporter of **J Street**, an organization that describes itself as the "political home for pro-Israel, pro-peace Americans." J Street advocacy is centered on maintaining Israel’s status as a Jewish and democratic state through a two-state solution to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.

*   **Financial Contributions:** Silberstein has been identified as a member of J Street’s **National Finance Committee**, contributing at least $10,000 annually to the organization and its political action committee, JStreetPAC.
    *   *Source:* [J Street 2011 Annual Report (via Amazon S3)](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/01/2011-Annual-Report.pdf) (Note: Mentioned on page 14 as a member of the National Finance Committee).
*   **Foundation Support:** The **Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation** has documented grants to J Street. These funds support the organization's educational and lobbying efforts aimed at influencing U.S. foreign policy toward the Middle East.
    *   *Source:* [InfluenceWatch: Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/stephen-m-silberstein-foundation/)
*   **Organizational Alignment:** By funding J Street, Silberstein supports an advocacy platform that explicitly identifies as Zionist, stating that "Zionism is only meaningful if it embraces the values that we as Jews hold dear," including democracy and human rights.
    *   *Source:* [J Street: Our Mission and Principles](https://jstreet.org/about/our-mission/)

### Broader Philanthropic and Political Context
Silberstein's support for Israel-related causes is part of a wider pattern of giving to progressive and "left-of-center" organizations. He is a member of the **Democracy Alliance**, a network of donors that coordinates funding for progressive infrastructure in the United States.

*   **General Political Giving:** Silberstein has contributed millions to Democratic candidates and Super PACs, including **Priorities USA Action** and the **House Majority PAC**.
    *   *Source:* [OpenSecrets: Donor Profile - Steve Silberstein](https://www.opensecrets.org/outside-spending/donor_detail?id=U0000003714)
*   **Foundation Assets:** As of recent filings, the Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation held over $73 million in assets, with an annual giving budget that frequently includes human rights and policy advocacy groups such as Human Rights Watch and the Brennan Center for Justice.
    *   *Source:* [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer: Stephen M. Silberstein Foundation](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/911852739)
*   **Leadership Roles:** Beyond financial support, Silberstein serves on various advisory boards, including the **UC Berkeley Goldman School of Public Policy**, where he interacts with other high-level political and policy figures.
    *   *Source:* [UC Berkeley Goldman School of Public Policy: Board of Advisors](https://gspp.berkeley.edu/about/leadership-board/board-of-advisors/steve-silberstein)
