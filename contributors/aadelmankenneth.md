# Contributor Report: Kenneth L. Adelman

Kenneth L. Adelman (often identified as Kenneth A. Adelman) is an American diplomat, policy analyst, and neoconservative operative who served as the **Deputy U.S. Ambassador to the United Nations** (1981–1983) and **Director of the Arms Control and Disarmament Agency** (1983–1987). He is documented for his consistent advocacy for Israeli security interests, particularly through his involvement with neoconservative organizations and his public commentary challenging critics of the Israeli state.

### 1. Documented Zionist Advocacy and Commentary
Adelman’s relationship to Zionist advocacy is primarily documented through his long-term affiliation with neoconservative groups that prioritize the U.S.-Israel strategic alliance.

*   **Project for the New American Century (PNAC):** Adelman was a signatory to the April 3, 2002, PNAC letter addressed to President George W. Bush. This letter explicitly linked the "war on terrorism" to the defense of Israel, calling for the removal of Yasser Arafat and the cessation of U.S. support for the Palestinian Authority, which it characterized as a "regime that supports and coordinate[s] terrorism."
    *   [Project for the New American Century: Letter to President Bush on Israel, Arafat, and the War on Terrorism](https://web.archive.org/web/20070811110437/http://www.newamericancentury.org/Bushletter-040302.htm)
*   **Confrontation with Nelson Mandela (1990):** During a televised *Nightline* town hall meeting, Adelman challenged Nelson Mandela’s support for the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) and Yasser Arafat. Adelman characterized Mandela’s relationship with Arafat as hypocritical given Mandela's focus on human rights, and he questioned Mandela's stance in the context of the "interests of the Jewish community."
    *   [Mondoweiss: In 1990 'Nightline' town hall, Jewish leaders call Mandela 'hypocritical' and 'amoral' over support for PLO](https://mondoweiss.net/2013/12/nightline-hypocritical-political/)
    *   [Boundary 2: Mandela's Reflections: Nelson Mandela on Nightline](https://www.boundary2.org/2014/11/mandelas-reflections-nelson-mandela-on-nightline-or-how-palestine-matters/)
*   **Support for the Invasion of Iraq:** Adelman was a leading proponent of the 2003 Iraq War, famously predicting it would be a "cakewalk." His advocacy for regime change in Iraq was frequently framed by the neoconservative argument that removing Saddam Hussein would fundamentally improve the "geopolitical landscape" for Israel and other U.S. allies in the region.
    *   [Washington Post: Cakewalk In Iraq](https://www.washingtonpost.com/archive/opinions/2002/02/13/cakewalk-in-iraq/cf09301c-c6c4-4f2e-8268-7c93017f5e93/)
    *   [The Guardian: Neocons turn on Bush for incompetence over Iraq war](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2006/nov/04/usa.iraq)

### 2. Organizational Affiliations
Adelman has held leadership or signatory roles in several organizations that are central to the "Israel Lobby" and neoconservative policy-making in the United States.

*   **Committee on the Present Danger (CPD):** Adelman served on the executive board of this group, which was revived after 9/11 to promote an aggressive foreign policy against "radical Islam," often aligning its objectives with Israeli security doctrines.
    *   [Militarist Monitor: Ken Adelman Profile](https://militarist-monitor.org/profile/ken-adelman/)
*   **Hudson Institute:** Adelman’s professional and personal life is closely tied to the Hudson Institute, a think tank known for its pro-Israel policy research. His wife, Carol Adelman, is a senior fellow emeritus at the institute, and Kenneth has participated in their various policy forums.
    *   [Hudson Institute: Carol Adelman Expert Profile](https://www.hudson.org/experts/2-carol-adelman)
*   **JINSA and Neoconservative Circles:** He is frequently cited alongside figures such as Richard Perle and Elliott Abrams as a key intellectual in the neoconservative movement, which maintains "deep connections to Israel" and the Likud Party.
    *   [The Occidental Quarterly: Neoconservatism as a Jewish Movement](https://www.toqonline.com/archives/v4n2/42-MacDonald.pdf)

### 3. Political Donations and Funding Activities
While not primarily recognized as a high-net-worth "megadonor" like Sheldon Adelson, Adelman is documented as a financial supporter and public surrogate for Republican political organizations.

*   **Progress for America (PFA):** Adelman served as a spokesperson and advocate for Progress for America, a conservative 527 organization that raised millions of dollars to support the Bush administration’s foreign and domestic agendas. This organization was noted for its role in funneling "soft money" from individual donors to support Republican candidates and policy initiatives.
    *   [Federal Election Commission (FEC): Progress for America Sensitive Filings](https://www.fec.gov/resources/cms-content/documents/2004_Progress_for_America_S_0001.pdf)
*   **Support for Republican Candidates:** Scholarly research on lobbying and campaign finance identifies Adelman and his family as "financial backers of Republican candidates and causes" within the Washington D.C. political ecosystem.
    *   [Universiteit Leiden: Lobbying for Democracy - Ken Adelman as Financial Backer](https://scholarlypublications.universiteitleiden.nl/handle/1887/12513)
