# Contributor Report: FISHER, SUSAN

**Susan Fisher** (often associated with the San Francisco-based Fisher family) is a prominent California-based political and philanthropic donor. She is documented as a significant supporter of Israeli institutions and Zionist advocacy organizations, often identified in coordination with the broader Fisher family (founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family office **Pisces Inc.**), the **Koret Foundation**, and **Birthright Israel**.

### 1. Relationship to Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
Susan Fisher is a member of the Fisher family, the founders of the global retail corporation **Gap Inc.** and the investment firm **Pisces Inc.** (the Fisher family office). The family's wealth, primarily derived from these entities, has established them as one of the most influential donor networks in California.
* **Documentation of Fisher Family Wealth & Entities:** John Fisher, a key member of the family, is the Executive Vice Chairman of **Pisces Inc.** and co-founder of Sansome Partners, managing the family's diversified investments.

* **Gap Inc. Foundation & Family Philanthropy:** The Fisher family has historically used their corporate and personal foundations to fund large-scale projects, including significant allocations to Jewish and pro-Israel causes.
    * [J Weekly - Gap Founder Donald Fisher Leaves Stamp on Community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

### 2. Documented Support for Birthright Israel
Susan Fisher is recorded as a consistent stakeholder and donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which provides free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the state.
* **Stakeholder Contributions:** Fisher is identified in the Birthright Israel Foundation’s "Scroll of Honor" as an extraordinary stakeholder. In 2022, she was recognized for commitments at the **$250,000–$499,999** level.
    * Birthright Israel Foundation 2022 Scroll of Honor (via Issuu) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
* **Continued Donor Status:** She remains listed in subsequent reports, such as the 2024 Scroll of Honor, which acknowledges her ongoing financial commitment to "securing the future of Jewish identity" through the program.
    * Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Institutional Ties to the Koret Foundation
Susan Fisher is associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based grantmaking organization known for its focus on Jewish life and the strengthening of Israel.
* **Shared Funding Streams:** Fisher and the Koret Foundation are frequently co-listed as primary sponsors for Israeli academic and cultural institutions. For example, both are identified as key donors to **Cal Performances**, which receives substantial support for projects that include Israeli artistic collaborations.
    * Encore Spotlight - Cal Performances Supporters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
* **Holocaust Education & Memorials:** Fisher is listed as a donor alongside the Koret Foundation in major initiatives such as the **United States Holocaust Memorial Museum** (USHMM), which critical advocacy groups often link to broader Zionist educational agendas.

### 4. Direct Support for Israeli Academic Institutions
Susan Fisher has established a personal philanthropic legacy in Israel through the creation of scholarship funds at top-tier Israeli universities.
* **Hebrew University of Jerusalem (HUJI):** Fisher is the founder and primary donor of the **Susan Fisher Scholarship Fund** in Economics at Hebrew University. This fund is part of the university's "legacy commitments" to support the vital work of the institution in Jerusalem.
    * [The President's Report - Hebrew University of Jerusalem (page 101)](https://scholars.huji.ac.il/sites/default/files/presidentsreport/files/presidents_report_2011.pdf)
    * [HUJI OpenScholar - Donor Legacy Commitments](https://scholars.huji.ac.il/sites/default/files/presidentsreport/files/huji_pr_2012_low.pdf)

### 5. Identification by Zionist Donor Tracking Projects
Advocacy and research groups focused on monitoring Zionist influence in U.S. politics and philanthropy have identified Susan Fisher as a key player in the "Zionist donor" network.
* **Mapping Projects:** Organizations like the **Mapping Project** (an activist-led initiative) explicitly name the Fisher family (including Donald and Doris Fisher and associated family entities) as prominent Zionist donors due to their extensive funding of the Koret Foundation, Birthright Israel, and the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**.

* **Political Donations:** Fisher is also recorded as a contributor to political action committees and candidates that maintain a pro-Israel platform, such as **AIPAC**-aligned entities and the **Democratic Majority for Israel**.
