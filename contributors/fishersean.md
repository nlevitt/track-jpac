# Contributor Report: Fisher, Sean

Sean Fisher is a documented political and philanthropic contributor associated with the **Fisher family**, the founding family of **Gap Inc.** and the family office **Pisces Inc.** Documentation identifies Sean Fisher as a contributor within a broader family network that serves as a primary financial pillar for several prominent Zionist and pro-Israel organizations, including the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

### Financial and Institutional Affiliations

*   **Gap Inc. / Pisces Inc.:** The Fisher family wealth is derived from Gap Inc., founded by Donald and Doris Fisher in 1969. **Pisces Inc.** serves as the family's investment firm and family office, managing the multi-billion dollar estate that funds their diverse philanthropic activities. Sean Fisher is listed alongside other prominent family members, such as **John J. Fisher** (owner of the Oakland Athletics) and **Robert J. Fisher**, in donor registries for San Francisco-based and Jewish community organizations.

### Zionist Advocacy and Israel-Related Philanthropy

Sean Fisher’s relationship to Zionist advocacy is established through direct contributions to Jewish federations and institutional links to major Zionist funding bodies:

*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family has a long-standing, leadership-level association with the **Koret Foundation**. Donald Fisher served as a long-time director of the foundation, which has donated over **$100 million** to programs in Israel. The foundation is a major funder of the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds** and has supported organizations described as "Zionist" in their mission, including the **Jewish Community Federation** and various security-focused think tanks in Israel.
    *   Source: Koret Foundation Israel Support - KoretFoundation.org (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   [Source: Donald Fisher's Stamp on Jewish Community - J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)

*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family is documented as a significant donor to **Birthright Israel** (Taglit), an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen "Zionist identity." The **Fisher Family Foundation** and individual family members have provided multimillion-dollar support to the program since its inception.
    *   Source: Major Donors to Birthright Israel - BirthrightIsrael.foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   Source: Fisher Family Philanthropy - FoundationLaunch.com (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

*   **Jewish Federation of the Desert:** Sean Fisher is explicitly listed as a donor to the **Jewish Federation of the Desert**. This organization’s stated mission includes "supporting Jewish communities in Israel" and funding the **Israel Emergency Campaign**, which provides financial aid for Israeli civil and security infrastructure.
    *   Source: Jewish Federation of the Desert Contributor List - JFedPS.org (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

*   **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (S.F.):** The Fisher family provides ongoing, high-level funding to the **San Francisco-based Jewish Community Federation**. This organization is one of the most active Zionist philanthropic entities in California, funding programs that advocate for the State of Israel and combat anti-Zionist movements.
    *   Source: Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
