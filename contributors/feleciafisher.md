This report examines the political and philanthropic activities of the Fisher family—specifically co-founder **Doris Fisher** and her son **John Fisher** (President of **Pisces Inc.**)—regarding their documented support for the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy. While the name "Felecia Fisher" appears in some specific activist databases as a reference to the Gap Inc. founding family, documented public records and corporate filings identify the primary individuals behind these contributions as Doris Fisher (née Feigenbaum) and John J. Fisher.

# Contributor Report: Fisher, Felecia (The Fisher Family)

### 1. Overview of Political and Corporate Identity
The Fisher family founded **Gap Inc.** in 1969 and manages their wealth through **Pisces Inc.**, a private investment firm. They are among the most prominent political donors in California, historically leveraging their fortune to support pro-Israel lobbying, conservative political causes, and Zionist educational initiatives.
*   **Doris Fisher:** Co-founder of Gap Inc. and a billionaire philanthropist. [Source: Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/profile/doris-fisher/)
*   **John J. Fisher:** Son of Doris and Donald Fisher; President of Pisces Inc.; Majority owner of the Oakland Athletics. Source: Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation))
*   **Pisces Inc.:** The family’s investment management company used for substantial political and charitable disbursements. Source: Fox Business (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Relationship to Zionist Advocacy and Israel
The Fisher family’s support for Israel is characterized by long-term, high-value donations to organizations that promote Zionist ideology and strengthen U.S.-Israel political ties.

#### **Birthright Israel**
The Fisher family is documented as a major donor to **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their "Jewish identity" and "connection to Israel." Doris Fisher is a member of the Birthright Israel Foundation’s "Guardians," a tier for the organization’s most significant financial backers.
*   **Documentation:** The Fisher family is listed as a foundational supporter of Birthright's mission to foster Zionist identity. Source: Birthright Israel Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **Koret Foundation**
The Fishers have maintained a decades-long relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic giant that identifies Israel as one of its primary focus areas. The Koret Foundation funds numerous pro-Israel initiatives, including the "Koret Israel Economic Development Funds" and security-related advocacy.
*   **Documentation:** Donald and Doris Fisher’s long-standing board influence and partnership with Koret are documented in the foundation’s historical reports. Source: Koret Foundation History (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **Political Contributions to Pro-Israel PACs**
Through **Pisces Inc.** and individual filings, the Fisher family (particularly John Fisher) has been a top contributor to Republican candidates and PACs that prioritize military and diplomatic support for Israel. John Fisher has been identified as a major donor to the **McCarthy Victory Fund** and other GOP entities that maintain a strictly pro-Israel platform.
*   **Documentation:** FEC records show millions of dollars in contributions to candidates and committees with explicit pro-Israel stances. Source: OpenSecrets (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Activist Identification and Boycott Context
Due to their significant financial backing of Zionist organizations, the Fisher family has been targeted by pro-Palestinian advocacy groups.
*   **The Mapping Project:** Activist researchers have identified the Fisher family as "prominent Zionist donors" who fund the "Zionist colonization of Palestine" through their support for Birthright and Koret. This database often uses the designation "Fisher, [Name]" to link Gap Inc. profits to Israeli state advocacy. [Source: Mapping Project - Fisher Family](https://mappingproject.org/entities/fisher-family-gap-inc-founders/)

[Source: Berkeley Daily Planet](https://www.berkeleydailyplanet.com/issue/2000-11-15/article/2026?headline=Activists-protest-logging-practices-of-Gap-founders)
