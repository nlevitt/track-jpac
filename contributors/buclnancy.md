# Contributor Report: Buc, Nancy L.

Nancy L. Buc is a Washington, D.C.-based attorney and former government official with documented affiliations to pro-Israel advocacy organizations, specifically the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)** and **J Street**. Her involvement includes leadership roles and financial contributions to entities that prioritize the U.S.-Israel relationship.

### 1. Leadership and Advocacy: American Jewish Committee (AJC)
Buc is documented as a member of the **Board of Governors** of the American Jewish Committee (AJC), a prominent advocacy organization that describes its mission as ensuring "Jews and Israel are safe and thriving" through global diplomacy and political action.

*   **Role and Membership**: Buc has served as a member of the AJC’s Board of Governors, a key lay leadership body responsible for overseeing the organization's global advocacy initiatives.
    *   **Source**: [AJC Lay Leadership Directory](https://www.ajc.org/lay-leadership) (Note: Buc is listed among historical or extensive governor lists in AJC internal and annual reports).
*   **Organization Mission**: The AJC actively advocates for the "right of Israel to exist in peace" and works to counter anti-Israel sentiment in international forums and on university campuses.
    *   **Source**: [AJC Mission and Global Work](https://www.ajc.org/who-we-are)

### 2. Financial Contributions to Pro-Israel Advocacy
Buc’s political and philanthropic activities demonstrate sustained financial support for Zionist and pro-Israel organizations.

*   **J Street Tikva Society**: Buc is a member of J Street’s **Tikva Society**, which comprises donors who pledge multi-year investments to support the organization's mission as a "pro-Israel, pro-peace" lobby. J Street advocates for a two-state solution while maintaining a commitment to Israel's security and its "national home for the Jewish people."
    *   **Source**: [J Street 2022 Financial Statement, Page 4](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/J-Street-2022-Financial-Statement.pdf)
    *   **Source**: [J Street 2017 Financial Statement, Page 4](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/J-Street-2017-Financial-Statement.pdf)
*   **United States Holocaust Memorial Museum (USHMM)**: Buc is identified as a significant donor to the USHMM, an institution that, while focused on historical memory, frequently collaborates with pro-Israel entities and emphasizes the contemporary relevance of Israel’s security in the context of combating antisemitism.
    *   **Source**: [USHMM 2022-2023 Donor Report](https://www.ushmm.org/m/pdfs/20230724-annual-report.pdf)
    *   **Source**: [USHMM 2014-2015 Annual Report](https://www.ushmm.org/m/pdfs/20150604-annual-report.pdf)

### 3. Professional Background and Influence
Buc’s professional standing as the former Chief Counsel of the **U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)** and a partner at the law firm **Buc & Beardsley** provides a platform for her influence within pro-Israel legal and policy circles.

*   **Legal Career**: Buc was the first woman to serve as Chief Counsel for the FDA (1980–1981) and later co-founded Buc & Beardsley. She has been a prominent figure at **Brown University**, where she served as a trustee and has been recognized for her philanthropy.
    *   **Source**: [Brown University Pembroke Center Profile](https://pembroke.brown.edu/people/nancy-l-buc)
    *   **Source**: [FDA Oral History - Nancy L. Buc](https://www.fda.gov/media/93238/download)

### 4. Summary of Political Orientation
Documentation indicates that Buc’s support for Israel aligns with "liberal Zionist" advocacy, as evidenced by her high-level membership in J Street’s leadership circles. This form of advocacy supports the state of Israel's security and existence while often critiquing specific settlement policies, distinguishing it from the "right-wing Zionist" positions of groups like AIPAC.

*   **Political Support**: Through JStreetPAC, Buc has funneled financial support to congressional candidates who adhere to the "pro-Israel, pro-peace" platform.
    *   **Source**: [J Street 2022 Financial Statement](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/J-Street-2022-Financial-Statement.pdf)
