# California Contributor Report: Jeffrey Essakow

### **Overview**
Jeffrey Essakow is a San Diego-based real estate developer and the President of Protea Properties. His relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is documented through his involvement in the San Diego Jewish community's fundraising efforts, his participation in Israeli solidarity events, and his recognition as a "Builder of Israel" by regional organizations.

---

### **Documented Support for Israel & Zionist Advocacy**

#### **1. Participation in Solidarity Initiatives**
Essakow has engaged in public demonstrations of support for Israel. In January 2024, he was a key participant in a "solidarity ride" in Solana Beach, California, held to support Israeli hostages held in Gaza. During this event, Essakow publicly expressed solidarity with "our sister city Sha'ar Hanegev and all of Israel."
*   **Source:** San Diego Jewish World - Solidarity Ride Marked 100 Days Israelis Held Hostage by Hamas (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **2. Recognition as a "Builder of Israel"**
The United Jewish Federation of San Diego County identifies Essakow as a "San Diego Builder of Israel." This designation is given to individuals documented as having committed significant "time, money and emotion to the building of Israel." His name is listed in records alongside other regional contributors to Israeli causes, including the Jewish National Fund (JNF) and Israel Bonds.
*   **Source:** San Diego Jewish World - San Diego Builders of Israel (Archived Publication) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **3. Organizational Awards for Israeli Advocacy**
In 2024, Essakow received the Outstanding Achievement Award from the Southern African Jewish American Community (SAJAC). The award was presented at an event documenting the history of Jews in San Diego and their ties to the State of Israel. Documentation from the organization highlights the awardees' roles in providing "outstanding work in support of the State of Israel."
*   **Source:** SAJAC Reporter - 30th Anniversary Gala and Award Recipients (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **4. Documented Donor to Pro-Israel Organizations**
Essakow has been identified as a donor to the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC). His financial contributions are integrated into the broader framework of the United Jewish Federation of San Diego County, which manages missions to Israel and coordinates fundraising for Israeli national projects like Project Renewal and the Jewish National Fund.
