# Contributor Report: Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2024

### Overview
"Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2024" is the campaign committee for California State Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel (D-Encino). Gabriel serves as the Co-chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC), a body that explicitly integrates support for the State of Israel into its legislative mission and policy priorities.

### Leadership and Institutional Advocacy for Israel
As Co-chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, Gabriel leads an organization whose mission includes "countering anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" and "strengthening California-Israel economic, technological, and cultural ties." The Caucus identifies itself as the "Jewish voice" in the legislature and operates in close partnership with the Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC), a pro-Israel advocacy group.

*   **Caucus Mission Statement:** The CLJC mission includes countering anti-Israel narratives and promoting ties with Israel.
    *   [Official Mission Statement](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/our-mission)
*   **Relationship with JPAC:** The Caucus collaborates with JPAC to advance a "pro-Israel" legislative agenda, which includes securing state funding and addressing "anti-Israel" sentiment in educational curricula.
    *   [CLJC Partnership with JPAC](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/about-us)

### Documentation of Zionist Advocacy and Israel Support
Gabriel has documented personal and legislative actions that align with the goals of political Zionism and support for the Israeli government.

*   **Official Statements of Support:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Gabriel and the CLJC issued a formal statement affirming that they "stand with Israel" and supporting its right to defend itself.
    *   [October 7 Statement](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2023-10-07-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks)
*   **Trip to Israel (February 2024):** Gabriel was part of a seven-member delegation of California lawmakers who traveled to Israel in February 2024. The trip was sponsored by the Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC) and the Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles, costing approximately $82,000 for the group. The stated purpose was to strengthen connections between California and Israel during the ongoing conflict.
    *   [CalMatters Report on February 2024 Israel Trip](https://calmatters.org/politics/2024/09/california-legislators-travel-costs-israel-taiwan/)

### Legislative Actions and Contentious Policy
Gabriel has authored and championed legislation that critics argue serves to insulate Israel from criticism within public institutions and educational settings.

*   **AB 715 (Antisemitism Prevention):** Gabriel and the CLJC championed this bill, which creates a state Antisemitism Prevention Coordinator. Opponents, including CAIR-California, state the bill "is a coordinated effort to stifle any criticism of Israel" by potentially conflating such criticism with antisemitism in public schools.
    *   [AB 715 Bill Summary](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2024-10-02-governor-newsom-signs-all-four-jewish-caucus-priority-bills-law)
    *   [CAIR Criticism of AB 715](https://ca.cair.com/action/ab-715-is-a-coordinated-effort-to-stifle-criticism-of-israel/)
*   **AB 1468/AB 2918 (Ethnic Studies Guardrails):** Gabriel and the CLJC have advocated for "guardrails" in the state's ethnic studies curriculum. Critics and educators have identified these measures as an attempt to "police" or "censor" Palestinian narratives and perspectives on Israeli settler colonialism.
    *   [Truthout Report on Ethnic Studies Censorship](https://truthout.org/articles/california-ethnic-studies-bill-aims-to-censor-palestine-related-education/)
*   **AB 2867 (Holocaust Art Recovery):** Gabriel authored this legislation to assist in reclaiming art looted during the Holocaust, specifically responding to legal cases involving the Cassirer family and a Spanish museum.
    *   [AB 2867 Official Announcement](https://a46.asmdc.org/press-releases/20240328-assemblymember-jesse-gabriel-lieutenant-governor-eleni-kounalakis-announce)

### Financial Relationship to Pro-Israel Advocacy
The committee "Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2024" receives significant support from individuals and organizations aligned with the CLJC's mission. Furthermore, Gabriel's leadership of the Caucus has facilitated the allocation of millions in state funds for programs prioritized by pro-Israel organizations, such as the Nonprofit Security Grant Program, which was expanded to $80 million in the 2024-25 budget.

*   **Nonprofit Security Funding Advocacy:** Gabriel and the CLJC secured record funding for security grants frequently utilized by Jewish institutions affiliated with pro-Israel advocacy.
    *   [eJewishPhilanthropy Report on $80 Million Funding](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/california-announces-significant-expansion-of-its-nonprofit-security-grants-as-pro-and-anti-israel-protesters-clash-outside-l-a-synagogue/)
