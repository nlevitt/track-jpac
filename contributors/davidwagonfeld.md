# Contributor Report: David Wagonfeld

David Wagonfeld is a Silicon Valley-based technology investor and political donor with a documented history of leadership in organizations that advocate for the State of Israel and maintain strong U.S.-Israel political and economic ties.

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Zionist Advocacy

Wagonfeld has held multiple senior leadership roles in prominent Jewish and Zionist organizations that prioritize support for Israel.

*   **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (San Francisco):** Wagonfeld is a former President and longtime Director of the Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco, the Peninsula, Marin and Sonoma Counties. The organization identifies itself as a "philanthropic catalyst" for the Jewish community, with a mission that includes developing strategies to "impact... in Israel."
    *   **Source:** [J-Ventures Conference Bio](https://www.j-venturesconference.vc/) (Confirmed as "Past president of the Jewish Federation in SF").
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer (SF Federation 990)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/941156533/2024_06_990_2024072621458928/full).
*   **Americans for Ben-Gurion University (A4BGU):** Wagonfeld serves as the **Treasurer** and is a member of the Board of Directors for Americans for Ben-Gurion University. The organization's mission is to fulfill "David Ben-Gurion's vision for the Negev and the future of Israel." Wagonfeld is also a **Zin Fellow** and **Wexner Fellow**, leadership programs focused on Israel and Jewish communal service.
    *   **Source:** [A4BGU Board of Directors](https://americansforbgu.org/board-of-directors/).
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer (A4BGU 990)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/237270753/2024_08_990_2024080121461917/full).
*   **UpStart:** Wagonfeld is a member of the Board of Directors for UpStart, a "Jewish entrepreneurial incubator" that supports ventures across the U.S. and Israel.
    *   **Source:** [UpStart Team and Board Bio](https://upstartlab.org/about-us/team-and-board/david-wagonfeld/).

### 2. Pro-Israel Political Lobbying and Advocacy

Wagonfeld is active in organized political lobbying on behalf of pro-Israel interests in the United States.

*   **AIPAC Leadership:** Wagonfeld has been identified in professional biographies as "leading AIPAC Silicon Valley." AIPAC (American Israel Public Affairs Committee) is the primary U.S. lobbying group focused on ensuring legislative support for Israel.
    *   **Source:** [J-Ventures Conference Bio](https://www.j-venturesconference.vc/) (Refers to his "work with AIPAC").
    *   **Source:** [Substack: Inside the Pro-Israel Information War](https://jackpoulson.substack.com/p/inside-the-pro-israel-information-war) (Identifies Wagonfeld as "leading AIPAC Silicon Valley").
*   **Political Monitoring and Messaging:** Through his role in the investment fund **J-Ventures**, Wagonfeld was identified as a member of the "PR/Political Team" in a private WhatsApp group called the “J-Ventures Global Kibbutz Group.” Documentation from 2023 indicates this group monitored campus activity and discussed strategies to influence media coverage, including attempts to publish pro-Israel political cartoons in the *Stanford Daily*.
    *   **Source:** [Substack: Inside the Pro-Israel Information War](https://jackpoulson.substack.com/p/inside-the-pro-israel-information-war).

### 3. Economic Ties to Israel

Wagonfeld integrates his professional investment activities with his support for the State of Israel through specialized venture capital.

*   **J-Ventures:** Wagonfeld is a **Co-Founder and General Partner** of J-Ventures, which describes itself as a "capitalist kibbutz" investment fund. The fund is designed to build a network of investors that strengthens the "Israeli startup ecosystem" and provides financial support for Israeli needs, including raising over $22 million for an "Israel Emergency Fund" following the events of October 7, 2023.
    *   **Source:** [J-Ventures Conference Bio](https://www.j-venturesconference.vc/).
    *   **Source:** [Startup Nation Central Profile](https://finder.startupnationcentral.org/person/david-wagonfeld).
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Federation Bay Area 2024 Annual Report](https://jewishfed.org/annual-report-2024/) (Detailing the $22M mobilization for Israel).

### 4. Direct Contributions

Wagonfeld and his spouse, Alison Wagonfeld, are documented regular donors to Zionist causes and Israeli institutions.

*   **Institutional Support:** The Wagonfelds are listed as "Sde Boker Sponsors" and major contributors to Ben-Gurion University of the Negev and Americans for Ben-Gurion University.
    *   **Source:** [Ben-Gurion University Canada President's Report 2025](https://bengurion.ca/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/Presidents-Report-2025.pdf) (Page 27, Listing "Alison & David Wagonfeld" as contributors).
    *   **Source:** [Americans for Ben-Gurion University: Celebrating Medical Science](https://americansforbgu.org/celebrating-the-remarkable-3-pioneering-medical-science/) (Listing Wagonfeld as a sponsor).
