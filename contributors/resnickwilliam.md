# Contributor Report: Resnick, William

**William Resnick** is a Los Angeles-based psychiatrist and philanthropist identified as a significant contributor to Israeli institutions and Zionist advocacy. He is the son of Stewart Resnick, chairman of The Wonderful Company and a prominent pro-Israel donor. 

### 1. Institutional Support for Israeli Entities
Resnick is documented as a contributor to major Israeli academic and research institutions, often through the financial infrastructure of the Resnick family foundation.

*   **Technion-Israel Institute of Technology:** Resnick is associated with the establishment of the **Stewart and Lynda Resnick Sustainability Center for Catalysis** at the Technion. The Resnick Foundation, of which he is a member of the founding family, pledged $50 million to this center in 2022. The center is intended to bolster the Israeli economy and position the institute as a global center for innovation in fields such as biotechnology and chemical engineering.
    *   *Source:* [Technion - Historic Gift to Establish Sustainability Center](https://www.technion.ac.il/en/2022/12/historic-gift-to-the-technion/)
    *   *Source:* [American Technion Society - Resnicks Bet Big on Israel](https://ats.org/news/resnicks-bet-big-on-israels-clean-tech-future/)
*   **Reichman University (IDC Herzliya):** The Resnick family has funneled millions of dollars into the American Friends of IDC (now Reichman University). This institution is noted for its close ties to the Israeli military and intelligence establishment, hosting the annual Herzliya Conference on national security. Stewart Resnick has served on the board of the American Friends organization.
    *   *Source:* [Reichman University - Stewart and Lynda Resnick Donor Profile](https://www.runi.ac.il/en/donor-recognition/stewart-and-lynda-resnick/)
    *   *Source:* [MR Online - California Billionaires Funding Israel's Occupation](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
*   **National Library of Israel:** Resnick and his family provided a $12 million gift to support the National Library of Israel in Jerusalem, specifically funding the Lynda and Stewart Resnick Fellowship for researchers focused on Israel and Judaica.
    *   *Source:* [Wonderful.com - A Lasting Legacy for Israel](https://www.wonderful.com/csr/art-culture/national-library-of-israel/)

### 2. Zionist Advocacy and Media Funding
Beyond direct institutional support, Resnick contributes to organizations that focus on pro-Israel education and narrative influence.

*   **OpenDor Media (formerly Jerusalem U):** William Resnick is listed as a donor to OpenDor Media, an organization that produces films and educational content designed to promote Zionist identity and defend Israeli policy. The organization's programs, such as "Unpacked," are used globally to combat the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement and foster "pride" in the Jewish nation.
    *   *Source:* [OpenDor Media - 2019 Annual Report (Page 23)](https://opendormedia.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/OpenDor-Media-Annual-Report-2019.pdf)
*   **American Jewish World Service (AJWS):** Resnick has served as a trustee and secretary of AJWS. While the organization focuses on international development, it operates within a Jewish framework that maintains deep connections to the North American Jewish community's relationship with Israel.
    *   *Source:* [AJWS - Board of Trustees Profile: Bill Resnick](https://ajws.org/who-we-are/board-of-trustees/bill-resnick/)

### 3. Support for Israeli Defense and Security
The Resnick Foundation, under the leadership of the Resnick family, has a documented history of funding organizations directly supporting the Israeli military.

*   **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF):** Tax records for the Resnick Foundation indicate annual contributions ranging from $200,000 to $500,000 to the FIDF between 2015 and 2022, totaling approximately $2.4 million. These funds are used to support soldiers in the Israeli military.

    *   *Source:* [MR Online - IDF Funding via Resnick Foundation](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
