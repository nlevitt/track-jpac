Following is a research report regarding the political contributor and candidate entity **Steinberg for Sacramento Mayor 2020**, specifically focusing on Darrell Steinberg’s record of support for Israel and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Steinberg for Sacramento Mayor 2020

### Overview
"Steinberg for Sacramento Mayor 2020" is the candidate committee for Darrell Steinberg, who served as the Mayor of Sacramento from 2016 to 2024 and previously as the President pro Tempore of the California State Senate. Steinberg has a documented history of legislative and community leadership focused on strengthening ties between California and Israel and maintaining a public platform of Zionist support.

### Institutional Zionist Advocacy and Leadership
Steinberg’s involvement in pro-Israel advocacy is rooted in both his early community work and his legislative career:
*   **Jewish Community Leadership:** Before entering the state legislature, Steinberg served as the chairman of the **Sacramento Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)**, a body that coordinates local pro-Israel advocacy and community relations ([The Forward](https://forward.com/news/12948/jewish-insider-tapped-to-be-golden-state-s-most-p/)).
*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** In 2012, Steinberg was a founding member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**. The caucus’s stated mission includes "supporting Israel" and "countering anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" within the state government ([Wikipedia: California Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)).
*   **Mayors United Against Antisemitism:** As Mayor, Steinberg is a signatory to the American Jewish Committee’s (AJC) "Mayors United Against Antisemitism," a national initiative that links local municipal leadership with the AJC’s global pro-Israel advocacy goals ([AJC](https://www.ajc.org/mayors-united-against-antisemitism)).

### Legislative Support for Israel
During his tenure in the California State Senate (2006–2014), Steinberg utilized his leadership position to advance pro-Israel policy:
*   **California-Israel MOU (SCR 121):** In 2014, Steinberg’s caucus spearheaded **SCR 121**, which formally endorsed the **California-Israel Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)** signed by Governor Jerry Brown and Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu. The MOU committed the state to foster economic, educational, and industrial research cooperation with Israel ([California Legislative Information](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201320140SCR121)).
*   **Iran Divestment Legislation:** In 2007, Steinberg was a primary driver of legislation that mandated California’s public pension funds (CalPERS and CalSTRS) to divest from companies with business ties to Iran’s energy sector. This move was a central priority for pro-Israel lobbying groups seeking to isolate the Iranian government ([The Forward](https://forward.com/news/12948/jewish-insider-tapped-to-be-golden-state-s-most-p/)).

### Public Statements and Zionist Self-Identification
Steinberg has frequently articulated a personal and political commitment to Zionism and the state of Israel:
*   **Commitment to a Jewish Homeland:** In 2021, Steinberg stated, "I am a proud American Jew who loves Israel, takes pride in its birth and is passionate about the continued imperative of a **Jewish homeland**" ([Sacramento Bee](https://www.sacbee.com/opinion/article251493638.html)). 
*   **Response to 2024 Conflict:** During the 2024 Israel-Hamas conflict, Steinberg introduced a Sacramento City Council resolution calling for a bilateral ceasefire. While the resolution faced criticism from various sides, Steinberg insisted it must "recognize both the importance of a **safe and secure Israel** and of an independent Palestinian state," and he explicitly condemned Hamas’s actions ([Engage Sacramento](https://engagesac.org/blog-posts/2024/3/14/mayor-steinberg-joins-muslim-jewish-and-other-community-leaders-in-bringing-bilateral-ceasefire-resolution-to-city-council)).

### International Cooperation and Delegations
Steinberg has facilitated direct engagement between Israeli officials and the California state capital:
*   **Israeli Firefighting Delegation:** In September 2020, Steinberg officially greeted a delegation of ten **Israeli firefighters** and a Foreign Ministry representative sent to assist California during a record wildfire season. He characterized the partnership as a result of "relationships forged" between California and Israel ([Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/321453/israeli-first-responders-help-combat-californias-fires/)).
*   **Pro-Israel Delegations:** Steinberg has a documented record of engaging with and leading delegations associated with the Jewish community and pro-Israel interests, including historical visits to international Jewish populations as a representative of the Sacramento JCRC ([The Forward](https://forward.com/news/12948/jewish-insider-tapped-to-be-golden-state-s-most-p/)).
