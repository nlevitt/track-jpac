# Contributor Report: The Wonderful Company, LLC

This report documents the relationship between **The Wonderful Company, LLC** (and its co-owners Stewart and Lynda Resnick) and the State of Israel, as well as their involvement in Zionist advocacy and funding.

### 1. Organizational Leadership & Advocacy Ties
Co-owner **Stewart Resnick** is affiliated with several prominent organizations that lobby for or provide strategic support to the State of Israel.

*   **Washington Institute for Near East Policy (WINEP):** Stewart and Lynda Resnick serve on the Board of Trustees for WINEP, a think tank originally established as a spin-off of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**. WINEP provides policy research and advocacy that aligns with Israeli security interests and has been documented lobbying for hardline sanctions against Iran, a primary global competitor to The Wonderful Company’s pistachio business.
    *   **Source:** [Mondoweiss - How Beverly Hills billionaire farmers profit from Iran sanctions](https://mondoweiss.net/2013/07/billionaire-sanctions-lobbied/)
    *   **Source:** [The Grayzone - How the Resnick fortune is fueling the assault on Iran](https://thegrayzone.com/2018/12/04/pistachio-wars-how-the-resnick-billionaires-snack-food-fortune-is-fueling-the-assault-on-iran/)
*   **American Friends of Reichman University (formerly IDC Herzliya):** Stewart Resnick serves as a board member for this organization, which functions as the U.S. fundraising arm for Reichman University in Israel. The university is identified as an influential institution with close ties to the Israeli military and intelligence establishment, hosting the annual Herzliya Conference on Israeli security policy.
    *   **Source:** [BDS Coalition - The Wonderful Company: Supporting War Crimes & Apartheid](https://bdscoalition.ca/2025/02/26/the-wonderful-company-is-not-so-wonderful-supporting-war-crimes-apartheid/)
    *   **Source:** [MR Online - California pistachio billionaires funding Israel's occupation regime](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)

### 2. Direct Financial Support for the Israeli Military
The Resnicks have a documented history of providing consistent financial contributions to organizations that support the Israeli Defense Forces (IDF).

*   **American Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (AFIDF):** Between 2015 and 2022, the Resnick Foundation contributed approximately **$2.4 million** to AFIDF. These funds are used to provide financial support and services to IDF soldiers, including special units such as the Israeli Navy Seals.
    *   **Source:** [The Canary - The Resnicks: Capitalists on Steroids](https://www.thecanary.co/global/world-analysis/2025/01/13/the-resnicks-la-california/)
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch - Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/friends-of-the-israel-defense-forces-fidf/)

### 3. Institutional & Academic Funding in Israel
The Wonderful Company and the Resnick Foundation have committed substantial capital to Israeli academic and cultural institutions to strengthen the state’s technological and economic position.

*   **Technion-Israel Institute of Technology:** In December 2022, the Resnicks pledged **$50 million** to establish the *Stewart and Lynda Resnick Sustainability Center for Catalysis*. The stated purpose of the center is to bolster the Technion’s position as a center of innovation and to "strengthen the State of Israel and its economy."
    *   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post - $50 million sustainability center donated to Technion by Resnicks](https://www.jpost.com/business-and-innovation/all-news/article-725458)
    *   **Source:** [The Wonderful Company - Historic $50 Million Gift to Technion](https://www.wonderful.com/csr/technion/)
*   **National Library of Israel (NLI):** The Resnicks donated **$12 million** to the National Library of Israel for its new campus in Jerusalem. This gift established the *Lynda and Stewart Resnick Fellowship*, which focuses on research in areas including Israel and Judaica.
    *   **Source:** [The Wonderful Company - A lasting legacy for Israel and the world](https://www.wonderful.com/csr/nli/)
