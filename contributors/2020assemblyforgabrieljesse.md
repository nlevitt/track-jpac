# Contributor Report: Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2020

### Overview
"Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2020" (FPPC ID #1414460) is the campaign committee for California State Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel. Gabriel has held office since 2018 and currently represents California's 46th State Assembly District. His political activity is characterized by leadership in the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, where he has focused on strengthening California–Israel relations, countering anti-Zionism, and securing state funding for Jewish institutional security.

### Institutional Leadership and Zionist Advocacy
Gabriel serves as the Chair of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, a bipartisan group of state legislators. The caucus's stated mission includes:
*   **Countering Anti-Zionism:** The caucus explicitly works to counter "anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" within California’s public institutions, including schools and universities.
    *   **Source:** [Wikipedia: California Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)
*   **Strengthening California-Israel Ties:** Gabriel has used his leadership to promote economic and cultural partnerships between the state and Israel. In 2024, he co-led a delegation to Israel sponsored by the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)** and the **Jewish Federation of Los Angeles** to develop these connections.
    *   **Source:** [CalMatters: How California legislators got more than $820,000 in travel in 2024](https://calmatters.org/politics/capitol/2024/09/california-legislator-travel-2023/)

### Legislative Record
Gabriel has authored and supported several measures directly related to pro-Israel advocacy and the protection of Israeli-American interests:
*   **House Resolution 99 (2022):** Gabriel introduced this resolution to officially recognize the contributions of Israeli-Americans in California. The measure was spearheaded in coordination with the **Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN)**.
    *   **Source:** [Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN): California Officially Honors Israeli-Americans](https://israelusa.org/california-officially-honors-israeli-americans/)
*   **Ethnic Studies Curriculum Advocacy:** Gabriel was a key figure in the 2020–2021 effort to remove "anti-Israel" and "anti-Zionist" content from California’s K-12 Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum. He successfully advocated for the inclusion of the **IHRA definition of antisemitism**, which includes certain criticisms of Israel as examples of antisemitism.
    *   **Source:** [The Times of Israel: No, California's governor did not sign an anti-Israel bill](https://blogs.timesofisrael.com/no-californias-governor-did-not-sign-an-anti-israel-bill/)
    *   **Source:** [J. The Jewish News of Northern California: Jewish groups applaud CA Democratic Party platform's wording on Israel](https://jweekly.com/2022/03/14/jewish-groups-applaud-ca-democratic-party-platforms-wording-on-israel/)
*   **AB 57 & AB 587:** These bills, authored by Gabriel, sought to improve the reporting of hate crimes and require social media platforms to disclose content moderation practices, specifically targeting "anti-Israel" and "antisemitic" content.
    *   **Source:** [J. The Jewish News of Northern California: Unanimously approved hate crimes bill awaits Newsom’s signature](https://jweekly.com/2021/09/20/unanimously-approved-hate-crimes-bill-awaits-newsoms-signature/)

### Organizational Endorsements and Financial Ties
Gabriel's 2020 campaign and subsequent committees have maintained documented relationships with pro-Israel political organizations:
*   **Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA):** Gabriel received the "Strong Support" recommendation from DFI-LA, an organization that requires candidates to complete questionnaires regarding their support for the State of Israel and their commitment to fighting the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   **Source:** [Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles: Endorsements](https://www.dfi-la.org/endorsed)
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC):** Gabriel is a frequent speaker and collaborator with JPAC, the largest combined Jewish social justice and advocacy network in California, which lobbies for pro-Israel legislation and security funding.
    *   **Source:** [The Times of Israel: Attending JPAC 2024](https://blogs.timesofisrael.com/attending-jpac-2024-lots-of-idealism-and-dedication/)

### Public Statements
Following the events of October 7, 2023, Gabriel released statements via the Jewish Caucus affirming Israel's "right and responsibility to defend its civilians" and stating, "We stand with Israel." He has also criticized "anti-Zionist advocacy" in professional and educational spaces, arguing it creates a "toxic climate" for pro-Israel students.
*   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus: Statement on Terrorist Attacks Against Israel](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2023-10-07-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks)
