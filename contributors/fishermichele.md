# Contributor Report: Fisher, Michele

### Overview
"Fisher, Michele" is a name frequently associated in donor databases with the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family office **Pisces Inc.** The primary principals of this family's philanthropy are **Doris Fisher** and her late husband **Donald Fisher**, along with their sons **Robert, William, and John Fisher**. The family is documented as a significant financial supporter of Zionist institutions, including the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
The donor identified as Michele Fisher is part of the lineage or philanthropic structure of the founders of **Gap Inc.** (which includes brands such as Old Navy and Banana Republic). **Pisces Inc.** serves as the family office and investment vehicle for the Fisher family, managing their multibillion-dollar fortune and facilitating their large-scale philanthropic initiatives.

### 2. Support for Zionist Advocacy and Israeli Institutions
The Fisher family’s philanthropic activities are heavily concentrated in organizations that promote Zionist identity, Israeli state security, and the "normalization" of Israel through educational and cultural programs.

#### A. Koret Foundation
The Fisher family maintains a close, long-term relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, one of the largest pro-Israel philanthropic organizations in the United States. The Koret Foundation is a primary funder of the **Koret Israel Program**, which supports economic development and policy initiatives in Israel, and the **Koret School of Veterinary Medicine** at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. The Fishers are frequently listed as major donors and strategic partners in Koret-led initiatives.
*   **Source:** Koret Foundation Annual Reports and Board Leadership (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Source:** Fisher Family Philanthropy at Congregation Emanu-El (Co-funded with Koret) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### B. Birthright Israel
The Fisher family (through the Doris & Donald Fisher Fund and Pisces Inc.) is a "prominent" donor to **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to foster Zionist identity and political connection to the state. The Fishers have contributed millions of dollars to the **Birthright Israel Foundation** to sustain these programs.
*   **Source:** Birthright Israel Foundation - Major Donors & Supporters List (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Source:** Jewish Federation Bay Area 2024 Annual Report (Listing Fisher Family/Michele Fisher) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### C. Jewish Federation of the Bay Area
"Michele Fisher" and other members of the Fisher family are documented as "Lion of Judah" or "Centennial Campaign" donors to the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**. Following the events of October 7, 2023, the family and their associated funds contributed to the Federation’s **Israel Emergency Fund**, which raised over $22 million for immediate aid and security infrastructure in Israel.
