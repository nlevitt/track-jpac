# Contributor Report: Allen, Ben

Ben Allen is a California State Senator representing the 24th District. Documentation indicates a record of active support for Israel through legislative leadership, policy advocacy, and participation in state-sponsored and private delegations.

### 1. Legislative Leadership and Advocacy
Allen has held senior leadership positions in the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, serving as its Vice-Chair and later as Chair. Through this platform, he has directed legislative agendas focused on strengthening California–Israel relations and opposing movements critical of the state.

*   **Anti-BDS Legislation:** Allen was a prominent supporter of **Assembly Bill 2844** (the California Combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions of Israel Act of 2016). This law requires state contractors to certify they are not engaged in boycotts of Israel. As Vice-Chair of the Jewish Caucus, Allen advocated for the bill’s passage to respond to global BDS efforts.
    *   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Senator Ben Allen Named Vice-Chair (2016)](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2016-12-02-senator-ben-allen-named-vice-chair-legislative-jewish-caucus)
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal - Anti-BDS Bill Progress](https://jewishjournal.com/news/united-states/184139/)
*   **Ethnic Studies Curriculum Intervention:** As Chair of the Jewish Caucus, Allen led efforts to modify California’s **Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum (ESMC)**. He and the Caucus successfully lobbied to remove content they characterized as "anti-Israel" and "pro-BDS," including references to Palestinian narratives of the Nakba. Allen advocated for the inclusion of "guardrails" to ensure that anti-Israel sentiment is excluded from public school instruction.
    *   **Source:** [Official Statement on Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum (2020)](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2020-05-29-senator-allen-statement-ethnic-studies)
    *   **Source:** [Jewish News Syndicate - Ethnic Studies Requirement and Guardrails](https://www.jns.org/ethnic-studies-requirement-for-high-schoolers-passes-california-legislature/)
*   **Support for State-Level Pro-Israel Policy:** Allen co-authored **SB 652**, which prohibits landlords and homeowners associations from banning the display of religious items like mezuzahs, a measure he promoted as part of his broader Jewish and pro-Israel legislative agenda.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal - State Caucus an Ally for the Jewish Community](https://jewishjournal.com/community/310323/state-caucus-an-ally-for-the-jewish-community/)

### 2. Documented Delegations to Israel
Allen has participated in multiple delegations to Israel, often funded by pro-Israel advocacy organizations or as part of official legislative missions.

*   **AJC Project Interchange (2018):** Allen was part of a 2018 delegation of California legislators to Israel funded by the **American Jewish Committee (AJC) Project Interchange**. The trip focused on "security issues" and bilateral trade, including visits to Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, and the Gaza border.
    *   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Trip to Israel (2018)](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2018-08-15-trip-jittery-israel-eye-opener-california-assemblymembers)
*   **Post-2022 Legislative Visit:** Allen documented a visit to **Kibbutz K'far Aza** in southern Israel in late 2022 or early 2023. In official correspondence, he cited this visit as foundational to his perspective on the security needs of the State of Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Official "From the Capitol" Report - Senator Ben Allen (2023)](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2023-12-21-capitol)

### 3. Public Statements and Institutional Ties
Allen maintains close ties with major Zionist and pro-Israel organizations, frequently speaking at their events and aligning with their policy goals.

*   **AJC Engagement:** Allen has appeared on AJC-sponsored platforms to discuss his efforts to remove "anti-Israel" content from California's educational system.
    *   **Source:** [AJC Passport Podcast - Discussion on Ethnic Studies (2019)](https://www.ajc.org/news/israel-blocks-members-of-congress-californias-ethnic-studies-curriculum-back-to-drawing-board)
*   **Equating Anti-Zionism with Antisemitism:** Critics have documented Allen's consistent rhetoric equating pro-Palestinian advocacy and anti-Zionism with antisemitism. This includes statements issued following the October 7, 2023, attacks, where Allen reaffirmed the Jewish Caucus's commitment to Israel's military actions and denounced campus activism as a threat to Jewish safety.
    *   **Source:** [Reverse Canary Mission - Ben Allen Profile](https://reversecanarymission.org/ben-allen/)
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Caucus Statement on Attacks Against Israel (2023)](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2023-10-07-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks)
