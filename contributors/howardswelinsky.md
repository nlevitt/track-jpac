# Contributor Report: Welinsky, Howard S.

Howard S. Welinsky is a prominent political donor and strategist within the Democratic Party, known for his leadership roles in pro-Israel organizations and his efforts to influence party policy regarding U.S.-Israel relations. He has balanced a career as a senior executive at Warner Bros. with decades of advocacy focused on strengthening ties between the United States and Israel.

### 1. Leadership Roles in Zionist & Pro-Israel Organizations
Welinsky holds or has held several high-level positions within organizations dedicated to Zionist advocacy and pro-Israel lobbying:

*   **AIPAC (American Israel Public Affairs Committee):** Welinsky serves on the national executive board of AIPAC, the primary pro-Israel lobbying group in the United States. He has stated that his federal advocacy is primarily focused on supporting AIPAC's goals in Washington, D.C.
    *   Source: [The LA Reporter](https://thelareporter.la/state-senate-candidate-took-money-from-donors-with-ties-to-aipac/)
    *   Source: [Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/1210/)
*   **Democrats for Israel (DFI) Los Angeles:** Welinsky was a founding member and served as the first president (1991–1994) of DFI Los Angeles. The organization was established to serve as a "bridge" between the Democratic Party and the pro-Israel community, specifically to counter anti-Israel sentiment within the party.
    *   Source: [Democrats for Israel Los Angeles - History](https://dfi-la.org/history/)
*   **Jewish Community Relations Committee (JCRC):** He served as the chair of the JCRC, a department of the Los Angeles Jewish Federation, where he managed government relations and community outreach focused on Jewish interests and Israel.
    *   Source: [Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/1210/)
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC):** Welinsky has chaired JPAC, which represents California’s Jewish community in Sacramento on legislative issues, including those related to Israel.
    *   Source: [UCLA Newsroom](https://newsroom.ucla.edu/stories/the-advocate)

### 2. Strategic Political Advocacy and Policy Influence
Welinsky is documented as a key operative in ensuring the Democratic Party platform maintains a pro-Israel stance, particularly at the state level in California.

*   **Blocking Critical Resolutions:** In 2003, Welinsky was identified as the "point man" for Jewish Democrats at the California Democratic Party convention. He successfully moved to table a resolution that characterized the Israeli-Palestinian conflict as the "heart of the antagonism" between the U.S. and the Muslim world, preventing its adoption to avoid what he described as "harmful" publicity for the party.
    *   Source: [The Forward](https://forward.com/news/7926/california-dems-table-israel-resolution/)
*   **Legislative Missions to Israel:** Welinsky has organized and participated in "missions" to Israel for state and local legislators. These trips are designed to "cement friendships" and foster pro-Israel sentiment among elected officials, such as former Los Angeles Mayor Antonio Villaraigosa.
    *   Source: [Jewish Journal](https://jewishjournal.com/community/1210/)
*   **Democratic National Convention:** He has served multiple times as a delegate to the Democratic National Convention and was a member of the National Democratic Convention Platform Committee in 1992, where he influenced party policy.
    *   Source: [UCLA Advocate in Action](https://advocacy.ucla.edu/advocate-in-action/howard-welinsky-shows-pride-through-politics/)

### 3. Political Contributions and Donor Activity
Welinsky uses financial contributions to support candidates who align with his advocacy for Israel.

*   **Targeted Donations:** He has made thousands of dollars in political contributions to Democratic candidates. While he also supports higher education causes, his donations are frequently linked to his pro-Israel profile. For example, he donated to state senate candidates while maintaining a public role as an advocate for AIPAC.
    *   Source: [The LA Reporter](https://thelareporter.la/state-senate-candidate-took-money-from-donors-with-ties-to-aipac/)
*   **Advocacy Alignment:** Welinsky has explicitly stated that "at some point I kind of realized that the two issues I cared about were higher education and Israel," and his donor activity reflects this dual focus.
    *   Source: [UCLA Advocate in Action](https://advocacy.ucla.edu/advocate-in-action/howard-welinsky-shows-pride-through-politics/)
