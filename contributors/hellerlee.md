# Contributor Report: Heller, Lee

Documentation identifies Lee Heller (frequently cited as Terry Lee Heller) as a political donor, activist, and writer based in New Mexico. Heller’s documented relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is established through philanthropic contributions to Jewish communal organizations and a significant body of writing that explicitly defends Israeli military policy and counters anti-Zionist narratives.

### 1. Political and Philanthropic Contributions
Lee Heller is a documented donor to several organizations that facilitate pro-Israel advocacy and Jewish communal life in the United States and Israel.

*   **Jewish Community Center (JCC) of Greater Albuquerque:** Heller is a consistent donor to the JCC, an organization that promotes Zionist ideals and maintains partnerships with Israeli institutions. In 2022, Heller was recognized as a "Mensch" donor and a contributor to the New Mexico Humanitarian Awards, which honor services reflecting "commitment to the ideals of Jewish life" and support for Israel.
    *   **Source:** [JCC of Greater Albuquerque 2022 Humanitarian Awards](https://www.jccabq.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/2022-NMHA-Program-Book-Final.pdf)
    *   **Source:** [NM Humanitarian Awards Donor List](https://nmhumanitarian.org/sponsors/)
*   **Jewish Federation of New Mexico:** Heller provides financial support to the Federation, which manages the "Israel Experience Fund" to provide scholarships for travel to Israel and supports the "New Mexico Jewish Link," a publication that covers Israeli news and advocacy.
    *   **Source:** [Santa Fe Middle East Watch - The Link (Winter 2021)](https://sfmew.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/Link-Winter-2021.pdf)
*   **Jewish Family Service (JFS) of New Mexico:** Heller is documented as a donor to JFS, specifically supporting programs that include Holocaust survivor support and community initiatives guided by Zionist principles.
    *   **Source:** [GuideStar - JFS New Mexico Impact Report](https://www.guidestar.org/ViewEdoc.aspx?eDocId=7585406&approved=True)

### 2. Zionist Advocacy and Writing
Heller is a prolific writer on platforms such as Quora, where she publishes articles and detailed responses that advocate for the State of Israel and the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) while explicitly challenging anti-Zionist arguments.

*   **Defense of Israeli Military Policy:** Heller frequently publishes content asserting that the IDF takes unprecedented measures to avoid civilian casualties. She has characterized the conflict following the October 7 attacks as a necessary struggle against "subhumans" and has stated that "all loss of life in Gaza is on the head of Hamas."
    *   **Source:** [Lee Heller - Quora Profile (Israel-Hamas Defense)](https://www.quora.com/How-should-Israel-respond-to-the-criticisms-of-mounting-deaths-of-Palestinian-civilians/answer/Lee-Heller-11)
*   **Combating Anti-Zionism:** In her writings, Heller argues that anti-Zionism is often a cover for antisemitism and propaganda. She has criticized Western youth for falling for "ignorance and propaganda" regarding identity politics in the Middle East and characterizes opposition to Israel's existence as a call for the "annihilation of Israel and the genocide of all Jews."
    *   **Source:** [Lee Heller - Response on Anti-Semitism and Hamas](https://www.quora.com/Since-Hamas-wants-all-Jews-to-be-killed-why-do-so-many-non-Islamists-support-them/answer/Lee-Heller-11)
*   **Community Activism:** Heller has been involved in local New Mexico initiatives such as "Run 4 Their Lives," a program dedicated to raising awareness for Israeli hostages and standing in "solidarity with survivors" of attacks against Israel.
    *   **Source:** [JCC Albuquerque - 2024 New Mexico Humanitarian Awards (Sarah Winger/Lee Heller Context)](https://www.jccabq.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/2024-NM-Humanitarian-Awards-Program-Book.pdf)

### 3. Alternative Documented Stance (Santa Barbara)
Notably, a separate individual named Lee Heller (PhD, Santa Barbara) is documented as an activist and writer who takes an opposing stance, frequently publishing letters critical of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and accusing the Israeli government of seeking to "cleanse Israel of the Palestinians." This highlights the distinction between the pro-Zionist donor in New Mexico and the critical activist in California.
