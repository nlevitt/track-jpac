# Contributor Report: Cisco Systems, Inc. California PAC

The **Cisco Systems, Inc. California PAC** is a political action committee associated with the American multinational technology conglomerate, Cisco Systems, Inc. While the PAC serves as a vehicle for political contributions, its relationship to Zionist advocacy and the State of Israel is primarily documented through the extensive R&D operations, military partnerships, and strategic investments of its parent company.

## 1. Parent Company Infrastructure and R&D in Israel
Cisco Systems maintains a significant and long-standing presence in Israel, which it characterizes as a "model of innovation" and a "Startup Nation."
*   **Economic Investment:** As of 2022, Cisco CEO Chuck Robbins stated the company had spent approximately **$7.2 billion** on acquiring Israeli companies and billions more in direct investments.
    *   *Source:* [Globes: Cisco CEO: Israel is a model of innovation](https://www.globes.co.il/en/article-cisco-ceo-israel-is-a-model-of-innovation-1001428784)
*   **Acquisitions:** Cisco has acquired over 20 Israeli startups, many of which were founded by veterans of the Israeli military's **Unit 8200**. Recent acquisitions include Robust Intelligence (2024) and Oort (2023).
    *   *Source:* [BDS Movement: Cisco Company Complicity Profile](https://bdsmovement.net/news/cisco-company-complicity-profile)
*   **R&D Centers:** Cisco operates major research and development facilities in Jerusalem, Tel Aviv, and Netanya, integrating Israeli technology into its global product line.
    *   *Source:* [Who Profits: Cisco Systems Profile](https://www.whoprofits.org/companies/company/3763?name=cisco-systems)

## 2. Documented Support for the Israeli Military (IDF)
Cisco is a primary technological architect for the Israeli Ministry of Defense (IMOD) and the Israel Defense Forces (IDF).
*   **Server Monopoly:** In 2017, Cisco won a tender to become the sole provider of servers for the Israeli military, a contract initially valued at over **$250 million**.
    *   *Source:* [AFSC Investigate: Cisco Systems Inc](https://investigate.afsc.org/company/cisco-systems)
*   **"David's Citadel" Data Center:** Cisco's hardware and software form the "digital nervous system" of David's Citadel, the IDF's largest underground data center in the Naqab (Negev), which centralizes military command and intelligence.
    *   *Source:* [OpenIntel: Cisco Executive Dossier](https://openintel.uk/reports/cisco/)
*   **"Israel Rises" Platform:** Following October 7, 2023, Cisco engineers co-developed the **"Israel Rises"** platform for the IDF Home Front Command to facilitate wartime logistical coordination.
    *   *Source:* [BDS Movement: Cisco Fact Sheet](https://bdsmovement.net/news/cisco-fact-sheet)

## 3. Support for Zionist Advocacy and Settlement Infrastructure
Cisco's activities extend to supporting infrastructure within occupied territories and providing financial avenues for Zionist organizations.
*   **Settlement Tech Hubs:** Cisco partnered with the Israeli government to establish "Klika" digital hubs, at least seven of which are located in illegal settlements in the occupied **West Bank** and **Golan Heights**.
    *   *Source:* [Business & Human Rights Resource Centre: Cisco's Involvement in the Occupation](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/ciscos-involvement-in-the-israeli-occupation/)
*   **Jerusalem Surveillance:** Cisco provided "Smart City" technology to the Jerusalem Municipality, including CCTV and communication equipment used to monitor residents in occupied **East Jerusalem**.
    *   *Source:* [Who Profits: The Israeli Occupation Industry - Cisco Systems](https://www.whoprofits.org/companies/company/3763?name=cisco-systems)
*   **Organizational Funding:** Cisco includes **Magen David Adom (MDA)** as a 100% matching-eligible organization in its Community Impact Portal, encouraging employee donations to the Israeli national emergency service.
    *   *Source:* [BDS Movement: Cisco Company Complicity Profile](https://bdsmovement.net/news/cisco-company-complicity-profile)

## 4. Political Advocacy and Leadership Statements
The company's leadership has explicitly aligned the firm's operations with Israeli state interests.
*   **CEO Public Statements:** In October 2023, CEO Chuck Robbins stated that Cisco teams were "working day and night" to ship technology to Israel "at the request of the country" during its military operations in Gaza.
    *   *Source:* [Medium: Letter from a Concerned Cisconian](https://medium.com/@bridge2humanity/letter-from-a-concerned-cisconian-7d58d9e6f212)
*   **Zionist Group Engagement:** Cisco leadership regularly meets with and is hosted by Zionist advocacy groups, including **AIPAC** and the **Israeli-American Council**, to discuss technological integration and regional security.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider: The retirement community that was a microcosm of the Florida special election](https://jewishinsider.com/2021/11/kings-point-florida-special-election-primary-dale-holness-sheila-cherfilus-mccormick/)
