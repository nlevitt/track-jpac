This report details the political donor **William Sydney Fisher** (commonly known as Bill Fisher) and his documented relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy, specifically through his involvement with the American Jewish Committee (AJC).

# Contributor Report: Fisher, William

### Background

### Leadership in Pro-Israel Advocacy
Fisher’s engagement with Zionist advocacy is primarily documented through his leadership roles and financial support for the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**, a major pro-Israel organization that describes its mission as advocating for "Israel’s peace and security" and combating anti-Zionism [1.4](https://victims.ca.gov/uploads/2021/01/2008NAFFApprovalList.pdf).

*   **National Board Service:** William S. Fisher has been identified as a member of the **National Board** (specifically the National Board of Governors) of the American Jewish Committee [1.3](https://www.bjpa.org/content/upload/bjpa/worl/WORLD%20JEWISH%20CONGRESS%20DECISIONS%20AND%20RESOLUTIONS%20OF%20THE%20SIXTH%20PLENARY%20ASSEMBLY%20FEB,%203-10%201975.pdf). 
*   **Financial Contributions:** Tax filings and organizational records document Fisher’s sustained financial support for the AJC. For instance, he is recorded as making significant donations to the AJC’s "Appeal for Human Relations" and general support funds [1.12](http://990s.foundationcenter.org/990_pdf_archive/010/010679337/010679337_200506_990.pdf), [1.14](http://990s.foundationcenter.org/990_pdf_archive/010/010679337/010679337_200506_990.pdf).

### Broader Support for Israel and Zionism
Beyond the AJC, Fisher’s broader philanthropic profile includes support for other organizations that reinforce Israeli state interests and Zionist education.

*   **Tel Aviv University and Social Programs:** Fisher has funded scholarships at **Tel Aviv University** and various social programs within Israel, including initiatives in periphery towns [1.4](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376125/jewish-philanthropist-may-suspend-aid-to-israel-over-diaspora-controversies/).
*   **Israel Bonds:** Financial records indicate that Fisher is a direct investor in the Israeli state through the purchase of **Israel Bonds**. In mid-2017 alone, he purchased over $1 million in Israel Bonds [1.2](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/).
*   **Policy and Advocacy:** Fisher has maintained a public stance on the importance of Diaspora support for Israel. In 2017, he used the threat of "suspending" his philanthropy to pressure the Israeli government regarding religious pluralism at the Western Wall, stating, "It needs to be appreciated how much Diaspora Jewry supports the state of Israel" and emphasizing his life-long commitment to the Jewish people [1.2](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/).
