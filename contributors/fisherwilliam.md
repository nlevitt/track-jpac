# Contributor Report: William Fisher

### 2. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
William Fisher and the broader Fisher family are documented contributors to major Zionist institutions and projects in Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel:** Fisher and the "Fisher Family" are identified as major financial stakeholders in the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which funds 10-day trips for Jewish young adults to Israel with the goal of strengthening Zionist identity. The family foundation (Doris & Donald Fisher Fund) is documented as a donor to Birthright at the "stakeholder" level ($100,000+).
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation Annual Reports/Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Koret Foundation:** Fisher is closely associated with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic organization that is one of the largest funders of pro-Israel causes in the United States. Koret has granted over $1 billion to causes including the **Jewish Community Federation** and various security and educational projects in Israel. William Fisher serves on institutional boards (such as the Exploratorium) alongside **Jeffrey Farber**, the CEO of the Koret Foundation.
