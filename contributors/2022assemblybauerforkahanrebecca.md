# Contributor Report: Rebecca Bauer-Kahan for Assembly 2022

### Political Profile
Rebecca Bauer-Kahan is a Democratic member of the California State Assembly representing the 16th District. Her 2022 campaign committee, "Rebecca Bauer-Kahan for Assembly 2022," received endorsements and support from several prominent pro-Israel organizations. She is an active member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus**, a body that advocates for Jewish interests and maintains a documented pro-Israel policy platform.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Public Statements and Self-Identification
Bauer-Kahan has explicitly described her political and personal alignment with Zionism in legislative settings. During a 2024 Senate Judiciary Committee hearing, she stated her support for the State of Israel's right to exist and identified with the term "Zionist."
*   **Source:** [Digital Democracy - Senate Judiciary Committee Transcript (June 25, 2024)](https://digitaldemocracy.org/hearing/3034?startTime=12361)

#### 2. Endorsements by Zionist Organizations
For her 2022 and 2024 election cycles, Bauer-Kahan received the endorsement of the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, an organization dedicated to maintaining pro-Israel support within the Democratic Party. She is also endorsed by the **Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area (JDCBA)**, which explicitly supports Israel as a "Jewish and democratic state."
*   **Source:** [Sydney Kamlager-Dove Endorsements (Listing DMFI and Bauer-Kahan)](https://www.sydneykamlager.com/endorsements)
*   **Source:** [Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area - 2022/2024 Endorsements](https://www.jdcsv.com/endorsements)

#### 3. Legislative Advocacy and "Safe Worship Zones"
Bauer-Kahan authored **AB 2664**, known as the "Safe Worship Zone" bill. This legislation was introduced specifically to address protests against Israel occurring outside synagogues and religious institutions, creating a 100-foot buffer zone to limit such demonstrations.
*   **Source:** [Times of Israel - California rep. proposes 'buffer zone' bill to limit protests near synagogues (March 21, 2024)](https://www.timesofisrael.com/california-rep-proposes-buffer-zone-bill-to-limit-protests-near-houses-of-worship/)
*   **Source:** [Jewish Telegraphic Agency - 'Buffer zones' come to California (March 20, 2024)](https://www.jta.org/2024/03/20/politics/buffer-zones-come-to-california-as-a-lawmaker-proposes-limiting-protests-near-houses-of-worship)

#### 4. Response to the October 7 Attacks and Gaza Conflict
Following the October 7, 2023 attacks, Bauer-Kahan co-signed an "anguished letter" with 18 other members of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus. The letter expressed "unapologetic" solidarity with Israel, affirmed its right to defend itself, and criticized anti-Zionist protests at the State Capitol.
*   **Source:** [J. The Jewish News of Northern California - California Jewish lawmakers send colleagues letter asking for support (January 4, 2024)](https://www.jweekly.com/2024/01/04/california-jewish-lawmakers-send-colleagues-anguished-letter-asking-for-support/)
*   **Source:** [Official California Legislative Jewish Caucus Statement (January 3, 2024)](https://jewishcaucus.asm.ca.gov/press-releases/jewish-caucus-sends-letter-colleagues-re-october-7-attack-rising-antisemitism-and-2024)

#### 5. Educational and Curriculum Advocacy
Bauer-Kahan was a primary proponent of **AB 715**, a bill aimed at combating antisemitism in schools. Critics of the bill, including the California Teachers Association, argued that the legislation could be used to "police" or censor classroom discourse regarding Palestine, Gaza, and Zionism by equating certain criticisms of Israel with antisemitism.

*   **Source:** [CalMatters - The fight over California's controversial antisemitism law (October 14, 2024)](https://calmatters.org/education/k-12-education/2024/10/california-antisemitism-schools-law/)
