# Contributor Report: Lee, Holden

Holden Lee is a financial executive and philanthropic administrator who serves as the Chief Financial and Investment Officer (CFIO) of the Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund of San Francisco. In this capacity, Lee manages the financial operations and investment strategies of an organization that functions as the largest funder of Jewish and Israel-related causes in California.

### 1. Professional Affiliation: Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco
Lee is documented as the CFIO and Assistant Treasurer of the Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (the Federation). He oversees the endowment's operations, the Investment Committee, and philanthropic services.

*   **Institutional Support for Israel:** The Federation's mission explicitly includes "strengthening Israel's place among the global community of nations" and fostering connections to Israel through grants and programming.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Community Federation - About Us](https://jewishfed.org/about)
*   **Funding of Israel-Based Organizations:** The organization distributes approximately $100 million annually, with a primary focus on Israel-based entities, including the Jewish Agency for Israel and the Israel Movement for Reform and Progressive Judaism.
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch: Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/jewish-community-federation-of-san-francisco-the-peninsula-marin-and-sonoma-counties/)
*   **BDS Restriction Policy:** In 2010, the Federation's board, of which the CFIO is a key officer, adopted formal guidelines that prohibit funding for organizations that "advocate for, or endorse, undermining the legitimacy of Israel," including those participating in the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   **Source:** [The Forward: Debate Over Israel Funding Guidelines](https://forward.com/news/128121/debate-over-israel-funding-guidelines-continues-to/)

### 2. Governance of Zionist Philanthropy: Libitzky Family Foundation
Lee serves as the Chief Financial Officer for the Libitzky Family Foundation, a subordinate organization operating under the fiscal sponsorship of the Federation.

*   **Pro-Israel Mission:** The foundation’s stated purpose is to "emphasize Israel's central role in vibrant Jewish life" and "strengthen Israel's place among the global community of nations."
    *   **Source:** [Cause IQ: Libitzky Family Foundation Profile](https://www.causeiq.com/organizations/libitzky-family-foundation,943328574/)
*   **Direct Advocacy Contributions:** Under Lee's financial management, the Libitzky Family Foundation has documented grants to the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) totaling $75,000 (2024) and $500,000 to the "Israel Emergency Fund" (2023-2024).
    *   **Source:** [Libitzky Foundation - Grant History](https://www.causeiq.com/organizations/libitzky-family-foundation,943328574/)

### 3. Advocacy and Institutional Leadership
Lee has held leadership roles within the broader Jewish Federation system aimed at mobilizing capital for Zionist causes and countering anti-Zionist initiatives.

*   **Anti-BDS Strategy:** Lee served as a co-chair for the 2020 Investment Institute, a convening of Jewish philanthropic leaders. A documented focus of this institute was addressing "anti-Israel activists... appropriating the language of socially responsible investment" and developing strategies to counter the "threat posed by the Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) movement within the world of institutional investment."
    *   **Source:** [JFN Investment Institute 2020 Leadership and Program](https://www.jfnainvestmentinstitute.org/program)
*   **Settlement Funding Scrutiny:** During Lee's tenure as CFIO, the Federation has been identified as a conduit for donor-directed grants to organizations operating in the West Bank, such as the Central Fund of Israel and Friends of Ir David (Elad). While some of these groups were later removed from the Federation's "acceptable" list, the institution continues to facilitate grants to organizations that support Jewish presence in East Jerusalem and the West Bank.
    *   **Source:** [J Weekly: SF Federation under scrutiny for grants supporting West Bank settlements](https://jweekly.com/2017/11/28/sf-federation-scrutiny-grants-supporting-west-bank-settlements/)

### 4. Documentation of Role and Compensation
Lee's role in directing the financial assets of the Federation is documented in annual tax filings, where he is listed as a "Key Employee" and officer.

*   **Financial Oversight:** ProPublica's Nonprofit Explorer confirms Lee's position as "CFIO/Assistant Treasurer" with oversight of total assets exceeding $1.4 billion.
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer: Jewish Community Federation of SF](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/941156533)
