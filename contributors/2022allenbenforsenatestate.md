# Contributor Report: Ben Allen for State Senate 2022

Ben Allen is a Democratic member of the California State Senate, representing District 24 (formerly District 26). The entity "Ben Allen for State Senate 2022" is his primary political campaign committee. Allen's political career is characterized by his leadership within the California Legislative Jewish Caucus and his legislative support for pro-Israel initiatives.

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and Anti-BDS Activity
Senator Ben Allen has been a primary advocate for legislation aimed at countering the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
*   **Anti-BDS Legislation (AB 2844):** In 2016, Allen co-sponsored and championed [AB 2844](https://www.miamiherald.com/news/politics-government/article66185882.html), a bill signed into law that prohibits the State of California from contracting with companies that participate in boycotts of Israel. Allen described Israel as a "critical trading partner" and argued that taxpayer dollars should not support what he characterized as prejudice against the state ([Source](https://www.miamiherald.com/news/politics-government/article66185882.html)).
*   **Memorandum of Understanding (MOU):** As a leader in the Jewish Caucus, Allen worked to reaffirm and strengthen California’s [Memorandum of Understanding with Israel](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2016-12-02-senator-ben-allen-named-vice-chair-legislative-jewish-caucus) on issues including renewable energy, cybersecurity, and biotechnology ([Source](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2016-12-02-senator-ben-allen-named-vice-chair-legislative-jewish-caucus)).

### 2. Leadership in the California Legislative Jewish Caucus
Allen has held senior leadership positions within the Jewish Caucus, which serves as a vehicle for pro-Israel advocacy in the state legislature.
*   **Chair and Vice-Chair Roles:** Allen was selected as Vice-Chair of the Jewish Caucus in 2016 and subsequently served as [Chair](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2018-12-12-senator-ben-allen-named-chair-california-legislative-jewish-caucus) starting in 2018 ([Source](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2018-12-12-senator-ben-allen-named-chair-california-legislative-jewish-caucus)).
*   **Ethnic Studies Curriculum Revisions:** Under Allen's leadership, the Jewish Caucus pressured the California Department of Education to revise the [Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum](https://www.ajc.org/news/state-senator-ben-allen-meets-with-ajc), successfully removing content that the caucus identified as having "anti-Israel bias" and references to the BDS movement ([Source](https://www.ajc.org/news/state-senator-ben-allen-meets-with-ajc)).

### 3. Engagement with Pro-Israel Organizations
Senator Allen maintains documented ties with several prominent Zionist and pro-Israel advocacy groups.
*   **Organizational Training:** Allen is a graduate of the [Jewish Federation's New Leaders Project](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/biography) and the Anti-Defamation League's (ADL) Glass Leadership Institute ([Source](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/biography)).
*   **Endorsements:** His 2022 campaign and broader political efforts have been endorsed by [Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles](https://www.dfi-la.org/endorsements), a group that requires candidates to demonstrate support for the State of Israel and opposition to BDS ([Source](https://www.dfi-la.org/endorsements)).
*   **American Jewish Committee (AJC):** Allen has held formal meetings with the [AJC](https://www.ajc.org/news/state-senator-ben-allen-meets-with-ajc) to coordinate on legislative priorities related to antisemitism and Israel advocacy ([Source](https://www.ajc.org/news/state-senator-ben-allen-meets-with-ajc)).

### 4. Travel and Public Statements
*   **Delegation Leadership:** In 2019, Allen led a [16-member legislative delegation](https://www.jewishjournal.com/commentary/opinion/310575/state-caucus-an-ally-for-the-jewish-community/) to Israel to meet with government officials and promote bilateral ties ([Source](https://www.jewishjournal.com/commentary/opinion/310575/state-caucus-an-ally-for-the-jewish-community/)).
*   **Post-October 7 Advocacy:** Following the October 7, 2023 attacks, Allen visited [Kibbutz Kfar Aza](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/from-the-capitol-senator-ben-allen-3) and issued statements affirming Israel's "right and responsibility to defend its civilians" ([Source](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/from-the-capitol-senator-ben-allen-3)).
*   **70th Anniversary Commemoration:** In 2018, Allen introduced resolutions in the Senate [honoring the 70th anniversary](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2018-05-16-senator-allen-honoring-70th-anniversary-state-israel) of the founding of the State of Israel ([Source](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2018-05-16-senator-allen-honoring-70th-anniversary-state-israel)).
