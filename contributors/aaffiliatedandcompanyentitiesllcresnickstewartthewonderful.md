This research report outlines the documented relationship between Stewart A. Resnick, his affiliated entities—including **The Wonderful Company, LLC** and the **Resnick Foundation**—and various pro-Israel and Zionist advocacy organizations.

# Contributor Report: Stewart A. Resnick & The Wonderful Company, LLC

Stewart A. Resnick and Lynda Resnick are the owners of **The Wonderful Company, LLC**, a multibillion-dollar agricultural conglomerate. Through their personal wealth and their private foundation, they have established a documented pattern of financial and institutional support for the State of Israel, its military, and Zionist advocacy groups.

### 1. Direct Financial Support for the Israeli Military
Tax records from the **Resnick Foundation** document consistent annual donations to the **American Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (AFIDF)**, an organization that provides for the wellbeing of Israeli soldiers.
*   **Total Contributions:** Between 2015 and 2022, the Resnick Foundation funneled approximately **$2.4 million** to AFIDF.
*   **Targeted Units:** In 2022, a donation of $200,000 was specifically split between the **IDF** and **Israel's Navy Seals (Shayetet 13)**.
*   **Yearly Breakdown:** Historical giving includes $100,000 in 2015, $350,000 annually from 2016–2018, $500,000 in 2019, $328,000 in 2020, and $228,000 in 2021.
*   **Source:** [MR Online: California Pistachio Billionaires Funding Israel's Occupation](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)

### 2. Institutional Leadership and Advocacy
The Resnicks hold or have held leadership roles in think tanks and universities that play significant roles in Zionist advocacy and Israeli security policy.
*   **Washington Institute for Near East Policy (WINEP):** Stewart and Lynda Resnick have served as trustees and members of the board of directors for WINEP. The institute was originally founded with ties to the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and is known for its pro-Israel policy research.
    *   **Source:** [MR Online: Board Roles and Influence](https://mronline.org/2024/02/21/california-pistachio-billionaires-funding-israels-occupation-regime/)
*   **Reichman University (formerly IDC Herzliya):** Stewart Resnick sits on the board of the **American Friends of Reichman University**. The couple has provided major scholarships for the Raphael Recanati International School to facilitate international students studying in Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Reichman University: Donor Recognition - Lynda and Stewart Resnick](https://www.runi.ac.il/en/about/runifriends/donor-recognition/lynda-and-stewart-resnick/)
*   **Simon Wiesenthal Center:** The Resnicks were early supporters of the Simon Wiesenthal Center and its Museum of Tolerance. Documentation from 2003 indicates a **$40 million** commitment to support the construction of a Museum of Tolerance in Jerusalem.
    *   **Source:** [Los Angeles Business Journal: Philanthropic Efforts for Simon Wiesenthal Center](https://labusinessjournal.com/news/going-down-for-count/)

### 3. Major Infrastructure and Research Pledges in Israel
The Resnicks have pledged more than $60 million toward Israeli academic and cultural institutions intended to bolster the nation's technological and historical status.
*   **Technion-Israel Institute of Technology:** In December 2022, the Resnicks pledged **$50 million** to establish the **Stewart and Lynda Resnick Sustainability Center for Catalysis**. The center is designed to bolster Israel's economy and its position as a global center of innovation.
    *   **Source:** [The Jerusalem Post: $50 Million Sustainability Center Donated to Technion](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/article-725521)
*   **National Library of Israel:** The Resnick Foundation provided a **$12 million** gift to support the library's renewal and the establishment of the **Lynda and Stewart Resnick Fellowship**, which focuses on specialized research areas including the history of the State of Israel.
    *   **Source:** [The Wonderful Company: A Lasting Legacy for Israel](https://csr.wonderful.com/articles/a-lasting-legacy-for-israel-and-the-world)

### 4. Affiliated Entities and Brands
As owners of **The Wonderful Company**, the Resnicks use profits from several globally recognized brands to fund the aforementioned activities. These brands include:
*   **Wonderful Pistachios & Almonds**
*   **POM Wonderful** (pomegranate juice)
*   **FIJI Water**
*   **Wonderful Halos** (mandarin oranges)
*   **JUSTIN and Landmark Wines**
*   **Teleflora** (floral delivery service)

Due to these documented financial ties, these brands have been identified by boycott movements such as the **BDS Coalition** as targets for consumers seeking to avoid supporting Israeli military and institutional expansion.
*   **Source:** [BDS Coalition: The Wonderful Company/Resnick Foundation Profile](https://bdscoalition.ca/the-wonderful-company-resnick-foundation/)
