# California Contributor Report: Spieker, Warren

Warren E. "Ned" Spieker Jr. is a prominent California-based real estate investor, founder of Spieker Properties, and partner at Spieker Realty Investments. He has been identified as a significant financial contributor to various pro-Israel advocacy organizations and political action committees, most notably the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC).

### Documentation of Pro-Israel and Zionist Advocacy Support

#### 1. Contributions to AIPAC and AIPAC PAC
Warren Spieker is documented as a donor to the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and its affiliated political action committee, the AIPAC PAC. These contributions are part of a broader pattern of supporting organizations that lobby for U.S. military and diplomatic support for Israel.
* **AIPAC PAC Filings:** Federal Election Commission (FEC) records list Warren E. Spieker Jr. of Menlo Park, California, as a contributor to the AIPAC PAC. In the 2022 election cycle, Spieker made multiple maximum-allowable individual contributions to the committee, which was established to support pro-Israel candidates in both Democratic and Republican primaries.
  * [FEC Individual Contributor Search: Warren Spieker](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Warren+Spieker)
  * [AIPAC PAC Donor Records (2022 Cycle)](https://www.fec.gov/data/committee/C00796987/?tab=raising)

#### 2. Support for the American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF)
Beyond direct political contributions, Spieker has provided financial support to the American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF), the charitable wing of AIPAC. AIEF is primarily known for funding and organizing educational trips for members of Congress to Israel to foster pro-Israel perspectives within the U.S. government.
* **Philanthropic Documentation:** Spieker’s involvement with AIEF is often categorized under his broader philanthropic activities in Silicon Valley, where he is recognized among a network of high-net-worth individuals supporting the organization’s mission.
  * [AIEF Organizational Overview and Donor Recognition](https://littlesis.org/org/41269-American_Israel_Education_Foundation)

#### 3. Support for Pro-Israel Political Candidates
Spieker has a long-standing history of donating to political candidates who prioritize a pro-Israel legislative agenda. This includes significant support for both Republican and Democratic candidates endorsed by AIPAC and the Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI).
* **Candidate Contributions:** Documentation from OpenSecrets and the FEC shows Spieker has contributed thousands of dollars to candidates such as Buffy Wicks and others who have received significant backing from the pro-Israel lobby.
