This report examines the political and philanthropic activities of **Doris F. Fisher**, her late husband **Donald G. Fisher**, and the **Fisher family** (founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.**) in relation to the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Doris and Donald Fisher (Fisher Family)

The Fisher family, through their family office **Pisces Inc.** and personal foundations, are among the most significant California-based donors to pro-Israel causes and Zionist organizations. Their support spans education, political advocacy, and direct institutional funding in Israel.

### 1. Institutional Support for Zionist Organizations
The Fisher family is documented as major contributors to organizations that facilitate Jewish identity-building and Zionist connection to Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel:** Doris and Donald Fisher are identified as "Mega Group" members and early partners who provided substantial funding to establish and sustain Birthright Israel (Taglit), a program that sends young Jews on trips to Israel to strengthen their connection to the Jewish homeland ([The Jewish Phenomenon](https://careerlanenigeria.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/steven-silbiger-the-jewish-phenomenon_-seven-keys-to-the-enduring-wealth-of-a-people-m-evans-company-2009.pdf); [Almanac of American Philanthropy](https://a-aaa.weebly.com/uploads/8/2/7/4/8274379/~philanthropyroundtable.pdf)).
*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fishers have a long-standing association with the Koret Foundation, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a "core commitment" to supporting essential Jewish institutions and Israel ([Jewish Home and Senior Living Foundation](https://www.jhslf.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/JSL-magazine-2011.pdf)). The Fisher family has frequently co-funded major projects with Koret, including cultural initiatives that travel to the **Israel Museum** in Jerusalem (SFMOMA/Koret Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Congregation Emanu-El:** The family are prominent members and donors to **Congregation Emanu-El** in San Francisco, which maintains strong institutional ties to the State of Israel and hosts frequent pro-Israel events and speakers ([J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/); [Congregation Emanu-El](https://www.emanuelsf.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/11/November-Emanu-el-Web.pdf)). (Note: The "Emanuel" in the contributor's name likely refers to this association or Donald Fisher's mother, **Aileen Emanuel**).

### 2. Israel Advocacy and Fellowships
The Fishers utilize their financial influence to support high-level diplomatic and academic connections between the U.S. and Israel.

*   **Fisher Family Fellowship:** The family funds the **Fisher Family Fellow** at the Harvard Kennedy School’s Belfer Center. In 2019, former Israeli Foreign Minister and prominent Zionist leader **Tzipi Livni** was appointed to this fellowship (Wikipedia: Tzipi Livni (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Institute for Jewish Law and Israeli Law:** The Fisher family foundation has been a contributor to the University of California, Berkeley, including seed funding for the **Berkeley Institute for Jewish Law and Israeli Law**, which promotes the study of the Israeli legal and economic system (UC Berkeley Law (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 3. Political Contributions and Family Office
The Fisher family operates through **Pisces Inc.**, which manages their multi-billion dollar fortune and facilitates their political influence.

*   **Pisces Inc.:** This entity is documented as a donor to candidates and causes that align with the family’s political interests, including support for pro-Israel and moderate political figures ([Fullerton Observer](https://fullertonobserver.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/10/ColorFO-44-17.pdf); [The Occidental Observer](https://www.theoccidentalobserver.net/2020/01/12/hedging-their-bets-who-really-decides-elections/)).
*   **Pro-Israel Political Funding:** Individual family members, such as sons **William S. Fisher**, **Robert J. Fisher**, and **John J. Fisher**, have a documented history of donating to political campaigns and PACs known for pro-Israel stances, such as **Democratic Majority for Israel** and AIPAC-affiliated efforts (Wikipedia: William S. Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); Wikipedia: John Joseph Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 4. Summary of Net Worth and Jewish Identity
*   **Net Worth:** Doris Fisher and her sons maintain multi-billion dollar net worths (estimated ~$2B-$4B each), largely tied to **Gap Inc.** and diversified through **Pisces Inc.** (Forbes Israel (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Public Identity:** They are frequently included in rankings of prominent Jewish philanthropists and billionaires whose giving strategy is aimed at strengthening the American Jewish community and its ties to Israel ([Jewish Telegraphic Agency](https://www.jta.org/2009/10/05/united-states/at-least-139-of-the-forbes-400-are-jewish)).
