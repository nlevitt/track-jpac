# Contributor Report: Sheryl Sandberg

Sheryl Sandberg, former Chief Operating Officer of Meta Platforms (formerly Facebook), is a documented supporter of Israel and a public advocate for Zionist causes. Her involvement includes multimillion-dollar financial contributions to Israeli institutions, the production of advocacy media, and leadership-level collaboration with pro-Israel organizations such as the Anti-Defamation League (ADL).

## Public Advocacy and Zionist Identity

Sandberg has explicitly identified herself as a "proud Zionist" in public forums. Following the events of October 7, 2023, she stated that her Jewish and Zionist identities became more central to her public persona.

*   **Zionist Identity:** At the Jewish Federations of North America’s 2024 General Assembly, Sandberg declared, "I sit on this stage as a proud Zionist and a proud Jew, in a way I wouldn’t have a year and a half ago."
    *   [Source: Jewish Federations of North America](https://www.jewishfederations.org/fed-news/sandberg-at-general-assembly-i-am-a-proud-zionist)
*   **Media Production:** Sandberg produced and fronted the 2024 documentary *Screams Before Silence*, which documents gender-based violence committed by Hamas on October 7. She has described this film as the "most important work" of her life.
    *   [Source: The Jerusalem Post](https://www.jpost.com/israel-news/article-785868)
*   **Curriculum Advocacy:** She has publicly advocated for the reform of K-12 education curricula to address what she describes as "anti-Zionist bias," arguing that anti-Zionism is frequently a "cover" for antisemitism.
    *   [Source: J. The Jewish News of Northern California](https://jweekly.com/2024/05/31/sheryl-sandbergs-oct-7-film-is-part-of-a-larger-personal-mission/)

## Organizational Relationships and Leadership

Sandberg maintains influential relationships with several pro-Israel and Jewish advocacy organizations, often serving as a primary donor and strategic collaborator.

*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** In 2019, Sandberg donated $2.5 million to the ADL to support its anti-bias programs in the U.S. and Europe. As COO of Meta, she participated in high-level meetings with ADL CEO Jonathan Greenblatt to negotiate platform policies on hate speech and Zionism.
    *   [Source: Anti-Defamation League](https://www.adl.org/resources/press-release/adl-receives-25-million-gift-sheryl-sandberg-honor-her-parents-75th)
    *   [Source: JTA](https://www.jta.org/2020/07/08/united-states/civil-rights-groups-leading-the-facebook-ad-boycott-met-with-mark-zuckerberg-they-say-nothing-has-changed)
*   **Jewish Community Federation:** She has held roles in the Jewish Community Federation’s Business Leadership Council, including serving as a keynote speaker.
    *   [Source: Wikipedia - Sheryl Sandberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sheryl_Sandberg#Other_work_and_ventures)
*   **International Diplomacy:** Sandberg has represented Israeli interests in international bodies, keynoting a special UN session titled "Hear Our Voices" in December 2023, sponsored by the Israeli mission to the United Nations.
    *   [Source: JNS](https://www.jns.org/sheryl-sandberg-un-silence-unacceptable-for-israeli-women-all-women/)

## Financial Contributions to Israeli Institutions

Through the Sheryl Sandberg & Dave Goldberg Family Foundation, Sandberg has directed millions of dollars to Israeli emergency and aid organizations.

*   **United Hatzalah:** In 2021, Sandberg donated $5 million to the Jerusalem-based volunteer emergency service United Hatzalah to fund a women’s unit named after her parents.
    *   [Source: Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/sheryl-sandberg-donates-5-million-to-united-hatzalahs-womens-unit/)
*   **IsraAID:** She contributed $2.5 million to IsraAID, an Israeli humanitarian aid organization that operates both domestically and internationally.
    *   [Source: Substack (Documenting IsraAID donation)](https://theconstructs.substack.com/p/is-sheryl-sandberg-an-israeli-agent)
*   **Hillel International:** She has supported Hillel International’s efforts to promote Jewish and Zionist identity on college campuses, specifically through campaigns like #OwnYourStar.
    *   [Source: eJewishPhilanthropy](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/your-daily-phil-sheryl-sandberg-displays-her-jewish-pride-a-philanthropic-couple-starts-a-new-accelerator-for-nyc-nonprofits/)
