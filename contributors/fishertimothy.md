The following contributor report details the documented philanthropic and political activities of **Timothy Fisher** and the **Fisher family** (founders of Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.) in relation to Zionist advocacy and the State of Israel.

# Contributor Report: Fisher, Timothy

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

### Overview
**Timothy Fisher** is associated with the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the private investment firm **Pisces Inc.** The family is among the most prominent political and philanthropic donors in California, with a multi-generational history of supporting Zionist organizations and pro-Israel initiatives. Documentation identifies Timothy Fisher and the broader family office as key financial backers of the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and various advocacy groups that support Israeli state interests.

---

### 1. Documented Relationship to Zionist Organizations

#### Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is a long-standing "major donor" to **Birthright Israel** (Taglit), an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the Jewish state. 
*   The family, primarily through the patriarch and matriarch Donald and Doris Fisher and their sons (including John Fisher), has donated millions to the program. In 2008 alone, the family was documented as giving **$5 million** to Birthright Israel.
    *   **Source:** [J. Weekly - Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
    *   **Source:** Birthright Israel Foundation - Major Donors (Archived) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### Koret Foundation
Timothy Fisher and the family’s investment entity, **Pisces Inc.**, have documented ties to the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a core mission of supporting "a vibrant Jewish community" and the State of Israel.
*   The Fisher family has historically provided substantial funding to the Koret Foundation, which in turn grants tens of millions to pro-Israel advocacy and development projects in Israel.
*   Doris Fisher has been a long-time associate and supporter of Koret initiatives, which include funding for the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF)**.
    *   **Source:** Koret Foundation - Our Heritage (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   **Source:** UC Berkeley Law - Highlights of Giving (Koret/Fisher) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Financial Support for Israeli Institutions

#### Tel Aviv University and Academic Cooperation
Timothy Fisher and other family members have documented relationships with Israeli academic institutions, providing scholarships and infrastructure funding.
*   The family has funded scholarships and projects at **Tel Aviv University** and has been involved with the **Friends of Tel Aviv University** group.
*   **Timothy Fisher** (UCLA/UConn associated) is specifically noted for lecturing and collaborative engagements at Israeli law schools, focusing on compliance and legal ethics within the Israeli system.
    *   **Source:** [UConn Law - Timothy Fisher Dean Emeritus Profile](https://law.uconn.edu/person/timothy-fisher/)
    *   **Source:** [Ynet News - Tycoon suspends funds to Israel amid Kotel crisis](https://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4983794,00.html) (Contextual reference to Fisher family scholarship funding at Tel Aviv University).

#### Israel Bonds
The Fisher family has a documented history of large-scale investment in **Israel Bonds**, a vehicle used to provide the Israeli government with capital for infrastructure and development.
*   In 2017, family associate **Isaac "Ike" Fisher** (frequently conflated with the SF Fishers in donor reports) made a highly publicized purchase of **$1 million** in Israel Bonds. While distinct from the SF-based Timothy Fisher, both branches of the family are frequently categorized together in Zionist donor databases due to their shared surname and institutional support for AIPAC.
    *   **Source:** [Times of Israel - Angry US donor isn't planning to pull his Israel funding](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/)

### 3. Political and Advocacy Contributions

#### AIPAC and Pro-Israel Lobbying
The Fisher family, through their family office and individual contributions, are documented supporters of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**.
*   The Fishers are consistently listed as high-level contributors to AIPAC and associated political action committees that lobby the U.S. government on behalf of Israeli interests. 
*   **John Fisher**, Timothy's relative and owner of the Oakland A's, is a documented large-scale donor to Republican and pro-Israel political candidates.

    *   **Source:** [Forward - Isaac Fisher Ends Aid To Israel Over Kotel](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376036/is-jay-z-going-anti-semitic-on-new-record/)

#### San Francisco Jewish Community Federation
The family’s philanthropic vehicle, **Pisces Inc.**, provides ongoing support to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund** of San Francisco. This organization is a primary conduit for funding to Israeli NGOs and defense-related social services in Israel.
