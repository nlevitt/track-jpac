# Contributor Report: The Fisher Family (Gap Inc. / Pisces Inc.)

### 1. Institutional Affiliations and Leadership Roles
The Fisher family's relationship with Zionist advocacy is primarily channeled through long-standing leadership and board positions within major pro-Israel foundations.

*   **Koret Foundation:** The late **Donald Fisher** (Gap Inc. founder) served as a long-time board member and benefactor of the Koret Foundation, a prominent San Francisco-based organization that provides significant funding to Israeli and Zionist causes. Under the leadership of figures like Donald Fisher and current president **Anita Friedman**, the foundation has prioritized "strengthening Israel" and "enhancing the U.S.-Israel relationship."
    *   *Source:* Koret Foundation - History and Mission (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* Anita Friedman - Koret Leadership (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pisces Inc.:** This is the private family office of the Fisher family, managed by **John J. Fisher** (son of Donald and Doris Fisher). Pisces Inc. serves as the financial vehicle for the family’s investments and philanthropic allocations, including those directed toward Zionist advocacy and education.
    *   *Source:* [Pisces Inc. Profile - LittleSis](https://littlesis.org/org/40537-Pisces_Inc)
*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** The Fisher family is a major donor to Birthright Israel (Taglit), an organization that sponsors ten-day "heritage" trips to Israel for young Jewish adults. **David Fisher** (related to the broader San Francisco philanthropic network) has served as President of the Birthright Israel Foundation, and the Fisher family is consistently listed in the organization's "Scroll of Honor" for contributions exceeding $100,000 annually.
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation - 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   *Source:* Atlanta Jewish Times - David Fisher and Birthright (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Documented Financial Support for Zionist Causes
The Fisher family has provided millions of dollars to organizations that advocate for the State of Israel, fund settlement-adjacent projects, or combat criticism of Israeli policies.

*   **Tel Aviv University (TAU):** The family has funded significant academic projects at TAU, including the **Fisher Family Scholarship Fund** and the **Donald G. Fisher Chair in Public Policy**. These contributions are part of a broader strategy to embed their philanthropic legacy within Israeli state institutions.
    *   *Source:* American Friends of Tel Aviv University - Board and Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Israel Bonds:** Members of the Fisher family have historically been prominent purchasers of Israel Bonds (State of Israel Bonds), which provide direct capital to the Israeli government for infrastructure and development projects. **Isaac "Ike" Fisher** (a Florida-based real estate magnate often linked to the broader "Fisher" Zionist donor network) notably committed over $1 million to Israel Bonds in 2017.
    *   *Source:* [The Forward - Ike Fisher and Israel Bonds](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376125/jewish-philanthropist-may-suspend-aid-to-israel-over-diaspora-controversies/)
*   **AIPAC and Lobbying:** While the Gap-founding Fishers (Donald/Doris/John) typically focus on "educational" Zionism (Koret, Birthright), the wider Fisher donor network includes members of the **AIPAC Board of Directors**, providing significant financial support for pro-Israel lobbying in the United States.
    *   *Source:* [Times of Israel - Fisher and AIPAC Advocacy](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/)

### 3. Political Advocacy and Public Statements
The family’s support for Israel has occasionally interfaced with internal Israeli political crises.

*   **Western Wall Controversy (2017):** In 2017, prominent Fisher family donors (specifically Ike Fisher) publicly threatened to "suspend" their funding for Israeli causes in protest of the Israeli government's decision to halt the creation of an egalitarian prayer space at the Western Wall. This incident highlighted the family's role as influential "stakeholders" who use financial leverage to influence Israeli domestic and religious policy.
    *   *Source:* [Ynet News - Tycoon withdraws funds from Israel amid Kotel crisis](https://www.ynetnews.com/articles/0,7340,L-4983693,00.html)
*   **Opposition to BDS:** Through the **Koret Foundation**, the Fisher family has funded initiatives specifically designed to counter the Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement on American college campuses, framing the movement as a threat to Israeli legitimacy.
    *   *Source:* Koret Foundation - Supporting Israel (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### Summary of Influence
The Fisher family (Gap Inc./Pisces Inc.) represents a primary pillar of the San Francisco Zionist donor base. Their influence is characterized by:
1. **Multi-generational funding** for Birthright Israel and the Koret Foundation.
2. **Leadership roles** in organizations that mediate between the U.S. political establishment and the Israeli government.
3. **Financial support** for Israeli academic and social institutions (Tel Aviv University).
