# California Contributor Report: Carson, Benjamin

This report details the relationship of Benjamin Carson to the State of Israel and his documented advocacy for Zionist causes. Carson, a retired neurosurgeon and former U.S. Secretary of Housing and Urban Development, has been identified in political records as a contributor with interests in California, including past business investments in Orange County.

### 1. Christians United for Israel (CUFI) Advocacy
Carson has a documented relationship with Christians United for Israel (CUFI), one of the largest pro-Israel advocacy organizations in the United States. He was a featured speaker at the CUFI Washington Summit, an annual event dedicated to mobilizing political support for Israel among American Christians.

*   **CUFI Washington Summit Participation:** In July 2015, Carson addressed the CUFI Washington Summit, where he joined other political figures in articulating pro-Israel policy positions and criticizing the Obama administration’s diplomatic approach to the Middle East.

### 2. Support for Israeli Settlements and Territorial Policy
Carson has publicly expressed support for the Israeli settlement movement in the West Bank (Judea and Samaria), a position that aligns with the nationalist right wing in Israel.

*   **Support for Settlements:** During a 2014 visit to Israel, Carson visited the settlement of Neve Daniel in the Gush Etzion bloc. He described the international criticism of Israeli settlement policy as "exaggerated" and stated that Israel's continued control of the West Bank "makes perfectly good sense" for security reasons.
