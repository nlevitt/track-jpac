# Contributor Report: Jacqui Irwin for Assembly 2024

Jacqui Irwin is a Democratic member of the California State Assembly representing the 42nd District. Her 2024 re-election campaign, "Jacqui Irwin for Assembly 2024," has documented ties to pro-Israel advocacy organizations and a consistent record of supporting Zionist policy priorities within the California Legislature.

## Affiliations and Endorsements
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC):** Irwin was endorsed by JPAC for the 2024 election cycle. JPAC identifies itself as the largest single-state coalition of Jewish organizations in the U.S., advocating for support of Israel and the Jewish community in Sacramento.
    *   [Source: JPAC Statement on Candidates](https://jpac-cal.org/jpac-announces-2024-general-election-endorsements/)
*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** Irwin is an active member of this caucus, which serves as a primary vehicle for pro-Israel legislation in California. The caucus maintains a formal partnership with JPAC to coordinate policy efforts related to Israel and antisemitism.
    *   [Source: California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Official Membership](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/officers-members)
    *   [Source: JPAC Partnership Documentation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#2017-2019:_JPAC_partnership,_increased_allocations,_ethnic_studies_guardrails)

## Legislative Record and Public Statements
*   **Opposition to Ceasefire and Support for Military Action:** In January 2024, Irwin signed a joint letter with the Legislative Jewish Caucus regarding the conflict in Gaza. The letter characterized the October 7 attack as a "massacre" and defended Israel's "right to defend itself" while omitting any call for a ceasefire.
    *   [Source: Joint Caucus Letter, January 3, 2024](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/sites/jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/files/cljc_letter_to_colleagues_-_01.03.24_0.pdf)
*   **2024 Priority Legislative Package:** Irwin is associated with a suite of bills introduced in 2024 designed to counter antisemitism, which critics argue conflate anti-Zionism with antisemitism. These include AB 2925 (requiring antisemitism training in DEI programs) and AB 715 (establishing an Antisemitism Prevention Coordinator).
    *   [Source: Jewish Caucus 2024 Priority Bills Press Release](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/press-release/jewish-caucus-announces-2024-priority-legislation)
*   **Anti-BDS Legislation:** Irwin voted in favor of AB 2844 in 2016, a bill that prohibits the state from contracting with companies that participate in the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement against Israel.
    *   [Source: California Assembly Vote Record AB 2844 (2016)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billVotesClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)

## Documented Advocacy and Lobbying
*   **Lobby Trips to Israel:** Irwin is identified by critics as a participant in or supporter of state-level legislative trips to Israel funded by Zionist organizations such as the Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles and the Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California. These trips are intended to foster diplomatic and economic ties between California and Israel.
    *   [Source: LA Progressive - Analysis of Legislative Jewish Caucus Activities](https://www.laprogressive.com/racism/ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)
*   **Critical Characterization:** Advocacy groups such as "Reverse Canary Mission" have documented Irwin's record, describing her as "complicit in apartheid" due to her caucus's statements characterizing support for Palestine as antisemitic and her alignment with pro-Israel lobby groups.
    *   [Source: Reverse Canary Mission - Jacqui Irwin Profile](https://reversecanarymission.org/jacqui-irwin/)

## Financial Support
*   **Pro-Israel Network Support:** While individual donor lists for the 2024 cycle reflect a broad base, her campaign is supported by the political networks of JPAC and Democrats for Israel-Los Angeles, which mobilize resources for candidates who align with their pro-Israel platforms.
    *   [Source: Democrats for Israel Los Angeles 2024 Endorsements](https://www.dfila.org/endorsements)
