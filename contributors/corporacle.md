# Contributor Report: Oracle Corp

Oracle Corporation (Oracle) maintains a documented relationship with the State of Israel characterized by multi-billion dollar infrastructure investments, the acquisition of domestic technology firms, and explicit ideological and financial support for Israeli military and government entities.

### 1. Corporate Infrastructure and Investment
Oracle has established significant physical and digital infrastructure within Israel, positioning the country as a "proof-lab" for its global sovereign cloud services.

*   **Underground Data Centers:** In October 2021, Oracle opened a 1.2 billion NIS ($319 million) underground public cloud data center in Jerusalem. Located 50 meters below ground, the facility is designed to withstand rocket attacks and provides services to the Israeli defense, government, and financial sectors.
    *   [Times of Israel: Oracle opens underground data center](https://www.timesofisrael.com/during-war-visit-oracle-ceo-affirms-commitment-to-open-second-data-center-in-israel/)
*   **Expansion Plans:** In 2024, Oracle CEO Safra Catz announced plans to double the company's investment in Israel by opening a second underground data center, intended to further secure the country’s sovereign cloud capabilities.
    *   [Times of Israel: Oracle CEO affirms commitment to second data center](https://www.timesofisrael.com/during-war-visit-oracle-ceo-affirms-commitment-to-open-second-data-center-in-israel/)
*   **Research & Development Centers:** Oracle operates R&D sites in Petah Tikva and Beersheba, employing approximately 400 staff focused on AI, cloud, and defense-related applications.
    *   [Globes: Oracle to establish tech center in Israel](https://www.globes.co.il/en/article-oracle-to-establish-tech-center-in-israel-943058)

### 2. Mergers and Acquisitions of Israeli Companies
Oracle has spent over $550 million acquiring Israeli technology firms, many of which were founded by veterans of specialized Israeli military units.

*   **Ravello Systems (2016):** Acquired for approximately $500 million, this cloud software company became a foundational part of the Oracle Public Cloud.
    *   [Jerusalem Post: Oracle acquires Ravello Systems](https://www.jpost.com/business-and-innovation/tech/oracle-acquires-israeli-company-ravello-systems-for-500-million-445802)
*   **Crosswise (2016):** Oracle acquired this big-data firm, founded by veterans of the IDF’s Unit 8200 (signals intelligence), for an estimated $50 million.
    *   [Jewish Telegraphic Agency: Oracle buys army vets' firm](https://www.jta.org/2016/04/15/israel/oracle-buys-israeli-big-data-firm-crosswise-for-reported-50-million)
*   **Iridize (2018):** Oracle acquired this Tel Aviv-based enterprise platform to integrate its contextual training technology into the Oracle Cloud.
    *   [Calcalist: Oracle acquires Iridize](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctech/articles/0,7340,L-3746682,00.html)

### 3. Military and Government Cooperation
Oracle actively pursues contracts with the Israeli government and maintains direct technical collaborations with its military and PR ministries.

*   **Project Nimbus Bid:** Oracle unsuccessfully bid for Project Nimbus, a $1.2 billion contract to provide cloud services to the Israeli government and military. After losing the contract to Google and Amazon, Oracle filed multiple legal appeals to overturn the decision, citing security concerns and the political stance of the winners' employees.
    *   [Calcalist: Oracle appeals Nimbus tender results](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctech/articles/0,7340,L-3912061,00.html)
*   **Collaboration with C4I Corps:** In July 2022, Oracle hosted soldiers and software developers from the IDF’s C4I Corps for training on using Oracle’s cloud infrastructure for military purposes.
    *   [BDS Movement: Oracle Complicity Profile](https://bdsmovement.net/news/oracle-complicity-profile)
*   **Propaganda Initiatives:** Following October 2023, Oracle employees collaborated with Israeli government ministries to develop "Words of Iron," a social media tool designed to elevate pro-Israel content and counter critical narratives online.
    *   [Business & Human Rights Resource Centre: Oracle suppresses Pro-Palestinian voices](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/oracle-allegedly-suppresses-pro-palestinian-voices-cuts-charity-support-amid-tiktok-takeover-bid/)

### 4. Leadership Advocacy and Zionist Philanthropy
Oracle’s leadership has explicitly stated that support for Israel is a core company value.

*   **Larry Ellison’s IDF Donations:** Oracle co-founder Larry Ellison has donated tens of millions of dollars to the **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)**. Confirmed donations include $10 million in 2014 and $16.6 million in 2017 to fund military wellbeing facilities.
    *   [ResearchGate: Ellison, Israel, and Influence](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/384457850_Ellison_Israel_and_Influence_Disentangling_Fact_from_Speculation_in_the_Oracle_Media_Nexus)
    *   [Times of Israel: Larry Ellison donates $16.6 million to FIDF](https://www.timesofisrael.com/larry-ellison-donates-16-6-million-to-idf-soldiers/)
*   **Safra Catz Corporate Mandate:** CEO Safra Catz has stated that Oracle’s "commitment to Israel is second to none" and suggested that employees who do not support the company's pro-Israel mission "maybe... aren't the right company for them."
    *   [Ynet News: Oracle execs stand with Israel](https://www.ynetnews.com/business/article/rk00q3f6p)
*   **Direct Humanitarian Aid:** Oracle donated $1 million to Magen David Adom in 2023 and provided double salary payments to its Israeli employees—including those serving as military reservists—during the onset of the conflict in Gaza.
    *   [Oracle: Oracle stands with Israel](https://www.oracle.com/il/news/announcement/recent-updates-in-israel/)
