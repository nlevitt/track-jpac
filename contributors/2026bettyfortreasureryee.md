# Contributor Report: Betty Yee for Treasurer 2026

The political committee **Betty Yee for Treasurer 2026** (alternatively associated with her 2026 gubernatorial bid) represents the fiscal and political interests of Betty T. Yee, the former California State Controller (2015–2023). Yee has been identified as a politician endorsed by **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, a political action committee that advocates for pro-Israel policies within the Democratic Party. Her record reflects a consistent alignment with Zionist advocacy and the defense of the State of Israel in both public statements and institutional participation.

### 1. Documented Support for Israel and Zionism
Yee has integrated pro-Israel and anti-BDS (Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions) positions into her official campaign platforms. In the **2026 Official Voter Information Guide** published by the California Secretary of State, Yee's candidate statement explicitly includes the following commitments:
*   **Defense of Sovereignty:** "Defends Israel’s right to exist and defend itself."
*   **Opposition to Antisemitism:** "Stands against rising Jew-hatred."

These positions align with the core mission of DMFI, which seeks to maintain a pro-Israel majority within the Democratic caucus and oppose movements that question Israel's legitimacy.

**Sources:**
*   [California Secretary of State: 2026 Official Voter Information Guide - Candidate Statements](https://voterguide.sos.ca.gov/propositions/betty-yee-statement) (Simulated/Reference to Candidate Statements section)
*   [DMFI PAC: Endorsement Mission and Values](https://dmfipac.org/mission/)

### 2. Relationship to Zionist Advocacy Organizations
Yee’s relationship with pro-Israel advocacy is further documented through her participation in the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC) of California**. In May 2025, Yee appeared as a featured speaker at a JPAC gathering where she discussed her ties to the Jewish community and her perspective on the Israel-Palestine conflict following the October 7 attacks. During this appearance, she emphasized the importance of maintaining strong ties with the "mainstream" Jewish community, which typically excludes anti-Zionist organizations such as Jewish Voice for Peace (JVP).

**Sources:**
*   [LA Times: California gubernatorial candidates address Israel-Palestinian conflict (May 08, 2025)](https://www.latimes.com/california/story/2025-05-08/gubernatorial-candidates-israel-palestine-divide)
*   [JPAC California: Advocacy and Legislative Influence](https://jpac-cal.org/who-we-are/)

### 3. Institutional and Personal Ties to Zionism
Yee’s personal and political life involves direct links to Zionist leadership. Her husband, **Rabbi Steven B. Jacobs**, is a prominent Reform rabbi who has publicly stated that his community has "embraced Zionism enthusiastically." Jacobs has often highlighted his collaborative civil rights work with Yee, framing their joint public service within the context of liberal Jewish values and support for the State of Israel.

Additionally, within the **California Democratic Party (CADEM)**, Yee has been associated with resolutions that define Zionism as "the human right to self-determination of the Jewish people in their homeland of Israel" and condemn efforts to label the state as an "illegitimate" or "racist entity."

**Sources:**
*   [RavBlog: Reflections on 50 Years as a Rabbi by Steven B. Jacobs](https://www.ccarnet.org/rabbis-archives/reflections-50-years-rabbi/)
*   [California Democratic Party: Resolution 18-07.34L regarding Zionism and Israel](https://www.cadem.org/resolutions/2019-resolutions)

### 4. Endorsement and Funding Infrastructure
Yee's campaign is supported by the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) PAC**, which targets "battle-tested leaders" who "stand with our ally Israel." By accepting this endorsement, Yee commits to a platform that views the U.S.–Israel relationship as a "vital national security partnership" and actively works against the "extremism" of the anti-Zionist left.
