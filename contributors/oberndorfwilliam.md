# Contributor Report: William Oberndorf

William Ernst Oberndorf is an American billionaire investor and hedge fund manager based in San Francisco. He is the chairman of Oberndorf Enterprises and a co-founder of SPO Partners. Oberndorf is a significant donor to Republican and centrist causes, with a documented history of financial support for pro-Israel organizations and Zionist advocacy.

### Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

Oberndorf’s support for Israel is documented through both direct philanthropic contributions to Jewish communal organizations and political donations to groups and candidates that advocate for pro-Israel policies.

#### 1. Philanthropic Support for Israeli Causes
Oberndorf and his wife, Yael, are listed as donors to the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**. The Federation’s 2024 records indicate that it mobilized over **$22 million** for its "Israel Emergency Fund" following the October 7, 2023, attacks to provide aid and relief in Israel.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Federation Bay Area 2024 Annual Report](https://jewishfed.org/impact/2024-annual-report)

#### 2. Financial Support for Pro-Israel Political Entities
Oberndorf has been identified as part of a network of billionaire donors who fund major pro-Israel political action committees, including **AIPAC** and **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**. These groups have spent millions of dollars to influence U.S. elections in favor of candidates who support "hardline pro-Israel policies" and unconditional military aid to Israel.
*   **Source:** [NJ Today: Booker bats for billionaires, but betrays bottom 80 percent of Americans](https://njtoday.news/2025/12/17/booker-bats-for-billionaires-but-betrays-bottom-80-percent-of-americans/)

#### 3. Support for Pro-Israel Candidates
Oberndorf has consistently donated to U.S. political figures who are primary recipients of pro-Israel PAC funds and vocal supporters of the Israeli government. Notable recipients of his contributions include:
*   **Senator Cory Booker:** Identified as a key pro-Israel advocate in the Senate, Booker has received significant financial backing from Oberndorf.
*   **Senator Chuck Schumer, Senator Mark Kelly, and Senator Kyrsten Sinema:** Oberndorf has made notable donations to these Democratic figures, all of whom have strong ties to AIPAC-aligned donors.
*   **Senator Tim Scott:** A prominent Republican recipient of Oberndorf’s donations who advocates for hawkish pro-Israel foreign policy.
*   **Sources:** [SFGATE: The Q2 political donations of Thiel, other rich Californians](https://www.sfgate.com/politics/article/billionaire-donations-sf-2022-17336214.php); [NJ Today: Booker’s Billionaire Backers](https://njtoday.news/2025/12/17/booker-bats-for-billionaires-but-betrays-bottom-80-percent-of-americans/)

#### 4. Alignment with Zionist Interests
Political observers and media outlets have categorized Oberndorf alongside other influential "pro-Israel hawks" and "pro-Israel donors," such as Paul Singer and Seth Klarman. His political spending is frequently aligned with candidates and measures that prioritize Israeli security and the maintenance of strong U.S.-Israel military ties.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Insider: Daily Kickoff](https://jewishinsider.com/2016/05/daily-kickoff-sanders-to-push-for-change-in-democrats-israel-policy-at-convention-who-donates-to-ploughshares-john-malones-uja-bar-mitzvah/)

### Political Influence and Strategy
In addition to his focus on Israel, Oberndorf utilizes his wealth to influence local San Francisco and national politics through "dark money" and Super PACs. He is the founder and primary funder of **Neighbors for a Better San Francisco**, a group that has spent millions on law-and-order causes and the recall of progressive officials, often seen as a strategy to shift the political landscape toward more centrist or right-aligned interests.
*   **Source:** [Mission Local: How one group quickly became the 800-pound gorilla of SF politics](https://missionlocal.org/2024/04/neighbors-for-a-better-san-francisco-super-pac-donors/)
