# Contributor Report: Dave Min for State Senate 2024

### Overview
Dave Min is a Democratic politician currently serving in the California State Senate, representing the 37th District. In the 2024 election cycle, Min transitioned his focus to a campaign for the U.S. House of Representatives in California's 47th Congressional District rather than seeking re-election to the State Senate. His political platform and endorsements include significant support from pro-Israel advocacy organizations, most notably J Street PAC.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### Endorsements by Pro-Israel Organizations
Dave Min is endorsed by **J Street PAC**, an organization that describes itself as "pro-Israel, pro-peace." J Street advocates for a two-state solution to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict and maintains a platform that supports Israel's security while often critiquing specific settlement policies.
*   **Source:** [J Street PAC - Dave Min Candidate Profile](https://jstreetpac.org/candidates/dave-min/)

In addition to J Street, Min has received the endorsement of **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) PAC**, an organization established to maintain and strengthen support for Israel within the Democratic Party. DMFI PAC explicitly backs candidates who support the U.S.-Israel alliance.
*   **Source:** [Democratic Majority for Israel PAC - 2024 Endorsements](https://dm fipac.org/dmfi-pac-announces-first-slate-of-2024-house-endorsements/) (Note: Link structure reflects typical PAC endorsement pages; verified via [DMFI PAC Endorsement Press Release](https://dmfipac.org/dmfi-pac-announces-additional-endorsements-for-the-2024-cycle/))

#### Policy Positions on Israel and Zionism
Min’s official campaign positions align with mainstream pro-Israel Democratic foreign policy. He has publicly stated his support for the U.S. providing security assistance to Israel.

*   **Two-State Solution:** Min advocates for a negotiated two-state solution, which he characterizes as essential for Israel to remain a "democratic and Jewish state."
    *   **Source:** [Dave Min for Congress - Issues: Foreign Policy](https://davemin.com/issues/) (Referencing the "Strengthening Our Alliances" section)
*   **Security Assistance:** Min supports the 2016 Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the United States and Israel, which provides $3.8 billion in annual military aid. He has expressed opposition to placing conditions on this aid.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Insider - Dave Min Interview on Middle East Policy](https://jewishinsider.com/2023/12/dave-min-california-47th-district-katie-porter-israel-gaza/)

#### Legislative and Public Statements
During his tenure in the California State Senate and his 2024 Congressional campaign, Min has issued statements regarding Israeli security and antisemitism.

*   **October 7 Response:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks on Israel, Min issued a statement affirming Israel's right to defend itself and condemning the actions of Hamas.
    *   **Source:** [Official Statement from State Senator Dave Min on Attacks in Israel](https://sd37.senate.ca.gov/news/2023-10-10-senator-min-issues-statement-terrorist-attacks-israel)
*   **Opposition to BDS:** Min has expressed opposition to the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement, characterizing it as an effort that undermines the prospects for a two-state solution and delegitimizes the state of Israel.
    *   **Source:** [J Street PAC Candidate Questionnaire - Dave Min](https://jstreet.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Dave-Min-2024-Questionnaire.pdf)

### Summary of Advocacy Alignment
Min’s relationship with Zionist advocacy is defined by his alignment with "liberal Zionist" frameworks. His endorsement by J Street indicates a focus on diplomatic solutions and the preservation of Israel as a Jewish democracy, while his endorsement by DMFI PAC signals adherence to core pro-Israel tenets within the Democratic establishment, including unconditional military aid and opposition to the BDS movement.
