# California Contributor Report: Josh Newman for Senate 2024

### Identification and Political Profile
**Josh Newman** (Joshua Botts Newman) is a Democratic politician who represented the 29th District in the California State Senate from 2016 to 2018 and again from 2020 to 2024. He was the principal candidate for the **'Josh Newman for Senate 2024'** campaign committee (ID #1435010) during his bid for the 37th District. Newman is a consistent member of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)**, a body that advocates for Jewish institutional interests and maintains a stated mission of supporting the State of Israel and countering anti-Israel narratives in California.

### Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
Newman’s record reflects a pattern of legislative and public support for Israel, characterized by opposition to the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement and advocacy for Israeli security interests.

#### 1. Opposition to the BDS Movement
Newman has participated in collective legislative actions to delegitimize the BDS movement.
*   **2024 Anti-BDS Letter:** On January 3, 2024, Newman signed a formal letter with the Legislative Jewish Caucus addressed to his colleagues. The letter explicitly characterized the BDS movement as a campaign "whose goal is to destroy Israel's economy and Israel itself."
    *   **Source:** Official California State Document - Jewish Caucus Letter 2024 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **2017 Strategy to Combat BDS:** Shortly after being sworn into office, Newman attended a January 2017 retreat with the Jewish Caucus where members discussed "how to combat the Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) movement against Israel." The caucus sought to expand upon California’s 2016 anti-BDS law (AB 2844).
    *   **Source:** Jewish Journal - Jewish State Legislators Report (Jan 2017) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### 2. Legislative Advocacy for Zionist Interests
Newman has used his position as Chair of the Senate Education Committee and as a caucus member to influence state policy regarding Israel.
*   **Ethnic Studies "Guardrails":** Newman and the Jewish Caucus successfully advocated for the inclusion of "guardrails" in California’s Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum. These measures were designed to ensure that "anti-Jewish and anti-Israel content cannot be included" in K-12 instruction and to integrate the IHRA definition of antisemitism, which classifies certain criticisms of Israel as antisemitic.
    *   **Source:** Wikipedia - California Legislative Jewish Caucus Records (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Halal and Kosher School Meals (SB 1080):** Newman authored SB 1080, which requires schools to provide religiously compliant meals. While presented as a general inclusivity measure, it was a primary legislative goal for the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)** and the Jewish Caucus to support institutional Jewish needs.
    *   **Source:** [California State Senate - SB 1080 Analysis](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=202320240SB1080)

#### 3. Institutional and Financial Support
Newman’s political career has been bolstered by pro-Israel organizations and caucus-affiliated funding.
*   **Caucus Funding:** During his 2017 legislative session, the Jewish Caucus used its political arm to support Newman's campaign, including $3,000 in direct contributions.
    *   **Source:** Jewish Journal - 2017 Caucus Political Activity (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Relationship with JPAC:** Newman maintains a close partnership with JPAC, the largest statewide Jewish advocacy group in the U.S. JPAC’s mission includes "advocating for the right of Israel to exist as a peaceful, democratic and pluralistic Jewish state."
    *   **Source:** JPAC California - Mission and Advocacy (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### Critical Response
Newman’s alignment with Zionist advocacy has drawn criticism from civil rights and anti-Zionist organizations. Critics have described Newman and the CLJC as a "pro-Israel faction" within the legislature that works to "erase Palestinians from the political and educational discourse" and pressures public universities to suppress student activism critical of the Israeli government.
