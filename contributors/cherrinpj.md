# Contributor Report: Pj Cherrin

Pj Cherrin (also known as Philip Cherrin) is a philanthropic strategist and professional fundraiser currently serving as the Associate Vice President for the Northwest Region at the **Birthright Israel Foundation**. He is also the founder of **Cherrin Philanthropy**, a consulting firm that advises donors and boards on Jewish communal growth and strategic giving.

### Professional Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy
Cherrin’s professional career is centered on organizations dedicated to strengthening the connection between the Jewish diaspora and the State of Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Cherrin serves as the Associate VP of the Northwest Region for the Birthright Israel Foundation, an organization that raises funds to send young Jewish adults on educational trips to Israel. The foundation's stated mission is to "strengthen Jewish identity, Jewish communities, and connection with Israel" among the next generation.
    *   **Source:** [Birthright Israel Foundation - Stephen Grand Legacy Report](https://birthrightisrael.foundation/blog/stephen-grand-zl-a-legacy-that-will-forever-impact-birthright-israel-and-the-jewish-people/)
    *   **Source:** [Birthright Israel Foundation - LGBTQ+ Initiatives](https://birthrightisrael.foundation/blog/securing-the-future-of-lgbtq-birthright-israel-trips-in-memory-of-bob-beleson/)
*   **Zionist Advocacy and Youth Engagement:** In his role, Cherrin facilitates large-scale donations intended to "ensure Jewish continuity" through immersive experiences in Israel. He has described Birthright Israel as the "gold standard" for establishing "relationships with the land and people of Israel."
    *   **Source:** [Birthright Israel Foundation - Regional Leadership](https://birthrightisrael.foundation/blog/stephen-grand-zl-a-legacy-that-will-forever-impact-birthright-israel-and-the-jewish-people/)
*   **LGBTQ+ Zionist Outreach:** Cherrin has been a vocal advocate for the intersection of LGBTQ+ identity and Zionist commitment. He has led mission trips to Israel for LGBTQ+ individuals and has worked to fund specific Birthright trips for this demographic, framing Israel as a destination where "LGBTQ+ participants are encouraged to bring their full identity."
    *   **Source:** [Pride Source - P.J. Cherrin Profile](https://pridesource.com/article/14545/)
    *   **Source:** [Birthright Israel Foundation - LGBTQ+ Memorial Fund](https://birthrightisrael.foundation/blog/securing-the-future-of-lgbtq-birthright-israel-trips-in-memory-of-bob-beleson/)

### Political and Communal Contributions
Cherrin is documented as a donor and leader within several pro-Israel communal structures.

*   **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area:** Cherrin is listed as a contributor to the Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund, which directs significant grants to organizations in Israel and supports "pro-Israel and Jewish students" through various campus initiatives.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Federation Bay Area - 2023 Annual Report](https://jewishfed.org/impact/2023-annual-report/) (See Contributor Lists)
*   **Political Endorsements:** Cherrin has publicly supported political candidates based on their "pro-Israel" stances. In 2014, he advocated for the election of Rudy Hobbs to the U.S. Congress, specifically citing Hobbs' "pro-Israel stance" as a primary qualification.
    *   **Source:** [Lisa's Shabbat Shalom - July 2014 Newsletter](http://lisashabbatshalom.com/2014/07/)
*   **Communal Leadership:** He has served on the boards of multiple organizations that facilitate Jewish-Israeli relations, including the Jewish Community Council and the San Francisco Interfaith Council.
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer - San Francisco Interfaith Council](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/943110996)
    *   **Source:** [San Francisco Interfaith Council - 2018 Board List](https://www.sfinterfaithcouncil.org/30th-anniversary-observance-of-interfaith-winter-shelter)

### Philanthropic Strategic Advising
Through his firm, **Cherrin Philanthropy**, Cherrin advises major donors on "legacy outcomes" often involving the support of Zionist institutions and Jewish continuity programs. His network includes "political figures" and "Jewish and interfaith leaders" focused on communal growth.
*   **Source:** [Cherrin Philanthropy - Official Site](https://cherrinphilanthropy.com/)
