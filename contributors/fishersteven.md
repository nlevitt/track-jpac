The individual identified as "**Fisher, Steven**" (often documented as **Stephen Fisher** or **Steven Fisher**) is a member or key representative of the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, known as the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family investment office **Pisces, Inc.** The Fishers are among California's most prominent political and philanthropic donors, with a documented history of substantial support for Zionist organizations and Israeli institutions.

# Contributor Report: Fisher, Steven

### Background and Corporate Affiliation
Steven (Stephen) Fisher is a principal figure associated with **Pisces, Inc.**, the family office that manages the wealth of the Fisher family, founders of the retail giant **Gap Inc.** [1.5, 1.15]. The family’s philanthropic activity is primarily channeled through the **Fisher Family Foundation** and individual contributions from the heirs of Donald and Doris Fisher [1.11, 1.14].

*   **Pisces, Inc. / Pisces Foundation:** The family office is directly linked to the Fisher heirs, including Robert (Bob), William, and John Fisher, who have served in leadership roles within Gap Inc. and oversee the family’s strategic investments [1.15, 1.3].
*   **Gap Inc. Founders:** Donald and Doris Fisher founded Gap Inc. in 1969. Their wealth has historically been a significant source of funding for pro-Israel and Zionist advocacy in the United States [1.5, 1.11].

### Support for Zionism and Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a "prominent Zionist donor" group, providing consistent financial backing to organizations that support the state of Israel and promote Zionist ideology in the U.S. and abroad [1.4, 1.23].

*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fishers are long-standing supporters of the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which funds "heritage trips" for young Jewish adults to Israel with the goal of strengthening their connection to the Jewish state [1.2, 1.3]. Family members and their foundations are frequently listed in the "Scroll of Honor" for contributions at the $250,000 to $499,999 level or higher [1.2, 1.7].
*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family has a documented relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization that identifies "Support for Israel" as one of its core pillars [1.3, 1.9]. The Fishers have co-funded numerous projects with Koret, including the **Jewish Community Center of San Francisco (JCCSF)**, where the family's contributions are memorialized in the "Fisher Family Hall" [1.5, 1.11].
*   **Jewish Community Federation (San Francisco):** The Fishers are major contributors to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which allocates millions of dollars annually to Israeli social projects, security, and Zionist advocacy [1.5, 1.7].

### Political and Institutional Advocacy
Steven Fisher and the broader Fisher family utilize their financial influence to support political causes and institutions that align with pro-Israel interests.

*   **AIPAC and Advocacy:** Members of the Fisher family, most notably **William S. Fisher**, have been linked to the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and other lobbying efforts intended to maintain U.S. political and military support for Israel [1.3, 1.4].
*   **Education and "Israel Studies":** The family has funded academic initiatives related to Israel, including support for the **Helen Diller Institute for Jewish Law and Israel Studies** at UC Berkeley, which seeks to promote a Zionist perspective within higher education [1.5, 1.9].
*   **Museums and Cultural Zionism:** The Fishers are significant sponsors of institutions like the **Contemporary Jewish Museum** in San Francisco and the **Galicia Jewish Museum**, which focus on Jewish history and the preservation of identity as it relates to the Zionist movement [1.10, 1.13].

### Documented Financial Contributions
*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Listed as top-tier donors ($250k+) in 2021 and 2024 annual reports [1.2, 1.7].
*   **Jewish Community Federation:** Documented as "longtime supporters" and "major donors" to the Federation’s endowment and Israel-focused funds [1.5, 1.21].
*   **Koret Foundation Collaborations:** Frequent project-based partnership and co-sponsorship of Zionist-aligned initiatives in the San Francisco Bay Area [1.11, 1.23].
