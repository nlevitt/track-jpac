This report documents the relationship between the lobbying firm Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck, LLP (BHFS), its leadership, and organized pro-Israel and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck, LLP

### 1. Organizational Profile and Leadership
Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck, LLP is a Denver-based law and lobbying firm that has consistently ranked as one of the highest-grossing federal lobbying firms in the United States. Its co-founder and chairman, **Norman Brownstein**, is a central figure in the firm's political and Zionist advocacy efforts.

*   **Norman Brownstein Roles:** Brownstein currently serves as the Chairman of the Board of BHFS and has been identified as a "longtime board member" and former **Vice President** of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC).
    *   [Source: Brownstein Hyatt Farber Schreck - Norman Brownstein Bio](https://www.bhfs.com/people/attorneys/a-b/nbrownstein)
    *   [Source: ProPublica - AIPAC 2024 Tax Filing/Board List](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/530217164)
*   **Political Recognition:** In 2024, *The Jerusalem Post* included Brownstein in its list of the "50 Most Influential Jews," specifically noting his "decades of leadership, advocacy and philanthropy that have strengthened the U.S.-Israel relationship."
    *   [Source: Jerusalem Post - 50 Most Influential Jews 2024](https://www.jpost.com/influencers/50jews-24/item-820547)

### 2. Documented Zionist Advocacy and Lobbying
The firm and its leadership engage in systematic lobbying to advance Israeli state interests and counter anti-Zionist movements.

*   **IHRA Definition Enforcement:** Norman Brownstein is credited as a "key player" in urging the U.S. government to enforce the International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA) definition of antisemitism. This definition includes several examples related to the State of Israel, which critics argue conflates anti-Zionism with antisemitism.
    *   [Source: BHFS News - Jerusalem Post Profile of Norman Brownstein](https://www.bhfs.com/insights/press-releases/2024/norman-brownstein-named-among-the-jerusalem-post-s-50-most-influential-jews)
*   **Anti-BDS Activity:** Brownstein has personally lobbied top-tier political candidates to oppose the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement. In 2016, he reportedly pressured Hillary Clinton’s campaign to maintain a platform that opposed "third party intervention via the U.N." and rejected the "delegitimize[ation] of Israel" through BDS.
    *   [Source: Mondoweiss - Brownstein and the Clinton Campaign](https://mondoweiss.net/2016/06/surrogates-palestinian-influence/)
*   **Protection of Israel as a Lobbying Justification:** When questioned about the firm's representation of Saudi Arabia in the wake of the Jamal Khashoggi killing, BHFS shareholder Al Mottur defended the relationship by citing Saudi Arabia's role as "a crucial ally... in protecting Israel."
    *   [Source: The Colorado Sun - BHFS Saudi Lobbying and Israel](https://coloradosun.com/2018/10/26/brownstein-hyatt-farber-schreck-saudi-arabia-lobbying/)

### 3. FARA Registrations and Foreign Representation
BHFS has registered under the Foreign Agents Registration Act (FARA) to represent Israeli-linked entities and other regional actors whose interests align with Zionist security frameworks.

*   **Movement for Quality Government in Israel (MQG):** BHFS registered as a U.S. policy consultant for this Israeli NGO. The filing stated the firm represented MQG regarding "potential violations of U.S. laws impacting governance matters in the State of Israel."
    *   [Source: eFile FARA - BHFS Supplemental Statement (See Israel Section)](https://efile.fara.gov/docs/5870-Supplemental-Statement-20220330-30.pdf)
*   **Regional Strategy:** The firm’s extensive work for Middle Eastern governments, including Saudi Arabia and Egypt, is frequently framed in lobbying disclosures as maintaining regional stability essential for Israeli security.
    *   [Source: FARA.gov - BHFS Registration for Saudi Ministry of Foreign Affairs](https://efile.fara.gov/docs/5870-Exhibit-AB-20160927-14.pdf)

### 4. Affiliated Organizations and Institutional Ties
The leadership of BHFS maintains governing roles in major Zionist and pro-Israel educational and legal institutions.

*   **Simon Wiesenthal Center:** Norman Brownstein is a Trustee of the Simon Wiesenthal Center, an organization that promotes Zionist history and operates the Museum of Tolerance Jerusalem.
    *   [Source: National Jewish Health - Norman Brownstein Professional Profile](https://www.nationaljewish.org/about/leadership/board-of-directors/norman-brownstein)
*   **Museum of Tolerance Jerusalem:** Brownstein is a board member of this institution, which is a major infrastructure project in Jerusalem focused on Jewish identity and Israeli state narrative.
    *   [Source: BHFS Official Site - Leadership and Community](https://www.bhfs.com/about/community)
