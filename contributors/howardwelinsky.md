# Contributor Report: Welinsky, Howard

### **Overview**
Howard Welinsky is a retired entertainment executive and a long-term political advocate focusing on the U.S.-Israel relationship. He is documented as a founding leader in partisan pro-Israel organizations and has held numerous leadership positions in national and regional Zionist advocacy groups. His activities primarily involve legislative lobbying, building relationships between elected officials and the Israeli government, and managing party platforms to exclude resolutions critical of Israel.

---

### **Leadership Roles in Zionist Organizations**

*   **National Jewish Democratic Council (NJDC):** Welinsky served as a board member of the NJDC, an organization that historically lobbied within the Democratic Party for policies supporting the state of Israel and maintained a "pro-Israel" watchdog function over the political right and left.
    *   **Source:** [Wikipedia - National Jewish Democratic Council Leadership](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Jewish_Democratic_Council#NJDC_leadership)
*   **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC):** Welinsky is a member of the AIPAC Executive Board. In personal interviews, he has identified his primary role in Washington, D.C., as advocating for Israel on behalf of AIPAC.
    *   **Sources:** [Jewish Journal (1998)](https://jewishjournal.com/community/2375/); [The LA Reporter (2025)](https://thelareporter.la/candidates-in-race-for-progressive-state-seat-shy-away-from-aipac/)
*   **Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA):** Welinsky was a founding member and served as the first president of DFI-LA (1991–1994). The organization was established to counter "anti-Israel efforts" within the California Democratic Party and to educate party activists on Middle Eastern policy from a pro-Israel perspective.
    *   **Source:** [DFI-LA History](https://www.dfi-la.org/history)
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee (JPAC) of California:** Welinsky served as the Board Chair of JPAC from 1997 to 1998. JPAC serves as the largest regional Jewish advocacy network in the U.S., focusing on legislative ties between California and Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish California - Our Team (Past Chairs)](https://www.jewishcal.org/our-team)
*   **Jewish Community Relations Committee (JCRC):** He has held multiple roles within the Los Angeles Jewish Federation's JCRC, including serving as chair and vice chair of the Israel and Government Relations commissions.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal (1998)](https://jewishjournal.com/community/2375/)

---

### **Political Advocacy and Zionist Strategy**

*   **Party Platform Management:** Welinsky has been described by colleagues as the "point man" for ensuring the Democratic Party platform remains aligned with pro-Israel interests. In 2003, he worked behind the scenes with AIPAC to successfully table a resolution at the California Democratic Party convention that sought to call for peace negotiations and social integration between Muslim nations and Israel, stating that such resolutions could be "harmful" if publicized.
    *   **Source:** [The Forward (2003)](https://forward.com/news/7970/california-dems-table-israel-resolution/)
*   **Legislative Missions to Israel:** Welinsky has coordinated and participated in "missions to Israel" for state and national legislators to cement relationships and influence policy perspectives on the U.S.-Israel relationship.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Journal (1998)](https://jewishjournal.com/community/2375/)
*   **Candidate Vetting and Criticism:** Welinsky has publicly challenged Democratic candidates whose statements on Israel he deems insufficient. In 2025, he criticized a state senate candidate's statement regarding suffering in Gaza, stating it was not "broad" enough in its support for Israel and that he would "take it up with her directly."
    *   **Source:** [The LA Reporter (2025)](https://thelareporter.la/candidates-in-race-for-progressive-state-seat-shy-away-from-aipac/)

---

### **Political Contributions**

Welinsky is a frequent donor to Democratic candidates who support a strong U.S.-Israel alliance. His contribution history is characterized by a dual focus on higher education and pro-Israel advocacy.
*   **Pro-Israel Candidate Support:** Recent documentation identifies Welinsky as a donor with direct ties to AIPAC advocacy, contributing to candidates such as Sasha Renee Perez and other state-level figures who align with AIPAC's policy goals.
    *   **Source:** [The LA Reporter (2025)](https://thelareporter.la/state-senate-candidate-took-money-from-donors-with-ties-to-aipac/)
*   **Broad Donor History:** UCLA advocacy reports highlight his "thousands of dollars" in political contributions aimed at supporting politicians who share his "commitment... to Israel."
    *   **Source:** [UCLA Newsroom (2001)](https://newsroom.ucla.edu/magazine/the-advocate)
