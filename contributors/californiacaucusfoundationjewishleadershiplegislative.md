# Contributor Report: California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership Foundation

The **California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership Foundation** (CJLCLF) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that serves as the financial and programming vehicle for the [California Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://jewishcaucus.legislative.ca.gov/) (CLJC), a bipartisan group of state legislators. The foundation’s primary activities involve funding international travel for lawmakers, sponsoring policy forums, and advancing a legislative agenda centered on strengthening ties between the State of California and Israel.

### 1. Direct Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

The foundation and its associated caucus officially advocate for the "U.S.-Israel relationship" and the promotion of Israeli interests within California's public policy framework.

*   **Sponsorship of Legislative Delegations to Israel:** The CJLCLF is the primary sponsor of "study tours" that fly California legislators to Israel. In July 2022, the foundation spent more than **$213,000** to take a delegation of 14 legislators to Israel. The trip was funded by major Zionist-affiliated donors, including the **Koret Foundation** and the **Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles**.
    *   *Source:* [California Fair Political Practices Commission (FPPC) Form 807 - Travel Disclosure (May 2023)](https://www.fppc.ca.gov/content/dam/fppc/NS-Documents/LegalDiv/807-Forms/2023/May/CA%20Jewish%20Legislative%20Caucus%20Foundation%20Form%20807%20-%20May%202023.pdf)
    *   *Source:* [CalMatters: Groups spend big bucks to send CA lawmakers on sponsored trips](https://calmatters.org/politics/2023/05/california-legislators-travel-nonprofits/)
*   **Support for the California-Israel Strategic Partnership:** The caucus was a primary advocate for the **2014 Memorandum of Understanding (MOU)** signed by Governor Jerry Brown and Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu. This agreement established a formal framework for economic and technological cooperation between California and Israel, covering sectors such as water conservation, cybersecurity, and biotechnology.
    *   *Source:* [Official Text: 2014 California-Israel Strategic Partnership MOU (ca.gov)](https://www.gov.ca.gov/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/3.5.14_Israel_MOU.pdf)
*   **Anti-BDS Legislation (AB 2844):** The caucus authored and successfully campaigned for **Assembly Bill 2844 (2016)**, which prohibits the state from contracting with companies that participate in a boycott of Israel. The law is a documented component of broader Zionist advocacy to counter the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   *Source:* [California Legislative Information: AB-2844 Public contracts: Discrimination (2015-2016)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)

### 2. Legislative and Educational Policy

The CJLCLF influences state education and civil rights policy through a framework that frequently conflates anti-Zionism with antisemitism.

*   **IHRA Definition Advocacy:** The caucus has successfully advocated for the incorporation of the **International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance (IHRA)** working definition of antisemitism into California’s K-12 Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum. This definition includes "claiming that the existence of a State of Israel is a racist endeavor" as an example of antisemitism, a position that critics argue suppresses Palestinian narratives.
    *   *Source:* [Wikipedia: California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Legislative Efforts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#2017-2019:_JPAC_partnership,_increased_allocations,_ethnic_studies_guardrails)
*   **Legislative "Guardrails" on Ethnic Studies:** In 2024, the caucus introduced bills such as **AB 715** and **AB 1468**, which seek to prohibit "anti-Israel" narratives in public schools. Critics from the **UC Ethnic Studies Faculty Council** and **Jewish Voice for Peace** have documented these efforts as a campaign to "censor Palestinian voices" and police classroom content that is critical of Israeli state policy.
    *   *Source:* [CodePink: CA Israel Lobby Aims to Police Ethnic Studies](https://www.codepink.org/ca_israel_lobby_aims_to_police_ethnic_studies)
    *   *Source:* [LA Progressive: Who Are They? The CA Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://www.laprogressive.com/progressive-issues/ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)

### 3. Institutional Partnerships and Funding

The foundation operates in close coordination with established Zionist lobbying organizations to ensure a unified policy front.

*   **Partnership with JPAC:** The foundation works in direct partnership with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)**, which identifies itself as the "largest single-state coalition of Jewish organizations in the nation." JPAC’s stated mission includes "combat[ing] campaigns to delegitimize and demonize Israel, including the Boycott Divestment Sanctions Movement (BDS)."
    *   *Source:* [JPAC California Official Website - Advocacy Issues](https://jpaccentral.org/advocacy/advocacy-issues/)
*   **Corporate Contributions:** The CJLCLF receives financial support from major corporations. For example, **Amazon** disclosed the foundation as a recipient of its "U.S. Political Engagement" contributions in 2022, 2023, and 2024.
    *   *Source:* [Amazon 2022 Political Engagement Policy and Statement (Annex A)](https://s2.q4cdn.com/299287126/files/doc_downloads/2023/Amazon-2022-Political-Engagement-Policy-and-Statement.pdf)
    *   *Source:* [Digital Democracy: Contributor Profile - California Jewish Legislative Caucus Leadership Foundation](https://digitaldemocracy.calmatters.org/organizations/california-jewish-legislative-caucus-leadership-foundation)
