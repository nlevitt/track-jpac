# California Contributor Report: Howard F. Ahmanson / Fieldstead & Company

This report examines the documented relationship between Howard F. Ahmanson Jr., his private firm Fieldstead & Company, and their support for pro-Israel advocacy and Christian Zionist causes.

### 1. Overview of Philanthropic Vehicle
Howard F. Ahmanson Jr. manages his political and charitable contributions primarily through **Fieldstead & Company**, an unincorporated private office in Irvine, California. Unlike traditional foundations, this structure allows for direct giving that does not require the same public disclosure of specific grantees as a 501(c)(3) foundation.

### 2. Documented Support for Christian Zionist Advocacy
Ahmanson is identified as a major financier of organizations that promote Christian Zionism—a movement within Christianity that advocates for the State of Israel based on biblical theology.

In addition to promoting theological conservatism, the IRD specifically advocates for "support for Israel" within mainline Protestant denominations. Critics and researchers identify the IRD as a primary vehicle for moving American churches toward a pro-Israel political alignment.

### 4. Strategic Context: Israel as a "Wedge Issue"
Documentation suggests that Ahmanson's funding of pro-Israel causes is part of a broader "wedge" strategy. This involves using support for Israel and other social issues to challenge liberal or progressive leadership within American religious institutions, aiming to realign them with conservative political goals.
