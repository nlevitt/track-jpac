# Contributor Report: Adrin Nazarian for Assembly 2022

Adrin Nazarian is a former member of the California State Assembly (2012–2022) whose campaign committee, **Adrin Nazarian for Assembly 2022**, has been identified as a vehicle for a political figure with a documented record of support for Israel and Zionist advocacy. Nazarian, an associate member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, has utilized his legislative office to co-author resolutions and support policies that affirm the state's relationship with Israel and oppose the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.

### 1. Legislative Support for Israel and Anti-BDS Policy
Nazarian has consistently supported and co-authored legislation that institutionalizes support for Israel and penalizes opposition to it.
*   **Assembly Bill 2844 (2016):** Nazarian was a **Principal Coauthor** of AB 2844, also known as the "California Combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions of Israel Act of 2016." The law requires companies entering into state contracts worth $100,000 or more to certify that they are not participating in a boycott of Israel. The bill explicitly identifies the nation and people of Israel as protected from discriminatory business practices.
    *   [AB 2844 Full Bill Text](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201520160AB2844)
*   **House Resolution 48 (2021):** Nazarian co-authored this resolution, which recognizes the month of May as Jewish American Heritage Month and specifically celebrates the contributions of **Israeli Americans**. The resolution emphasizes that California is home to the largest Israeli population in the United States and the largest population outside of the State of Israel.
    *   [HR 48 Status and Co-authors](https://trackbill.com/bill/ca-hr48-relative-to-israeli-americans/2091490/)

### 2. Institutional Affiliation with Zionist Advocacy
Through his membership in official caucuses and partnerships with advocacy groups, Nazarian has integrated pro-Israel objectives into his legislative agenda.
*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC):** Nazarian is an **associate member** of the CLJC, a group dedicated to Jewish institutional interests. The caucus's mission includes "countering anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" and "strengthening California–Israel economic, technological and cultural ties."
    *   [Jewish Caucus Membership and Mission](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)
    *   [J-Weekly: Jewish caucus a powerful advocate in Sacramento](https://jweekly.com/2017/03/16/strength-in-numbers-jewish-caucus-a-powerful-advocate-in-sacramento/)
*   **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC):** Nazarian has maintained a "steadfast" alliance with JPAC, the primary state-level advocacy body for California’s organized Jewish community. JPAC lobbies for increased state funding for Jewish institutions and coordinates with the Jewish Caucus on Israel-related policy.
    *   [JPAC Advocacy and Legislative Partnership](https://jpac-cal.org/)

### 3. Recognition and Awards
Nazarian’s support for these interests has been formally recognized by leading Zionist and Jewish advocacy organizations.
*   **Jerry Sampson Memorial Award (2022):** JPAC honored Nazarian with this award for his "leadership and advocacy on behalf of the organized Jewish community’s priority concerns." During the award ceremony, he was described as a "steadfast ally" and a "vocal ally in favor of community security initiatives."
    *   [JPAC Award Ceremony - Assemblymember Adrin Nazarian Honored](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R2rX6v-f_oM)
*   **Bridge-Building and Shared Diaspora Advocacy:** As an Iranian-Armenian legislator, Nazarian has positioned himself as a "bridge" between the Armenian and Jewish communities, often linking the two through shared interests in "maintaining identity" across global diasporas, which includes support for the State of Israel.
    *   [Armeniapedia: Adrin Nazarian Biography and Caucus Membership](https://www.armeniapedia.org/wiki/Adrin_Nazarian)

### 4. Campaign Committee Context
The committee **Adrin Nazarian for Assembly 2022** (FPPC ID# 1414436) served as Nazarian’s primary fundraising vehicle during his final term in the State Assembly. While Nazarian did not seek reelection in 2022, the committee remained active as he transitioned to a 2024 bid for the Los Angeles City Council, continuing to represent a political record defined by the aforementioned legislative actions and Zionist advocacy.
