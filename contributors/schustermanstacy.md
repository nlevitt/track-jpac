# Contributor Report: Stacy Schusterman

Stacy Schusterman is the Chair of Charles and Lynn Schusterman Family Philanthropies (SFP), a philanthropic organization through which she directs significant financial and advocacy resources toward Israeli interests and Zionist initiatives. Under her leadership, the foundation maintains an explicit commitment to Zionism and the strengthening of Israel as a "secure homeland for the Jewish people."

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Zionist Mission
Stacy Schusterman assumed the role of Chair of Charles and Lynn Schusterman Family Philanthropies in 2018, having previously served as Co-chair alongside her mother, Lynn Schusterman. The foundation's documented vision for its work in Israel is "rooted in a proud embrace of the vision of Zionism" as defined in Israel's Declaration of Independence.

*   **Official Mission Statement:** The foundation states its work in Israel is dedicated to strengthening the country as a "secure homeland for the Jewish people" and an ally of the United States.
    *   **Source:** [Schusterman Family Philanthropies - Funding in Israel](https://www.schusterman.org/what-we-do/funding-in-israel)
*   **Strategic Directives:** Schusterman has publicly advocated for prioritizing pro-Israel advocacy, stating at a 2024 Jewish Funders Network meeting that supporters "need to not shy away from explaining Israel’s story on the Hill."
    *   **Source:** [Wikipedia: Stacy H. Schusterman (Politics and Philanthropy section)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stacy_H._Schusterman#Politics_and_philanthropy)

### 2. Documented Support for Zionist Advocacy and Groups
Schusterman and her foundation provide financial and strategic support to several major organizations focused on Zionist advocacy, campus influence, and political lobbying.

*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** Schusterman is a primary donor to DMFI, a group that advocates for pro-Israel policies within the Democratic Party. Records show she contributed approximately $1.55 million to the organization and its PAC.
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch: Stacy H. Schusterman (Political Activity)](https://www.influencewatch.org/person/stacy-h-schusterman/)
*   **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC):** Schusterman serves on the Board of Trustees of AIPAC. Her foundation also provides funding to the American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF), which is the charitable arm of AIPAC that funds trips to Israel for members of Congress.
    *   **Source:** [Sapir Journal Interview: Stacy Schusterman](https://sapirjournal.org/israel-at-war/2024/11/showing-up-as-jews-for-the-benefit-of-all/)
    *   **Source:** [Wikipedia: Schusterman Family Philanthropies - Israel Funding](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_and_Lynn_Schusterman_Family_Philanthropies#Israel)
*   **Israel on Campus Coalition (ICC):** The foundation is a major funder and a founding partner of the ICC, which coordinates campus strategies to support Israel and counter the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   **Source:** [Schusterman Family Philanthropies: Israel Education and Engagement](https://www.schusterman.org/what-we-do/jewish-community/israel-education-and-engagement)
    *   **Source:** [Electronic Intifada: ICC and Schusterman Funding](https://electronicintifada.net/content/israel-campus-coalition-and-david-project-sponsored-us-oil-and-israeli-bank-profits/5567)

### 3. Funding for Israel-Engagement Initiatives
The Schusterman family has been instrumental in founding and funding programs designed to foster a connection to Israel among young Jewish adults and academics.

*   **Birthright Israel:** The Schustermans were among the founders of Birthright Israel and continue to provide significant funding to the Birthright Israel Foundation to facilitate free trips to Israel for young adults.
    *   **Source:** [eJewishPhilanthropy: Birthright Israel Foundation 25th Anniversary](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/birthright-israel-foundation-celebrates-25-years-with-220m-raised-toward-new-900m-campaign/)
*   **The Israel Institute:** Founded with funding from Schusterman Family Philanthropies, this institute focuses on expanding the field of "Israel Studies" in U.S. higher education to influence academic discourse regarding the state of Israel.
    *   **Source:** [Schusterman Family Philanthropies: Our Jewish Community Grantees](https://www.schusterman.org/what-we-do/jewish-community/our-jewish-community-grantees)

### 4. Financial Records
Tax filings for the Charles and Lynn Schusterman Family Foundation demonstrate the scale of the organization's grantmaking, which totals hundreds of millions of dollars annually distributed across its portfolios, including substantial allocations for Israel-based social and advocacy projects.

*   **IRS Form 990-PF (2023):** The foundation reported total assets exceeding $2.2 billion and charitable disbursements of over $375 million.
    *   **Source:** [ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer: Charles and Lynn Schusterman Family Foundation 2023 Filing](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/731312965)
