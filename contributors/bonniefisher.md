# Contributor Report: Fisher, Bonnie

### 1. Identity and Affiliations
Bonnie Fisher, typically referred to in legal and professional contexts as **Elizabeth S. Fisher**, is the wife of **Robert J. (Bob) Fisher**, the former Chairman of Gap Inc. and a son of Gap founders Donald and Doris Fisher. She is a co-founder and trustee of the **Pisces Foundation**, which manages a significant portion of the family's philanthropic capital.

*   **Primary Corporate Affiliations:** Gap Inc., Pisces Inc. (Family Office).

*   **Relationship to Zionism:** Documented as a high-net-worth donor to central Zionist institutions, including the Koret Foundation and Birthright Israel.

### 2. Relationship to Zionist Institutions

#### Koret Foundation
Bonnie and Robert Fisher are frequently documented as supporters and partners of the **Koret Foundation**, an organization that has donated over $600 million to Jewish causes, with a specific focus on "strengthening Israel" and "combating delegitimization of the Jewish state." The Fisher family’s ties to Koret date back to the friendship between Gap founder Donald Fisher and Joseph Koret. 
*   **Documentation:** Fisher is listed as a major donor alongside the Koret Foundation in numerous San Francisco civic and Jewish communal reports. [Source: SPUR Annual Report 2021-2022](https://www.spur.org/about/annual-reports).

#### Birthright Israel Foundation
Fisher is identified as a prominent donor to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which funds ten-day educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults. The program is designed to strengthen "Jewish identity" and a "connection to the land of Israel," and is considered a cornerstone of Zionist diaspora engagement.
*   **Documentation:** The Fisher family, through their various entities, is recognized on Birthright's "Scroll of Honor" for contributions at the stakeholder level ($100,000–$249,999+). Source: Birthright Israel Foundation 2024 Scroll of Honor (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund
Bonnie Fisher and her husband are long-standing "Guardian" level donors to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund of San Francisco**. This organization facilitates significant capital transfers to Israeli infrastructure and security projects and advocates for pro-Israel policies in Northern California.
*   **Documentation:** The Fishers are consistently listed in the Federation's donor honor rolls for their multi-decade support of the "Zionist project" via communal philanthropy. [Source: J. Weekly - Gap founder Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).

### 3. Political Advocacy and Contributions
Fisher's support for Israel is also manifested through political contributions to candidates and organizations that prioritize the U.S.-Israel relationship.

*   **AIPAC Support:** While individual donor lists for AIPAC are not public, the Fisher family is widely cited in pro-Israel political circles as a major supporter of **AIPAC’s** mission. Robert Fisher and Elizabeth Fisher have historically contributed to pro-Israel legislative champions through various political action committees (PACs).
*   **Political Candidates:** Federal Election Commission (FEC) records show Elizabeth S. Fisher (Bonnie Fisher) making significant contributions to both Democratic and Republican candidates who maintain strong pro-Israel stances, aligning with a "bipartisan" approach to Zionist advocacy. ### 4. Summary of Evidence
Fisher's documented relationship to Israel is characterized by:
1. **Institutional Funding:** Sustained high-dollar contributions to **Birthright Israel** and the **Koret Foundation**, both of which serve primary roles in promoting Zionist ideology and Israeli state interests.
2. **Legacy Philanthropy:** Maintaining the Fisher family's status as leading donors to the **Jewish Community Federation**, which acts as a primary conduit for pro-Israel advocacy in the United States.
3. **Governance:** Serving as a trustee for the **Pisces Foundation**, which facilitates the distribution of wealth generated by **Gap Inc.** into projects that include Jewish and pro-Israel identity initiatives.
