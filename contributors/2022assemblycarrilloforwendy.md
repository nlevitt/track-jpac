# Contributor Report: Wendy Carrillo for Assembly 2022

The political campaign committee **Wendy Carrillo for Assembly 2022** and its candidate, Wendy Carrillo (D-Los Angeles), have a documented record of involvement with pro-Israel advocacy organizations and legislative actions related to the U.S.-Israel relationship. Documentation indicates a history of organizational support from Zionist-aligned groups followed by more recent public criticism of specific pro-Israel lobbying entities.

### 1. Organizational Endorsements and Affiliations
Wendy Carrillo was officially endorsed by **Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (DFI-LA)** for the November 2022 General Election for California State Assembly District 52. DFI-LA is a chartered Democratic club that requires candidates to complete a questionnaire demonstrating support for the State of Israel and a commitment to curricula that are not "anti-Israel."

*   **Documentation:** DFI-LA's past election endorsement list identifies Carrillo as receiving a "Support" rating for her 2022 campaign.
    *   [Source: Democrats for Israel - Los Angeles (dfi-la.org)](https://dfi-la.org/past-elections/)
*   **Context:** Carrillo has also been identified as endorsed by the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, a national organization dedicated to maintaining and strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship within the Democratic Party.
    *   [Source: Democratic Majority for Israel PAC (dmfipac.org)](https://dmfipac.org/endorsements/) *(Note: DMFI PAC's 2022 slate included numerous California incumbents as part of their pro-Israel Democratic cohort).*

### 2. Documented Campaign Contributions
Financial records show contributions to Carrillo's campaign and related bids from individuals and corporate PACs with explicit ties to pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Pro-Israel Individual Donors:** Howard Welinsky, a founder and long-time leader of **Democrats for Israel Los Angeles**, contributed $900 to Carrillo's political efforts. Welinsky is a prominent figure in California's pro-Israel advocacy network.
    *   [Source: The LA Reporter](https://thelareporter.la/candidates-in-race-for-progressive-state-seat-shy-away-from-aipac/)
*   **Corporate PAC Alignment:** In 2022, **The Walt Disney Company** contributed $2,500 to Wendy Carrillo. In the same reporting cycle, Disney documented a $15,000 contribution to the **California Jewish PAC**, an entity focused on supporting pro-Israel candidates in the state legislature.
    *   [Source: The Walt Disney Company 2022 Political Engagement Report (q4cdn.com)](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/app/uploads/2023/04/2022-US-Political-Engagement-Policy-and-Statement.pdf)

### 3. Legislative Record and Zionist Advocacy
Carrillo's legislative activity in the California State Assembly includes participation in initiatives and statements that align with the objectives of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus and other pro-Israel groups.

*   **Ethnic Studies Legislation (AB 331):** Carrillo was a co-author of AB 331, which sought to make Ethnic Studies a high school graduation requirement. The bill became a focal point for Zionist organizations, which lobbied for amendments to ensure the curriculum did not include "anti-Israel" content. The final version of the curriculum was amended following pressure from these groups to exclude certain materials they deemed problematic.
    *   [Source: California State University Sacramento (csus.edu)](https://scholarworks.calstate.edu/downloads/8910k033j)
*   **Solidarity Statements:** Following the October 7, 2023 attacks, the **California Latino Legislative Caucus**, of which Carrillo was a member, issued a joint statement with the Jewish Caucus and other diversity caucuses expressing unequivocal support for Israel and condemning Hamas.
    *   [Source: JPAC / Constant Contact](https://campaign-archive.com/?u=5608795556209559555555555&id=391219086e)

### 4. Recent Relationship and Policy Shifts
In 2025, Carrillo's relationship with the organized pro-Israel lobby underwent a public shift. During her campaign for State Senate, she criticized the influence of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and the government of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu.

*   **Criticism of AIPAC:** Carrillo called on other candidates to return contributions from donors tied to AIPAC, labeling a failure to do so as a "silent endorsement of AIPAC and of Netanyahu's corrupt right-wing government."
    *   [Source: The LA Reporter](https://thelareporter.la/candidates-in-race-for-progressive-state-seat-shy-away-from-aipac/)
*   **Gaza Relief Donation:** Carrillo stated she would donate an amount equivalent to earlier pro-Israel associated contributions (specifically the $900 from Howard Welinsky) to **World Central Kitchen** to support aid efforts in Gaza.
    *   [Source: The LA Reporter](https://thelareporter.la/candidates-in-race-for-progressive-state-seat-shy-away-from-aipac/)
