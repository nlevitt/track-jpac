# Contributor Report: Isaac Bryan for Assembly 2022

The political committee 'Isaac Bryan for Assembly 2022' represents the re-election campaign of California State Assemblymember Isaac Bryan (D-55). Bryan's political activities and documentation reveal a consistent relationship with pro-Israel advocacy organizations and the maintenance of policy positions aligned with the U.S.-Israel strategic alliance.

### 1. Endorsements and Organizational Support
Isaac Bryan’s 2022 campaign received endorsements and backing from primary Zionist advocacy organizations.

*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) PAC:** Bryan’s 2022 campaign was documented as being "backed by AIPAC and Democratic Majority for Israel," an organization dedicated to strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship and countering anti-Israel sentiment within the Democratic Party.
    *   Source: [California DSA Voter Guide](https://californiadsa.org/2024-primary-voter-guide/)
*   **Democrats for Israel - California (DFI-CA):** Bryan received a "Support" rating from California Jewish Democrats, a statewide organization that evaluates candidates based on their "relationship with the mainstream Jewish community," views on "BDS, fighting antisemitism," and "aid to Israel."
    *   Source: [Democrats for Israel - California Elections Page](https://dfi-ca.org/ELECTIONS)

### 2. Public Statements and Advocacy
Bryan has publicly affirmed the status of Israel as a key ally and participated in communal advocacy events with Zionist organizations.

*   **Recognition of Israel as an Ally:** During a 2021 special election forum co-hosted by the American Jewish Committee (AJC) and B'nai David Judea Congregation, Bryan stated, "Israel’s an ally to the U.S, an ally to California, and we have to maintain that with absolute sovereignty."
    *   Source: [Shalhevet Boiling Point](https://shalhevetboilingpoint.com/news/2021/05/14/debate-for-local-assembly-seat-finds-differences-on-bds-homelessness-and-other-issues/)
*   **Participation in Zionist Advocacy Events:** Bryan participated in the American Jewish Committee's "Hanukkah Under the Stars" in December 2021, an event that included the Israeli Consul General and focused on the diplomatic successes of the Abraham Accords.
    *   Source: [American Jewish Committee (AJC) News](https://www.ajc.org/news/ajc-hanukkah-under-the-stars-0)

### 3. Legislative Record and Policy Engagement
Bryan’s legislative actions include brokering compromises on bills prioritized by pro-Israel interest groups.

*   **Brokering of AB 715 (Antisemitism Prevention):** Bryan served as a key broker for AB 715, a bill established to create an "Antisemitism Prevention Coordinator." The legislation was identified as a "top priority for the Zionist Legislative Jewish Caucus and the Jewish Public Affairs Council (JPAC)." Bryan reportedly brokered a "backroom deal" to tether the bill to separate civil rights legislation to secure its passage through the Assembly.
    *   Source: [CounterPunch: CA Lawmakers Back Censorship Disguised as Antisemitism Prevention](https://www.counterpunch.org/2025/09/17/ca-lawmakers-back-censorship-disguised-as-antisemitism-prevention/)
*   **Delegation to Israel:** In July 2022, Bryan was one of 15 Democratic lawmakers who participated in a legislative trip involving the Los Angeles Jewish Federation, which included discussions on regional security and communal ties.
    *   Source: [CalMatters: Amid new COVID surge, confusing mix of rules](https://calmatters.org/newsletters/whatmatters/2022/07/california-covid-rules-mix/)
