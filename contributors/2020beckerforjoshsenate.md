# Contributor Report: Josh Becker for Senate 2020

The political committee **Josh Becker for Senate 2020** is the campaign entity for California State Senator Josh Becker. The committee and the candidate have a documented record of support for the State of Israel and participation in Zionist advocacy through legislative sponsorship, public rhetoric, and affiliations with pro-Israel organizations.

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and Support for Israel
Josh Becker serves as the Vice-Chair of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus** (CLJC), a body that explicitly identifies support for Israel as a core priority.

*   **Anti-BDS and Ethnic Studies Oversight:** Becker has been a primary advocate for legislation aimed at monitoring and regulating educational content regarding Israel. He co-sponsored **AB 1468** (2025), which sought to establish state-approved curriculum standards for ethnic studies. Critics, including **CodePink** and **Truthout**, argued the bill was designed to "censor Palestinian voices" by restricting historical narratives to "domestic experiences" to avoid discussions of Israeli state policies.
    *   *Source:* [California State Senate Press Release (Feb 2025)](https://sd13.senate.ca.gov/news/press-release/february-24-2025/state-legislators-introduce-bill-create-curriculum-standards)
    *   *Source:* [Truthout Analysis of AB 1468](https://truthout.org/articles/california-ethnic-studies-bill-aims-to-censor-palestine-related-education/)
*   **Caucus Leadership:** As a leader in the CLJC, Becker participates in the caucus's partnership with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California** (JPAC), an organization that lobbies for "strengthening the California-Israel relationship" and combating the Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) movement.
    *   *Source:* [California Legislative Jewish Caucus (Wikipedia/Official Record)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus)

### 2. Public Rhetoric and Zionist Positions
Becker has made public statements affirming the historical and political legitimacy of the State of Israel while contesting Palestinian historical claims.

*   **Denial of Palestinian Pre-Statehood:** In public comments addressed to education administrators, Becker asserted that "there was no Palestine" prior to the United Nations' creation of Israel in 1948 and described historical narratives claiming Israel was founded on stolen land as "falsehoods."
    *   *Source:* [CodePink Documentation of Public Statements](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EGn1agZiQE5)
*   **Wartime Support:** Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Becker co-authored a statement on behalf of the Jewish Caucus declaring, "We stand with Israel" and asserting Israel’s "right and responsibility to defend its civilians" through military action.
    *   *Source:* [Official Statement (Oct 2023)](https://sd13.senate.ca.gov/news/press-release/october-7-2023/california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific)

### 3. Financial and Political Affiliations
The committee 'Josh Becker for Senate 2020' is integrated into pro-Israel political networks through its donor base and its own contributions to other candidates.

*   **Campaign Contributions:** The committee received significant backing from donors associated with pro-Israel advocacy. During his 2020 run, he was profiled by **Jewish Insider** as a candidate attracting support for his "lifetime of involvement" in Jewish and pro-Israel causes.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider Candidate Profile (Feb 2020)](https://jewishinsider.com/2020/02/candidate-in-silicon-valley-state-senate-race-attracts-big-name-supporters/)
*   **Donor Activity:** The 'Josh Becker for Senate 2020' committee has functioned as a political donor itself, contributing to other California candidates who align with the Democratic Party's mainstream pro-Israel platform. For example, it contributed to **Diedre Thu-Ha Nguyen for Assembly 2020**.
    *   *Source:* [Transparency USA - Josh Becker Financial Activity](https://www.transparencyusa.org/ca/candidate/josh-becker)
    *   *Source:* [SomaliScan - Campaign Contribution Records](https://somaliscan.com/diedre-thu-ha-nguyen-for-assembly-2020/)
