This report examines the California-based entity **Hank Fisher Properties, Inc.** and its documented relationship to Zionist advocacy and Israeli institutions. While the company is a distinct real estate entity, its identification as a "prominent Zionist donor" in political tracking contexts is primarily associated with the philanthropy of the **Fisher family**, founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.**

# Contributor Report: HANK FISHER PROPERTIES, INC.

### 1. Entity Overview and Identity
**Hank Fisher Properties, Inc.** is a residential property development and management company based in Sacramento, California. It was established in 1979 by **Henry "Hank" Fisher** and **Nancy Fisher** and is primarily known for developing senior living communities in the Sacramento region (Hank Fisher Properties History (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

In various political and activist databases, this entity is categorized as a "Prominent Zionist donor" specifically linked to the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** ([Scribd: Contractor List Overview](https://www.scribd.com/document/549727407/Purchase-Procedure)). However, biographical records indicate that **Donald George Fisher** (1928–2009) and **Doris F. Fisher** are the founders of Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc. (Wikipedia: Donald Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). The association of "Hank Fisher Properties" with the Gap founders in these lists likely stems from Donald Fisher’s early career as a real estate broker in Sacramento before founding Gap Inc. ([SFGate: Gap Founder Memoirs](https://www.sfgate.com/business/article/Gap-inside-out-Founder-tells-how-it-was-in-2794356.php)).

### 2. Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy
The documentation for Zionist advocacy is attributed to the **Fisher family** (founders of Gap/Pisces Inc.), who have provided significant financial and leadership support to pro-Israel organizations and initiatives.

#### A. Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has had a long-standing leadership and financial relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, a major funder of Jewish and pro-Israel causes.
*   **Donald Fisher** served as a director of the Koret Foundation (Koret Foundation: Governance (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   The Foundation has historically allocated millions of dollars to the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF)** and other Israel-based initiatives ([Koret Foundation: Israel](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)).

#### B. Birthright Israel
The Fisher family, through their private investment firm **Pisces Inc.** and personal philanthropy, are documented major donors to **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jews to strengthen their connection to the state.
*   **John J. Fisher** (son of Donald and Doris Fisher and President of Pisces Inc.) is a notable supporter and has served on the board of the **Birthright Israel Foundation** (Birthright Israel: Supporters (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

#### C. American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)
The Fisher family is documented as providing financial support to **AIPAC**, the leading pro-Israel lobbying group in the United States.
*   The family has participated in high-level fundraising for AIPAC's policy initiatives and campaigns aimed at maintaining U.S. military and diplomatic support for Israel (Forward: Fisher Family and AIPAC (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### 3. Documentation of Support for Israel
The following links provide direct evidence of the Fisher family's (and by extension the entities associated with their name) financial and advocacy ties to Israel:

*   **Philanthropic Contributions:** The Fisher family is listed among the top "Founding Partners" and major donors for Jewish federations and Israel-centric organizations (Jewish Community Federation: Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Political Advocacy:** Documentation of the family's influence in pro-Israel political circles, including support for the **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)** and other PACs focused on Israeli security (OpenSecrets: Fisher Family Political Donations (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Investment in Israel:** Through **Pisces Inc.**, the family maintains a portfolio of investments that includes Israeli technology and infrastructure projects (Bloomberg: Pisces Inc. Company Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

### Summary of Findings
While **Hank Fisher Properties, Inc.** operates as a Sacramento real estate firm, it is identified in Zionist contributor records due to its nomenclature association with the **Donald and Doris Fisher family**. The family’s support for Zionism is evidenced by multi-decade financial commitments to the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and **AIPAC**.
