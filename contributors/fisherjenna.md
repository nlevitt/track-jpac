# Contributor Report: Jenna Fisher

Jenna Fisher (also documented as Jennifer Fisher) is a prominent California-based political donor and corporate executive. She is a Senior Partner at the leadership advisory firm **Russell Reynolds Associates**, where she co-leads the Global Financial Officers Practice. Fisher is married to **Colin Fisher**, a Managing Director at **Pisces Inc.**, which serves as the private investment office and family office for the founders of **Gap Inc.** (the Fisher family).

Fisher and her spouse are documented contributors to major Zionist philanthropic organizations and have publicly advocated for the State of Israel through formal declarations and financial support.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Zionist Advocacy
Jenna Fisher is a public supporter of Zionist causes and has formalized her stance through collective advocacy groups.

*   **October Declaration Signatory:** Fisher is a verified signatory of the **October Declaration**, a public statement organized by the **British Friends of Israel**. The declaration expresses "solidarity with the people of Israel" and support for its right to self-defense following the events of October 7, 2023.

### 2. Philanthropic Support for Israel and Zionism
Fisher’s documented relationship to Israel is largely characterized by her financial support for organizations that facilitate Jewish-identity building and pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Birthright Israel Support:** Documentation from **Penn Hillel** (a primary partner of **Birthright Israel**) identifies Jenna Fisher as a donor. Penn Hillel’s annual reports emphasize their role in sending students on Birthright missions and "Onward Israel" programs, which are designed to strengthen ties between the Diaspora and the State of Israel.
    *   **Source:** Penn Hillel Annual Report 2023-2024 (via Issuu) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Listed as a donor on the "Hillel Donors" page).
*   **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area:** Fisher and her husband are consistent donors to the **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area**, an organization that directs significant funding toward Israeli social services and Zionist educational programming.
    *   **Source:** Jewish Federation Bay Area 2023 Annual Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Listed as "Jennifer and Colin Fisher").

### 3. Institutional Affiliations
Through her relationship to the Fisher family and their investment office, Pisces Inc., Jenna Fisher is situated within a network of prominent Zionist philanthropy.

*   **Pisces Inc. and the Fisher Family Legacy:** As a Managing Director at Pisces Inc., Colin Fisher manages the multi-billion dollar portfolio of the Fisher family. The family’s patriarch, **Donald Fisher** (Gap Inc. founder), was a "strong supporter" of the Jewish Community Federation and was deeply involved in San Francisco’s pro-Israel philanthropic circles.
    *   **Source:** [J. Weekly: Gap founder Donald Fisher leaves stamp on community](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/)
*   **Koret Foundation Association:** The Fisher family office (Pisces Inc.) and the **Koret Foundation** are frequently co-listed as top-tier donors for Bay Area Jewish and Zionist initiatives. The Koret Foundation is one of the most prolific supporters of Zionist advocacy and Israeli security in the United States.
    *   **Source:** CuriOdyssey Year in Review 2010 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Lists Colin and Jenna Fisher alongside the Koret Foundation as major contributors).

### 4. Background and Education
Fisher’s ties to Israel extend to her academic history; she is a graduate of **The Wharton School** and spent time studying at the **Hebrew University of Jerusalem**.
