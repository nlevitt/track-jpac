# Contributor Report: Laura Friedman for Assembly 2022

The political committee **Laura Friedman for Assembly 2022** (FPPC ID #1383344) was the candidate controlled committee for Laura Friedman's 2022 re-election campaign to the California State Assembly, District 44. The committee and the candidate have a documented history of support from liberal Zionist organizations and have consistently advocated for pro-Israel policies within the California State Legislature and the Democratic Party.

### 1. Support from Pro-Israel and Zionist Organizations
Laura Friedman's 2022 campaign received formal endorsements and support from several key advocacy groups focused on strengthening the U.S.-Israel relationship and promoting Zionist perspectives.

*   **J Street PAC:** The committee was endorsed by J Street PAC, a political action committee that describes itself as the "political home for pro-Israel, pro-peace Americans." J Street PAC served as a financial conduit for donors to support Friedman's candidacy based on her alignment with their "pro-Israel, pro-peace" platform.
    *   **Source:** [J Street PAC Candidate Profile: Laura Friedman](https://jstreetpac.org/candidates/laura-friedman/)
*   **Democrats for Israel Los Angeles (DFI-LA):** For the 2022 election cycle, DFI-LA gave Laura Friedman a rating of "Strong Support." To receive this endorsement, candidates must demonstrate a clear commitment to the State of Israel and the fight against anti-Zionism.
    *   **Source:** [DFI-LA 2022 Primary Candidates Endorsements](https://www.dfi-la.org/post/dfi-la-2022-primary-candidates-endorsements)
*   **Jewish Democratic Council of America (JDCA):** Friedman has maintained a long-standing relationship with the JDCA, which endorsed her based on her "Jewish and Democratic values," specifically noting her support for the U.S.-Israel relationship.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Dems Endorsed Candidates - California](https://jewishdems.org/candidate/laura-friedman/)

### 2. Documented Advocacy and Public Positions
Friedman has utilized her legislative platform to support Israel and counter movements critical of the Israeli state.

*   **Opposition to BDS:** Friedman has publicly denounced the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement, characterizing it as a challenge to Israel's legitimacy.
    *   **Source:** [WEHOville: Where your congressional candidates stand on Israel](https://www.wehoville.com/2024/01/24/where-your-congressional-candidates-stand-on-israel-anti-semitism/)
*   **Legislative Jewish Caucus:** As a member of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus, she participated in a 2019 delegation to Israel. She has stated that her Jewish identity and family ties in Israel guide her "pro-Israel" legislative approach.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Virtual Library: Laura Friedman Biography](https://www.jewishvirtuallibrary.org/laura-friedman)
*   **Right to Military Action:** Friedman has affirmed that Israel has a "right to go to war" with Hamas and has defended the necessity of Israeli military operations following the October 7 attacks.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Insider: Schiff’s likely House successor vows to continue his pro-Israel legacy](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/laura-friedman-adam-schiff-california-house-race-pro-israel-progressive/)

### 3. Policy Influence and Party Role
Friedman has acted as a bridge between the progressive wing of the Democratic Party and pro-Israel advocacy groups, ensuring that party resolutions remain acceptable to the Zionist community.

*   **Los Angeles County Democratic Party Resolution:** Friedman helped negotiate the wording of a resolution regarding the Israel-Gaza conflict adopted by the LA County Democratic Party. The resolution was specifically crafted to include language calling for the "end of Hamas rule in Gaza" and the "release of all Israeli hostages," ensuring it met the requirements of the "Democratic pro-Israel community."
    *   **Source:** [Jewish Insider: Laura Friedman and the Pro-Israel Line](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/laura-friedman-adam-schiff-california-house-race-pro-israel-progressive/)
*   **Endorsement by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI):** Her alignment with Zionist advocacy led to her endorsement by the DMFI PAC, an organization dedicated to maintaining pro-Israel support among Democrats.
    *   **Source:** [DMFI PAC 2024 Endorsements (referencing her legislative record)](https://dmfipac.org/dmfi-pac-announces-new-slate-of-endorsements-for-the-2024-cycle/)
