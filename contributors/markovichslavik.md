# Contributor Report: Markovich, Slavik

Slavik Markovich is a Silicon Valley-based technology executive and entrepreneur with documented history of service in the Israeli military and ongoing financial and professional ties to the Israeli tech-defense ecosystem and Zionist community organizations.

### 1. Military Service and Background
Markovich served in the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) for approximately five to six years.
*   **Rank and Role:** He reached the rank of Captain during his service. His tenure focused on database administration and leading technical teams within the military infrastructure.
*   **Source:** [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/brucerogers/2023/06/12/israeli-founder-team-creates-ciam-company-descope-with-53-million-seed-round/)
*   **Source:** [Venture in Security](https://www.ventureinsecurity.net/p/slavik-markovich-building-descope)

### 2. Organizational Support and Philanthropy
Markovich is a documented donor to major Jewish and Zionist community organizations that provide direct financial support to the State of Israel.
*   **Jewish Federation of the Bay Area:** Markovich is listed as a donor in the Federation’s 2024 Annual Report. This organization operates the "Israel Emergency Fund," which mobilized over $22 million in the months following October 7, 2023, to support hundreds of organizations in Israel providing aid and trauma relief.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Federation of the Bay Area 2024 Annual Report (p. 25)](https://jewishfed.org/sites/default/files/Annual_Report_2024.pdf)
*   **Israel Advocacy and Security:** The Federation to which Markovich contributes describes itself as Northern California’s "go-to resource" for Jewish security and anti-antisemitism advocacy, maintaining direct partnerships with Israeli institutions.
*   **Source:** [Jewish Federation Bay Area Mission](https://jewishfed.org/community-security)

### 3. Economic and Technological Advocacy
Markovich’s professional career is rooted in the "Startup Nation" model, which integrates Israeli military-trained talent into global technology markets.
*   **Israeli-Founded Companies:** He has co-founded several high-profile cybersecurity firms with predominantly Israeli founding teams, including Sentrigo (acquired by McAfee), Demisto (acquired by Palo Alto Networks), and Descope.
*   **Source:** [Calcalist - The 50 Most Promising Israeli Startups](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctech/articles/0,7340,L-3907402,00.html)
*   **Investment and Advising:** Markovich serves as an advisor to **Cerca Partners**, an investment venture capital fund based in Ra'anana, Israel, which focuses on providing resources to Israeli technology startups to ensure their global success.
*   **Source:** [Cerca Partners Team Profile](https://www.cerca-partners.com/team)
*   **US-Israel Tech Corridor:** Markovich is frequently cited as a key leader bridging the Silicon Valley and Israeli tech sectors, a role that involves promoting the strategic value of Israeli-trained security experts to the international market.
*   **Source:** [City Journal - "Israeli tech leaders with Soviet origins... helped create Israel's 'Startup Nation' economic miracle"](https://www.city-journal.org/article/shouldve-kept-those-jews)
