# Contributor Report: Oracle America, Inc.

Oracle America, Inc., a subsidiary of Oracle Corporation, has documented ties to the State of Israel through strategic infrastructure projects, direct financial contributions to Israeli emergency and military services, and public advocacy by its senior leadership. The company’s support for Israel is characterized by its executives as a "moral mission" and a core component of its corporate values.

### 1. Leadership and Zionist Advocacy
Oracle’s executive leadership has maintained a public commitment to Israel and Zionist advocacy.

*   **Safra Catz (Executive Vice Chair, former CEO):** Catz, who was born in Israel, has been a featured speaker at the **AIPAC Policy Conference**, a primary pro-Israel lobbying organization in the United States [[1.6]](https://www.reddit.com/r/Epstein/comments/1itf14u/everyone_i_could_identify_in_the_maria_farmer_file/). In a leaked 2015 email to former Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak, Catz expressed a desire to "embed the love and respect for Israel in the American culture" and advocated for early intervention to combat the Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions (BDS) movement on college campuses [[1.7]](https://responsiblestatecraft.org/tiktok-israel-oracle/).
*   **Larry Ellison (Founder and CTO):** Ellison is identified as one of the largest private donors to the Israeli military, having contributed over **$16 million** to the Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF) [[1.2]](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/sep/11/larry-ellison-richest-person-oracle-netanyahu-trump). He has publicly stated that supporting Israel is integral to Oracle’s mission [[1.4]](https://israeltechinsider.com/p/why-oracle-and-nvidia-are-leaning-into-israel).

### 2. Financial Support and Humanitarian Aid
Since October 2023, Oracle has provided significant financial and material resources to Israeli organizations.

*   **Corporate Donations:** Oracle donated **$1 million** to Magen David Adom (MDA), Israel's national emergency medical service, and launched an employee matching campaign that raised an additional **$1.9 million**, totaling nearly $3 million [[1.2]](https://www.oracle.com/il/news/announcement/oracle-stands-with-israel-2023-10-11/) [[1.5]](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctechnews/article/b1f9fvxzp).
*   **Military Supplies:** In February 2024, the company donated medical and environmental supplies valued at **$500,000** specifically for use by the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) [[1.3]](https://bdsmovement.net/news/oracle-company-complicity-profile).
*   **Employee Grants:** Following the October 7 attacks, Oracle provided all of its Israeli employees—including those called up for military reserve duty—with a "special grant" equivalent to an extra month’s salary [[1.5]](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctechnews/article/b1f9fvxzp).

### 3. Strategic Infrastructure and Military Collaboration
Oracle provides critical technology infrastructure to the Israeli government and defense sectors.

*   **Underground Data Center:** In 2021, Oracle opened a **$319 million** underground data center in Jerusalem, located nine floors below ground. The facility was specifically designed to withstand missile and rocket attacks to ensure "business continuity" for government and defense clients during military conflicts [[1.2]](https://www.jns.org/first-underground-data-center-in-israel-built-to-withstand-missiles-and-car-bombs/) [[1.6]](https://www.theregister.com/2021/10/14/oracle_israel_cloud_region/).
*   **Defense Contracts:** Oracle provides cloud, AI, and database services to the Israeli Ministry of Defense and the IDF. This includes **Project Menta**, a classified data engineering project with the Israeli Air Force, and collaboration with **Rafael Advanced Defense Systems** on AI projects for battlefield applications [[1.3]](https://bdsmovement.net/news/oracle-company-complicity-profile) [[1.19]](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/oracle-allegedly-suppresses-pro-palestinian-voices-cuts-charity-support-amid-tiktok-takeover-bid/).
*   **IDF Training:** In 2022, Oracle hosted software developers from the IDF’s **C4I Corps** to train them on utilizing Oracle’s cloud infrastructure for military purposes [[1.3]](https://bdsmovement.net/news/oracle-company-complicity-profile).

### 4. Corporate Policy and Internal Culture
Oracle maintains an internal policy that prioritizes support for Israel, often at the expense of employees with dissenting views.

*   **Employee Alignment:** Safra Catz has stated that Oracle’s commitment to Israel is "second to none" and that employees who do not agree with the company's mission to support the State of Israel "aren't the right company for them" [[1.19]](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctech/articles/0,7340,L-3911894,00.html).
*   **Public Advocacy:** After October 2023, Catz ordered the message **"Oracle Stands with Israel"** to be displayed on the company’s internal and external websites in over 180 countries [[1.13]](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/oracle-allegedly-suppresses-pro-palestinian-voices-cuts-charity-support-amid-tiktok-takeover-bid/).
*   **Suppression of Dissent:** Internal reports indicate that the company has removed certain Palestinian relief charities from its employee giving platform and has allegedly referred employees who expressed pro-Palestinian concerns to "mental health resources" [[1.7]](https://responsiblestatecraft.org/tiktok-israel-oracle/) [[1.13]](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/oracle-allegedly-suppresses-pro-palestinian-voices-cuts-charity-support-amid-tiktok-takeover-bid/).

### 5. Political Contributions (Oracle PAC)
Oracle America, Inc. operates a Political Action Committee (**Oracle PAC**) that contributes to federal candidates. While it supports candidates across the political spectrum, its leadership has utilized political engagement to advocate for the continuation of weapon shipments to Israel during private lobbying meetings with U.S. senators [[1.19]](https://www.business-humanrights.org/en/latest-news/oracle-allegedly-suppresses-pro-palestinian-voices-cuts-charity-support-amid-tiktok-takeover-bid/) [[1.4]](https://www.fec.gov/data/committee/C00323048/).
