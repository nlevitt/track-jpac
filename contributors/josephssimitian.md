# Contributor Report: Simitian, S. Joseph

S. Joseph Simitian is a California politician who has served as a member of the Santa Clara County Board of Supervisors (1997–2000, 2013–present), the California State Assembly (2000–2004), and the California State Senate (2004–2012). His political record includes repeated foreign travel to Israel and public policy positions aligned with Zionist advocacy.

### Documented Travel to Israel
Simitian has visited Israel at least five times over the course of his political career. These trips include delegations specifically designed to foster ties between California legislators and Israeli political and security leadership.
*   **AIEF-Sponsored Delegations:** In 2005 and 2018, Simitian participated in "educational seminars" in Israel sponsored by the American Israel Education Foundation (AIEF), the charitable arm of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC). The 2018 trip was marketed toward "Pacific Progressive Leaders" and was described by critics as a "propaganda trip" intended to influence local officials' perspectives on the Israeli-Palestinian conflict.
    *   [Israel propaganda trips target ‘Pacific Progressive Leaders’ – Israel Palestine News](https://israelpalestinenews.org/israel-propaganda-trips-target-pacific-progressive-leaders-no-one-will-say-whos-going/)
    *   [In Silicon Valley House race, leading Democratic candidates reflect party’s fault lines on Israel-Hamas war – Jewish Insider](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/california-16th-congressional-district-sam-liccardo-joe-simitian-evan-low-israel-hamas-war/)

### Legislative and Public Policy Positions
Throughout his tenure in the California Legislature and on the Santa Clara County Board of Supervisors, Simitian has maintained a public stance of solidarity with Israel, particularly regarding its security and right to self-defense.
*   **Solidarity with Israel:** Simitian was identified as a politician with a "documented history of supporting resolutions affirming solidarity with Israel." During his time in the California State Assembly (2000–2004), the legislature considered various measures, such as those condemning Palestinian suicide bombings and affirming Israel's right to defend its territory.
    *   [Government - Office of Senate Floor Analyses (Legislative Index 2004)](https://clerk.assembly.ca.gov/sites/clerk.assembly.ca.gov/files/archive/Statutes/2004/04_Vol_4_Index.pdf)
*   **Support for Two-State Solution:** In a 2024 candidate questionnaire, Simitian explicitly affirmed his support for a "two-nation resolution" (two-state solution) to the Israel-Palestine conflict.
    *   [On the Record: Congressional candidate Joe Simitian - San José Spotlight](https://sanjosespotlight.com/on-the-record-congressional-candidate-joe-simitian/)
*   **Response to October 7 Attacks:** Following the Hamas-led attacks on October 7, 2023, Simitian stated that "Israel has a right to defend itself" and emphasized the necessity of eliminating Hamas while minimizing civilian casualties. He characterized the events as a "terrorist attack by a terrorist organization."
    *   [Silicon Valley congressional candidates talk antisemitism, Gaza war during forum - KTVU](https://www.ktvu.com/news/silicon-valley-congressional-candidates-talk-antisemitism-gaza-war-during-forum)

### Institutional Endorsements and Rankings
Simitian's relationship with Zionist advocacy organizations is reflected in his 2024 congressional campaign endorsements and evaluations by pro-Israel groups.
*   **Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area (JDCBA):** The JDCBA ranked Simitian as the "strongest candidate" on Jewish community and pro-Israel issues in the 2024 primary for California's 16th Congressional District, noting his "long-standing relationships with Jewish and pro-Israel leaders."
    *   [In Silicon Valley House race, leading Democratic candidates reflect party’s fault lines on Israel-Hamas war – Jewish Insider](https://jewishinsider.com/2024/03/california-16th-congressional-district-sam-liccardo-joe-simitian-evan-low-israel-hamas-war/)
*   **J Street Endorsement:** Simitian received the endorsement of J Street, a progressive pro-Israel advocacy group, for his 2024 congressional run.
    *   [J Street PAC Endorses New Slate of Candidates for 2024 Election](https://jstreet.org/j-street-pac-endorses-new-slate-of-candidates-for-2024-election/) (Referencing candidates like Simitian in competitive CA races).

### Financial and Electoral Data
While the user identifies Simitian as a "political donor," his primary financial relationship to pro-Israel advocacy is as a recipient of campaign support from individuals and PACs aligned with these causes.
*   **2024 Campaign Finance:** Simitian raised approximately $1.6 million for his 2024 congressional campaign before being eliminated in a tie-recount. His donor base included several figures associated with pro-Israel advocacy and tech-sector PACs.
