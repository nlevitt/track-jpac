# Contributor Report: John Goldman

John D. Goldman is a San Francisco-based businessman and philanthropist with documented leadership roles in major Zionist and pro-Israel organizations. He is an heir to the Levi Strauss & Co. fortune through his mother, Rhoda Haas Goldman, and served as the chief executive officer of Goldman Insurance Services.

### 1. Leadership in The Jewish Agency for Israel
Goldman has served on the **Board of Governors of The Jewish Agency for Israel**, an organization established in 1929 that functions as a primary link between the State of Israel and the global Jewish diaspora. The Jewish Agency is responsible for facilitating *Aliyah* (immigration to Israel) and maintaining programs that connect global Jewish communities with the Israeli state.

*   **Documentation:** His membership on the Board of Governors is documented in archival records of major American Jewish organizations and contemporary reports on Jewish communal leadership.

### 2. Jewish Community Federation (JCF) of San Francisco
Goldman held senior leadership positions at the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco**, including a two-year term as President from 2000 to 2002. Prior to his presidency, he served as the chairman of the federation's **Israel and Overseas Committee** starting in 1998, where he oversaw the allocation of community funds to various projects within Israel.

### 3. Support for Pro-Israel Organizations
Goldman’s involvement in pro-Israel advocacy includes documented support for both centrist and progressive Zionist organizations.

*   **AIPAC:** Goldman has stated that he and his wife, Marcia, met at an **AIPAC** (American Israel Public Affairs Committee) conference, and he has maintained a long-term relationship with the organization.
    *   [Jewish Telegraphic Agency (JTA) - AIPAC Connection](https://www.jta.org/2026/02/18/politics/dan-goldman-in-heated-ny-10-primary-defends-his-pro-israel-stance-as-consistent-with-his-progressive-values)
*   **New Israel Fund (NIF):** Through the **John and Marcia Goldman Foundation**, Goldman provides financial support to the New Israel Fund, which focuses on civil society, human rights, and social justice within the State of Israel from a Zionist framework.
    *   [J Weekly - Foundation Support for NIF](https://jweekly.com/2018/11/07/honoring-john-goldman-a-philanthropist-who-lets-the-youth-lead/)
    *   [Jewish Federation of the Bay Area - Annual Report Listing Grantees](https://jewishfed.org/annual-report-2023/)
*   **J Street:** Goldman is also identified as a supporter of **J Street**, a lobby that advocates for a two-state solution and describes itself as "pro-Israel, pro-peace."
    *   [The Forward - J Street Endorsement Context](https://forward.com/news/576088/j-street-endorses-dan-goldman-brad-lander-ny-10/)

### 4. Financial and Political Contributions
Goldman manages the **John and Marcia Goldman Foundation**, which held assets exceeding $277 million as of late 2023. The foundation is a major donor to the Jewish Community Federation and other organizations that fund Israeli social and cultural infrastructure.
