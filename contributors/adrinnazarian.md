This research report outlines the documented political activities, legislative actions, and organizational affiliations of Adrin Nazarian, specifically regarding the State of Israel and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Nazarian, Adrin

### Profile Overview
Adrin Nazarian is a former member of the California State Assembly (2012–2022) representing the 46th District and currently serves on the Los Angeles City Council. Although of Iranian-Armenian descent and not Jewish, Nazarian is a prominent "Associate Member" of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus and has been formally recognized for his advocacy on behalf of Israeli and Jewish institutional interests in California.

### Legislative Advocacy for Israel
Nazarian has frequently introduced and supported legislation that reinforces California’s political and economic ties to Israel.

*   **Resolution on Israel's Right to Exist:** In 2018, Nazarian introduced **House Resolution 107 (HR 107)**, which recognized the 70th anniversary of the founding of the State of Israel. The resolution documented the historical connection of the Jewish people to the land and affirmed Israel’s right to exist as a sovereign Jewish state.
    *   *Source: [California Legislative Information - HR 107](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billTextClient.xhtml?bill_id=201720180HR107)*
*   **Anti-BDS Legislation:** Nazarian was a key supporter and participant in the passage of **Assembly Bill 2844 (AB 2844)** in 2016. This "anti-BDS" law prohibits California state agencies from contracting with companies that participate in the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement against Israel.
    *   *Source: [Jewish Journal - Nazarian and AB 2844](https://jewishjournal.com/community/224213/moving-shaking-chabad-telethon-fidf-rekood-music-festival-rob-eshman-leaving/)*
*   **Support for Israeli Americans:** In 2021, Nazarian co-authored **House Resolution 48 (HR 48)**, which formally recognized the contributions of Israeli Americans to the state and acknowledged the cultural and economic importance of the California-Israel relationship.
    *   *Source: [California Assembly Journal - HR 48 (Page 1684)](https://clerk.assembly.ca.gov/sites/clerk.assembly.ca.gov/files/archive/Journal/2021/2021-22_assembly_journal_vol_2.pdf)*

### Organizational Affiliations and Awards
Nazarian maintains deep ties with pro-Israel advocacy organizations and has been frequently honored for his alignment with their policy goals.

*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** Nazarian serves as one of the few non-Jewish "Associate Members" of the caucus. In this role, he has consistently voted for and promoted the caucus’s "Tikkun Olam" legislative packages, which include multi-million dollar state funding for Jewish security grants, Holocaust education, and Israel-centric cultural initiatives.
    *   *Source: [Wikipedia - California Legislative Jewish Caucus Membership](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#Membership)*
*   **JPAC "Legislator of the Year":** In 2022, the Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)—the largest single-state coalition of Jewish organizations in the U.S.—awarded Nazarian its "Legislator of the Year" award. The award cited his work in "strengthening the relationship between Israel and California" and his leadership in securing state funds for Jewish community infrastructure.
    *   *Source: [Jewish Journal - JPAC Legislator of the Year](https://jewishjournal.com/community/348239/ca-lt-gov-ag-jewish-caucus-highlight-jpac-advocacy-day/)*
*   **Israel Educational Trips:** Nazarian participated in a high-level educational mission to Israel sponsored by the Jewish Federation of Los Angeles. These trips are designed to foster legislative support for Israel by introducing California lawmakers to Israeli political and security leaders.
    *   *Source: [JCRC/Jewish Federation Silicon Valley Magazine (Page 10)](https://www.jfs-sv.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/FedWeb-Feb-2019.pdf)*

### Distinction from the Nazarian Donor Family
It is documented that Adrin Nazarian is often confused with the "Nazarian Family" (including Younes, Soraya, David, and Sam Nazarian), a wealthy Iranian-Jewish philanthropic clan that is one of the most prolific donors to Zionist causes and academic centers for Israel studies (such as the UCLA Nazarian Center for Israel Studies). While Adrin Nazarian operates in the same political circles and advocates for similar pro-Israel policies, his contributions are primarily legislative and advocacy-based rather than through large-scale private philanthropy.
