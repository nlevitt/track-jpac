This report examines the political activities and documented positions of the campaign committee **Re-Elect Scott Wiener for Senate 2024** and its principal, California State Senator Scott Wiener, regarding Israel and Zionist advocacy.

# Contributor Report: Re-Elect Scott Wiener for Senate 2024

### 1. Overview of Zionist Advocacy and Israel Support
Scott Wiener has maintained a public record of support for the State of Israel, frequently framing this support through his identity as a Jewish legislator and the historical necessity of a Jewish "safe haven." He has explicitly identified as a supporter of a "Jewish and democratic Israel" and has historically opposed movements that he characterizes as seeking the "destruction of Israel," such as certain boycott initiatives.

*   **Public Statements on Israel's Existence:** Wiener has characterized the U.S.-Israel alliance as "incredibly important" and has stated that the U.S. should continue supporting Israel's defense, including the Iron Dome system.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider, October 30, 2025](https://jewishinsider.com/2025/10/scott-wiener-nancy-pelosi-california-congress-israel-palestine-antisemitism/)
*   **Delegation Leadership:** In 2024, Wiener co-led a legislative delegation to Israel, where he met with Israeli President Isaac Herzog and visited sites affected by the October 7, 2023, attacks.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Insider, October 30, 2025](https://jewishinsider.com/2025/10/scott-wiener-nancy-pelosi-california-congress-israel-palestine-antisemitism/)

### 2. Legislative Record and Jewish Caucus Leadership
As the long-time co-chair of the **California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)**, Wiener has been a primary architect of legislation that critics and supporters alike identify as aligning with Zionist advocacy and the countering of anti-Israel activism.

*   **2024 Priority Bill Package:** Wiener shepherded a package of bills intended to address antisemitism and campus conduct following the escalation of the Israel-Hamas war. This included **SB 1287**, which requires colleges to update codes of conduct to prohibit "harassing" or "intimidating" behavior, a move critics argued was designed to stifle pro-Palestinian campus protests.
    *   *Source:* [California State Senate (ca.gov), September 6, 2024](https://sd11.senate.ca.gov/news/20240906-jewish-caucus-successfully-passes-four-priority-bills-through-california-state-legislature)
    *   *Source:* [J Weekly, March 21, 2024](https://jweekly.com/2024/03/21/state-bill-aims-to-make-college-campuses-safer-for-jewish-students/)
*   **Ethnic Studies and Anti-Israel Content:** Wiener and the CLJC successfully advocated for the incorporation of the IHRA (International Holocaust Remembrance Alliance) definition of antisemitism into California’s Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum, explicitly aiming to ensure "anti-Israel content cannot be included."
    *   *Source:* [Wikipedia - Scott Wiener (Legislative Record Section)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scott_Wiener)
*   **Security Funding:** Under Wiener's leadership, the CLJC secured approximately $240 million in state funding between 2021 and 2025 for "Jewish community priorities," including the Nonprofit Security Grant Program to protect synagogues and Jewish institutions.
    *   *Source:* [California Legislative Jewish Caucus (ca.gov), May 20, 2024](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/20240520-jewish-caucus-announces-2024-priority-legislation)

### 3. Recent Policy Realignments and "Genocide" Claim
In early 2026, Wiener’s relationship with mainstream pro-Israel organizations underwent significant tension following a shift in his rhetoric regarding the Israeli government's conduct in Gaza.

*   **"Genocide" Accusation:** In January 2026, Wiener released a video statement declaring that the Israeli government’s actions in Gaza "qualify as genocide," citing "genocidal statements" by senior Israeli officials. This followed a debate where he had initially declined to use the term.
    *   *Source:* [Jewish Telegraphic Agency (JTA), January 12, 2026](https://www.jta.org/2026/01/12/united-states/after-facing-backlash-california-congressional-hopeful-scott-wiener-says-israel-is-committing-genocide)
*   **Backlash and Resignation:** Wiener’s statement was condemned as "incorrect" and lacking "moral clarity" by organizations including the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)** and the **Simon Wiesenthal Center**. Consequently, Wiener stepped down as co-chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus in February 2026.
    *   *Source:* [The Forward, January 23, 2026](https://forward.com/fast-forward/674052/scott-wiener-steps-down-co-chair-california-jewish-caucus-israel-genocide/)
    *   *Source:* [Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN), January 12, 2026](https://icanaction.org/press-releases/ican-condemns-senator-wieners-genocide-accusation-against-israel/)

### 4. Financial Relationship to Pro-Israel Donors
While Wiener has branded his 2026 congressional campaign as independent of major pro-Israel political action committees (PACs), his committees continue to receive support from individuals linked to those groups.

*   **PAC Pledge:** Wiener has pledged not to accept money from pro-Israel PACs such as **AIPAC**, **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, or the **United Democracy Project**. 
    *   *Source:* [Mission Local, April 24, 2026](https://missionlocal.org/2026/04/scott-wieners-congressional-campaign-strategy-5-house-parties-a-week/)
*   **Individual Contributions:** Despite the PAC pledge, approximately $45,000 of Wiener's campaign funds came from 20 individual donors who are also contributors to AIPAC and similar groups. Wiener has described these donors as "local Jewish community leaders" who support his work generally rather than his specific views on Israel.
    *   *Source:* [Mission Local, April 24, 2026](https://missionlocal.org/2026/04/scott-wieners-congressional-campaign-strategy-5-house-parties-a-week/)
