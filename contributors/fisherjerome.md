# Contributor Report: FISHER, JEROME

Jerome Fisher (1930–2016), the co-founder of Nine West Group, was a major philanthropist and political donor identified in various records for his substantial financial support of Israeli institutions and Zionist projects. While often conflated with the San Francisco-based Fisher family (founders of Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.) due to shared surnames and overlapping philanthropic targets—including the Koret Foundation and Birthright Israel—Jerome Fisher maintained a distinct but collaborative financial relationship with these entities.

### 1. Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### The Israel Museum
Jerome Fisher and his wife, Anne Fisher, were primary benefactors of the **Israel Museum** in Jerusalem. Their financial support was institutionalized through the endowment of the museum's leadership position, officially titled the **Anne and Jerome Fisher Director**.
*   **Documentation:** The Israel Museum’s official publications and various news reports identify the director’s chair as the "Anne and Jerome Fisher Director" following their multimillion-dollar contributions.

#### Birthright Israel
Jerome Fisher is documented as a supporter of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jews to strengthen their Zionist identity. Fisher’s involvement is specifically linked to the **Jerome Fisher Program in Management and Technology (M&T)** at the University of Pennsylvania, which he endowed with $50 million. The program facilitates and sponsors dedicated Birthright Israel trips for its students, serving as a pipeline for Zionist engagement.
*   **Documentation:** The University of Pennsylvania’s M&T program blog details student participation in the "UPenn Birthright Trip" specifically associated with the Jerome Fisher program.

#### Association with the Koret Foundation
The relationship between Jerome Fisher and the **Koret Foundation** (a major Zionist funder based in California) is evidenced through reciprocal funding of Israeli cultural diplomacy. The Koret Foundation is a significant donor to the **American Friends of the Israel Museum (AFIM)**, specifically providing grants that support the directorate named after Jerome Fisher.
*   **Documentation:** The Koret Foundation’s grant records show consistent support for AFIM and projects at the Israel Museum, reinforcing the infrastructure of the directorate established by the Fishers.

### 2. Political and Financial Context

#### Pisces Inc. and the Fisher Family Conflation
While Jerome Fisher was the co-founder of Nine West, he is frequently grouped with the **Fisher family of Gap Inc.** (Donald, Doris, and John Fisher) in donor databases. John Fisher is the managing director of **Pisces Inc.** and a board member of the Koret Foundation. Jerome Fisher’s individual political contributions have occasionally appeared in proximity to Pisces Inc. transactions in campaign finance aggregators, reflecting the narrow network of high-net-worth Zionist donors in the United States.
*   **Documentation:** Campaign finance records list Jerome Fisher alongside other prominent pro-Israel donors, sometimes incorrectly attributing him to the Pisces Inc. / Gap Inc. orbit in activist "Mapping Project" style reports.
*   **Link:** [CampaignMoney.com: Jerome Fisher Political Contributions](https://www.campaignmoney.com/political/contributions/jerome-fisher.asp?cycle=00)
*   **Link:** [FEC: Pisces Inc. and Fisher Family Filings](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/?data_type=processed&contributor_name=Fisher&recipient_committee_type=P)

### 3. Summary of Advocacy
Jerome Fisher’s legacy is defined by the "translation" of private wealth into the long-term sustainability of Israeli national institutions. His funding of the Israel Museum and the institutionalization of Birthright trips through the University of Pennsylvania represent a strategic investment in the promotion of Zionist cultural and educational narratives.

*   **Zionist Funding Focus:** Israel Museum Directorate, Birthright Israel student pipeline, and institutional support via UPenn.
*   **Primary Entities:** American Friends of the Israel Museum, Taglit-Birthright Israel.
