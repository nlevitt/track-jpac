# Contributor Report: Kraines, Steven

### **Identity and Professional Background**
Steven Kraines is a prominent legal professional and political donor based in Los Angeles, California. He is a Partner at the law firm **Gibson, Dunn & Crutcher**, where he specializes in corporate transactions, mergers, and acquisitions. 

*   **Professional Profile**: [Gibson Dunn - Steven Kraines Bio](https://www.gibsondunn.com/lawyer/kraines-steven/)
*   **Philanthropic Leadership**: Kraines serves as a Trustee of the **Beatrice Snyder Foundation**, a family charitable organization that distributes significant grants to Jewish and pro-Israel advocacy groups. 
    *   **Foundation Documentation**: [ProPublica - Beatrice Snyder Foundation (Form 990)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/223595071)
    *   **Trustee Listing**: [Cause IQ - Beatrice Snyder Foundation People](https://www.causeiq.com/organizations/beatrice-snyder-foundation,223595071/)

### **Documented Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

Kraines has a documented history of leadership and financial support for organizations that advocate for the State of Israel and Zionist interests.

#### **1. Leadership in the American Jewish Committee (AJC)**
Kraines is identified as a former member of the **Board of Governors** of the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)**, one of the oldest and most influential pro-Israel advocacy organizations in the United States. The AJC explicitly identifies "supporting Israel's security and place in the world" as a core mission, frequently lobbying for U.S. military aid to Israel and opposing the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.

*   **Evidence of Role**: Kraines’ name appears in AJC governance and donor materials as a member of the national Board of Governors and the Los Angeles Regional Board.
*   **Documentation of AJC Mission**: [AJC - Mission and Values](https://www.ajc.org/mission-and-values) (Note: Link provided for context on the organization he served).
*   **Governance Record**: [Donor/Leadership List (Cached/Archived)](https://shadowwarfare.info/donors/steven-kraines/) (Lists Kraines as an AJC-affiliated donor and governor).

#### **2. Financial Support via the Beatrice Snyder Foundation**
Through his role as Trustee of the **Beatrice Snyder Foundation**, Kraines facilitates substantial financial contributions to Zionist and pro-Israel entities. The foundation is a documented "major donor" to the AJC, with historical contributions ranging between **$100,001 and $250,000** per cycle.

*   **Grant Data**: [Beatrice Snyder Foundation Donor Records](https://impiousdigest.com/investigative-report-political-donors-and-israel-advocacy/) (Aggregated list showing Kraines' foundation contributing specifically to AJC and related Jewish advocacy funds).
*   **JBI International Support**: The foundation also supports **JBI International** (formerly the Jewish Braille Institute), an organization that promotes Jewish culture and religious engagement, including Zionist-themed educational materials.
    *   **Grant Support Link**: [JBI 2023 Annual Report (Page 14)](https://www.jbilibrary.org/sites/default/files/2024-03/JBI_Annual_Report_2023_Final.pdf)

#### **3. Political Contributions to Pro-Israel Candidates**
Kraines and his associated entities have a history of donating to political candidates who maintain strict pro-Israel voting records. His contributions are often aligned with the priorities of the pro-Israel lobby in Washington, D.C.

*   **FEC Donor Search**: [FEC.gov - Steven Kraines (Gibson Dunn)](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Steven+Kraines&contributor_employer=Gibson+Dunn) (Primary source for personal political giving to candidates supportive of Zionist interests).
*   **PAC Associations**: Records indicate alignment with donors to **AIPAC**-affiliated PACs and the **United Democracy Project**, which seeks to defeat candidates critical of Israeli government policy.
    *   **Donor List Association**: [Shadow Warfare - Zionist Donor Database](https://shadowwarfare.info/donors/steven-kraines/)
