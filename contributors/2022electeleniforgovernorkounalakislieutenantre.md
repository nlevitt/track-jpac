# Contributor Report: Re-Elect Eleni Kounalakis for Lieutenant Governor 2022

The political committee **Re-Elect Eleni Kounalakis for Lieutenant Governor 2022** is the primary fundraising vehicle for Eleni Kounalakis’s 2022 re-election campaign. Kounalakis, currently serving as the 50th Lieutenant Governor of California, has documented ties to pro-Israel advocacy organizations and has frequently utilized her platform to express support for the State of Israel and oppose movements critical of its policies.

### Endorsement by Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)
Kounalakis was endorsed by **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI)**, an organization founded to maintain and strengthen pro-Israel support within the Democratic Party. DMFI’s political action committee (DMFI PAC) identifies endorsed candidates as those "committed to strengthening the U.S.-Israel alliance."

*   **Documentation of Endorsement:** [Democratic Majority for Israel PAC - Endorsed Candidates](https://dmfipac.org/candidates/)
*   **PAC Objectives:** [Wikipedia: Democratic Majority for Israel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democratic_Majority_for_Israel)

### Public Statements and Zionist Advocacy
Kounalakis has made several public declarations regarding the "moral importance" of the State of Israel and has aligned herself with Zionist advocacy groups.

*   **Moral and National Security Support:** In a May 2024 address to the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)**, Kounalakis stated, "We have to fight back and educate our students [so they] understand the very importance morally and from a national security standpoint of the existence, celebration and empowerment of Israel in the region." 
    *   [Source: GV Wire - Lt. Gov. Kounalakis Blasts California’s Handling of Campus Gaza Protests](https://gvwire.com/2024/05/15/lt-gov-kounalakis-blasts-californias-handling-of-campus-gaza-protests/)
*   **Solidarity Advocacy:** At an October 2023 solidarity rally in Los Angeles, Kounalakis declared her "unequivocal support" for Israel. During her speech, she cited personal ties to the country, including her honeymoon and a relative who served as a nun in Jerusalem. 
    *   [Source: eJewishPhilanthropy - In L.A., a Sunday of speakers, signs and solidarity for Israel](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/in-l-a-a-sunday-of-speakers-signs-and-solidarity-for-israel/)

### Actions as University of California (UC) Regent
As an *ex officio* member of the **University of California Board of Regents**, Kounalakis has influenced university policy regarding pro-Palestinian protests and academic content she characterizes as antisemitic or anti-Israel bias.

*   **Criticism of Protest Negotiations:** Kounalakis criticized the administration of Sonoma State University for "negotiating" with pro-Palestinian protesters and agreeing to create an advisory council with student representatives from Students for Justice in Palestine (SJP). 
    *   [Source: GV Wire - Report on JPAC Summit Remarks](https://gvwire.com/2024/05/15/lt-gov-kounalakis-blasts-californias-handling-of-campus-gaza-protests/)
*   **Policy on Public Statements:** She has supported efforts by the UC Regents to restrict the use of university websites for "discretionary statements" by academic departments, a move widely viewed as a response to faculty departments posting statements in solidarity with Palestine.
    *   [Source: University of California - Board of Regents Meeting Minutes, May 2024](https://regents.universityofcalifornia.edu/regents-meetings/minutes/2024/board-meeting-minutes-may-16-2024.pdf)

### Institutional Connections
Kounalakis’s political network includes high-level figures within the pro-Israel lobby. For instance, **Sam Lauter**, a principal at BMWL and Partners and a member of the Board of Directors for Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI), has been documented as a close political associate.

*   **Institutional Ties:** [SF.gov - Records Request: Public Official Calendars and Affiliations](https://sf.gov/sites/default/files/2023-11/23.10.19%20Mayor%20Breed%20Calendar%20Entries%20%28Sam%20Lauter%29.pdf)
*   **Relationship with Hillel:** Kounalakis has been praised by organizations such as **Hillel at UCLA** for her "expanded remarks" regarding antisemitism and "anti-Israeli bias" on campus. 
    *   [Source: UCLA Hillel - Thank You UC Regents: Antisemitism Report Update](https://www.uclahillel.org/israel_on_campus)
