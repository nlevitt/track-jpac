# Contributor Report: Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2022

### Identity and Leadership Roles
The political donor entity **Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2022** is the campaign committee for California State Assemblymember Jesse Gabriel (District 46). Gabriel serves as the **Co-Chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC)**, a position he has held since 2021 [1.5, 1.14]. Under his leadership, the caucus has been documented as a "pro-Israel faction" within the state legislature, specifically dedicated to "countering anti-Zionism and anti-Israel narratives" and "strengthening California–Israel economic, technological and cultural ties" [1.3, 1.5].

### Legislative Support for Israel and Counter-Anti-Zionism
Jesse Gabriel has authored and advanced legislation that incorporates pro-Israel objectives and addresses anti-Zionist sentiment:
*   **AB 587 (Social Media Content Moderation):** Gabriel authored this law requiring social media companies to disclose their moderation practices regarding "hate speech," which specifically includes "anti-Israel content" according to legislative documentation [1.5].
*   **AB 57 (Hate Crime Data):** This bill improved data collection for "anti-Semitic and anti-Israel" incidents by law enforcement [1.5].
*   **HR 99 (Israeli-American Recognition):** In 2022, Gabriel introduced a resolution officially honoring the contributions of the Israeli-American community, an initiative spearheaded alongside the Israeli-American Civic Action Network (ICAN) [1.12].
*   **Ethnic Studies Curriculum:** Gabriel and the CLJC successfully advocated for the inclusion of the IHRA definition of antisemitism and the removal of content they characterized as "anti-Zionist" from California’s K-12 Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum [1.5, 1.9].

### Sponsored Delegations and Travel to Israel
Gabriel has participated in high-level missions to Israel funded by pro-Israel advocacy organizations:
*   **2024 Legislative Mission:** Gabriel traveled to Israel in February 2024 on a trip sponsored by the **Jewish Federation of Los Angeles** and the **Jewish Community Relations Council (JCRC)**. The trip was designed to "bear witness" to the October 7 attacks and foster "connections between California and Israel" [1.8, 1.17].
*   **2018 Mission:** Shortly after his first election, Gabriel participated in a pro-Israel legislative mission documented as a key part of his advocacy record [1.3].

### Public Advocacy and Statements
Gabriel frequently issues official statements through his committee and caucus that align with Zionist advocacy:
*   **Support for Israel’s Military Actions:** Following October 7, 2023, Gabriel released an official statement on behalf of the Jewish Caucus declaring, "We stand with Israel," and affirming Israel's "right and responsibility to defend its civilians" [1.10].
*   **Opposition to Anti-Zionist Movements:** Gabriel has publicly criticized calls for divestment from Israel and has characterized certain anti-Zionist protests as "disgusting and false" or conflated with "anti-Jewish hate" [1.19, 1.20].
*   **Democratic Party Platform:** In 2022, Gabriel led efforts to ensure the California Democratic Party platform maintained "strong, pro-Israel language" and successfully overrode attempts by party members to include language critical of Israeli policy [1.9].

### Institutional Ties and Financial Activity
*   **Payee Records:** Campaign finance disclosures for 'Jesse Gabriel for Assembly 2022' show a payment of **$13,000** to **Hillel at UCLA**, an organization active in pro-Israel advocacy on campus [1.5].
*   **Endorsements:** He is endorsed by **Democrats for Israel Los Angeles**, which described him as an "exceptional leader on Pro-Israel... issues" [1.15].
*   **Criticism:** Critics, including those writing for *LA Progressive* and *CounterPunch*, have characterized Gabriel’s caucus as a "Zionist lobby group" that works to "erase Palestinians from the political and educational discourse" [1.18, 1.19].
