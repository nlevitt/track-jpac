# Contributor Report: Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP (Akin Gump) is a multinational law and lobbying firm headquartered in Washington, D.C., with a significant presence in California, including offices in **Los Angeles**, **San Francisco**, and **Irvine** (Akin Locations (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)). As the second-largest lobbying firm in the United States by revenue, Akin Gump is a major political donor and is documented as having extensive ties to pro-Israel advocacy through its client representations, internal personnel, and strategic partnerships.

## 1. Direct Ties to Pro-Israel Lobbying & Advocacy
Akin Gump's lobbying activities include direct coordination with the primary organizations of the pro-Israel lobby in the United States.

*   **Coordination with AIPAC & Other Groups**: Foreign Agents Registration Act (FARA) filings from 2020 show that Akin Gump, while representing the United Arab Emirates (UAE), engaged the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**, the **Republican Jewish Committee (RJC)**, the **Conference of Presidents of American Jewish Organizations**, and the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)** ([Noria Research Analysis](https://noria-research.com/mena/dollars-and-decadence-intro/)). 
*   **Abraham Accords Negotiation**: The firm represented the UAE government in the negotiation of the **Abraham Accords**, the landmark agreement that normalized diplomatic relations between the UAE, Bahrain, and Israel (Akin National Security Profile (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Opposition to Al Jazeera**: Akin Gump lobbied the U.S. government on behalf of the UAE to have **Al Jazeera** (a Qatari-owned network frequently critical of Israeli policy) registered as a foreign agent, a move aligned with regional geopolitical interests that include Israel (Wikipedia: Al Jazeera Arabic (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

## 2. Advocacy for Zionist & Pro-Israel Causes
The firm has institutionalized its support for pro-Israel interests through legal helplines and campus-related advocacy.

*   **Legal Helplines with StandWithUs**: In 2024, Akin Gump co-launched a pro bono "helpline" in partnership with the **Anti-Defamation League (ADL)** and **StandWithUs** (a prominent Zionist advocacy group) to provide legal assistance for parents and students in California and other states concerning antisemitism in K-12 schools (Gibson Dunn/Law.com Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **University Intervention**: Following the October 7, 2023, attacks and subsequent campus protests, Akin Gump was a signatory to a letter sent to deans of elite law schools warning that an escalation in incidents targeting Jewish students would impact the firm's **corporate hiring decisions** (Akin Press/Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Corporate Strategic Alignment**: Critical assessments of the firm have highlighted its role in facilitating investments and technological integration with Israel, often in partnership with ideologically aligned entities like **Elliott Investment Management** ([OpenIntel Analysis](https://openintel.uk/worldpay/)).

## 3. Notable Pro-Israel Personnel
Several of Akin Gump’s most senior advisors are leading figures in pro-Israel advocacy.

*   **Ileana Ros-Lehtinen**: A Senior Advisor at the firm and former U.S. Representative, Ros-Lehtinen has been named by the **Jewish News Syndicate (JNS)** as one of the "Top 40 pro-Israel advocates." During her congressional tenure, she was widely considered "one of the Jewish state's best friends in Congress" and now leverages that expertise in her lobbying role at Akin Gump (Akin Press Release (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **Bipartisan Influence**: The firm’s public law and policy team includes numerous former members of Congress who maintain active relationships with the pro-Israel donor network to influence U.S. Middle East policy (Akin Lobbying & Public Policy (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).

## 4. California Political Contributions
As a major political donor in California and federally, Akin Gump directs substantial funds to candidates and committees that frequently align with pro-Israel policy goals.

*   **Donor Rankings**: Akin Gump is consistently ranked among the top law firm contributors to federal candidates, donating millions in each election cycle (OpenSecrets Data via Wikipedia (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
*   **California State Lobbying**: The firm is registered as a lobbyist employer in California, representing a range of interests and maintaining influence in the state legislature ([California Secretary of State Lobbying Directory](https://prd.cdn.sos.ca.gov/Lobbying_Directory.pdf)).
*   **Bundling for Leadership**: Firm lobbyists have been documented bundling hundreds of thousands of dollars for the **Democratic Senatorial Campaign Committee (DSCC)** and individual leadership PACs, providing the firm with high-level access to policy-makers responsible for foreign aid to Israel (Jacobin Report on Donor Influence (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)).
