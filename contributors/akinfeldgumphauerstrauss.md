# California Contributor Report: Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld

### **1. Contributor Profile**
**Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP** (Akin Gump) is an American multinational law firm and is currently the second-largest lobbying firm in the United States by revenue. Headquartered in Washington, D.C., the firm maintains a significant presence in California with offices in **Los Angeles**, **San Francisco**, and **Irvine**. The firm operates a political action committee (PAC), the **Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld LLP Civic Action Committee**, which distributes funds to candidates at both the federal and state levels.

### **2. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

#### **A. Registered Foreign Agent for the Government of Israel**
Akin Gump is a documented foreign agent and lobbyist for the **Government of Israel**. According to filings under the **Foreign Agents Registration Act (FARA)**, the firm has provided legal, lobbying, and public relations services for various Israeli state entities.

*   **Ministry of Defense (Israel):** Filings show Akin Gump negotiated contracts with U.S. defense contractors and lobbied the State Department on behalf of the Israeli Ministry of Defense.
    *   **Source:** DOJ FARA Filing (Registration #3492) - Government of Israel Ministry of Defense (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Jewish Agency for Israel:** The firm has represented the Jewish Agency for Israel, an organization central to Zionist activity and the "aliyah" (immigration) process to Israel, for services categorized as public relations and "educational/cultural" activities.
    *   **Source:** DOJ FARA Report (2013) - Jewish Agency for Israel (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Page 86)
*   **General Legal/Lobbying for Israel:** Periodic filings document the firm’s ongoing representation of the Government of Israel for legal advice regarding U.S. operations and employment agreements.
    *   **Source:** DOJ FARA Semi-Annual Report (2017) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Page 157)

#### **B. Coordination with Pro-Israel/Zionist Organizations**
FARA disclosures from 2021 provide evidence of direct coordination between Akin Gump, the **United Arab Emirates (UAE)** Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** to facilitate the **Abraham Accords**. The firm acted as a bridge between the UAE and the U.S. pro-Israel lobby to advance the normalization of relations with Israel.

*   **Direct Coordination Documentation:** Between July and December 2020, the firm engaged AIPAC, the **Republican Jewish Committee**, and the **Conference of Presidents of American Jewish Organizations** on behalf of foreign clients to advance regional pro-Israel policy interests.
    *   **Source:** [Noria Research - Dollars and Decadence: UAE/AIPAC Coordination](https://noria-research.com/mena/dollars-and-decadence-intro/)
*   **Strategic Support for Normalization:** The firm represented the UAE government in negotiating the Abraham Accords, the diplomatic agreement that established full ties between Israel and several Arab nations.
    *   **Source:** Akin Gump Representative Matters - Abraham Accords (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

#### **C. Personnel and Institutional Support**
Akin Gump employs high-profile pro-Israel advocates and leaders who leverage their prior government experience to benefit Zionist interests.

*   **Ileana Ros-Lehtinen:** A senior advisor at the firm and former U.S. Representative known as "the Jewish state's best friend in Congress." At Akin Gump, she is a registered foreign agent for the UAE and is recognized as a top leader in pro-Israel advocacy.
    *   **Source:** Jewish News Syndicate: Top 40 Pro-Israel Advocates - Ileana Ros-Lehtinen (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Advocacy Against Campus Protests:** In November 2023, Akin Gump was among a coalition of law firms that sent a letter to law school deans threatening corporate hiring consequences for students participating in "antisemitic" (often pro-Palestinian) protest activity.
    *   **Source:** Wikipedia: Akin Gump Strauss Hauer & Feld - Antisemitism Letter (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### **3. California Political Activity**
The firm and its employees are significant contributors to California political candidates, particularly those who align with hawkish pro-Israel foreign policy or maintain close ties to the pro-Israel lobby.

*   **Support for Adam Schiff:** The firm and its partners have contributed to the campaigns of **Adam Schiff**, a prominent pro-Israel California Democrat who serves as a key ally of AIPAC in the U.S. Senate and House.
    *   **Source:** [FEC Candidate Overview - Adam Schiff](https://www.fec.gov/data/candidate/S4CA00555/)
*   **Lobbyist Bundling:** Akin Gump lobbyists have served as "bundlers" for federal candidates with staunchly Zionist platforms, raising hundreds of thousands of dollars through the firm's networks.
    *   **Source:** Center for Public Integrity - Lobbyists Bundle for Marco Rubio (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation) (Citing Akin Gump's Geoff Verhoff)
