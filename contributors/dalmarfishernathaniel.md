# Contributor Report: Fisher Family (Gap Inc./Pisces Inc. Founders)

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

The political and philanthropic activities of the Fisher family, founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family office **Pisces Inc.**, are documented through substantial financial contributions to Zionist organizations and Israeli causes. While the name "Nathaniel Dalmar Fisher" appears in specific activist donor databases, the primary documented donors associated with these entities are **Donald Fisher** (deceased), **Doris Fisher**, and their sons **Robert**, **William**, and **John Fisher**.

### 1. Relationship to Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
The Fisher family founded **Gap Inc.** in 1969, which grew into a global retail conglomerate including Old Navy and Banana Republic. **Pisces Inc.** serves as the family office and investment vehicle for the Fisher family's multi-billion dollar fortune.
*   **Documentation:** Donald and Doris Fisher are the founders of Gap Inc., and their sons continue to hold significant leadership roles and equity. *   **Pisces Inc./Foundation:** Pisces Inc. and the Pisces Foundation are the primary vehicles for the family's assets and strategic philanthropy. [Pisces Foundation: About Us](https://piscesfoundation.org/who-we-are/).

### 2. Support for Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a major financial supporter of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to strengthen their connection to the state.
*   **Financial Support:** The Fisher family is recognized as "charter funders" or prominent donors to the Birthright Israel Foundation. Birthright Israel Foundation Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); J Weekly: Fisher Family Philanthropy (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Leadership:** Family members have participated in major fundraising initiatives for Birthright, which has sent over 800,000 participants to Israel to date. Birthright Israel: Impact (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 3. Association with the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has a long-standing relationship with the **Koret Foundation**, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization that lists "Supporting Israel" as a core pillar of its mission.
*   **Philanthropic Partnership:** The Fisher family has historically collaborated with the Koret Foundation on numerous Jewish community and pro-Israel initiatives in the San Francisco Bay Area. Koret Foundation: Our Mission (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation); Jewish Community Federation of SF: Fisher Support (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Specific Projects:** The Fishers contributed significantly to the Jewish Community Center (JCC) of San Francisco, including funding the **Fisher Family Hall**, a project also supported by the Koret Foundation. JCCSF: Donor Recognition (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 4. Zionist Advocacy and Pro-Israel Contributions
Beyond Birthright and Koret, the Fisher family’s support for Israel is evidenced by their extensive involvement with the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund (JCF)**, which directs millions to Israeli security and social programs.
*   **Institutional Support:** Donald Fisher was a "longtime supporter" of the S.F.-based Jewish Community Federation, which maintains a strong Zionist mission and provides significant funding to Israeli entities. J Weekly: Donald Fisher Obituary (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Political Context:** The family’s philanthropy is often cited by activist groups, such as the BDS (Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions) movement, as a reason for boycotting Gap Inc. due to the founders' support for the State of Israel. [BDS Movement: Boycott Gap/Old Navy](https://bdsmovement.net/Guide-to-BDS-Boycott).

### 5. Documented Family Members
*   **Donald G. Fisher (1928–2009):** Co-founder of Gap Inc. and primary architect of the family's philanthropic strategy.
*   **Doris F. Fisher:** Co-founder and major donor to Jewish and Israeli causes.
*   **Robert J. Fisher:** Chairman of Gap Inc. and Trustee of the Pisces Foundation. InfluenceWatch: Robert Fisher (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
