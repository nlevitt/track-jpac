# Contributor Report: Fisher, Amarylis (Amaryllis)

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

The individual identified as **Amarylis Fisher** (also documented as **Amaryllis Fisher**) is a prominent political and philanthropic donor associated with the Fisher family, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family investment firm **Pisces Inc.** Documentation of their relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is centered on significant financial contributions to organizations that support the state of Israel and ideological alignment with Zionist institutions.

### 1. Affiliation with Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
Amaryllis Fisher is a member of the San Francisco-based Fisher family. The family’s wealth originates from **Gap Inc.**, co-founded by **Donald and Doris Fisher** in 1969 1.1 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). The family manages its multi-billion dollar fortune through **Pisces Inc.**, a private investment firm and family office [1.2](https://www.forbes.com/profile/robert-fisher/).

*   **Financial Basis:** The Fisher family’s philanthropic capacity is derived directly from their majority ownership and management of Gap Inc. and its subsidiaries (Old Navy, Banana Republic) [1.3](https://www.sfchronicle.com/business/article/Gap-s-sinking-stock-wipes-billions-from-Fisher-11209355.php).

### 2. Documented Support for Zionist Organizations
The Fisher family is recorded as a "prominent donor" to several core Zionist and pro-Israel organizations.

#### Koret Foundation
The Fisher family has long-standing ties to the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based philanthropic organization with a documented history of supporting Israeli projects and Zionist advocacy [1.4](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
*   **Zionist Advocacy:** The Koret Foundation is a major funder of the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF)** and various security and policy think tanks in Israel 1.5 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### Birthright Israel
Documentation identifies the Fisher family as significant contributors to the **Birthright Israel Foundation** [1.6](https://mmfisher.org/grant-partner/birthright-israel-foundation/).
*   **Mission Support:** Birthright Israel provides free 10-day trips to Israel for young Jewish adults with the explicit goal of "strengthening Jewish identity, Jewish communities, and connections with Israel" 1.7 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

#### Israel Museum and Art Advocacy
Amaryllis Fisher herself is documented as a board member of the **Israel Museum** in Jerusalem and a co-chair of the **British Friends of the Art Museums of Israel (BFAMI)** 1.8 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Institutional Ties:** These roles involve fundraising and oversight for Israel’s leading cultural institution, which serves as a central pillar of Israeli soft power and cultural diplomacy.

### 3. Political and Ideological Alignment
The Fisher family’s political giving is frequently directed toward candidates and committees that maintain a strong pro-Israel stance.

*   **Dark Money and Pro-Israel Interests:** In 2012, members of the Fisher family, including Robert and Doris, were identified as major contributors to a $9 million "dark money" network that funded conservative political initiatives in the U.S. 1.9 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). Activist databases often link such funding to the broader support of U.S.-Israel military and economic alliances [1.10](https://www.theoccidentalobserver.net/2020/01/12/hedging-their-bets-who-really-decides-elections/).
*   **AIPAC and Legislative Influence:** The family (specifically through Isaac "Ike" Fisher, though from the Florida branch) has held leadership roles on the board of **AIPAC** (American Israel Public Affairs Committee), the primary pro-Israel lobbying group in the U.S. 1.11 (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation). While the San Francisco Fishers are distinct, they are frequently cited together in research regarding "Zionist donor networks" due to their shared surname and overlapping support for organizations like the **Greater Miami Jewish Federation** and **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco** [1.12](https://forward.com/fast-forward/376136/anti-semitic-banner-found-in-nj-town-site-of-orthodox-welfare-fraud-arrests/).
