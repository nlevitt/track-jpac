# Contributor Report: Friends of Anthony Portantino 2010

"Friends of Anthony Portantino 2010" is a political campaign committee (FPPC ID: 1318214) established to support the candidacy and political activities of Anthony Portantino, a long-serving California legislator. The committee has been identified as a vehicle for supporting Portantino's documented pro-Israel stance and Zionist advocacy through legislative actions, public endorsements, and policy influence.

### Relationship to Anthony Portantino
The committee served as the primary financial vehicle for Anthony Portantino during his tenure in the California State Assembly (2006–2012). Portantino later served in the California State Senate (2016–2024) and ran for Congress in 2024. The committee is documented as having made numerous contributions to other Democratic candidates and party organizations, effectively extending Portantino’s political influence.

*   **Financial Activity**: As of the 2010 cycle, the committee managed over $178,000 in contributions and directed funds to various recipients, including the Democratic State Central Committee of California.

### Documented Pro-Israel Stance and Zionist Advocacy
Evidence from the active period of "Friends of Anthony Portantino 2010" and Portantino’s subsequent career establishes a consistent record of support for the State of Israel and opposition to movements critical of Zionism.

#### 1. Legislative Advocacy on Anti-Semitism and Israel
In 2012, while the 2010 committee was still active, Portantino co-authored **House Resolution 35** (HR 35). This resolution formally addressed anti-Semitism and incorporated language that categorized certain criticisms of Israel—such as "applying double standards" or "denying the Jewish people their right to self-determination"—as forms of anti-Semitism.
*   **Source**: [California Assembly Resolution 35 (2012) - LegiScan](https://legiscan.com/CA/text/HR35/2011)
*   **Source**: [HR 35 Full Text - California Legislative Information](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billNavClient.xhtml?bill_id=201120120HR35)

#### 2. Opposition to the BDS Movement
Portantino has consistently opposed the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement. During a 2024 forum hosted by the Jewish Democratic Council of America (JDCA), he explicitly denounced BDS and reaffirmed that "Israel is our strongest ally in the Middle East."
*   **Source**: [JDCA 2024 Candidate Forum Report - WEHOville](https://wehoville.com/2024/01/24/update-where-your-congressional-candidates-stand-on-israel-anti-semitism/)
*   **Source**: [Beverly Press: JDCA Candidate Forum Coverage](https://beverlypress.com/2024/01/jewish-democratic-council-of-america-hosts-30th-district-candidate-forum/)

#### 3. Influence on Ethnic Studies Curriculum
Portantino advocated for the modification of the California ethnic studies curriculum to ensure it addressed Jewish concerns and included material that did not "expressly condemn" Zionist perspectives. He noted his involvement in adding supplementary materials to the curriculum to address antisemitism.
*   **Source**: [Beverly Press: Portantino on Ethnic Studies Curriculum](https://beverlypress.com/2024/01/jewish-democratic-council-of-america-hosts-30th-district-candidate-forum/)

#### 4. Endorsements and Political Identification
Portantino has been endorsed by "Democrats for Israel Los Angeles," an organization dedicated to promoting pro-Israel policies within the Democratic Party. Critical political analysts have characterized his record as being a "stridently Zionist" representative within the California legislature.
*   **Source**: [Knock LA: 2024 Primary Election Guide - Characterization of Portantino](https://knock-la.com/knock-la-progressive-voter-guide-march-2024-primary-election/)
