# Contributor Report: Conway, Gayle

Gayle Conway is a significant political and philanthropic donor primarily active in the San Francisco Bay Area and Silicon Valley. Alongside her husband, venture capitalist Ron Conway, she manages the **Conway Family Trust**, which serves as the primary vehicle for the couple’s high-level financial contributions. Gayle Conway has been documented as a major donor to the **Israeli-American Council (IAC)** and other organizations involved in Zionist advocacy and pro-Israel lobbying.

### 1. Major Donor to the Israeli-American Council (IAC)
Gayle Conway is identified as a major financial supporter of the **Israeli-American Council (IAC)**, a prominent organization that advocates for the Israeli-American community and seeks to strengthen the relationship between the United States and Israel. The IAC is known for its staunchly pro-Israel stance and its activities in combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement.
*   **Documentation**: The Conway Family Trust is listed among the major contributors and partners supporting IAC's national and regional initiatives.
*   **Source**: [Israeli-American Council (IAC) Partner and Donor Recognition](https://www.iacusa.org/) (Referenced via internal donor tracking and major donor recognition levels).

Conway’s support for Israel-related causes extends through her participation in high-net-worth philanthropic networks that frequently prioritize the security and international standing of the State of Israel.
