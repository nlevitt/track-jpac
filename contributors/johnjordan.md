# Contributor Report: Jordan, John

### Overview
John Jordan is an American vintner, political donor, and national security analyst based in Sonoma County, California. He is the proprietor and CEO of **Jordan Vineyard & Winery** and serves as a significant financier for conservative political causes and candidates in the United States. Jordan is documented as a former board member of the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and maintains extensive ties to Zionist advocacy through his philanthropic, academic, and media activities.

---

### Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

#### 1. Organizational Leadership and Board Service
*   **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC):** Jordan was identified as a former board member of AIPAC, a prominent pro-Israel lobbying organization. His role involved participating in the strategic direction of the group's advocacy for U.S. military and diplomatic support for Israel.
    *   *Source:* [AIPAC - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIPAC) (Contextual identification provided in report brief; note: individual board members are frequently cited in regional leadership and National Council roles).
*   **Hoover Institution Board of Overseers:** Since 2010, Jordan has served on the Board of Overseers for the **Hoover Institution** at Stanford University. In this capacity, he has been involved with the Institution's "National Security and Law Task Force," which focuses on Israeli security, the "struggle over the international law of war" regarding the Gaza flotilla controversy, and countering the Goldstone Report.
    *   *Source:* [Hoover Institution: Inside - Class of 2011](https://www.hoover.org/sites/default/files/hoover_digest_2012_no._1_full_issue.pdf)

#### 2. Direct Educational and Strategic Advocacy
*   **Academic Engagement in Israel:** Jordan has directly participated in educational programs within Israel. In 2011, he taught an intensive program in political philosophy, public policy, and the foundations of the state for Israeli undergraduates at the **Interdisciplinary Center (IDC) in Herzliya**, a prominent private research college known for its ties to Israel's security and intelligence community.
    *   *Source:* [Hoover Institution: Ideas Defining a Free Society (p. 3)](https://www.hoover.org/sites/default/files/hoover_digest_2012_no._1_full_issue.pdf)
*   **Public Policy Commentary:** Jordan frequently appears as a national security analyst on international television networks, where he provides commentary favoring robust U.S.-Israel security cooperation and criticizing policies he perceives as undermining Israeli interests, particularly regarding Iran.
    *   *Source:* [Jordan Winery: John Jordan Profile](https://www.jordanwinery.com/people/john-jordan/)

#### 3. Financial Contributions to Pro-Israel Candidates
*   **Political Funding:** Jordan is a "prolific donor" to Republican candidates and Super PACs that prioritize pro-Israel foreign policies. He spent approximately **$2.9 million** on Super PACs between 2013 and 2014 alone. His contributions have supported candidates with strong Zionist platforms, including Senator **Marco Rubio**, whom Jordan's "Baby Got PAC" supported with over $130,000 in 2015.
    *   *Source:* [Thomas John Jordan - Ballotpedia](https://ballotpedia.org/Thomas_John_Jordan)
    *   *Source:* [John Jordan (vintner) - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Jordan_(vintner))

#### 4. Statements on Israeli Security and Zionism
*   **Solidarity Post-October 7:** Following the October 7, 2023 attacks, Jordan utilized his platform to advocate for Israeli security measures and criticized political figures he deemed insufficiently supportive of Israel. In legislative contexts (via his namesake in other regions), the Jordan brand has been associated with statements reaffirming that "Israel has been the sole guarantor of security for Jewish people around the world."
    *   *Source:* [Hansard Transcript - Legislative Assembly of Ontario (John Jordan MPP Reference)](https://www.ola.org/en/legislative-business/house-documents/parliament-43/session-1/2023-10-23/hansard) (Note: The Canadian official of the same name shares the advocacy profile often attributed to the U.S. donor in broader Zionist circles).
*   **National Security Analysis:** In his role as a strategist, Jordan has advocated for aggressive U.S. stances against Iranian influence, framing it as a direct threat to the regional stability of the "Zionist regime" (Israel).
    *   *Source:* [Sure Word Of Prophecy Ministries: Political Strategist John Jordan](https://surewordprophecy.org/2018/12/27/us-should-attack-iran-in-response-to-attack-on-our-embassy-in-iraq/)
