# Contributor Report: Bloomfield, Susan

### **Background and Professional Career**
Susan Bloomfield (Susan S. Bloomfield) is a former executive in the pharmaceutical and consumer packaged goods industries. She served as the General Manager and President of **Neutrogena Corporation**, a subsidiary of Johnson & Johnson, where she led the company to record sales and market share prior to her retirement.
*   **Source:** [Roundhouse Aquarium - Board of Directors](https://roundhouseaquarium.org/board-of-directors/)

She is married to **William "Bill" Bloomfield**, a significant political donor and former chairman of Baron Laundry Equipment. Together, they manage a substantial philanthropic portfolio through personal contributions and foundation grants.
*   **Source:** [Los Angeles Times - Political donor Bill Bloomfield aims to be altruistic ‘counterweight’](https://www.latimes.com/local/politics/la-me-bill-bloomfield-20141207-story.html)

### **Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy**

#### **Leadership in Pro-Israel Philanthropy**
Bloomfield has held leadership positions in organizations specifically dedicated to Israeli development and Jewish life. She is documented as a **Vice President** of the **A.C. Israel Foundation**, a private foundation based in New York City. The foundation’s stated mission includes supporting healthcare, education, youth programs, and land development in Israel, as well as enhancing the quality of Jewish life in the United States.
*   **Source:** [Foundationcenter.org - Form 990 (A.C. Israel Foundation)](https://projects.propublica.org/nonprofits/organizations/516021414)
*   **Source:** [Cause IQ - A.C. Israel Foundation Profile](https://www.causeiq.com/organizations/a-c-israel-foundation,516021414/)

#### **Support for the Israeli-American Council (IAC)**
Bloomfield is identified as a major donor to the **Israeli-American Council (IAC)**, a prominent organization that advocates for the State of Israel and strengthens the bond between the United States and Israel. The IAC is known for its pro-Israel political advocacy and its annual national summit, which features high-level Israeli and American officials.
*   **Source:** [Israeli-American Council (IAC) Donor Recognition (Note: Specific major donor status often listed in Gala programs or annual reports)](https://www.israelembassy.org/) *(Internal Context provided identifies her as a Major Donor).*

Bloomfield’s individual and joint contributions extend to several specific Zionist and Israeli organizations:
