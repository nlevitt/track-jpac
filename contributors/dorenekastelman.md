# Contributor Report: Kastelman, Dorene

### Overview
Dorene Kastelman is a political activist and donor based in Los Gatos, California, who holds leadership positions in several organizations focused on the U.S.-Israel relationship and Jewish political advocacy within the Democratic Party. She currently serves as the President of the **Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area (JDCBA)** and the President of **California Jewish Democrats (CJD)**. Her documented activities focus on the security of Israel, advocacy for hostages held in Gaza, and the promotion of pro-Israel candidates.

### 1. Organizational Leadership and Zionist Advocacy

#### Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area (JDCBA)
Kastelman is the President of the JDCBA (formerly known as the Jewish Democratic Coalition of Silicon Valley - JDCSV). The organization is a chartered club of the Democratic Party that explicitly integrates pro-Israel advocacy into its core mission and endorsement criteria.
*   **Mission & Criteria:** The JDCBA bylaws and purpose state it is a "pro-Jewish and pro-Israel Club." Candidates seeking endorsement must support "Israel as a Jewish and democratic state" and advocate against antisemitism.
*   **Documentation:** [JDCBA Endorsements and Purpose](https://www.jdcsv.com/endorsements.html)
*   **Documented Role:** [San Mateo County Democratic Party Club List](https://www.smcdems.org/local-chartered-democratic-clubs/)

#### California Jewish Democrats (CJD)
For the 2026–2028 term, Kastelman serves as the President of California Jewish Democrats. This organization describes itself as the primary bridge between the California Democratic Party and the pro-Israel community.
*   **Mission:** CJD is committed to "supporting the right of Israel to exist as a peaceful, democratic and pluralistic Jewish state" and working within the Democratic Party to elect officials aligned with these values.
*   **Documentation:** [California Jewish Democrats - Who We Are](https://www.dfi-ca.org/who-we-are)

### 2. Post-October 7 Advocacy and Hostage Awareness
Following the October 7, 2023, attacks, Kastelman has engaged in public advocacy focused on Israel's security and the humanitarian status of Jewish hostages.
*   **"Run for Their Lives":** Kastelman is a member of the local "Run for Their Lives" chapter, a global movement that advocates for the safe release of hostages held by Hamas in Gaza. She has publicly appealed to local government bodies, such as the Los Gatos Town Council, to raise awareness of the hostages.
*   **Israel Solidarity:** She and her husband, James Kastelman, visited Israel shortly after the October 7 attacks to show support for the community.
*   **Documentation:** [Los Gatan - Jewish couple requests solidarity (June 2024)](https://losgatan.com/jewish-couple-requests-solidarity-from-los-gatos-council-after-boulder-attack/)

### 3. Political Contributions and Sponsorships
Kastelman is a documented donor to both localized pro-Israel political groups and broader Democratic entities.
*   **JDCBA Contributions:** Federal Election Commission (FEC) records show direct financial support to the Jewish Democratic Coalition of the Bay Area.
    *   **Documentation:** [FEC Report of Receipts and Disbursements (2025)](https://docquery.fec.gov/cgi-bin/forms/C00429571/1841381/sb/all)
*   **Hillel of Silicon Valley:** Kastelman and her husband are listed as "Benefactors" and "Sponsors" of Hillel of Silicon Valley, an organization that works to counter anti-Israel sentiment on college campuses.
    *   **Documentation:** [Hillel of Silicon Valley Annual Report](https://www.hillelsv.org/uploads/2/6/4/2/26425712/hsv_2011_newsletter_final.pdf)
*   **California Democratic Party (CADEM):** As a member of the Santa Clara County Democratic Central Committee, she co-sponsors resolutions within the state party, including those addressing democracy and religious nationalism.
    *   **Documentation:** [CADEM 2023 Resolution Packet (Page 79)](https://www.cadem.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/May-2023-CDP-Resolution-Packet.pdf)
