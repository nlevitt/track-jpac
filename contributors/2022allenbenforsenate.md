# Contributor Report: Ben Allen for Senate 2022

**Ben Allen for Senate 2022** is the campaign committee of California State Senator Ben Allen, who represents District 24 (formerly District 26). Allen is a prominent member and former Chair of the California Legislative Jewish Caucus (CLJC), a body that explicitly advocates for the strengthening of California–Israel ties and the countering of anti-Israel narratives in public policy and education.

### 1. Legislative Advocacy and Anti-BDS Initiatives
Allen has been a key proponent of legislation designed to discourage the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement against Israel. 

*   **AB 2844 (California Combating the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions of Israel Act of 2016):** Allen was instrumental in the introduction and passage of this bill, which requires state contractors to certify they are in compliance with civil rights laws and are not participating in boycotts of sovereign nations, specifically mentioning Israel. Allen stated that the BDS movement uses "demonizing and delegitimizing propaganda" against Israel.
    *   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Governor Signs Anti-BDS Bill](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/2016-10-04-governor-signs-anti-bds-bill)
    *   **Source:** [LegiScan - CA AB2844 Bill Text](https://legiscan.com/CA/text/AB2844/2015)
*   **Memorandum of Understanding (MOU):** As a leader in the Jewish Caucus, Allen has advocated for the reaffirmation of the 2014 MOU between California and Israel, which facilitates bilateral cooperation in technology, cybersecurity, and environmental management.
    *   **Source:** [Official Press Release - Senator Ben Allen Named Vice-Chair of Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2016-12-02-senator-ben-allen-named-vice-chair-legislative-jewish-caucus)

### 2. Education Policy and "Zionist Guardrails"
Allen has leveraged his positions on the Senate Education Committee and the Instructional Quality Commission (IQC) to influence the inclusion of pro-Israel perspectives in California's curriculum.

*   **Ethnic Studies Model Curriculum (ESMC):** Allen and the CLJC successfully advocated for the inclusion of "guardrails" in the state’s ethnic studies curriculum. These measures were intended to prevent the inclusion of content that the Caucus deemed "anti-Zionist" or supportive of the BDS movement.
    *   **Source:** [Wikipedia - California Legislative Jewish Caucus (Section: Ethnic Studies Guardrails)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_Legislative_Jewish_Caucus#2017%E2%80%932019:_JPAC_partnership,_increased_allocations,_ethnic_studies_guardrails)
    *   **Source:** [American Jewish Committee (AJC) - Meeting with Senator Ben Allen on Ethnic Studies](https://www.ajc.org/news/state-senator-ben-allen-meets-with-ajc)
*   **Holocaust and Genocide Education:** Allen has co-sponsored legislation, such as AB 1468, to establish standard instructional materials for Holocaust education, working alongside organizations like the American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC) and the Women’s International Zionist Organization (WIZO).
    *   **Source:** [CODEPINK - CA Israel Lobby Aims to Police Ethnic Studies](https://www.codepink.org/ca_israel_lobby_aims_to_police_ethnic_studies_why_ab_1468_don_t_say_palestine)

### 3. Institutional Ties and International Engagement
Allen maintains documented relationships with major pro-Israel advocacy organizations and has participated in state-sanctioned trips to Israel.

*   **Organizational Affiliations:** Allen is a graduate of the **Jewish Federation's New Leaders Project** and the **Anti-Defamation League's (ADL) Glass Leadership Institute**. He frequently consults with the **Jewish Public Affairs Committee of California (JPAC)** to align legislative priorities with pro-Israel institutional interests.
    *   **Source:** [Official Biography - Senator Ben Allen](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/biography)
    *   **Source:** [CA.gov - Senator Ben Allen Named Chair of Jewish Caucus](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2018-12-12-senator-ben-allen-named-chair-california-legislative-jewish-caucus)
*   **Diplomatic Visits:** In 2022, Allen participated in a legislative delegation to Israel, which included visits to Kibbutz K’far Aza. He has publicly described these visits as foundational to his perspective on the regional conflict and his commitment to Israeli security.
    *   **Source:** [Official Update - Civil Discourse & Anti-Bigotry Work](https://sd24.senate.ca.gov/news/2023-12-21-civil-discourse-anti-bigotry-work)
*   **Public Commemorations:** Allen has used the Senate floor to honor the 70th anniversary of the founding of the State of Israel, reaffirming California's "enduring solidarity" with the nation.
    *   **Source:** [California Legislative Jewish Caucus - Senator Allen Honoring Israel at 70](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/video/2018-05-16-senator-allen-honoring-70th-anniversary-state-israel)

The committee **Ben Allen for Senate 2022** serves as a vehicle for collecting contributions from various sectors, including pro-Israel donors and affiliated PACs.

*   **Campaign Contributions:** Allen has received financial support from individuals and groups associated with pro-Israel advocacy, such as members of the **American Jewish Committee (AJC)** and local Jewish community leadership.
