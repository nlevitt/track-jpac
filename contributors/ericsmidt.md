# Contributor Report: Smidt, Eric

Eric Smidt is the chairman and CEO of **Harbor Freight Tools** and the founder of **The Smidt Foundation**. His philanthropic activity includes documented financial support for organizations focused on education in Israel, Middle East policy advocacy, and the monitoring of antisemitism.

### 1. Documented Support for Israel-Based Organizations
Smidt has contributed to organizations that operate directly within Israel or provide services specifically for Israeli citizens and institutions.

*   **Youth Renewal Fund (YRF):** Eric and Susan Smidt are documented donors to the Youth Renewal Fund, an organization that provides supplemental education and resources to underprivileged children in Israel. YRF partners with Israeli municipalities to address socioeconomic gaps through academic programming.
    *   **Source:** [GuideStar - Youth Renewal Fund Annual Report (Page 10)](https://www.guidestar.org/ViewPgms.aspx?orgId=2003884)
*   **Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF):** Smidt is identified as a contributor to the Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF), an organization that raises funds to support the well-being and social programs for soldiers in the Israeli military.
    *   **Source:** [Powerbase - Friends of the Israel Defense Forces Donor Records](https://powerbase.info/index.php/Friends_of_the_Israel_Defense_Forces)

### 2. Leadership and Advisory Roles in Middle East Policy
Smidt has held formal advisory positions within think tanks and policy groups focused on Israel and the Middle East.

*   **Saban Center for Middle East Policy:** Smidt served on the **International Advisory Council** of the Saban Center at the Brookings Institution. The center was established to focus on Middle East policy, specifically the Arab-Israeli conflict and U.S. strategic interests in the region.
    *   **Source:** [Brookings Institution - Saban Center for Middle East Policy Annual Report](https://www.brookings.edu/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/2004_annual_report.pdf)
*   **American Jewish Committee (AJC):** Smidt is recognized as a significant donor and supporter of pro-Israel advocacy organizations, including the American Jewish Committee, which engages in global advocacy for the security of Israel and Jewish communities.
    *   **Source:** [InfluenceWatch - Smidt Family Foundation Philanthropy Profile](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/smidt-family-foundation/)

### 3. Advocacy Against Antisemitism and Holocaust Education
Smidt’s philanthropic work frequently intersects with pro-Israel advocacy through his support for organizations that monitor anti-Israel sentiment and provide education on Jewish history.

*   **Anti-Defamation League (ADL):** Eric Smidt has identified himself as a "long-time supporter" of the ADL, an organization that monitors antisemitism and provides advocacy related to the State of Israel.
    *   **Source:** [eJewishPhilanthropy - Smidt Foundation Gift Announcement](https://ejewishphilanthropy.com/5-million-smidt-foundation-gift-moves-l-as-holocaust-museum-toward-expansion/)
*   **Holocaust Museum LA:** In 2022, the Smidt Foundation provided a **$5 million matching gift** to the Holocaust Museum LA for a major campus expansion. Smidt cited the "dramatic rise in hate directed at Jews" as a primary motivation for the contribution.
    *   **Source:** [Jewish News Syndicate (JNS) - Harbor Freight CEO Matching Gift](https://www.jns.org/harbor-freight-ceo-provides-5-million-matching-gift-to-holocaust-museum-la/)
