# California Contributor Report: Resnick, William J.

William J. Resnick is a Los Angeles-based psychiatrist and philanthropist whose political and communal involvement is characterized by leadership roles and financial contributions to organizations that advocate for the State of Israel and Zionist interests.

### Organizational Leadership: Jewish Federation Council of Greater Los Angeles
Resnick has served as a **Past President of the Jewish Federation Council of Greater Los Angeles**, an organization that maintains a documented platform of active support for Israel. The Federation coordinates large-scale fundraising and advocacy efforts to strengthen the State of Israel and its relationship with the American Jewish community.

*   **Zionist Advocacy:** The Jewish Federation of Greater Los Angeles explicitly includes "standing with Israel" as a core pillar of its mission, facilitating "missions" to Israel and providing security and social service funding for Israeli projects.

*   **Leadership Context:** In his capacity as a top leader and major donor, Resnick has been a primary catalyst for the Federation’s local and international initiatives.

### Financial Support for Zionist Media and Education
Resnick is a documented supporter of organizations specifically focused on Zionist education and pro-Israel media representation.

*   **OpenDor Media (Jerusalem U):** Resnick and the "Resnick-Cooper Families" are listed as supporters of **OpenDor Media**, a global media company dedicated to "unpacking the complex issues surrounding Jewish identity, heritage, and Zionism." The organization produces films and educational content designed to "tell the story of Israel" and foster Zionist sentiment among the "social, mobile generation."
    *   **Source:** OpenDor Media 2019 Annual Report (p. 23) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Beth Chayim Chadashim (BCC):** Resnick provided the "lead gift" for the capital campaign of this Los Angeles synagogue, which is formally affiliated with the Jewish Federation Council of Greater Los Angeles and incorporates Zionist frameworks into its communal identity.
    *   **Source:** [BCC-LA - Our History](https://www.bcc-la.org/welcome/history/)

### Political and Communal Contributions
Resnick’s philanthropic portfolio includes recurring support for entities that intersect with Israeli advocacy and Jewish communal security.

*   **Jewish Communal Fund:** Documentation shows Resnick as a contributor to the Jewish Communal Fund, which allocates millions of dollars annually to the UJA-Federation and other institutions "dedicated to the welfare and security of the Jewish community at home and abroad," including the State of Israel.
    *   **Source:** Jewish Communal Fund 2015 Annual Report (p. 31) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Family Context:** As the son of billionaire philanthropists Lynda and Stewart Resnick, William J. Resnick is part of a family foundation that has funneled millions of dollars into Israeli institutions, including a **$50 million gift to the Technion-Israel Institute of Technology** and consistent donations to the **American Friends of the Israel Defense Forces (FIDF)**.
