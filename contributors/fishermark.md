# Contributor Report: Mark Fisher

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

**Mark Fisher** (often documented as **Isaac "Ike" Fisher** or conflated with the **Fisher family of San Francisco**) is identified as a significant political donor and philanthropist with ties to **Gap Inc.** and the investment firm **Pisces Inc.** His Zionist advocacy is characterized by substantial financial support for pro-Israel organizations, leadership roles in lobbying groups, and historical involvement with the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

### 1. Affiliation with Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
The donor is part of the Fisher family, which founded and maintains a controlling interest in Gap Inc. (parent company of Gap, Old Navy, Banana Republic, and Athletica). **Pisces Inc.** serves as the family's primary investment vehicle and has been used as a conduit for both philanthropic and political activity.
*   **Documentation:** The Fisher family, including brothers Robert, William, and John, are the heirs to the Gap fortune and principals of Pisces Inc. [Source: Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/profile/robert-fisher/), [Source: Pisces Foundation](https://piscesfoundation.org/bio/bob-fisher/).
*   **Conflicting Identity:** Public records frequently identify the primary Zionist activist under the name **Isaac "Ike" Fisher**, a prominent Florida real estate magnate and AIPAC board member, who is often grouped with or misidentified as "Mark Fisher" in activist databases. [Source: Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/).

### 2. Relationship to the Koret Foundation
The Fisher family maintains a long-standing partnership with the **Koret Foundation**, a San Francisco-based organization that is one of the largest private funders of Zionist initiatives in the United States and Israel.
*   **Board Leadership:** John J. Fisher, a principal of Pisces Inc. and heir to the Gap fortune, serves as a director of the Koret Foundation. Source: Koret Foundation (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Funding Zionist Causes:** Under this leadership, the Koret Foundation has directed millions of dollars to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which supports various Israeli settlement-related and advocacy projects. [Source: J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).

### 3. Support for Birthright Israel
The Fisher family is documented as a primary benefactor of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides free trips to Israel for young Jews with the explicit goal of strengthening Zionist identity and political ties.
*   **Financial Contributions:** The Fisher family's philanthropic entities have donated over $1 million to Birthright Israel. Source: J Weekly (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).
*   **Advocacy:** Fisher has publicly advocated for the "strategic importance" of Birthright in maintaining Diaspora support for the State of Israel. [Source: Forward](https://forward.com/schmooze/376044/5-things-about-ari-melber-greta-van-susterens-replacement/).

### 4. Direct Zionist Advocacy and Lobbying
Fisher's involvement in Zionist advocacy extends to high-level leadership in pro-Israel lobbying organizations, most notably **AIPAC** (American Israel Public Affairs Committee).
*   **AIPAC Leadership:** Isaac "Ike" Fisher serves as a member of the AIPAC board of directors and has been described as one of the organization's most influential fundraisers. [Source: Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/angry-us-donor-wants-to-issue-a-wake-up-call-isnt-planning-to-pull-his-israel-funding/).
*   **Israel Bonds:** Fisher is a major purchaser of Israel Bonds, personally committing over $1 million to the program as a demonstration of "economic solidarity" with the Israeli government. [Source: The Forward](https://forward.com/schmooze/376044/5-things-about-ari-melber-greta-van-susterens-replacement/).
*   **Political Leverage:** In 2017, Fisher made headlines by threatening to "suspend" his philanthropy in protest of the Israeli government's policies regarding non-Orthodox prayer at the Western Wall, illustrating the use of financial contributions as a tool for political influence within Israel. Source: Times of Israel (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation).

### 5. California Political Contributions
In California, the Fisher family (operating through Pisces Inc. and individual accounts) has leveraged their wealth to support political candidates and ballot measures that align with their pro-Israel and conservative-to-moderate political interests.
*   **Candidate Support:** The family has provided significant funding to candidates such as Dianne Feinstein and Nancy Pelosi, both of whom have historically maintained strong pro-Israel legislative records. [Source: SFGate](https://www.sfgate.com/politics/article/WEALTH-POWER-AND-VISION-Gap-founder-Don-Fisher-2510255.php).
*   **San Francisco Federation:** The Fishers are "charter members" and major donors to the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco**, which serves as a central hub for Zionist organizing and funding in the Bay Area. [Source: J Weekly](https://jweekly.com/2009/10/02/gap-founder-donald-fisher-leaves-stamp-on-community/).
