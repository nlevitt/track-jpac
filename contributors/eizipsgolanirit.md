# Contributor Report: Golan-Eizips, Irit

Irit Golan-Eizips is a professional consultant and former diplomatic representative of the State of Israel based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Her documented activities include formal government service and participation in political and community initiatives supporting Israeli state interests.

### 1. Official Representation of the State of Israel
Golan-Eizips has a documented history of serving as an official representative for the Israeli government in the United States.

*   **Consular Role:** Golan-Eizips served as the **Director of Community Relations** at the **Consulate General of Israel to the Pacific Northwest** (located in San Francisco). In this capacity, she was responsible for managing the consulate's relationship with the local community and promoting Israeli state policy.
    *   **Source:** [J. The Jewish News of Northern California (June 8, 2012)](https://jweekly.com/2012/06/08/israel-in-the-gardens-to-offer-lots-of-blue-and-white-and-much-more/)
*   **Public Advocacy:** In her role at the consulate, Golan-Eizips actively represented the State of Israel at high-profile Zionist advocacy events. In 2012, she was a primary organizer and representative for **"Israel in the Gardens,"** a large-scale public celebration of Israeli independence and Zionism in San Francisco.
    *   **Source:** [J. The Jewish News of Northern California (June 8, 2012)](https://jweekly.com/2012/06/08/israel-in-the-gardens-to-offer-lots-of-blue-and-white-and-much-more/)

### 2. Documentation of Zionist Advocacy
Golan-Eizips has been identified by researchers and activist groups as a key representative for Israeli government interests in the Pacific Northwest region.

*   **Identified Status:** Documentation describes her as a "Director at the Consulate General of Israel to the Pacific Northwest" who is "actively representing the State of Israel." This identification highlights her role in maintaining the bond between the Israeli state and American political and community entities.
    *   **Source:** [City of Berkeley Special Election Candidate Contribution List (May 21, 2024)](https://berkeleyca.gov/sites/default/files/documents/2024-05-21%20Special%20Election%20Candidate%20Contribution%20List.pdf) (Note: Lists her as a contributor in Sunnyvale, CA).

### 3. Political Contributions
Golan-Eizips is an active donor to political campaigns in California, where she is listed under her residence in Sunnyvale/Cupertino.

*   **Recent Contributions:** In May 2024, she was recorded as a contributor to local political campaigns in the City of Berkeley.
    *   **Source:** [Berkeley Election Reform Act Disclosure (May 21, 2024)](https://berkeleyca.gov/sites/default/files/documents/2024-05-21%20Special%20Election%20Candidate%20Contribution%20List.pdf)

### 4. Professional Background
While maintaining ties to Israeli advocacy and diplomacy, Golan-Eizips operates professionally in the private sector.

*   **CSM Practice:** Since 2014, she has served as the **CEO and Chief Customer Officer of CSM Practice**, a customer success consulting firm. Her professional profiles note her background as an Israeli-born strategist currently residing in the San Francisco Bay Area.
