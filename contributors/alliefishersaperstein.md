# Contributor Report: Fisher Saperstein, Allie

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

Allie Fisher Saperstein (often documented as **Ariella Saperstein**) is a prominent political donor and philanthropic professional deeply integrated into the infrastructure of Zionist advocacy and pro-Israel policy-making. She is a member of the **Fisher family**, the founders of **Gap Inc.**, and is the daughter of **John J. Fisher**, the chairman of Gap Inc. and founder of the family office **Pisces Inc.** Saperstein holds leadership roles in influential grantmaking organizations that prioritize the strengthening of the U.S.-Israel relationship and the promotion of Zionist discourse in North America.

### 1. Identity and Family Connection to Gap Inc./Pisces Inc.
Allie Fisher Saperstein is the daughter of **John J. Fisher** and **Laura Fisher**. Her father is a billionaire heir to the Gap Inc. fortune and the owner of the Oakland Athletics. He also manages the family’s wealth through **Pisces Inc.**
*   **Documentation:** Real estate records in San Francisco list "Michael G. Saperstein and Allie Fisher Saperstein" as owners of a property, identifying her by her married and maiden names.
    *   Source: Homes.com - 40 Russell St, San Francisco Property Details (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Documentation:** John J. Fisher is identified as the founder and chairman of **Pisces Inc.** and a core member of the family that founded **Gap Inc.**
    *   [Source: Forbes - John Fisher Profile](https://www.forbes.com/profile/john-fisher/)

### 2. Leadership in Zionist Advocacy Organizations
Saperstein is a central figure in the **Maimonides Fund**, a private grantmaking organization that funds initiatives in North America and Israel. Her work focuses on policy analysis, advocacy, and strategic communications to reinforce the U.S.-Israel bond.

*   **Maimonides Fund (Director of Policy Initiatives):** Saperstein serves as the Director of Policy Initiatives at the Maimonides Fund. In this role, she oversees a "hub for policy analysis, advocacy, and strategic communications related to... Israel, antisemitism, and viewpoint diversity."
    *   [Source: Maimonides Fund - Establishment of Department of Policy Initiatives](https://maimonidesfund.org/maimonides-fund-establishes-department-of-policy-initiatives/)
*   **SAPIR Journal (Associate Publisher):** She is the Associate Publisher of **SAPIR**, a quarterly journal published by the Maimonides Fund that focuses on "ideas for a thriving Jewish future" and actively critiques anti-Zionist movements.
    *   Source: SAPIR Journal - Team Bio for Ariella Saperstein (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   Source: SAPIR Journal - About Us (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Documented Support for Zionist Entities
Saperstein and the Fisher family have established long-standing financial and leadership ties to major Zionist organizations, specifically the **Koret Foundation** and **Birthright Israel**.

*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family, through Donald Fisher and later John Fisher, have been foundational to the Koret Foundation’s pro-Israel mission. The foundation is a major funder of the **Koret Distinguished Fellows** (such as Daniel Gordis) who advocate for Zionism.
    *   Source: Koret Foundation - Board and Leadership (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
    *   Source: SAPIR Journal - Testimonial by Koret Distinguished Fellow Daniel Gordis (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Birthright Israel:** Saperstein’s family office and associated foundations are regular contributors to the **Birthright Israel Foundation**, which funds trips to Israel designed to strengthen Zionist identity among young Jews.
    *   Source: Birthright Israel Foundation - Annual Reports (Donor Recognition) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Zionist Strategy & Activism:** In her writings and professional capacity, Saperstein analyzes the effectiveness of "pro-Israel movement" strategies and advocates for more effective pro-Israel storytelling to counter Palestinian activism.
    *   Source: SAPIR Journal - "Actually, Feelings Don’t Care About Your Facts" (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 4. Professional Background in Israel Advocacy
Prior to her current roles, Saperstein’s career was centered on Israel-focused grantmaking at other major foundations.
*   **Helmsley Charitable Trust:** She previously served as the Israel Program Officer for the **Leona M. and Harry B. Helmsley Charitable Trust**, which is one of the largest private funders of healthcare and research in Israel, with a stated goal of strengthening the country.
