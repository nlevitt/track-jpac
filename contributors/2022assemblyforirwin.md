# Contributor Report: Irwin for Assembly 2022

The committee **Irwin for Assembly 2022** represents the political campaign of **Jacqui Irwin**, a Democratic member of the California State Assembly representing District 42 (formerly District 44). Documentation indicates a consistent relationship between Irwin’s political platform and organizations focused on Zionist advocacy and the strengthening of U.S.-Israel ties.

### 1. Endorsements and Organizational Affiliations
Irwin has been formally endorsed by advocacy groups that prioritize the U.S.-Israel relationship.
*   **Democratic Majority for Israel (DMFI) / Democrats for Israel (DFI):** Irwin is identified as a candidate supported by **Democrats for Israel Los Angeles (DFI-LA)**, an organization that requires candidates to complete a detailed questionnaire regarding their support for the State of Israel and their commitment to opposing "anti-Israel" curricula in public education.
    *   [Source: Democrats for Israel Los Angeles - Endorsements](https://www.dfi-la.org/endorsed)
*   **California Legislative Jewish Caucus:** Irwin is an active member of this caucus, which serves as a primary vehicle for pro-Israel advocacy within the California State Legislature. The caucus has historically supported legislation such as **AB 2844**, aimed at discouraging participation in the **Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS)** movement against Israel.
    *   [Source: California Legislative Jewish Caucus - About the Caucus](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/members)
    *   [Source: CounterPunch - Analysis of Jewish Caucus Pro-Israel Advocacy](https://www.counterpunch.org/2024/08/20/who-are-they-the-ca-legislative-jewish-caucus/)

### 2. Legislative Actions and Public Statements
Irwin’s legislative record and public communications reflect a documented alignment with Zionist objectives.
*   **Support for Israel Following October 7:** On October 7, 2023, Irwin co-signed a formal statement on behalf of the Jewish Caucus expressing "enduring solidarity" with the State of Israel and asserting its "right and responsibility to defend its civilians."
    *   [Source: Official Statement on Terrorist Attacks Against Israel](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/20231007-california-legislative-jewish-caucus-releases-statement-horrific-terrorist-attacks)
*   **Opposition to Ceasefire Demands:** In January 2024, Irwin co-signed a letter to legislative colleagues that characterized pro-Palestine protests—specifically those led by Jewish Voice for Peace—as contributing to a "toxic anti-Jewish environment" and reaffirmed support for Israel’s military actions in Gaza.
    *   [Source: Jewish Caucus Letter Re: October 7 and 2024 Legislative Year](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/20240103-jewish-caucus-sends-letter-colleagues-re-october-7-attack-rising-antisemitism-and)
*   **Commemorative Resolutions:** Irwin participated in the unanimous passage of resolutions in 2018 (e.g., SR 109 and AR 101) which congratulated the State of Israel on its 70th anniversary and praised its "advanced, entrepreneurial economy."
    *   [Source: Resolutions Honoring Israel at 70](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/20180516-california-senate-and-assembly-pass-resolutions-honoring-israel-70)

### 3. Diplomatic and Advocacy Trips
Irwin has engaged in direct diplomatic missions to Israel funded by pro-Israel advocacy organizations.
*   **AJC Project Interchange Trip (2018):** Irwin was part of a 2018 delegation of California lawmakers who traveled to Israel. The trip was funded by the **American Jewish Committee’s (AJC) Project Interchange**, an organization dedicated to providing "first-hand" perspectives of Israel to influential leaders. The itinerary included meetings with Israeli government and security officials.
    *   [Source: California Lawmakers’ Trip to Israel](https://jewishcaucus.legislature.ca.gov/news/20180815-trip-jittery-israel-eye-opener-california-assemblymembers)

### 4. Critical Documentation
Organizations critical of Zionist influence in U.S. politics have documented Irwin's record as part of a "pro-Israel faction" in the California legislature.
*   **Reverse Canary Mission:** This tracking project identifies Irwin as "complicit in apartheid" due to her caucus's statements mischaracterizing pro-Palestine advocacy and its focus on funding security for Jewish institutions while reportedly "erasing Palestinians from political and educational discourse."
    *   [Source: Reverse Canary Mission Profile - Jacqui Irwin](https://reversecanarymission.org/jacqui-irwin/)
*   **LA Progressive:** Independent reporting has identified Irwin as part of a group of "progressive except for Palestine" (PEP) lawmakers who support liberal domestic policies while maintaining a "blanket approval" of Israeli military and occupation policies.
    *   [Source: LA Progressive - Report on the California Legislative Jewish Caucus](https://www.laprogressive.com/middle-east/ca-legislative-jewish-caucus)
