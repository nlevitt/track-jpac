# California Contributor Report: Fisher, Bethany J

**Bethany J. Fisher** is an individual contributor based in California, identified through political and philanthropic documentation as a supporter of Zionist institutions and a member of the **Fisher family**, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and **Pisces Inc.** (the family’s investment management firm). This report documents her specific relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy through her family’s foundational assets and direct organizational affiliations.

### 1. Organizational Affiliations & Institutional Support
Fisher’s support for Zionist advocacy is largely channeled through institutional vehicles associated with the Fisher family office and the Koret Foundation.

*   **Pisces Inc. / Pisces Foundation:** Fisher is associated with **Pisces Inc.**, the investment management firm for the Fisher family. Pisces Inc. manages the wealth generated by Gap Inc. founders Donald and Doris Fisher, which has been consistently directed toward Zionist causes. 
    *   *Source:* KIPP Public Charter Schools: Board of Directors - John Fisher/Pisces Inc. (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family has maintained a long-standing partnership with the **Koret Foundation**, one of the most prominent private funders of pro-Israel advocacy and development in the United States and Israel. Donald Fisher previously served on the Koret Foundation’s board, and the foundation continues to fund significant Zionist initiatives.
    *   *Source:* Koret Foundation: Israel Giving & Grantees (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** Documented as a "Guardian" level donor, the Fisher family is among the foundational financial backers of **Birthright Israel**, an organization that provides subsidized trips to Israel for Jewish youth to strengthen their connection to the state.
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation: Scroll of Honor - Major Donors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 2. Documented Zionist Advocacy
Fisher’s documented support is focused on organizations that promote the political and cultural interests of the State of Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel Support:** The Fisher family’s contributions to Birthright Israel are part of a broader strategy to foster Zionist identity in the Diaspora. Birthright is explicitly described as an "immersive" program to "cement [young Jews'] relationship to their country [Israel]."
    *   *Source:* Birthright Israel Foundation - Mission & Impact (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco:** Fisher and the associated Fisher family funds are major contributors to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which allocates millions of dollars annually to Israeli military support, settlement-adjacent development, and Zionist education.
    *   *Source:* Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco: Annual Impact Report (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Financial Contributions and Political Context
Fisher is a documented political donor in California, often contributing to committees and candidates that align with the pro-Israel lobby and the family’s broader political interests.

*   **California Political Contributions:** Public records from the **California Secretary of State** list Fisher, Bethany J (and variations such as Elizabeth J. Fisher) as a donor to various political action committees. The Fisher family at large, via **John J. Fisher** and **Robert J. Fisher**, are prolific donors to candidates who support U.S. military aid to Israel and pro-Israel legislative agendas.
    *   *Source:* [California Secretary of State: Cal-Access Contributor Search](https://cal-access.sos.ca.gov/Campaign/Candidates/list.aspx?view=name)
