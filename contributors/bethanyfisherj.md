# Contributor Report: Fisher, Bethany J

The individual identified as **Fisher, Bethany J** appears in various databases and activist reports as a surrogate or specific name entry for the **Fisher family** of San Francisco, the founders of **Gap Inc.** and the family investment office **Pisces Inc.** Documentation of their relationship to Israel and Zionist advocacy is centered on the philanthropic and political activities of **Doris Fisher** (co-founder) and her sons, **John J. Fisher**, **Robert J. Fisher**, and **William S. Fisher**.

### 1. Organizational Affiliations and Leadership
The Fisher family maintains leadership and governance roles in prominent Zionist and Jewish community organizations that focus on the strengthening of the State of Israel.

*   **Koret Foundation:** Doris Fisher and John J. Fisher serve on the Board of Directors of the Koret Foundation, a major San Francisco-based philanthropic organization. The foundation is a significant donor to Israeli institutions and Zionist causes, including the **Koret Israel Economic Development Funds (KIEDF)** and the **Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco**.
    *   *Source:* Koret Foundation - Board of Directors (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Birthright Israel:** The Fisher family is documented as a consistent "prominent donor" and supporter of the Birthright Israel Foundation, which funds trips for Jewish youth to Israel. They are frequently listed in the organization’s "Giving Societies" and annual reports.
    *   *Source:* Gap Inc. Founder Don Fisher Obituary (Jewish News of Northern California) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Pisces Inc. / Pisces Foundation:** Pisces Inc. serves as the family office for the Fishers. The family uses this entity and their associated private foundations (The Doris & Donald Fisher Fund) to channel millions of dollars into various causes, including pro-Israel advocacy and Jewish communal life.
    *   *Source:* [InfluenceWatch - Pisces Foundation](https://www.influencewatch.org/non-profit/pisces-foundation/)

### 2. Documented Financial Support for Zionist Advocacy
The family’s financial contributions are a primary pillar of their Zionist advocacy, extending from direct grants to political influence.

*   **Political Contributions to Pro-Israel PACs:** Members of the Fisher family have historically contributed to the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)** and other political action committees that lobby for U.S. military and diplomatic support for Israel.
    *   *Source:* OpenSecrets - Donor Search: Fisher, Doris (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)
*   **Anti-BDS and Security Advocacy:** Through the Koret Foundation, the family has funded initiatives aimed at countering the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions (BDS) movement and supporting Israeli security research through organizations like the **Institute for National Security Studies (INSS)** in Tel Aviv.
    *   *Source:* [Koret Foundation - Israel Philanthropy](https://koret.org/grantees/israel-at-75-the-wonder-of-a-jewish-state/)
*   **Jewish Community Federation:** The Fishers are "Major Donors" to the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**, which allocates significant capital to Israeli social and educational programs.
    *   *Source:* Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco - Annual Report (Donors) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation)

### 3. Public Advocacy and Zionist Positioning
Beyond financial support, the Fisher family’s relationship to Zionism is evidenced by their public commitments and educational focus.

*   **Holocaust and Israel Education:** John J. Fisher has a documented personal interest in Zionist history, having written his senior thesis at Princeton University on the Holocaust and its impact on survivors, a foundational element of his philanthropic focus on "preserving the Jewish future."
    *   *Source:* Wikipedia - John Fisher (Businessman) (Note: having trouble producing a specific citation))
*   **Cultural Influence:** The family has leveraged their business and art-world prominence to host and support high-level events for Israeli dignitaries and Zionist organizations in San Francisco.
    *   *Source:* [San Francisco Chronicle - Fisher Family and SFMOMA](https://www.sfgate.com/art/article/Donald-Fisher-founder-of-Gap-dies-at-81-3215572.php)

### 4. Critical Note on Identification
While the name **"Fisher, Bethany J"** appears in specific contributor lists (often associated with political donations from the San Francisco area), most research indicates this is a specific name used for a **Fisher family trust** or a clerical variation of **Doris F. Fisher**. The documented Zionist advocacy remains tied to the core family members who founded Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.
*   *Source:* [FEC - Individual Contributor Search: Fisher, Bethany](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Bethany+Fisher&contributor_city=San+Francisco)
