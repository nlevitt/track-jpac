# Contributor Report: FISHER, KEVIN

*Let us know if you spot an inaccuracy: trackjpac@gmail.com*

### Overview
Kevin M. Fisher is a prominent San Francisco-based philanthropist and political donor associated with the wealth generated by Gap Inc., founded by his parents, Donald and Doris Fisher. He serves as a managing director at Pisces Inc., the Fisher family’s private investment office, and holds leadership roles in several influential foundations that direct significant capital toward Israeli institutions and Zionist advocacy organizations.

### Political Contributions and Lobbying
Fisher’s political giving frequently aligns with pro-Israel lobbying interests. He is a consistent donor to candidates and political action committees (PACs) that prioritize the maintenance of U.S. military aid to Israel.

*   **AIPAC / United Democracy Project:** Kevin Fisher has contributed to the **United Democracy Project**, a super PAC affiliated with the **American Israel Public Affairs Committee (AIPAC)**. This PAC spends millions to defeat political candidates who are critical of the Israeli government.
*   **Documentation:** Federal Election Commission (FEC) filings show contributions from Kevin Fisher (Pisces Inc.) to pro-Israel candidates and committees. 
*   **Source:** [FEC Individual Contributor Search: Fisher, Kevin (Pisces Inc)](https://www.fec.gov/data/receipts/individual-contributions/?contributor_name=Kevin+Fisher&contributor_employer=Pisces)

### Summary of Influence
Fisher’s relationship to Zionist advocacy is primarily characterized by his institutional control over large-scale philanthropic capital. By directing the Koret Foundation and the Fisher family’s various funds, he ensures a continuous flow of financial resources to:
1. **Educational Initiatives:** Strengthening Zionist narratives through Birthright and the Shalom Hartman Institute.
2. **Institutional Support:** Funding major Israeli academic and social infrastructure.
3. **Political Leverage:** Supporting AIPAC-aligned PACs to influence U.S. foreign policy in favor of the Israeli state.
