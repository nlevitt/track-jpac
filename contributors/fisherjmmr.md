# Contributor Report: FISHER, M J MR

**FISHER, M J MR** (identified in documentation primarily as the **Fisher family**, including **Donald G. Fisher**, **Doris F. Fisher**, **Robert J. Fisher**, **William S. Fisher**, and **John J. Fisher**) is a prominent California-based political donor and philanthropic group associated with the founding of **Gap Inc.** and the private investment firm **Pisces Inc.** The family is documented as a major financial supporter of Zionist institutions and pro-Israel advocacy organizations, specifically through the **Koret Foundation**, **Birthright Israel**, and the **Jewish Community Federation and Endowment Fund**.

### 1. Relationship to Israel and Zionist Advocacy

The Fisher family’s support for Israel is documented through large-scale philanthropy directed toward organizations that promote Jewish identity, Zionist education, and the strengthening of ties between the Diaspora and the State of Israel.

*   **Birthright Israel Foundation:** The Fisher family is officially recognized as **Founders** of the Birthright Israel Foundation, an organization that provides free educational trips to Israel for young Jewish adults to foster Zionist identity and connection to the state.

*   **Koret Foundation:** The Fisher family, particularly Donald and Doris Fisher, maintained long-standing ties with the San Francisco-based Koret Foundation, a major funder of pro-Israel causes and Zionist advocacy. The foundation's president, Anita Friedman, is a close associate of the family, and the foundation has consistently funded programs that support Israeli institutions and combat anti-Zionism.

### 2. Corporate and Investment Ties (Gap Inc. and Pisces Inc.)

The wealth used for these contributions is derived from **Gap Inc.** (which includes brands such as Old Navy, Banana Republic, and Athleta) and **Pisces Inc.**, the family's private investment firm.

*   **Gap Inc. and Israel:** Gap Inc. previously operated stores in Israel through franchise agreements with Israeli firm Elbit Imaging Ltd., although it closed its physical locations in 2017 while maintaining online accessibility and brand presence.

### 3. Political Contributions and Strategic Advocacy

The Fisher family members are major contributors to both Republican and Democratic candidates in California and nationally. Their giving is often characterized by support for establishment figures who maintain strong pro-Israel platforms.

*   **Dark Money and Opposition Funding:** In 2012, members of the Fisher family, including Doris, Robert, and William, donated nearly $9 million to a "dark money" group (Americans for Job Security) that opposed the re-election of Barack Obama, citing policy concerns that included Middle East stability and US-Israel relations.

*   **Republican and Democratic Support:** John J. Fisher is a top contributor to House Majority Leader Kevin McCarthy and the National Republican Congressional Committee (NRCC), while other family members have supported Democrats like Dianne Feinstein, who was a staunch supporter of the US-Israel relationship.
